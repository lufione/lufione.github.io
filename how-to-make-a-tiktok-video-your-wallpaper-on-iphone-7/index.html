How To Make A Tiktok Video Your Wallpaper On Iphone 7

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a TikTok fan? Do you want to have your favorite TikTok video as your wallpaper on your iPhone 7? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to make a TikTok video your wallpaper on iPhone 7.

Step 1: Download the TikTok Video

The first step is to download the TikTok video that you want to set as your wallpaper. Open the TikTok app, find the video, and click on the "Share" button. Select the "Save Video" option to save the video to your camera roll.

Step 2: Download the "IntoLive" App

Next, you need to download the "IntoLive" app from the App Store. This app allows you to convert videos into live wallpapers. Once you have downloaded the app, open it and select the video that you want to use as your wallpaper.

Step 3: Customize Your Wallpaper

After selecting the video, you can customize your wallpaper by adjusting the start and end points of the video. You can also add filters, text, and music to your wallpaper to make it more personalized.

Step 4: Save and Set Your Wallpaper

Once you have customized your wallpaper, click on the "Save" button. Your wallpaper will be saved to your camera roll. To set it as your wallpaper, go to your iPhone's "Settings," select "Wallpaper," and choose the video from your camera roll. You can choose to set the wallpaper for your lock screen, home screen, or both.

FAQs

Can I Use Any TikTok Video as My Wallpaper?

Yes, you can use any TikTok video as your wallpaper as long as you have downloaded it to your camera roll.

Is the IntoLive App Free?

The IntoLive app is free to download, but it offers in-app purchases for additional features.

Can I Customize My Wallpaper After Setting It?

Yes, you can customize your wallpaper at any time by going back to the IntoLive app and editing the video.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make the most out of your TikTok wallpaper:

  • Choose a video with a high resolution for better quality wallpaper.
  • Experiment with different filters, text, and music to create a unique wallpaper.
  • Use a short video for your wallpaper to avoid draining your battery.
  • Change your wallpaper regularly to keep your phone looking fresh and interesting.

That's it! You have successfully made a TikTok video your wallpaper on iPhone 7. We hope this article was helpful. Happy customizing!
