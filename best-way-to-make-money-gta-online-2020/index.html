Best Way To Make Money In Gta Online 2020

Written by Aprilia Jun 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

GTA Online is an online multiplayer game that allows players to participate in a wide range of criminal activities to earn money. However, it can be difficult to know where to start or what the best methods are to make money in the game. In this article, we will provide tips and tricks for the best way to make money in GTA Online in 2020.

1. Invest in a Business

One of the best ways to make money in GTA Online is by investing in a business. After completing the initial setup missions, players can start earning income from businesses such as the nightclub, bunker, and CEO office. These businesses require an initial investment but can generate passive income over time.

2. Participate in Heists

Heists are a great way to earn a large amount of money in a short period of time. These missions require teamwork and coordination with other players, but the payout can be well worth it. Players can also replay heists multiple times for even more money.

3. Utilize the Stock Market

Players can also make money in GTA Online by investing in the stock market. Players can monitor the in-game stock market and buy and sell stocks to earn a profit. It's important to do research and monitor the market to make informed decisions.

4. Complete Daily Objectives

Completing daily objectives can earn players a sizeable amount of money and RP. These objectives are updated daily and can include tasks such as stealing a vehicle or participating in a deathmatch.

5. Participate in Races

Participating in races is a fun way to earn money and RP. Players can participate in races alone or with friends and earn money based on their performance. There are also different types of races, such as stunt races and off-road races, that offer different payouts.

6. Rob Convenience Stores

Robbing convenience stores is a quick and easy way to earn money in GTA Online. Players can point a gun at the cashier and demand money, then quickly escape before the police arrive. This method does not provide a large payout but is a good option for players who need quick cash.

7. Sell Stolen Vehicles

Players can steal vehicles and sell them to earn money. The amount of money earned depends on the type of vehicle and its condition. Players can sell vehicles to the chop shop or export them to other countries for a higher payout.

8. Complete Special Cargo Missions

Special cargo missions involve collecting and delivering cargo to earn money. These missions require players to have a CEO office and a warehouse, but they can generate a significant amount of money over time.

9. Participate in Contact Missions

Contact missions involve completing tasks for NPCs to earn money and RP. These missions can be completed alone or with friends and offer varying payouts depending on the difficulty of the mission.

10. Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many ways to make money in GTA Online in 2020. Players can invest in businesses, participate in heists and races, utilize the stock market, complete daily objectives, rob convenience stores, sell stolen vehicles, complete special cargo missions, and participate in contact missions. By combining these methods, players can quickly earn money and progress in the game.
