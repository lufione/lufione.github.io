How To Get Payment From Youtube In Pakistan A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

YouTube has become a popular platform for content creators to showcase their talents and make money. However, getting paid from YouTube in Pakistan can be challenging due to various restrictions and limitations. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting paid from YouTube in Pakistan.

Step 1: Create a YouTube Channel

The first step in getting paid from YouTube is to create a YouTube channel. You can do this by signing in to your Google account and navigating to YouTube. Once there, you can click on the “Create a Channel” button and follow the prompts to set up your channel.

Step 2: Meet the Eligibility Criteria

In order to get paid from YouTube, you must meet certain eligibility criteria. These include having at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months. You must also comply with YouTube’s terms of service and community guidelines.

Step 3: Monetize Your Videos

Once you meet the eligibility criteria, you can monetize your videos by enabling monetization in your YouTube account settings. This will allow YouTube to place ads on your videos and pay you a percentage of the ad revenue.

Step 4: Set Up Google AdSense

In order to receive payment from YouTube, you must set up a Google AdSense account. This will allow YouTube to pay you through your AdSense account. To set up AdSense, you will need to provide your personal and bank account information.

Step 5: Reach the Payment Threshold

YouTube pays its creators once they reach a payment threshold. In Pakistan, the payment threshold is $100. Once you earn $100 in ad revenue, YouTube will send a payment to your AdSense account.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to get paid from YouTube?

It can take up to 60 days for YouTube to process your payment and send it to your AdSense account.

2. How can I increase my ad revenue?

You can increase your ad revenue by creating high-quality content that is engaging and relevant to your audience. You can also promote your videos on social media and collaborate with other creators.

3. Are there any restrictions on the type of content I can monetize?

Yes, there are certain types of content that YouTube does not allow to be monetized. These include content that is violent, hateful, or sexually explicit.

Tips and Tricks

1. Engage with your audience

Engaging with your audience can help you build a loyal following and increase your views and ad revenue. Respond to comments and messages and ask for feedback on your videos.

2. Use keywords and tags

Using relevant keywords and tags can help your videos appear in search results and increase your views and ad revenue. Make sure to research keywords and tags that are relevant to your content.

3. Collaborate with other creators

Collaborating with other creators can help you reach new audiences and increase your views and ad revenue. Look for creators in your niche and reach out to them to collaborate on a video. In conclusion, getting paid from YouTube in Pakistan is possible, but it requires meeting certain eligibility criteria and following the steps outlined in this article. By creating high-quality content, engaging with your audience, and collaborating with other creators, you can increase your views and ad revenue and achieve success on YouTube.
