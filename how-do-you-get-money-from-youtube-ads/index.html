How Do You Get Money From Youtube Ads

Written by Bardi Sep 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

YouTube is a popular video-sharing platform that offers an opportunity for content creators to monetize their videos. One of the ways to earn money on YouTube is through ads. In this article, we will take you through the step-by-step process of earning money from YouTube ads.

Step 1: Create a YouTube Channel

The first step to earning money from YouTube ads is to create a YouTube channel. You can create a channel for free by signing up with your Google account. Choose a name for your channel that reflects the type of content you plan to upload.

Step 2: Meet YouTube's Partner Program Requirements

Before you can earn money from YouTube ads, you need to meet the platform's Partner Program requirements. You must have at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months. You also need to adhere to YouTube's community guidelines and terms of service.

Step 3: Enable Monetization on Your Videos

Once you meet the Partner Program requirements, you can enable monetization on your videos. Go to the YouTube Studio and click on the Monetization tab. Follow the prompts to set up an AdSense account and link it to your YouTube channel.

Step 4: Create Ad-Friendly Content

To earn money from YouTube ads, you need to create content that is ad-friendly. This means creating high-quality videos that are suitable for advertisers. Avoid controversial or sensitive topics that could potentially harm your chances of getting ads on your videos.

Step 5: Promote Your Videos

The more views your videos get, the more money you can earn from YouTube ads. Promote your videos on social media platforms and collaborate with other YouTubers to increase your reach. You can also use keywords and tags to optimize your videos for search engines.

Step 6: Track Your Earnings

YouTube provides analytics that show you how much money you are earning from ads. You can track your earnings in the YouTube Studio and adjust your content strategy to maximize your earnings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much money can you earn from YouTube ads?

A: The amount of money you can earn from YouTube ads depends on various factors, such as the number of views, the type of ads, and the niche. On average, YouTubers can earn between $0.01 to $0.03 per view.

Q: How do you get paid for YouTube ads?

A: YouTube pays its content creators through AdSense. Once you reach the payment threshold, which is $100, you can withdraw your earnings through your linked AdSense account.

Q: Can you monetize all types of videos on YouTube?

A: No, not all types of videos are eligible for monetization. YouTube has strict guidelines on what type of content can be monetized. Videos that contain inappropriate or harmful content are not eligible for monetization.

Tips and Tricks

  • Engage with your audience by responding to comments and creating videos based on their feedback.
  • Collaborate with other YouTubers to increase your reach and exposure.
  • Experiment with different types of content to see what works best for your audience.
  • Stay up to date with YouTube's policies and guidelines to ensure your content is always ad-friendly.

By following these steps, you can start earning money from YouTube ads. Remember, creating high-quality content and engaging with your audience is key to building a successful YouTube channel.
