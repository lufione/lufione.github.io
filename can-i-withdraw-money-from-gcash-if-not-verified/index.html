Can I Withdraw Money From Gcash If Not Verified

Written by Luffy Sep 09, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are a GCash user, you might be wondering if you can withdraw money from your account even if you haven't verified it yet. In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with all the necessary information you need to know about GCash verification and withdrawal process.

What is GCash Verification?

GCash verification is a process where you provide your personal information to GCash to prove your identity. This is necessary for security reasons and to comply with the rules and regulations set by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP). Once you are verified, you can access all the features of GCash, including withdraw money from your account.

How to Verify Your GCash Account?

There are two ways to verify your GCash account. The first one is through the GCash app, and the second one is through a partner outlet. To verify your account through the app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the GCash app on your mobile device.
  2. Tap on the menu icon (three horizontal lines) on the upper left corner of the screen.
  3. Select 'Verify Now'.
  4. Provide all the necessary personal information and follow the instructions on the screen.
  5. Submit your application and wait for the confirmation message.

To verify your account through a partner outlet, you need to visit any of the following establishments:

  • SM Business Centers
  • Robinsons Business Centers
  • Puregold
  • 7-Eleven
  • Bayad Center
  • Villarica Pawnshop

Bring a valid ID and your mobile device with the GCash app installed. Follow the instructions of the outlet staff and wait for the confirmation message.

Can You Withdraw Money from GCash if Not Verified?

The short answer is no. You need to verify your GCash account before you can withdraw money from it. This is a security measure to prevent fraud and protect your account from unauthorized transactions. Without verification, you can only use your GCash account to pay bills, buy load, and transfer money to other GCash users.

What are the Benefits of GCash Verification?

Aside from being able to withdraw money from your account, there are other benefits of GCash verification:

  • Higher transaction limits - Verified users have higher transaction limits compared to unverified users.
  • Access to exclusive promos and discounts - GCash offers exclusive promos and discounts to verified users.
  • Increased security - Verifying your account adds an extra layer of security to prevent unauthorized access and fraudulent activities.

Conclusion

GCash verification is a simple process that you can do through the app or partner outlets. It is necessary if you want to withdraw money from your account and access other exclusive features. Don't hesitate to verify your account to enjoy the benefits of being a verified GCash user.
