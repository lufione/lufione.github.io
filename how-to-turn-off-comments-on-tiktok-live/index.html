How To Turn Off Comments On Tiktok Live In 2023

Written by William May 13, 2023 · 6 min read
How to Turn Off Comments on TikTok Live in 2023 - Tips and Tricks

Are you tired of dealing with inappropriate comments during your TikTok Live sessions? Do you want to create a safer and more positive environment for your viewers? Turning off comments on TikTok Live can help you achieve that. In this article, we’ll show you how to do it in just a few easy steps.

Step 1: Start Your TikTok Live Session

The first thing you need to do is start your TikTok Live session. Go to your profile page and click on the plus sign icon at the bottom of the screen. Then, select “Live” from the options that appear. You can choose to go live immediately or schedule a future live session.

Step 2: Tap on the Three Dots Icon

Once you’re in your live session, tap on the three dots icon located at the bottom right corner of the screen. This will open up a menu with various options.

Step 3: Select “Turn Off Commenting”

From the menu, select “Turn Off Commenting”. This will disable comments for your live session. You can still interact with your viewers through the “Q&A” feature or by using stickers and emojis.

Step 4: Turn On Commenting (Optional)

If you want to turn commenting back on during your live session, simply tap on the three dots icon again and select “Turn On Commenting”. This will allow your viewers to leave comments again.

Step 5: End Your Live Session

When you’re ready to end your live session, tap on the “X” icon located at the top left corner of the screen. This will take you out of your live session and back to your profile page.

FAQ

Why Should I Turn Off Comments on TikTok Live?

Turning off comments can help create a safer and more positive environment for your viewers. It can also prevent inappropriate or spam comments from appearing during your live session.

Can I Turn Off Comments for All My TikTok Live Sessions?

Yes, you can turn off comments for all your TikTok Live sessions by going to your profile page, tapping on the three dots icon, selecting “Privacy”, and then turning on the “Turn Off Commenting” feature.

Can I Still Interact with My Viewers?

Yes, you can still interact with your viewers through the “Q&A” feature or by using stickers and emojis. Turning off comments only disables the ability for viewers to leave comments.

Tips and Tricks

Use the “Q&A” Feature to Interact with Your Viewers

The “Q&A” feature allows your viewers to ask you questions during your live session. You can then answer their questions live on camera. This is a great way to interact with your viewers without having to deal with inappropriate comments.

Use Stickers and Emojis to Engage with Your Viewers

You can use stickers and emojis to show your viewers that you’re listening to them and appreciate their support. This is a fun and easy way to engage with your viewers during your live session.

Moderate Your Comments

If you decide to keep comments on for your live session, make sure to moderate them regularly. Delete any inappropriate or spam comments and block any users who are being abusive or harassing.
