6. Slidejoy

7. Dosh

Dosh is a cashback app that allows you to earn money by shopping at participating stores. You can earn up to 10% cashback on your purchases. Dosh is available on both Android and iOS platforms.

8. Drop

Drop is a cashback app that allows you to earn points by shopping at participating stores. You can redeem these points for gift cards or cash via PayPal. Drop is available on both Android and iOS platforms.

9. InboxDollars

InboxDollars is an app that pays you for completing surveys, watching videos, and reading emails. You can earn up to $5 per survey. The app is available on both Android and iOS platforms.

10. Achievement

Achievement is an app that pays you for staying healthy. You can earn points by tracking your steps, sleep, and other healthy activities. These points can be redeemed for cash via PayPal. The app is available on both Android and iOS platforms.
