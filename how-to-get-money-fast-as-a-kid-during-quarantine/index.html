How To Get Money Fast As A Kid During Quarantine

Written by William Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Being a kid during quarantine can be tough, especially when it comes to making money. With limited opportunities to work outside the home, it can be challenging to find ways to earn some extra cash. However, there are still plenty of ways to make money from home, and in this article, we'll explore some of the best options.

1. Sell Your Stuff Online

One of the easiest ways to make money as a kid during quarantine is to sell your stuff online. This could be anything from old toys and clothes to electronics and video games. There are many online marketplaces where you can sell your items, such as eBay, Amazon, and Facebook Marketplace. Just make sure to get your parents' permission and help before listing anything for sale.

2. Offer Your Services

Another way to make money as a kid is to offer your services to others. This could include things like pet-sitting, lawn care, or even virtual tutoring. You can advertise your services on social media or through word of mouth, and make sure to set fair prices for your time and effort.

3. Do Online Surveys

Many companies are willing to pay for feedback from consumers, and that includes kids too. You can sign up for online survey sites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, or Vindale Research to earn money for completing surveys. Just make sure to check with your parents before signing up, and be wary of any sites that ask for personal information.

4. Start a YouTube Channel

If you have a talent or interest that you're passionate about, consider starting a YouTube channel. You can make money from ads and sponsorships once you have enough subscribers and views. Just make sure to follow YouTube's community guidelines and get your parents' permission before creating a channel.

5. Freelance Writing or Graphic Design

If you enjoy writing or graphic design, you can offer your services as a freelancer. Many companies and individuals are looking for talented writers and designers to create content for their websites and social media. You can find freelance opportunities on sites like Upwork or Fiverr.

6. Create and Sell Crafts

If you're crafty, consider making and selling your creations online. You can sell your items on sites like Etsy or create your own website or social media page to showcase your work. Just make sure to factor in the cost of materials and time when setting prices.

7. Participate in Online Contests

Many companies and organizations hold online contests for kids, with prizes ranging from cash to gift cards. Keep an eye out for these contests on social media or through email newsletters, and make sure to follow the rules and guidelines carefully.

8. Offer Social Media Management

If you're savvy with social media, you can offer your services as a social media manager to small businesses or individuals. This could include creating and scheduling posts, responding to comments and messages, and growing their follower base. Just make sure to set fair prices for your time and expertise.

9. Complete Microtasks

There are many websites and apps that offer small tasks or gigs that you can complete for money. These could include things like data entry, transcription, or testing websites or apps. Some popular sites for microtasks include Amazon Mechanical Turk and Clickworker.

10. Create an Online Course

If you're an expert in a certain subject or skill, consider creating an online course to sell. You can use platforms like Udemy or Teachable to create and sell your course, and earn money each time someone enrolls. Just make sure to create high-quality content and market your course effectively.

Conclusion

Making money as a kid during quarantine might seem challenging, but with a little creativity and effort, it's definitely possible. Whether you sell your stuff online, offer your services, or start a YouTube channel, there are plenty of options to explore. Just make sure to get your parents' permission and help along the way, and always prioritize your safety and well-being.
