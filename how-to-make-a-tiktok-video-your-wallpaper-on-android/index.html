How to Make a TikTok Video Your Wallpaper on Android - 2023 Guide

If you're a TikTok addict, you probably want to keep your favorite videos in sight all the time. The good news is that you can now set a TikTok video as your wallpaper on Android. Here's how to do it in a few easy steps.

Step 1: Download TikTok and Wallpaper Setter Apps

First, make sure you have both the TikTok and Wallpaper Setter apps installed on your Android device. You can download them for free from Google Play Store.

Step 2: Find the TikTok Video You Want to Set as Wallpaper

Open TikTok and find the video you want to set as your wallpaper. Tap the "Share" icon and select "Live Photo" from the options.

Step 3: Save the Live Photo

The Live Photo will be saved to your camera roll. Open the Wallpaper Setter app and select "Live Wallpapers" from the options. Find the Live Photo you just saved and select it.

Step 4: Adjust the Live Photo Settings

You can now adjust the Live Photo settings to fit your preferences. You can change the size, position, and speed of the video. Once you're satisfied with the settings, tap "Set Wallpaper" to apply them.

Step 5: Enjoy Your TikTok Video Wallpaper

You can now go to your Android home screen and enjoy your TikTok video wallpaper. You can even share it with your friends and family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Set Any TikTok Video as Wallpaper?

Yes, you can set any TikTok video as your wallpaper as long as it's a Live Photo.

Is the Wallpaper Setter App Free?

Yes, the Wallpaper Setter app is available for free on Google Play Store.

Can I Change the Live Photo Settings Later?

Yes, you can always go back to the Wallpaper Setter app and change the Live Photo settings anytime you want.

Tips and Tricks

Choose a Short TikTok Video

Choose a short TikTok video to set as your wallpaper to avoid draining your battery and using up your phone's storage.

Experiment with the Live Photo Settings

Play around with the Live Photo settings to find the perfect fit for your wallpaper. You can adjust the speed, size, and position of the video.

Share Your TikTok Video Wallpaper

Show off your TikTok video wallpaper to your friends and family by sharing it on social media or through messaging apps.

Setting a TikTok video as your wallpaper on Android is a fun and easy way to personalize your phone. Try it out and enjoy your favorite TikTok videos all the time.
