How To Make 20 Dollars Fast As A Kid In 2023

Written by Oliver May 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a kid who wants to earn some extra cash? You might be surprised to know that there are many ways to make 20 dollars fast. Here are some tips to get you started:

Sell Your Stuff Online

One of the easiest ways to make money as a kid is to sell your stuff online. You can sell anything from old toys to clothes that you no longer wear. There are many websites that allow you to sell your items, such as eBay, Craigslist, and Facebook Marketplace. Just make sure to get your parent's permission before selling anything.

Offer Your Services

Another way to make money is to offer your services. Do you love animals? Offer to pet-sit for your neighbors. Are you good at mowing lawns? Offer to mow your neighbor's lawns for a fee. The possibilities are endless.

Do Chores for Your Parents

If you want to make money quickly, offer to do chores for your parents. This can include anything from washing dishes to cleaning the house. Ask your parents if they have any tasks that they need help with and negotiate a fair price.

Create and Sell Crafts

If you're creative, you can make and sell crafts. You can sell your crafts at local markets or online. Some ideas include creating jewelry, greeting cards, or even homemade soap.

Participate in Paid Surveys

Many companies are willing to pay for your opinion. You can participate in online surveys and earn money for each survey you complete. Just make sure to use a reputable website, such as Survey Junkie or Swagbucks.

Offer Tutoring Services

If you're good at a particular subject, offer your tutoring services to other kids in your area. You can charge a fee for each tutoring session and help other kids improve their grades.

Deliver Newspapers

Many local newspapers are looking for kids to deliver newspapers on a regular basis. This can be a great way to earn some extra cash while also getting some exercise.

Organize a Car Wash

Organizing a car wash can be a fun way to make money with your friends. Ask your parents if you can use their driveway and charge a fee for each car that you wash.

Sell Baked Goods

If you love to bake, you can sell your baked goods to your neighbors. Offer to bake cookies, cupcakes, or even bread for a fee. Just make sure to get your parent's permission before using the kitchen.

Offer Babysitting Services

Many parents are looking for a reliable babysitter. If you're responsible and love kids, offer your babysitting services to families in your area. You can charge an hourly rate and even offer special discounts for repeat customers.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are many ways to make 20 dollars fast as a kid. Whether you sell your stuff online, offer your services, or do chores for your parents, the possibilities are endless. Just make sure to get your parent's permission before starting any money-making activities and have fun!
