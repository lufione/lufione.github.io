How To See Instagram Posts In Order

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 · 5 min read

Instagram is one of the most popular social media platforms today, and its algorithm plays a big role in how users see posts in their feed. However, many users prefer to see posts in chronological order rather than the algorithm-based order that Instagram uses. If you're one of those users, you're in luck, because there are several ways to see Instagram posts in order.

Option 1: Use the "Most Recent" Feature

Instagram has a feature that allows you to see posts in chronological order. Here's how to use it:

  1. Open the Instagram app on your phone.
  2. Tap the three lines in the top right corner to open the menu.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the menu and tap "Settings."
  4. Tap "Account."
  5. Tap "Posts You've Liked."
  6. Tap the three dots in the top right corner.
  7. Select "Most Recent."

Once you've selected "Most Recent," your feed will be updated to show posts in chronological order.

Option 2: Use a Third-Party App

If you're looking for a more permanent solution to seeing posts in chronological order, you can use a third-party app. Here are some popular options:

  • Later
  • Hopper
  • Planoly

These apps allow you to schedule your Instagram posts and see them in chronological order. They also offer other features like analytics and the ability to repost content.

Option 3: Turn on Post Notifications

If you want to see posts from specific users in chronological order, you can turn on post notifications for their accounts. Here's how:

  1. Go to the user's profile.
  2. Tap the three dots in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Turn on Post Notifications."

Once you've turned on post notifications, you'll receive a notification every time the user posts something new. This is a great option if you only want to see posts from a few select users in chronological order.

Option 4: Create a List

You can also create a list of the users whose posts you want to see in chronological order. Here's how:

  1. Go to your profile.
  2. Tap the three lines in the top right corner to open the menu.
  3. Tap "Settings."
  4. Tap "Account."
  5. Tap "Following."
  6. Select the users you want to add to your list.
  7. Tap "Next."
  8. Name your list and tap "Done."

Once you've created your list, you can view it by going to your profile and tapping the three lines in the top right corner. Select "Lists" and then select the list you created. You'll see all the posts from the users on that list in chronological order.

Conclusion

There are several ways to see Instagram posts in order, whether it's using the "Most Recent" feature, a third-party app, turning on post notifications, or creating a list. Experiment with these options to find the one that works best for you and enjoy seeing Instagram posts in the order you prefer!
