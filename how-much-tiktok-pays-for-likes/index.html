How Does TikTok Payment System Work?

TikTok has a payment system that rewards creators for their content. The payment system is based on the number of likes, views, and engagement that a video receives. So, if a video has 10,000 views, the creator will be paid for 10 views per 1000 views.

How Much TikTok Pays for Likes?
