How Much Can We Earn From Youtube Shorts In India

Written by Luffy Apr 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

YouTube Shorts is a relatively new feature on YouTube that allows users to create and share short-form videos that are 60 seconds or less in length. With the increasing popularity of YouTube Shorts, a lot of people are wondering how much money they can earn from this feature. In this article, we will explore how much one can make from YouTube Shorts in India.

How Does YouTube Shorts Monetization Work?

Like other YouTube videos, you can monetize your Shorts by enabling ads on them. YouTube will then display ads on your Shorts, and you will earn a share of the revenue generated by those ads. However, to be eligible for monetization, you need to meet certain criteria.

Eligibility Criteria for Monetizing YouTube Shorts in India

To monetize your YouTube Shorts in India, you need to meet the following criteria:
  • Your YouTube channel should have at least 1,000 subscribers.
  • Your Shorts should have at least 10,000 views in the last 7 days.
  • You should comply with YouTube's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.
  • You should have an AdSense account linked to your YouTube channel.

How Much Can You Earn from YouTube Shorts in India?

The amount you can earn from YouTube Shorts in India depends on various factors, such as the number of views, the length of the Shorts, the region where the viewers are located, and the ad formats used. Typically, YouTube pays creators around $0.01 to $0.03 per view, which means you can earn around Rs. 0.75 to Rs. 2.25 per view in India. However, it is worth noting that the actual earnings may vary based on the above-mentioned factors. Also, YouTube takes a cut of the revenue generated by ads, so you will not receive the full amount.

Tips to Maximize Your Earnings from YouTube Shorts in India

Here are some tips that can help you maximize your earnings from YouTube Shorts in India:
  • Create engaging and high-quality Shorts that are likely to attract a lot of views.
  • Promote your Shorts on social media and other platforms to increase their visibility.
  • Use relevant hashtags to make it easier for viewers to discover your Shorts.
  • Experiment with different ad formats to see which ones work best for your Shorts.

Conclusion

YouTube Shorts is a promising feature that offers a new way for creators to reach audiences and earn money. While the earnings from Shorts may not be as high as those from longer videos, it is still a good source of income for creators who can create engaging and high-quality content. With the right strategy and effort, you can make a decent amount of money from YouTube Shorts in India.
