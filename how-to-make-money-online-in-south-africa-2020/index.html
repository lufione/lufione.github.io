How To Make Money Online In South Africa 2020

Written by Luffy Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

South Africa is a country with great potential for making money online. With the advancement of technology, the internet has become a platform for various income-generating opportunities. Here are some ways you can make money online in South Africa in 2020.

1. Freelancing

Freelancing is a great way to make money online in South Africa. You can offer your services as a writer, graphic designer, web developer, or any other skill you have. Websites like Upwork, Freelancer, and Fiverr are great platforms to get started.

2. Online Surveys

Online surveys are a quick and easy way to earn money online. Companies are always looking for feedback on their products and services, and they are willing to pay for it. Websites like Toluna, Swagbucks, and SurveySavvy are great places to start.

3. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a way to earn commission by promoting other people's products. You can do this by creating a blog or a YouTube channel and promoting products related to your niche. Websites like Amazon Associates and ClickBank are great platforms to get started.

4. Online Trading

Online trading is a way to buy and sell stocks, currencies, and other financial instruments online. Websites like eToro and Plus500 are great platforms to get started. However, it is important to do your research and understand the risks involved before investing.

5. Online Tutoring

If you have a skill or knowledge that others want to learn, you can offer your services as an online tutor. Websites like Tutor.com and Chegg are great platforms to get started. You can teach anything from math and science to English and music.

6. Dropshipping

Dropshipping is a way to sell products online without keeping an inventory. You can create an online store and promote products from suppliers. When a customer buys a product, the supplier ships it directly to the customer. Websites like Shopify and AliExpress are great platforms to get started.

7. Online Writing

If you have a passion for writing, you can make money online by creating content for websites and blogs. Websites like iWriter and Textbroker are great platforms to get started. You can also create your own blog and monetize it with ads or affiliate marketing.

8. Virtual Assistant

If you have administrative skills, you can offer your services as a virtual assistant. You can help businesses with tasks like data entry, email management, and social media management. Websites like Upwork and Freelancer are great platforms to get started.

9. Online Transcription

If you have good typing skills, you can offer your services as an online transcriptionist. You can transcribe audio and video files for businesses and individuals. Websites like TranscribeMe and Rev are great platforms to get started.

10. Online Design

If you have a talent for design, you can make money online by creating logos, business cards, and other graphic design work. Websites like 99designs and DesignCrowd are great platforms to get started. You can also create your own website and promote your services.

Conclusion

These are just a few ways you can make money online in South Africa in 2020. With the right skills and determination, you can turn the internet into a source of income. Remember to always do your research and understand the risks involved before investing your time and money.
