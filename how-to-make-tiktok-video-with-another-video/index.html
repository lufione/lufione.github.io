How to Make TikTok Video with Another Video

TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create and share short videos. One of the features of TikTok is the ability to use other videos to create your own. In this article, we will show you how to make TikTok video with another video.

Step 1: Choose a Video

The first step in making a TikTok video with another video is to choose the video you want to use. This can be a video you have saved on your phone or a video from another TikTok user. To use a video from another user, you will need to download it to your phone.

Step 2: Open TikTok

Once you have chosen the video you want to use, open TikTok on your phone. Tap on the plus sign at the bottom of the screen to create a new video.

Step 3: Choose the Video

In the TikTok video editor, tap on the "Upload" button and choose the video you want to use. The video will be added to your TikTok video editor.

Step 4: Edit the Video

Now that you have added the video to your TikTok video editor, you can edit it to fit your needs. You can trim the video, add filters or effects, and adjust the volume.

Step 5: Add Music

TikTok videos are often accompanied by music. To add music to your video, tap on the "Sounds" icon and choose a song from the library. You can also use your own music if it is saved on your phone.

Step 6: Add Text or Stickers

If you want to add text or stickers to your video, tap on the "Text" or "Stickers" icon and choose the ones you want to use. You can move and resize the text or stickers to fit your video.

Step 7: Preview the Video

Before you post your video, preview it to make sure it looks how you want it to. You can make any final edits before posting.

Step 8: Post the Video

Once you are happy with your video, tap on the "Next" button and choose the audience you want to share it with. You can post the video to your profile or share it with your friends.

Step 9: Engage with Your Audience

After posting your video, engage with your audience by responding to comments and liking other users' videos. This will help increase your visibility on TikTok.

Step 10: Keep Creating

The more you create on TikTok, the more likely you are to gain followers and increase your visibility on the platform. Keep creating and experimenting with different types of videos to find what works best for you.

Conclusion

In conclusion, making a TikTok video with another video is a fun and creative way to create content on the platform. By following these steps, you can easily create your own TikTok video using another video as a starting point. Remember to keep experimenting and engaging with your audience to increase your visibility on the platform.
