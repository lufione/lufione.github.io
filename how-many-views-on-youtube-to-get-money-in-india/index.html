How Many Views On Youtube To Get Money In India

Written by Christine Oct 05, 2023 · 9 min read

YouTube has become a popular platform for people to showcase their talents, share their knowledge or simply entertain others. With millions of users worldwide, it has also become a source of income for many content creators. However, one question that arises is how many views on YouTube does one need to get money in India? In this article, we will answer this question and provide you with tips on how to monetize your YouTube channel.

How Does YouTube Monetization Work?

YouTube monetization is the process of earning money from the ads that are shown on your YouTube videos. The more views your videos get, the more money you can earn. YouTube pays its content creators a portion of the revenue generated from ads that are displayed on their videos. The amount of money you can earn depends on various factors, such as the number of views, the niche of your channel, and the engagement rate of your audience.

How Many Views Do You Need to Get Money on YouTube in India?

The number of views you need to get money on YouTube in India varies depending on the type of ads being displayed on your videos. YouTube has different types of ads, such as skippable and non-skippable ads, which pay differently. Generally, you need to have at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months to be eligible for monetization. This means that your videos need to have a total of 240,000 minutes of watch time to qualify for monetization.

Tip:

To increase your chances of getting more views, you can optimize your videos for SEO, use engaging titles and descriptions, and promote your videos on social media platforms. You can also collaborate with other YouTubers in your niche to reach a wider audience.

How Much Money Can You Make on YouTube in India?

The amount of money you can make on YouTube in India depends on various factors, such as the number of views, engagement rate, type of ads, and niche of your channel. Generally, YouTube pays its creators $0.01 to $0.03 per view, which means that if your video gets 1,000 views, you can earn $10 to $30. However, this is just an estimate, and the actual amount can be lower or higher depending on the factors mentioned above.

Tip:

To increase your earnings, you can diversify your income streams by using other monetization methods, such as sponsorships, merchandise sales, and affiliate marketing. You can also create a Patreon account to get support from your fans.

How to Monetize Your YouTube Channel in India?

To monetize your YouTube channel in India, you need to follow these steps:

  1. Create a Google AdSense account and link it to your YouTube channel.
  2. Enable monetization on your YouTube channel settings.
  3. Meet the eligibility criteria of 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months.
  4. Ensure that your videos comply with YouTube's community guidelines and copyright policies.
  5. Start earning money from the ads displayed on your videos.

Tip:

Make sure to read the terms and conditions of YouTube's monetization program and stay updated with any changes in the policies to avoid any issues with your channel.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How long does it take to get monetized on YouTube in India?

It can take up to a month or more to get monetized on YouTube in India, depending on the number of channels applying for monetization and the time taken for the review process. However, if your channel meets the eligibility criteria and follows YouTube's policies, you can get monetized faster.

2. Can you monetize copyrighted content on YouTube in India?

No, you cannot monetize copyrighted content on YouTube in India. You need to have the necessary permissions or licenses to use copyrighted material in your videos. Otherwise, your videos can be flagged or removed by YouTube, and you can face legal consequences.

3. How much does YouTube take from the revenue generated from ads?

YouTube takes a 45% share of the revenue generated from ads displayed on your videos, and you get the remaining 55%. This means that if the ads generate $100, you get $55, and YouTube gets $45.

4. Can you appeal if your YouTube channel is demonetized?

Yes, you can appeal if your YouTube channel is demonetized. You need to follow the appeal process provided by YouTube and provide any relevant information to support your appeal. However, there is no guarantee that your channel will be remonetized.

Conclusion

Monetizing your YouTube channel in India can be a great way to earn money from your content. However, it requires hard work, dedication, and patience to build a successful channel. By following the tips and guidelines provided in this article, you can increase your chances of getting more views and earning more money on YouTube. Remember to focus on creating quality content that engages your audience and adheres to YouTube's policies.
