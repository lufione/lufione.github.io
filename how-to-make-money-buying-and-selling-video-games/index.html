How To Make Money Buying And Selling Video Games

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Video games have become a popular pastime for people of all ages. With the rise of online gaming, the industry has grown tremendously, and so has the demand for video games. However, did you know that you can make money buying and selling video games? In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to make money buying and selling video games.

1. Determine the Value of Video Games

The first step to making money buying and selling video games is to determine their value. You can research the value of video games on different websites such as Amazon, eBay, and GameStop. You can also check the prices of new video games and compare them to the prices of used ones. This will give you an idea of how much you can sell your video games for.

2. Buy Video Games on Sale

One of the best ways to make money buying and selling video games is to buy them on sale. Many retailers offer discounts on video games during holidays, Black Friday, and Cyber Monday. You can buy video games at a lower price during these times and sell them at a higher price later on.

3. Sell Video Games Online

You can sell video games online on websites such as Amazon, eBay, and Craigslist. These websites have a large audience, and you can sell your video games quickly. You can also sell your video games on your own website or social media platforms.

4. Trade-in Video Games

You can also make money by trading in your old video games. Some retailers such as GameStop and Best Buy offer trade-in programs where you can trade in your old video games for cash or store credit. You can then use the store credit to buy new video games and sell them for a profit.

5. Attend Video Game Conventions

Attending video game conventions is another way to make money buying and selling video games. You can buy rare and collectible video games at these conventions and sell them for a profit. You can also network with other video game enthusiasts and learn more about the industry.

6. Buy Video Games in Bulk

You can buy video games in bulk from wholesalers and sell them at a higher price. This is a great way to make a larger profit, but it requires more upfront investment. You can also sell video games in bundles or sets to increase your profit.

7. Look for Rare and Collectible Video Games

Rare and collectible video games are worth more than regular video games. You can buy these video games at a lower price and sell them for a higher price. Some rare and collectible video games include limited edition versions, signed copies, and out-of-print video games.

8. Keep Your Video Games in Good Condition

The condition of your video games can affect their value. To make the most money selling video games, you should keep them in good condition. This includes keeping them in their original packaging, avoiding scratches and damage, and keeping them away from heat and moisture.

9. Offer Competitive Prices

To sell your video games quickly, you should offer competitive prices. You can research the prices of similar video games online and offer a slightly lower price. This will attract more buyers and help you sell your video games faster.

10. Build a Reputation

Finally, to make money buying and selling video games, you need to build a good reputation. You should provide excellent customer service, ship your video games quickly, and offer refunds or exchanges if necessary. This will help you gain repeat customers and attract new ones.

In Conclusion

Buying and selling video games can be a profitable business if you know what you're doing. By following these tips and tricks, you can start making money buying and selling video games. Remember to do your research, offer competitive prices, and provide excellent customer service to build a good reputation in the industry.
