How Do Instagram Posts Get On Explore Page

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Instagram is one of the most popular social media platforms, with over a billion active users. One of the features that users love is the Explore page, which is a collection of posts that Instagram thinks you might be interested in. But have you ever wondered how Instagram decides which posts to feature on the Explore page? In this article, we will explain how Instagram posts get on the Explore page.

What is the Explore Page?

The Explore page is a section of Instagram that shows you posts from accounts that you don't follow. The posts are chosen based on your past activity on Instagram, including the accounts you follow, the posts you like, and the hashtags you use. The Explore page is a great way to discover new content and connect with other users.

How Does Instagram Choose Posts for the Explore Page?

Instagram uses a complex algorithm to choose posts for the Explore page. The algorithm considers many factors, such as the engagement rate of a post, the relevance of the post to your interests, and the recency of the post. Instagram also takes into account the accounts that you follow and the hashtags that you use.

Engagement Rate

The engagement rate of a post is a measure of how many people interact with it. This includes likes, comments, shares, and saves. The higher the engagement rate of a post, the more likely it is to be featured on the Explore page. However, Instagram also considers the quality of the engagement. For example, if a post has a lot of spammy comments, it will be less likely to appear on the Explore page.

Relevance to Your Interests

Instagram tries to show you posts that are relevant to your interests. This means that if you like a lot of posts about food, you are more likely to see food-related posts on the Explore page. Instagram uses machine learning to analyze your activity on the platform and determine your interests.

Recency of the Post

The recency of a post is also a factor in whether it appears on the Explore page. Instagram wants to show you the latest and most relevant content, so it is more likely to feature posts that were published recently. However, Instagram also considers the quality of the post. A post that is a few days old but still getting a lot of engagement may still appear on the Explore page.

Accounts You Follow

The accounts that you follow also play a role in the posts that appear on the Explore page. Instagram will show you posts from accounts that are similar to those you already follow. For example, if you follow a lot of travel accounts, you are more likely to see posts from other travel accounts on the Explore page.

Hashtags You Use

The hashtags that you use in your posts can also influence whether they appear on the Explore page. Instagram will show your posts to people who are following or searching for those hashtags. However, Instagram also considers the quality of the hashtags. If you use a lot of spammy or irrelevant hashtags, your post is less likely to appear on the Explore page.

Conclusion

The Explore page is a great way to discover new content and connect with other users on Instagram. However, getting your posts featured on the Explore page requires a combination of factors, including engagement rate, relevance to your interests, recency of the post, accounts you follow, and hashtags you use. By understanding how Instagram chooses posts for the Explore page, you can optimize your content to increase your chances of being featured.
