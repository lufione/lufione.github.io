Can Money Plant Grow in Water Only?

Money plant, scientifically known as Epipremnum aureum, is a popular indoor plant that is easy to care for and grows well in a range of conditions. One of the most common questions asked about this plant is whether it can grow in water only. The short answer is yes, money plant can grow in water only. However, there are a few things you should keep in mind if you want to grow money plant in water only.

How to Grow Money Plant in Water Only?

If you want to grow money plant in water only, here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Choose a Healthy Money Plant Cutting

The first step in growing money plant in water only is to choose a healthy cutting. Look for a cutting with at least two or three leaves and a few nodes. Nodes are the small bumps on the stem where leaves and roots emerge.

Step 2: Place the Cutting in Water

Once you have chosen a healthy cutting, place it in a jar or vase filled with water. Make sure the water covers the nodes but does not cover the leaves. You can use tap water or filtered water, but avoid using distilled water as it lacks essential nutrients that money plant needs to grow.

Step 3: Change the Water Regularly

Money plant needs clean water to grow. Change the water in the jar or vase every few days to keep it fresh and free of bacteria. If you notice the water turning cloudy or smelling bad, change it immediately.

Step 4: Provide Adequate Light

Money plant needs adequate light to grow. Place the jar or vase in a bright, indirect light location. Avoid direct sunlight as it can scorch the leaves and damage the plant.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you grow money plant in water only:

  • Choose a healthy cutting with at least two or three leaves and a few nodes.
  • Change the water in the jar or vase every few days to keep it fresh and free of bacteria.
  • Provide adequate light by placing the jar or vase in a bright, indirect light location.
  • Use tap water or filtered water, but avoid using distilled water as it lacks essential nutrients that money plant needs to grow.
  • Consider adding a small amount of liquid fertilizer to the water every few weeks to provide additional nutrients to the plant.

By following these tips and tricks, you can grow a healthy and beautiful money plant in water only.
