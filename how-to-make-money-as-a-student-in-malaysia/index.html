How To Make Money As A Student In Malaysia

Written by Luffy Oct 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Being a student in Malaysia can be tough financially. With the rising cost of living, tuition fees, and other expenses, it can be challenging to make ends meet. However, there are many ways for students to earn money and become financially independent. In this article, we will discuss some of the best ways to make money as a student in Malaysia.

Sell Online

One of the most popular ways to make money as a student in Malaysia is by selling products online. You can sell anything from clothes, accessories, and gadgets to handmade crafts and home-cooked food. There are many online platforms such as Lazada, Shopee, and Carousell that you can use to sell your products. You can also use social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram to reach a wider audience.

Part-Time Jobs

Another way to earn money as a student in Malaysia is by working part-time. Many companies and businesses offer part-time jobs to students, such as working in restaurants, cafes, retail stores, and supermarkets. Part-time jobs can be a great way to earn extra income and gain work experience at the same time. You can also look for part-time jobs that are related to your field of study to gain relevant experience.

Tutoring

If you are good at a particular subject, you can offer tutoring services to other students. Many students in Malaysia are willing to pay for extra tuition to improve their grades. You can advertise your services online or through your university or college. You can also offer group tutoring sessions to make more money.

Freelancing

Freelancing is another great way to make money as a student in Malaysia. If you have skills such as writing, graphic design, programming, or video editing, you can offer your services to clients online. There are many freelance platforms such as Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer that you can use to find clients. Freelancing can be a flexible and lucrative way to earn money as a student.

Participate in Surveys

You can also earn money by participating in surveys. Many companies and research firms are willing to pay for your opinions on various topics. You can sign up for survey websites such as Toluna, PanelPlace, and i-Say to start earning money. Although participating in surveys may not make you rich, it can be an easy and fun way to earn extra income.

Start a Blog or Vlog

If you have a passion for writing or creating videos, you can start a blog or vlog and monetize it. You can write about topics such as food, fashion, travel, or lifestyle, or create videos about your interests or hobbies. You can monetize your blog or vlog through advertising, sponsorships, or affiliate marketing. However, starting a blog or vlog requires dedication and hard work, and it may take some time to generate income.

Rent Out Your Room

If you are living in a rented apartment or house, you can rent out your extra room to earn extra income. Many students in Malaysia are looking for affordable accommodation, and you can offer them a room at a reasonable price. You can advertise your room online or through your university or college. However, before renting out your room, make sure to check with your landlord and follow the legal procedures.

Conclusion

There are many ways for students to make money in Malaysia. Whether you choose to sell online, work part-time, offer tutoring services, freelance, participate in surveys, start a blog or vlog, or rent out your room, the key is to find a way that suits your skills, interests, and schedule. Remember to manage your time wisely and prioritize your studies, as your education should always come first. With determination and hard work, you can become financially independent and achieve your goals.
