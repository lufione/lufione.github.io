Can You Use Google Play To Pay For Youtube Premium

Written by Aprilia May 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

YouTube Premium is a subscription service that offers ad-free streaming, background playback, and access to exclusive content. If you are a regular YouTube user, you might want to subscribe to this service to enhance your viewing experience. But how can you pay for YouTube Premium? Can you use Google Play to pay for it? Let's find out.

What is Google Play?

Google Play is a digital distribution platform operated by Google. It serves as the official app store for the Android operating system and allows users to download apps, music, movies, and books. Google Play also offers various payment methods, such as credit/debit cards, Google Pay, and carrier billing.

Can You Use Google Play to Pay for YouTube Premium?

Yes, you can use Google Play to pay for YouTube Premium. Since YouTube is owned by Google, the two services are integrated, and you can link your YouTube account to your Google account. This means that you can use your Google Play balance or payment method to subscribe to YouTube Premium.

How to Use Google Play to Pay for YouTube Premium?

To use Google Play to pay for YouTube Premium, follow these steps:

  1. Open the YouTube app on your Android device or go to the YouTube website on your computer.
  2. Sign in to your YouTube account.
  3. Click on your profile picture and select "Paid Memberships".
  4. Select "YouTube Premium".
  5. Choose your preferred plan and click on "Try it Free".
  6. Enter your payment details, such as credit/debit card or Google Pay.
  7. Click on "Buy" to complete the transaction.

FAQ

Can you pay for YouTube Premium with PayPal?

Yes, you can pay for YouTube Premium with PayPal. However, this option is only available in certain countries. To check if PayPal is available in your country, go to the YouTube Premium sign-up page and click on "See More Payment Options". If PayPal is listed, you can use it to pay for your subscription.

Can you use Google Play balance to pay for YouTube Premium?

Yes, you can use your Google Play balance to pay for YouTube Premium. Your Google Play balance is a credit that you can use to buy apps, games, and digital content on Google Play. To check your Google Play balance, open the Google Play Store app and go to "Payment methods" in the menu.

Can you cancel YouTube Premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your YouTube Premium subscription at any time. To cancel, go to your "Paid Memberships" page on YouTube and click on "Manage". Then, select "Cancel Membership" and follow the instructions. Keep in mind that you will lose access to all the benefits of YouTube Premium after the cancellation date.

Tips and Tricks

If you want to save money on YouTube Premium, you can try the following tips and tricks:

  • Sign up for a family plan: If you have a family or a group of friends who also want to use YouTube Premium, you can sign up for a family plan. This allows up to 6 accounts to share the same subscription at a lower cost per user.
  • Use student discount: If you are a student, you can get a discount on YouTube Premium by signing up for a student plan. This plan offers the same benefits as the regular plan but at a lower price.
  • Wait for promotions: YouTube sometimes offers promotions and discounts on its Premium service. Keep an eye on the YouTube website or social media pages to see if any promotions are available.

In conclusion, you can use Google Play to pay for YouTube Premium, and it is a convenient and easy way to subscribe to this service. By following the steps and tips above, you can enjoy ad-free streaming, background playback, and exclusive content on YouTube without breaking the bank.
