1. Complete Missions and Heists

The easiest way to make money in GTA 5 online is by completing missions and heists. You can find them on the map or through your phone. These missions and heists will reward you with cash, which you can use to buy properties, vehicles, and weapons. Make sure to complete them with your friends or other players to earn more money.

2. Invest in Properties

Investing in properties is another great way to make money in GTA 5 online. You can buy properties such as apartments, garages, and businesses, which will generate passive income. The more properties you own, the more money you will earn. Make sure to invest wisely and choose properties that have a high return on investment.

3. Participate in Races and Events

Races and events are a fun way to make money in GTA 5 online. You can participate in various races such as street races, air races, and water races. Events such as survival mode and king of the hill can also earn you cash. Make sure to practice and upgrade your vehicles to increase your chances of winning.

4. Sell Stolen Vehicles

You can steal vehicles and sell them to make money in GTA 5 online. You can steal cars, bikes, and even planes. Make sure to sell them to the right person to get the maximum profit. You can also upgrade the stolen vehicles and sell them for a higher price.

5. Rob Stores and Armored Trucks

You can rob stores and armored trucks to make money in GTA 5 online. You can find stores and armored trucks on the map. Make sure to have a good weapon and be prepared for the police chase. The more stores and armored trucks you rob, the more money you will make.

6. Participate in Daily Objectives

Completing daily objectives is a great way to make money in GTA 5 online. You can find them on the map or through your phone. These objectives will reward you with cash, RP, and even vehicles. Make sure to complete them every day to get the maximum reward.

7. Sell Special Items

You can sell special items such as drugs, weapons, and stolen goods to make money in GTA 5 online. You can find these items through missions, heists, and other players. Make sure to sell them to the right person to get the maximum profit.

8. Participate in VIP Work

Participating in VIP work is a great way to make money in GTA 5 online. You can become a VIP by owning a high-end apartment or yacht. VIP work includes tasks such as escorting a vehicle, stealing cargo, and protecting a VIP. Make sure to complete them with your friends or other players to earn more money.

9. Take Advantage of Double RP and Cash Events

Rockstar Games often hosts double RP and cash events in GTA 5 online. These events will reward you with double the amount of RP and cash for completing missions, heists, and events. Make sure to take advantage of these events to earn more money.

10. Be Patient and Persistent

Making money in GTA 5 online requires patience and persistence. It may take some time to earn a significant amount of money, but don't give up. Keep completing missions, participating in events, and investing in properties. With the right strategy and techniques, you can become a millionaire in no time.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make money in GTA 5 online as a beginner, it's time to put these tips and tricks into action. Remember to have fun and be safe while playing the game. Good luck!
