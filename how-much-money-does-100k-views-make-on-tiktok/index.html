How Much Money Does 100K Views Make On Tiktok

Written by Oliver May 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Since its inception in 2016, TikTok has been one of the most popular social media platforms, especially among the younger generation. With millions of users worldwide, it has become a great platform for content creators to showcase their skills and creativity. But the question that arises is, how much money does one make from TikTok?

The Basics of TikTok Earnings

Before we dive into the specifics of how much money 100k views make on TikTok, it's essential to understand how TikTok earnings work. TikTok creators can earn money through various methods, including brand partnerships, sponsored posts, and live streaming.

One of the most common ways creators earn money on TikTok is through the Creator Fund. The Creator Fund is a program that TikTok has created to monetize the content of its users. In this program, TikTok pays creators for the views they get on their videos.

The Math Behind TikTok Earnings

Now that we understand how TikTok earnings work let us dive into the math behind it. TikTok pays its creators based on the number of views their videos get. The amount of money one earns per view depends on various factors, including the location, audience demographics, and engagement rate.

According to TikTok's official website, creators can earn anywhere between $0.02 to $0.04 per thousand views on their videos. Therefore, if a creator gets 100k views on their video, they can expect to make anywhere between $2 to $4.

The Factors That Affect TikTok Earnings

As mentioned earlier, various factors affect the earnings of TikTok creators. These factors include:

Location

The location of the creator plays a significant role in determining their earnings. For instance, creators based in the United States earn more than those in other countries.

Audience Demographics

The demographics of the creator's audience also affect their earnings. Creators with a predominantly younger audience tend to earn less than those with an older audience.

Engagement Rate

The engagement rate of the creator's videos also plays a significant role in determining their earnings. Creators with a higher engagement rate tend to earn more than those with a lower engagement rate.

Other Ways TikTok Creators Can Earn Money

Aside from the Creator Fund, there are other ways TikTok creators can earn money. These include:

Brand Partnerships

Creators with a significant following can partner with brands to promote their products or services. In exchange, they receive payment or free products.

Sponsored Posts

Similar to brand partnerships, creators can also earn money through sponsored posts. In this case, the creator promotes a product or service in their video for a fee.

Live Streaming

Creators can also earn money through live streaming on TikTok. During the live stream, viewers can send virtual gifts, which the creator can exchange for real money.

Conclusion

In conclusion, TikTok creators can earn money through various methods, including the Creator Fund, brand partnerships, sponsored posts, and live streaming. However, the amount of money one earns per view depends on various factors like location, audience demographics, and engagement rate. So, if a creator gets 100k views on their video, they can expect to make anywhere between $2 to $4.
