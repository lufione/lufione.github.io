Heists are one of the most lucrative ways to make money in GTA 5 Online. You can team up with other players to complete a variety of heists that range from simple to complex. Collecting bounties is another way to make money in GTA 5 Online. You can collect bounties by killing other players who have a bounty on their head. The amount of money you can earn depends on the size of the bounty. The maximum bounty is $9,000.

Selling Cargo

You can also make money in GTA 5 Online by selling cargo. You can purchase cargo from various locations and sell them for a profit. The amount of money you can make depends on the type of cargo you sell. The most expensive cargo is the Narcotics which can fetch up to $10,000.

Selling Property

If you have purchased property in GTA 5 Online, you can sell it for a profit. The amount of money you can make depends on the type of property you sell. The most expensive property is the Eclipse Towers which can earn you up to $500,000.

Collecting Hidden Packages

Another way to make money in GTA 5 Online is by collecting hidden packages. You can find hidden packages scattered throughout the game world. Each package can earn you up to $7,000.

Participating in Races

Participating in races is another way to make money in GTA 5 Online. You can participate in various races and earn money based on your performance. The payouts for races can range from $1,000 to $20,000 depending on the difficulty of the race and the number of players involved.

Conclusion

These include selling cars, completing missions, participating in heists, investing in the stock market, collecting bounties, selling cargo, selling property, collecting hidden packages, and participating in races.
