How Do I Make Money As A 10 Year Old

Written by Robby Sep 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Introduction

As a 10 year old, you might be looking for ways to make some extra pocket money. Whether it's to save up for a new toy or to treat yourself to some candy, there are plenty of ways you can make money at a young age. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to make money as a 10 year old.

1. Babysitting

One of the most common ways for young people to make money is by babysitting. If you're responsible and good with children, you can offer your services to families in your neighborhood. Make sure to let your parents know where you'll be and who you'll be with, and always get permission from the child's parents before babysitting.

2. Yard Work

Another way to make money is by doing yard work for your neighbors. This can include mowing lawns, raking leaves, and watering plants. You can charge a reasonable fee for your services, and you might even be able to turn it into a regular job.

3. Pet Sitting

If you're an animal lover, pet sitting could be a great way to make some extra cash. You can offer to take care of pets while their owners are away, whether it's for a day or a week. This might include feeding them, walking them, and giving them attention.

4. Lemonade Stand

A classic way to make money as a kid is by setting up a lemonade stand. You can make your own lemonade, set up a table and chairs outside your house, and sell cups of lemonade to people passing by. Make sure to get permission from your parents and check with your local laws to see if you need a permit.

5. Garage Sale

Another way to make money is by having a garage sale. You can gather up things you no longer need or want, such as toys, clothes, and books, and sell them to people in your neighborhood. Make sure to get permission from your parents and advertise your sale to attract more customers.

6. Online Surveys

There are also some online survey websites that allow kids to earn money by taking surveys. You might not make a lot of money, but it can be a fun and easy way to earn some extra cash. Make sure to get permission from your parents and only sign up for reputable survey sites.

7. Dog Walking

If you enjoy spending time outside and love dogs, you can offer your services as a dog walker. You can advertise your services to people in your neighborhood who have dogs and need help taking them for walks.

8. Sell Crafts

If you're crafty, you can make and sell your own creations. This might include things like jewelry, keychains, or artwork. You can sell your items online or at local craft fairs.

9. Recycling

You can also make money by recycling. Collect cans, bottles, and other recyclable items in your neighborhood and bring them to a recycling center. You can earn money based on the weight of the items you collect.

10. Car Wash

Finally, you can offer to wash cars for your neighbors for a fee. You can set up a station outside your house with a bucket, sponge, and soap, and offer to clean cars for a reasonable price.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of ways for 10 year olds to make money. Whether it's by babysitting, doing yard work, or selling crafts, there are plenty of opportunities to earn some extra cash. Just make sure to get permission from your parents and always stay safe.
