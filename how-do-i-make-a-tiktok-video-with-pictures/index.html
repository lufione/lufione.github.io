How To Make A Tiktok Video With Pictures

Written by William May 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to create a TikTok video with pictures, but don't know where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered! TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create and share short videos. In this article, we will show you step-by-step how to make a TikTok video with pictures.

Step 1: Download the TikTok App

The first step to making a TikTok video with pictures is to download the TikTok app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Once you have downloaded the app, create an account or sign in if you already have one.

Step 2: Choose Your Pictures

Next, you will need to choose the pictures you want to include in your TikTok video. You can either take new photos directly from the app or choose existing photos from your camera roll.

Step 3: Open the TikTok App

Open the TikTok app and click on the plus sign icon in the center of the bottom menu bar. This will open the camera screen.

Step 4: Select the "Photo Templates" Option

At the bottom of the camera screen, you will see a row of options. Select the "Photo Templates" option, which is the second option from the left.

Step 5: Choose a Template

You will now be taken to the photo templates page. Browse through the different templates until you find one that you like. You can also choose to create your own template by selecting the "Create" button in the top right corner.

Step 6: Add Your Pictures

Once you have selected a template, click on the "Add" button to add your pictures. You can add up to 12 pictures to your TikTok video.

Step 7: Edit Your Pictures

After you have added your pictures, you can edit them by cropping, flipping, or rotating them. You can also adjust the duration of each picture by clicking on it and dragging the slider to the desired length.

Step 8: Add Music and Effects

Once you are happy with your pictures, you can add music and effects to your TikTok video. Click on the "Sounds" or "Effects" buttons at the bottom of the screen to browse through the available options.

Step 9: Preview Your Video

Once you have added your music and effects, preview your TikTok video by clicking on the "Preview" button. If you are happy with your video, click on the "Next" button.

Step 10: Post Your Video

Finally, post your TikTok video by adding a caption and selecting the "Post" button. Your video will now be available for others to view and share!

Conclusion

Creating a TikTok video with pictures is a great way to showcase your creativity and share your memories with others. By following these simple steps, you can easily make a TikTok video that is sure to impress!
