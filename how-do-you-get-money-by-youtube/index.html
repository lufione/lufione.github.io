How Do You Get Money By Youtube

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

YouTube is the world's largest video sharing platform, and it can be an excellent way to earn money if you know how to do it right. If you're wondering how to get money by YouTube, you're in the right place. In this article, we will be discussing some tips and tricks on how to earn money by creating content on YouTube.

What is YouTube Monetization?

YouTube monetization is the process of making money from your YouTube channel. YouTube pays you for displaying ads on your videos. You can also earn money from YouTube by getting sponsorships, selling merchandise, and using affiliate marketing.

How to Enable Monetization on Your YouTube Channel?

To enable monetization on your YouTube channel, you need to meet the following requirements:

1. Have at least 1,000 subscribers

You need to have at least 1,000 subscribers on your YouTube channel to enable monetization. If you don't have 1,000 subscribers yet, focus on creating quality content that people will love and share with others.

2. Have at least 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months

You need to have at least 4,000 watch hours on your YouTube channel in the past 12 months. This means people have watched your videos for at least 4,000 hours in the past year. This requirement is to ensure that only serious creators can monetize their channels.

3. Follow YouTube's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service

YouTube has strict community guidelines, and you need to follow them to enable monetization on your channel. Make sure you don't violate any of YouTube's terms of service, or you may risk getting your channel suspended or terminated.

How Much Money Can You Make on YouTube?

It depends on several factors, such as the number of views, the number of clicks on ads, and the type of ads displayed on your videos. Generally, YouTube pays $0.01 to $0.03 per view, but this can vary depending on the niche and the audience.

Other Ways to Make Money on YouTube

Aside from displaying ads on your videos, there are other ways to earn money on YouTube:

1. Sponsorships

You can get sponsorships from companies that are interested in your niche. For example, if you have a cooking channel, you can get sponsorships from kitchen appliance companies. Make sure you disclose any sponsorships in your videos.

2. Merchandise

You can sell merchandise, such as T-shirts, mugs, and phone cases, to your fans. This can be a great way to earn money and promote your brand at the same time.

3. Affiliate Marketing

You can use affiliate marketing to earn money by promoting products in your videos. If someone clicks on your affiliate link and makes a purchase, you get a commission.

Conclusion

Creating content on YouTube can be a fun and rewarding experience, and it can also be a great way to earn money. By following the tips and tricks in this article, you can start monetizing your YouTube channel and earning money from your videos.
