Free Legit Ways To Make Money Online In 2023

Written by Bardi Sep 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for ways to make money online without spending a dime? In this article, we will share with you some free and legit ways to earn money from the comfort of your home.

1. Take Online Surveys

Many companies are willing to pay for your opinion on their products or services. You can sign up for free online survey sites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, and Vindale Research to start earning money by taking surveys.

2. Sell Your Stuff Online

If you have unwanted items lying around your house, you can sell them online on websites like eBay, Craigslist, and Facebook Marketplace. You can also sell your handmade crafts on Etsy.

3. Become a Freelancer

If you have skills like writing, web design, or graphic design, you can become a freelancer and offer your services on websites like Fiverr, Upwork, and Freelancer.

4. Participate in Affiliate Marketing

You can make money by promoting other people's products through affiliate marketing. You can sign up for affiliate programs of companies like Amazon, ClickBank, and ShareASale and earn a commission for every sale you refer.

5. Start a Blog

If you have a passion for writing, you can start a blog and monetize it through advertising, sponsored posts, and affiliate marketing. You can start a free blog on platforms like WordPress or Blogger.

6. Teach English Online

If you are a native English speaker, you can teach English online to students from around the world. You can sign up for websites like VIPKid, Qkids, and Magic Ears to start teaching and earning money.

7. Do Micro Jobs

You can complete small tasks like data entry, transcription, and online research on websites like Amazon Mechanical Turk and Clickworker and earn money for each completed task.

8. Create and Sell Online Courses

If you have expertise in a particular field, you can create and sell online courses on platforms like Udemy, Teachable, and Skillshare and earn money from course sales.

9. Rent Your Property on Airbnb

If you have a spare room or an entire property, you can rent it out on Airbnb and earn money from guests who book your space.

10. Participate in Online Focus Groups

You can participate in online focus groups and provide your feedback on products or services. You can sign up for websites like Respondent and User Interviews to start participating in focus groups and earning money.

Conclusion

These are just some of the free and legit ways to make money online in 2023. While these methods may not make you rich overnight, they can provide you with some extra cash and help you achieve your financial goals.
