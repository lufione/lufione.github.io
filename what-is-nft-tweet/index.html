How does it work?

When a tweet is turned into an NFT, it is essentially being transformed into a digital asset that can be bought, sold, and traded just like any other type of collectible. This means that ownership of the NFT can be easily tracked and verified, making it a safe and reliable way to invest in unique tweets.
