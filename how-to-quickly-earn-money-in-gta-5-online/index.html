The Basics

GTA 5 Online is a vast open-world game with a lot of content to explore. However, if you want to earn money quickly, you need to focus on the right activities. Here are the basics:

1. Jobs: Look for missions and jobs that offer high payouts. Some of the best options are heists, CEO work, and VIP missions. These activities require a lot of preparation, but the rewards are worth it.

2. Daily Objectives: Complete the daily objectives to earn a bonus of $25,000. These objectives are different every day and include tasks like killing a certain number of players or stealing a vehicle.

3. Selling Cars: You can sell cars to Los Santos Customs for a decent amount of cash. Look for rare or high-end vehicles to get the best payouts.

Advanced Strategies

3. Gambling: Visit the Diamond Casino & Resort and try your luck at the slot machines, blackjack tables, or roulette wheels. There is no guarantee that you will win, but the payouts can be huge.

Conclusion
