What is ASMR?

Before we dive into the earnings of food ASMR YouTubers, let's first understand what ASMR is. ASMR is a tingling sensation that some people experience in response to certain sounds or visual triggers. It's often described as a pleasant, relaxing sensation that starts in the scalp and moves down the body.

What is Food ASMR?

Food ASMR channels combine the sounds and visuals of food with ASMR triggers. You might see someone eating a plate of noodles with a microphone close to their mouth, or someone pouring hot chocolate into a mug with a microphone next to the mug. These sounds are meant to trigger the ASMR sensation in viewers.
