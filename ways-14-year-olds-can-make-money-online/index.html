14 Ways For 14 Year Olds To Make Money Online

Written by Christine May 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As a 14-year-old, you may not be able to work a traditional job yet, but that doesn't mean you can't earn money. Thanks to the internet, there are plenty of ways you can make money online. Here are 14 ways for 14-year-olds to make money online:

1. Take Surveys

One easy way to make money online is by taking surveys. There are many websites that pay you to take surveys, such as Swagbucks and Survey Junkie. While you won't get rich taking surveys, it's an easy way to earn some extra cash.

2. Sell Your Crafts

If you're crafty, you can make money by selling your creations online. Websites like Etsy and Handmade by Amazon allow you to sell your handmade items to a global audience.

3. Become a Freelance Writer

If you enjoy writing, you can make money by becoming a freelance writer. Many websites and companies hire freelance writers to create content for their blogs and websites.

4. Start a YouTube Channel

If you have a talent or passion, you can start a YouTube channel and make money through advertising revenue. YouTubers can make money through ads, sponsorships, and merchandise sales.

5. Become a Social Media Influencer

If you have a large following on social media, you can become a social media influencer and make money through brand collaborations and sponsored posts.

6. Sell Your Photography

If you love taking photos, you can make money by selling your photography online. Websites like Shutterstock and iStockphoto allow you to sell your photos to a global audience.

7. Become a Virtual Assistant

If you're organized and good at administrative tasks, you can make money as a virtual assistant. Many businesses and entrepreneurs hire virtual assistants to handle tasks like email management and social media scheduling.

8. Sell Your Products on Amazon

If you have products to sell, you can list them on Amazon and take advantage of their massive customer base. You can sell products like books, electronics, and beauty products.

9. Create an Online Course

If you have expertise in a particular subject, you can create an online course and sell it on platforms like Udemy and Skillshare.

10. Become a Tutor

If you're good at a particular subject, you can make money by becoming a tutor. You can offer your services online through websites like Tutor.com.

11. Sell Your Services on Fiverr

If you have a skill or talent, you can sell your services on Fiverr. You can offer services like graphic design, video editing, and writing.

12. Sell Your Music

If you're a musician, you can make money by selling your music online. Websites like iTunes and Amazon Music allow you to sell your music to a global audience.

13. Sell Your eBooks

If you enjoy writing, you can make money by self-publishing eBooks on platforms like Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing.

14. Become an Affiliate Marketer

If you have a website or blog, you can make money by becoming an affiliate marketer. You can promote products and services on your website and earn a commission for every sale made through your unique affiliate link.

Overall, there are plenty of ways for 14-year-olds to make money online. Whether you're crafty, tech-savvy, or have a talent to share, there's a way for you to earn some extra cash.
