How Much Tiktok Pays Per 1000 Views In 2023

Written by Eveline Oct 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a TikTok creator wondering how much you can earn from your content? You're not alone! Many TikTok users want to know how much the app pays per 1000 views. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about TikTok payments in 2023.

How TikTok Pays Creators

TikTok pays its creators through the Creator Fund, which was launched in 2021. The Creator Fund is a $1 billion fund that TikTok has set up to support and reward its creators. To be eligible for the Creator Fund, you need to meet certain criteria:

  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You must have at least 100,000 followers
  • You must have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days
  • You must meet TikTok's community guidelines and terms of service

How Much Does TikTok Pay per 1000 Views?

The amount that TikTok pays per 1000 views varies, and it's not disclosed publicly by the company. However, reports suggest that TikTok pays between $0.01 and $0.02 per view. This means that if your video gets 1000 views, you can expect to earn between $10 and $20.

Factors That Affect How Much TikTok Pays

There are several factors that can affect how much TikTok pays you per 1000 views:

  • The type of content you create: TikTok may pay more for certain types of content, such as educational or entertaining videos
  • Your engagement rate: If your videos have a high engagement rate, TikTok may pay you more per view
  • Your audience demographics: If your audience is highly engaged and from a valuable demographic (such as 18-24 year olds), TikTok may pay you more per view

How to Increase Your TikTok Earnings

If you want to increase your earnings on TikTok, there are a few things you can do:

  • Create high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience
  • Post consistently, ideally at least once a day
  • Use relevant hashtags to make your content discoverable
  • Collaborate with other creators to reach new audiences

FAQs

How often does TikTok pay its creators?

TikTok pays its creators on a monthly basis.

Do you need to be part of the Creator Fund to earn money on TikTok?

No, you don't need to be part of the Creator Fund to earn money on TikTok. You can also earn money through sponsored content, brand deals, and merchandise sales.

How can I join the Creator Fund?

If you meet the eligibility criteria for the Creator Fund, you can apply through the TikTok app. However, keep in mind that not all creators who apply will be accepted into the program.

Is TikTok a good platform for earning money?

TikTok can be a good platform for earning money, especially if you have a large and engaged following. However, it's important to remember that TikTok earnings are not guaranteed, and it takes time and effort to build a successful account.

Final Thoughts

While TikTok payments per 1000 views may not be as high as other platforms, such as YouTube and Instagram, TikTok offers a unique opportunity for creators to reach a young and engaged audience. By creating high-quality content and building a loyal following, you can potentially earn a significant income on TikTok.
