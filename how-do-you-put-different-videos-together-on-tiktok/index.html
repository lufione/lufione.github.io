How To Put Different Videos Together On Tiktok In 2023

Written by Bardi May 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of using the same old video on TikTok? Do you want to make your content more interesting and engaging? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will show you how to put different videos together on TikTok in 2023. With these easy steps, you can create amazing TikTok videos that will surely impress your followers.

Step 1: Download the TikTok App

If you haven't already, download the TikTok app from your app store. Once you have installed the app, sign up for an account or log in if you already have one. This will allow you to access all the features that TikTok has to offer.

Step 2: Choose Your Videos

The first thing you need to do is choose the videos that you want to put together. You can either record new videos or use videos that you have already uploaded to TikTok. Select the videos that you want to use and make sure they are saved on your device.

Step 3: Open the TikTok App

Open the TikTok app and click on the plus sign (+) icon at the bottom of the screen. This will take you to the video recording and editing screen.

Step 4: Add Your First Video

Click on the "Upload" button and select the first video that you want to use. The video will then appear on the editing screen. You can edit the video by trimming it or adding effects if you want.

Step 5: Add Your Second Video

Once you have edited your first video, click on the "Add" button and select the second video that you want to use. The second video will then appear on the editing screen. You can edit this video as well if you want.

Step 6: Adjust the Timing

You can adjust the timing of each video by dragging the clips to the left or right. This will change the order of the videos and how long each clip plays for. You can also add transition effects between each clip to make the video flow better.

Step 7: Add Music and Text

If you want, you can add music to your video by clicking on the "Sounds" button and selecting a song from the TikTok library. You can also add text by clicking on the "Text" button and typing in the words that you want to use.

Step 8: Preview Your Video

Once you have finished editing your video, click on the "Preview" button to see how it looks. If you are happy with the result, click on the "Next" button.

Step 9: Add a Description and Hashtags

Add a description to your video and include relevant hashtags. This will help your video get discovered by other TikTok users.

Step 10: Post Your Video

Click on the "Post" button to upload your video to TikTok. Your video will then be available for other users to view and interact with. Congratulations, you have successfully put different videos together on TikTok!

Conclusion

Creating TikTok videos is easy and fun. By following these simple steps, you can put different videos together and create amazing content that will impress your followers. Remember to always be creative and experiment with different editing techniques to make your videos stand out from the rest.
