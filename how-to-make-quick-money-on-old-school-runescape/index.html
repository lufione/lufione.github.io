How to Make Quick Money on Old School Runescape

Old School Runescape (OSRS) is a popular massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) that has been around since 2001. Players can engage in various activities such as combat, skilling, and questing. However, one of the most essential aspects of the game is making money. In this article, we will explore some of the best ways to make quick money on Old School Runescape.

Killing Monsters

One of the most common ways to make money in OSRS is by killing monsters. Certain monsters, such as dragons and demons, drop valuable items such as dragon bones and infernal ashes, which can be sold for a high price. Some popular monster-killing methods include slayer tasks, bossing, and wilderness slayer.

Slayer Tasks

Slayer tasks involve killing a specific number of monsters assigned by a slayer master. The monsters assigned usually drop valuable items, making it a profitable method of making money. Some popular slayer monsters include abyssal demons, gargoyles, and brutal black dragons.

Bossing

Bossing involves killing bosses, which are more powerful than regular monsters. Bosses drop rare and valuable items such as the abyssal whip and the dragonfire shield. Some popular bosses include the Giant Mole, Zulrah, and the Corporeal Beast.

Wilderness Slayer

Wilderness slayer involves killing specific monsters located in the wilderness. These monsters drop valuable items such as the looting bag, which can hold up to 28 items. The wilderness can be dangerous, so it is essential to bring appropriate gear and be aware of other players.

Skilling

Skilling involves training various skills such as fishing, woodcutting, and mining. Some of these skills can be profitable, especially when using high-level methods.

Fishing

Fishing involves catching fish and selling them. Some profitable fishing methods include fishing for sharks and anglerfish.

Woodcutting

Woodcutting involves cutting down trees and selling the logs. Some profitable woodcutting methods include cutting magic trees and redwood trees.

Mining

Mining involves mining ores and selling them. Some profitable mining methods include mining runite ore and volcanic ash.

Flipping

Flipping involves buying items for a low price and selling them for a higher price. This method requires some knowledge of the game economy and market trends. Some popular items to flip include runes, weapons, and armor.

Conclusion

Making money in Old School Runescape can be a fun and rewarding experience. Whether you prefer killing monsters, skilling, or flipping, there are many ways to make quick money. It is essential to find a method that suits your playstyle and level of experience. With dedication and persistence, you can become a wealthy player in OSRS.
