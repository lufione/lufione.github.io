How Much Do Presents Cost on TikTok?

If you're an avid TikTok user, you may have noticed the option to send virtual gifts to your favorite creators during their live streams. But have you ever wondered how much these gifts actually cost? In this article, we'll break down the prices of TikTok gifts and explain how to purchase them.

What Are TikTok Gifts?

TikTok gifts are virtual items that can be purchased with real money and sent to creators during live streams. These gifts range from simple emojis to more elaborate animations and effects. Each gift has a different value, and the more expensive gifts can even earn the creator real money.

How Much Do TikTok Gifts Cost?

The cost of TikTok gifts varies depending on the gift. The cheapest gifts are worth 5 coins, which translates to $0.05 USD. The most expensive gifts, such as the Drama Queen and Drama King gifts, are worth 2000 coins, or $20 USD.

It's important to note that TikTok takes a percentage of the money spent on gifts. According to TikTok's website, creators earn 50% of the gift value, while TikTok takes the other 50%. This means that if you send a creator a 100 coin gift worth $1 USD, they will only receive $0.50 USD.

How Do I Purchase TikTok Gifts?

To purchase TikTok gifts, you'll need to have a TikTok account and a payment method set up. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Open the Live Stream

First, find the live stream of the creator you want to send a gift to. You can do this by tapping the "Live" button on the bottom menu of the TikTok app.

Step 2: Tap the Gift Icon

Once you're in the live stream, tap the gift icon on the bottom right corner of the screen. This will bring up the gift shop.

Step 3: Choose a Gift

Browse through the different gifts and choose the one you want to send. You can also see the gift's value and the creator's earnings for each gift.

Step 4: Purchase the Gift

Once you've chosen a gift, tap the "Buy Now" button and enter your payment information. You can use a credit card, debit card, or PayPal account to make the purchase.

Step 5: Send the Gift

After your payment has been processed, you can send the gift to the creator by tapping the gift icon again and selecting the gift from your purchased gifts.

Conclusion

TikTok gifts are a fun way to support your favorite creators and show your appreciation during their live streams. The cost of gifts varies depending on the gift, but the cheapest gifts are worth 5 coins, or $0.05 USD, while the most expensive gifts are worth 2000 coins, or $20 USD. Remember that TikTok takes a percentage of the money spent on gifts, so creators will only receive 50% of the gift value.
