How To Remove Comments On Tiktok Live In 2023

Written by William Sep 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction:

TikTok is a popular social media platform where people can share and watch short videos. The app also allows users to go live and interact with their followers in real-time. While live videos can be fun and engaging, sometimes users may receive negative comments or spam during their live session. In this article, we will discuss how to remove comments on TikTok Live in 2023.

Step by Step Guide:

Step 1: Start your TikTok live session

To remove comments on TikTok Live, you first need to start a live session. Open the TikTok app on your device and tap on the plus (+) sign at the bottom of the screen. Now select "Live" and tap on "Go Live" to start your session.

Step 2: Tap on the Comment icon

Once you are live, you will see a Comment icon at the bottom of the screen. Tap on it to view the comments section.

Step 3: Swipe left on the comment you want to remove

To remove a comment, simply swipe left on the comment you want to delete. You will see a "Delete" option on the right side of the screen. Tap on it to remove the comment.

Step 4: Block the user

If you want to prevent a specific user from leaving comments on your live session, you can block them. To do this, tap on the user's profile picture in the comment section and select "Block" from the options.

Step 5: Turn off comments

If you want to disable comments on your live session altogether, you can do so by tapping on the three dots at the bottom of the screen and selecting "Turn Off Commenting."

Tips and Tricks:

Tip 1: Monitor your comments

To ensure a positive and safe live session, it's important to monitor your comments regularly. Keep an eye out for any negative or spam comments and remove them promptly.

Tip 2: Use moderation tools

TikTok provides several moderation tools that can help you manage your live session more effectively. You can use the "Keyword Filters" option to block certain words or phrases from appearing in your comments section.

Tip 3: Set clear guidelines

Before starting your live session, it's a good idea to set clear guidelines for your viewers. Let them know what kind of behavior is acceptable and what is not. This can help prevent negative comments and create a more positive atmosphere.

FAQ:

Q1: Can I remove multiple comments at once?

A: No, you need to remove comments one by one by swiping left on each comment.

Q2: What happens if I block a user?

A: If you block a user, they will no longer be able to view your live session or leave comments on your videos.

Q3: Can I turn off comments for specific users?

A: No, you can only turn off comments for the entire live session.

Conclusion:

Removing comments on TikTok Live is a simple process that can help create a more positive and engaging live session. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can quickly remove negative or spam comments and maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for your viewers. Don't forget to use moderation tools and set clear guidelines to ensure a successful live session.
