When Do You Start Getting Paid From Tiktok

Written by Oliver Sep 04, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a TikTok user who is constantly creating and sharing content on the platform, you may be wondering when you will start to get paid for your efforts. While TikTok does offer a Creator Fund to eligible users, the process of receiving payment can be a bit confusing. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about getting paid on TikTok.

What is the TikTok Creator Fund?

The TikTok Creator Fund is a program that was launched by the platform in 2020 to support and reward its most popular creators. The fund provides financial support to eligible users who are consistently creating engaging and high-quality content on TikTok.

Who is eligible for the TikTok Creator Fund?

To be eligible for the TikTok Creator Fund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You must have at least 100,000 followers
  • You must have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days
  • Your account must comply with TikTok's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service

How does the TikTok Creator Fund work?

Once you have been accepted into the TikTok Creator Fund, you will start receiving payments based on the engagement and performance of your videos. The exact amount you receive will vary depending on a number of factors, including your location, the number of views your videos receive, and the overall performance of your account.

How do I apply for the TikTok Creator Fund?

To apply for the TikTok Creator Fund, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Make sure your account meets the eligibility criteria
  2. Open the TikTok app and navigate to your profile page
  3. Tap on the three dots in the top-right corner
  4. Select "Creator Fund" from the menu
  5. Follow the prompts to submit your application

When will I receive my first payment?

Once you have been accepted into the TikTok Creator Fund and have started receiving payments, you can expect to receive your first payment within 30-60 days. After that, you will continue to receive payments on a monthly basis.

How do I track my earnings on TikTok?

To track your earnings on TikTok, you can follow these steps:

  1. Open the TikTok app and navigate to your profile page
  2. Tap on the three dots in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Balance" from the menu
  4. You will be able to view your earnings for the current month, as well as your total earnings since joining the Creator Fund

What other ways can I make money on TikTok?

While the TikTok Creator Fund is one way to make money on the platform, there are other avenues you can explore as well. These include:

  • Sponsored content: Brands may approach you to create sponsored content for their products or services
  • TikTok Live: You can earn money through TikTok Live by receiving gifts from your viewers
  • Affiliate marketing: You can earn a commission by promoting products or services through affiliate links in your videos

Final Thoughts

While getting paid on TikTok may seem like an elusive goal, it is definitely possible if you are dedicated and consistent in your content creation. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can increase your chances of being accepted into the Creator Fund and start earning money for your efforts.
