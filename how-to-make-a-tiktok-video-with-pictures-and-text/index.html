How To Make A Tiktok Video With Pictures And Text

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Introduction

TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create and share short videos. One of the features of TikTok is the ability to add pictures and text to your videos. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of making a TikTok video with pictures and text.

Step 1: Open TikTok App

First, open the TikTok app on your mobile device. If you don’t have it yet, download it from your app store.

Step 2: Create a New Video

Tap the plus sign at the bottom of the screen to create a new video. You’ll be taken to the recording screen.

Step 3: Add Pictures to Your Video

Tap the “Effects” icon on the bottom-left corner of the screen. Swipe right until you see the “Green Screen” effect. Tap it to open the camera roll on your device. Choose the picture you want to add to your video.

Pro Tip:

You can add multiple pictures to your video by repeating this step.

Step 4: Add Text to Your Video

Tap the “Text” icon on the bottom of the screen. Type the text you want to add to your video. You can change the font, size, and color of your text by tapping the “Edit” button next to the text.

Pro Tip:

You can add multiple text boxes to your video by repeating this step.

Step 5: Record Your Video

Tap the red record button to start recording your video. You can record multiple clips to add to your video by tapping the record button again.

Step 6: Edit Your Video

After you’ve recorded your video, you can edit it by adding filters, music, and other effects. You can also trim and rearrange your clips by tapping the “Edit” button on the bottom-right corner of the screen.

Step 7: Add Captions and Hashtags

Tap the “Caption” icon on the bottom of the screen to add a caption to your video. You can also add hashtags to make your video more discoverable.

Pro Tip:

Use popular hashtags in your niche to reach a wider audience.

Step 8: Publish Your Video

Once you’re happy with your video, tap the checkmark on the bottom-right corner of the screen. You can choose to publish your video immediately, or save it as a draft to publish later.

Step 9: Promote Your Video

Share your video on your other social media platforms to promote it. You can also collaborate with other TikTok users to increase your reach.

Step 10: Analyze Your Performance

Check your TikTok analytics to see how your video is performing. This will help you improve your content and reach more people.

Conclusion

Creating a TikTok video with pictures and text is a fun and creative way to engage with your audience. By following these steps, you can make your videos more interesting and increase your reach on the platform.
