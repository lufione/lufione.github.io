How To Make A Tiktok Video With Pictures And Music

Written by Oliver Jun 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

If you're looking to create a TikTok video with pictures and music, you're in the right place! TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create short videos with various effects and music. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to make your own TikTok video with pictures and music.

Step 1: Download the TikTok App

The first step to creating a TikTok video is to download the app. You can find it in the App Store or Google Play Store. Once downloaded, open the app and create an account.

Step 2: Choose Your Pictures and Music

Before you start creating your TikTok video, you need to choose the pictures and music you want to use. You can either take photos within the app or choose pictures from your camera roll. As for the music, TikTok has a vast library of songs to choose from, or you can use your own music.

Step 3: Create Your TikTok Video

Once you have your pictures and music selected, it's time to create your TikTok video. To do this, tap the plus sign at the bottom of the screen and then select "Create a Video." You'll be taken to the video editing screen where you can add your pictures and music.

Step 4: Add Your Pictures

To add your pictures, tap the "Images" button at the bottom of the screen. From there, you can select the pictures you want to use and adjust their order and duration.

Step 5: Add Your Music

To add music to your TikTok video, tap the "Sounds" button at the bottom of the screen. From there, you can search for a specific song or browse through the various categories. Once you've found the perfect music, tap the "Add to Video" button.

Step 6: Edit Your Video

After adding your pictures and music, you can edit your TikTok video further by adding effects, filters, and text. To add effects and filters, tap the "Effects" button at the bottom of the screen. To add text, tap the "Text" button.

Step 7: Preview Your Video

Before publishing your TikTok video, you can preview it to make sure everything looks right. To do this, tap the "Preview" button at the bottom of the screen.

Step 8: Publish Your Video

Once you're satisfied with your TikTok video, it's time to publish it. To do this, tap the checkmark in the bottom right corner of the screen. From there, you can add a caption, hashtags, and choose who can see your video.

Step 9: Share Your Video

Now that your TikTok video is published, it's time to share it with the world. You can share your video on other social media platforms or send it to your friends.

Step 10: Engage with Your Audience

Finally, engage with your audience by responding to comments and creating more TikTok videos. The more you interact with your audience, the more likely you are to gain followers and increase your reach on the platform.

Conclusion

Making a TikTok video with pictures and music is a fun and creative way to express yourself on social media. With the steps outlined in this article, you can create your own TikTok video and start sharing it with the world. So what are you waiting for? Get started today and show off your creativity!
