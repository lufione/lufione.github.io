How To Earn Money Online In Kenya Via Mpesa

Introduction

In today's digital age, earning money online has become a popular way to supplement your income. In Kenya, one of the most popular payment methods is Mpesa. Mpesa is a mobile money transfer service that allows users to send and receive money, pay bills, and buy goods and services using their mobile phones. In this article, we will explore different ways to earn money online in Kenya using Mpesa.

Online Surveys

One way to earn money online is through online surveys. Many companies are willing to pay for your opinion on their products and services. You can sign up for survey websites such as Toluna, Swagbucks, and Vindale Research. Once you complete a survey, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Online Freelancing

Online freelancing is another way to earn money online in Kenya. You can offer your services as a freelancer on websites such as Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer. You can offer services such as article writing, graphic design, website development, and social media management. Once you complete a project, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Online Trading

Online trading is a popular way to earn money online in Kenya. You can trade stocks, forex, and cryptocurrencies online. You can open an account with a broker such as EGM Securities or FXTM. Once you make a profit, you can withdraw your funds via Mpesa.

Online Betting

Online betting is another way to earn money online in Kenya. You can bet on sports such as football, basketball, and tennis. You can create an account with a betting site such as Betway or SportPesa. Once you win, you can withdraw your funds via Mpesa.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a popular way to earn money online in Kenya. You can promote products and services on your website or social media platforms. You will earn a commission for each sale made through your unique affiliate link. You can sign up for affiliate programs such as Jumia Affiliate, Kilimall Affiliate, and Amazon Associates. Once you make a sale, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Online Tutoring

Online tutoring is another way to earn money online in Kenya. You can offer your services as an online tutor on websites such as Chegg, TutorMe, and Skooli. You can offer tutoring services in subjects such as math, science, and English. Once you complete a session, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Online Writing

Online writing is a popular way to earn money online in Kenya. You can write articles, blog posts, and eBooks for clients. You can find clients on websites such as Problogger, Contena, and Freelance Writing. Once you complete a project, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Selling Products Online

Selling products online is another way to earn money online in Kenya. You can sell products such as clothes, shoes, and electronics on websites such as Jumia and Kilimall. Once you make a sale, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Online Transcription

Online transcription is another way to earn money online in Kenya. You can transcribe audio and video files for clients. You can find clients on websites such as TranscribeMe, GoTranscript, and Rev. Once you complete a project, you will be paid via Mpesa.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many ways to earn money online in Kenya using Mpesa. You can participate in online surveys, offer your services as a freelancer, trade online, bet online, promote products and services as an affiliate marketer, offer online tutoring services, write online, sell products online, and transcribe audio and video files. With dedication and hard work, you can earn a decent income online using Mpesa.
