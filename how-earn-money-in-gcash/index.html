How To Earn Money On Gcash

Written by William Aug 12, 2023 · 7 min read

Introduction

Gcash is a popular mobile wallet app that allows users to send and receive money, pay bills, and buy load. But did you know that you can also earn money on Gcash? In this article, we will discuss different ways to earn money using Gcash.

1. Refer a Friend

Gcash offers a referral program where you can earn Php 50 for every friend you refer to the app. To refer a friend, simply share your referral code and ask them to sign up for Gcash using your code. Once they complete their first transaction, you will receive your reward.

2. Join Promotions

Gcash regularly offers promotions and cashback rewards for using the app. Check the app's "Promos" section to see the latest offers. These promotions can range from cashback for bill payments to discounts on online purchases. Make sure to read the terms and conditions before participating.

3. Invest in Gcash Invest Money

Gcash Invest Money is a feature that allows you to invest in different funds with as little as Php 50. These funds are managed by reputable investment companies and can provide higher returns than traditional savings accounts. However, keep in mind that all investments come with risks.

4. Sell Items on Gcash Marketplace

Gcash Marketplace is an online selling platform where you can sell items to other Gcash users. This can be a great way to earn extra income if you have items to sell. Take clear photos of your items and list them on the marketplace. Make sure to set a fair price and respond promptly to inquiries.

5. Play Gcash Games

Gcash has several games within the app that allow you to earn Gcash credits, which can be used to pay for purchases or converted to cash. These games range from trivia quizzes to slot machines. Keep in mind that the chance of winning is based on luck.

6. Complete Surveys

Gcash partners with survey companies to offer paid surveys to users. These surveys can be found in the "Offers" section of the app. Answering surveys can be a quick and easy way to earn extra cash. Make sure to answer honestly and thoroughly to increase your chances of qualifying for more surveys.

7. Cash-in and Cash-out Services

Gcash also offers cash-in and cash-out services where you can earn a commission for helping others load or withdraw cash from their Gcash accounts. This can be a great opportunity to earn extra income if you have a lot of friends or family who also use Gcash.

8. Use Gcash for Online Purchases

Gcash offers a secure and convenient way to pay for online purchases. Some online merchants offer cashback or discounts for using Gcash as a payment method. Make sure to check for these offers before making a purchase.

9. Participate in Gcash Events

Gcash hosts events and contests throughout the year where users can win cash prizes or other rewards. Keep an eye on the app's "Events" section to stay updated on the latest contests and promotions.

10. Cash-in at Partner Merchants

Gcash has partnered with several merchants, such as 7-Eleven and SM Business Centers, where you can cash-in or load your Gcash account. Some of these partners offer discounts or promotions for using their services. Make sure to check for these offers before cashing in.

Conclusion

Earning money on Gcash can be a great way to supplement your income. Whether it's through referrals, promotions, investments, or selling items, there are plenty of opportunities to earn on the app. Make sure to read the terms and conditions before participating in any offers and always be cautious with your investments. Happy earning!
