How To Get Fast Cash In Singapore

Written by William Aug 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you in need of quick cash in Singapore? Whether it's for an emergency expense, to pay off debt, or to make a big purchase, there are several options available to you. Here are some tips and tricks to help you get fast cash:

1. Consider a Personal Loan

If you need a large sum of money, a personal loan may be your best option. You can apply for a personal loan at a bank, credit union, or online lender. Keep in mind that you'll need to have a good credit score and a steady income to qualify for the best interest rates.

2. Sell Your Unwanted Items

If you have items around your home that you no longer need, consider selling them for cash. You can sell items online through websites like Carousell or on Facebook Marketplace, or you can sell them in person at a flea market or garage sale.

3. Participate in Paid Surveys

You can earn cash by participating in paid surveys online. Companies are always looking for consumer feedback, and they're willing to pay for it. Some popular paid survey websites in Singapore include Toluna, Swagbucks, and YouGov.

4. Rent Out Your Spare Room

If you have a spare room in your home, consider renting it out on Airbnb. It's a great way to earn extra cash, especially if you live in a popular tourist destination.

5. Drive for Ride-Hailing Services

If you have a car, you can earn cash by driving for ride-hailing services like Grab or Gojek. It's a flexible way to earn extra income, and you can choose when and where you want to work.

6. Do Freelance Work

If you have a particular skill, such as writing, graphic design, or web development, you can offer your services on freelance websites like Upwork or Fiverr. You can set your own rates and choose which projects you want to work on.

7. Get a Gig Job

If you need fast cash, consider getting a gig job. These are short-term jobs that pay quickly, such as delivering food or driving for a courier service. You can find gig jobs on websites like Deliveroo, Foodpanda, or GrabFood.

8. Pawn Your Items

If you have valuable items like jewelry or electronics, you can pawn them for cash. You'll receive a loan based on the value of the item, and you'll need to pay back the loan plus interest to get your item back.

9. Get a Payday Loan

If you need cash quickly and don't have good credit, you can consider getting a payday loan. These loans usually have high interest rates, but they're a good option if you need cash urgently. Keep in mind that you'll need to pay back the loan plus interest on your next payday.

10. Ask for Help

If you're in a tough spot and need cash urgently, don't be afraid to ask for help. You can reach out to family and friends or consider getting a loan from a licensed moneylender. Just make sure that you're able to pay back the loan on time to avoid getting into further financial trouble.
