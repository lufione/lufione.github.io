How To Get Instagram Posts In Time Order

Instagram is a popular social media platform that allows users to share photos and videos with their friends and followers. However, Instagram's algorithm can sometimes make it difficult to see posts in chronological order. In this article, we'll show you how to get Instagram posts in time order.

Step 1: Turn Off Instagram's Algorithmic Feed

Instagram's algorithmic feed is designed to show users the posts that Instagram thinks they will be most interested in. To get Instagram posts in time order, you need to turn off the algorithmic feed. Here's how:

1. Open Instagram and go to your profile.

2. Tap the three lines in the top right corner.

3. Go to Settings > Account > Posts You've Liked.

4. Turn off the "Posts You've Liked" toggle.

Step 2: Follow Accounts in Chronological Order

One way to get Instagram posts in time order is to follow accounts in chronological order. This means that the posts will appear in your feed in the order they were posted. Here's how:

1. Find an account you want to follow.

2. Tap the "Follow" button.

3. Once you've followed the account, tap the three dots in the top right corner of their profile.

4. Select "Turn on Post Notifications."

Step 3: Use Instagram's "Most Recent" Feature

Instagram's "Most Recent" feature allows you to see posts in the order they were posted. Here's how to access it:

1. Open Instagram and go to your profile.

2. Tap the three lines in the top right corner.

3. Tap "Settings."

4. Scroll down to "News Feed Preferences."

5. Tap "Most Recent."

Step 4: Use a Third-Party App

Another way to get Instagram posts in time order is to use a third-party app. These apps allow you to view your Instagram feed in chronological order. Here are some popular third-party apps:

1. Later

2. Hootsuite

3. Planoly

4. Buffer

FAQs

Why doesn't Instagram show posts in chronological order?

Instagram's algorithmic feed was designed to show users the posts that Instagram thinks they will be most interested in. This means that the posts may not appear in the order they were posted.

Can I turn off Instagram's algorithmic feed?

Yes, you can turn off Instagram's algorithmic feed. Follow the steps outlined in Step 1 above.

What are some popular third-party apps to view Instagram posts in time order?

Some popular third-party apps include Later, Hootsuite, Planoly, and Buffer.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for getting Instagram posts in time order:

- Follow accounts in chronological order

- Use Instagram's "Most Recent" feature

- Use a third-party app

Conclusion

Getting Instagram posts in time order can be a challenge, but by following the steps outlined in this article, you can see posts in the order they were posted. Whether you choose to turn off Instagram's algorithmic feed or use a third-party app, there are many ways to view your Instagram feed in chronological order.
