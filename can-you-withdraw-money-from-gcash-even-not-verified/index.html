Can You Withdraw Money From Gcash Even If You re Not Verified

Written by William Sep 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

GCash has been one of the most popular e-wallets in the Philippines since its launch in 2004. With its convenience and user-friendly interface, it has become a go-to platform for Filipinos for transactions such as bills payment, online shopping, and money transfers. However, one of the most frequently asked questions about GCash is whether you can withdraw money from it even if you're not verified.

What is GCash Verification?

GCash verification is the process of verifying your identity through the GCash app. Once you have verified your account, you will have access to more features such as higher transaction limits and the ability to withdraw money from your GCash wallet to your bank account. In order to get verified, you will need to submit valid identification documents and a selfie for facial recognition.

Can You Withdraw Money from GCash Without Verification?

Yes, you can withdraw money from your GCash wallet even if you're not verified. However, there are limitations to this. Unverified users can only withdraw up to Php 5,000 per transaction and a maximum of Php 8,000 per day. This is significantly lower compared to verified users who can withdraw up to Php 50,000 per transaction and a maximum of Php 100,000 per day.

How to Withdraw Money from GCash Without Verification?

Here are the steps to withdraw money from your GCash wallet without verification:

  1. Open the GCash app on your mobile device.
  2. Tap "Cash Out" on the main menu.
  3. Select "Remittance" as the cash-out option.
  4. Choose the remittance center where you want to withdraw your money.
  5. Enter the amount you want to withdraw.
  6. Confirm your transaction and wait for the reference number.
  7. Go to the remittance center and present the reference number and valid ID.
  8. Receive your money and don't forget to keep the receipt.

Why Should You Get Verified?

Getting verified has its benefits. For one, you'll have access to higher transaction limits which is useful if you need to do larger transactions. You'll also be able to enjoy more features such as the ability to link your credit card to your GCash account, and the option to invest in GCash's investment platform, GInvest.

What Are the Requirements for GCash Verification?

The requirements for GCash verification may vary depending on your account status. If you're a new user, you will need to submit a valid ID and a selfie for facial recognition. If you're an existing user, you may need to provide additional documentation such as proof of address or proof of income.

How Long Does GCash Verification Take?

The verification process typically takes around 24-48 hours. However, it may take longer if there are issues with your submitted documents or if there is a high volume of verification requests. You can check the status of your verification on the GCash app under the "Verification" tab.

Conclusion

While it is possible to withdraw money from GCash even if you're not verified, it comes with limitations. If you plan on using GCash for larger transactions or want to enjoy more features, it's recommended to get verified. The verification process may take some time, but it's worth it in the long run.
