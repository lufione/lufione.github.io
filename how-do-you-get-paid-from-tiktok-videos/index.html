How Do You Get Paid From Tiktok Videos In 2023

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

With TikTok becoming one of the most popular social media platforms, it's no surprise that many people are wondering how they can make money from their videos. In this article, we'll go over the various ways to get paid from TikTok videos in 2023.

The Basics of TikTok

TikTok is a social media platform that allows users to create and share short-form videos. The app has become incredibly popular, with millions of users around the world. The videos on TikTok can be anything from dance routines to comedy skits to educational content.

Becoming a TikTok Creator

If you want to get paid from TikTok videos, you'll need to become a TikTok creator. This means that you'll need to create content that is engaging and entertaining for your audience. You'll also need to build up a following on the app.

Building Your Audience

One way to build your audience on TikTok is by creating content that is relevant to your niche. If you're a makeup artist, for example, you could create videos that show off your skills and give tips and tricks to your viewers. You can also engage with your audience by responding to comments and creating videos that are interactive.

Getting Verified on TikTok

Once you've built up a following on TikTok, you can apply to become verified. This means that TikTok will give you a blue checkmark next to your name, which signifies that you're a legitimate creator on the app. Being verified can help you gain more followers and make more money from your videos.

How Do You Get Paid from TikTok Videos?

There are several ways to get paid from TikTok videos. Here are some of the most common methods:

Brand Deals

One way to make money from TikTok videos is by partnering with brands. This is known as a brand deal. Brands will pay you to create videos that promote their products or services. The amount you can make from a brand deal varies depending on the brand and the scope of the project.

TikTok Creator Fund

In 2023, TikTok has introduced the TikTok Creator Fund, which is a program that pays creators for their content. The fund is open to anyone who meets the eligibility requirements. To be eligible, you must be 18 years or older, have at least 100,000 followers, and have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days.

Live Streaming

Another way to make money from TikTok videos is through live streaming. TikTok allows creators to go live on the app and interact with their audience in real-time. Viewers can send virtual gifts to creators during their live streams, which can be converted into real money.

Conclusion

Getting paid from TikTok videos requires dedication and hard work. But with the right strategy, you can turn your TikTok account into a source of income. Whether you're partnering with brands, participating in the TikTok Creator Fund, or live streaming, there are plenty of opportunities to make money on the app.
