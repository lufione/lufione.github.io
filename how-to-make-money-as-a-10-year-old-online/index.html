How To Make Money As A 10 Year Old Online

Written by Eveline May 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a 10 year old looking to make some extra cash online? Look no further! With the power of the internet, there are endless opportunities for you to earn money from the comfort of your own home. Here are some tips and tricks to get you started.

1. Take Online Surveys

Many companies are looking for feedback on their products and services, and they are willing to pay for it. Sign up for websites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, and Vindale Research to start earning money for your opinions. Just be sure to have your parent's permission before signing up for any websites.

2. Sell Your Crafts or Art

If you're a creative 10 year old, consider selling your crafts or art online. Websites like Etsy and Redbubble allow you to create a store and sell your handmade items or digital art. Make sure to take high-quality photos and write detailed descriptions to attract buyers.

3. Offer Your Services

Are you good at video editing, writing, or social media management? Offer your services to friends and family or post on websites like Fiverr and Upwork to get paid for your skills. Just make sure to set appropriate prices for your services.

4. Participate in Affiliate Marketing

If you have a blog or social media following, consider joining an affiliate marketing program. This allows you to earn a commission by promoting products or services to your audience. Amazon Associates and ShareASale are great programs to start with.

5. Create a YouTube Channel

Do you love creating videos? Consider starting a YouTube channel and monetizing your content with ads. Just make sure to follow YouTube's guidelines and get your parent's permission before creating an account.

6. Become a Virtual Assistant

Virtual assistants help businesses and individuals with tasks like data entry, scheduling, and email management. Websites like Zirtual and Time Etc. allow you to sign up and start working as a virtual assistant, even as a 10 year old.

7. Sell Your Photos

If you have a knack for photography, consider selling your photos online. Websites like Shutterstock and iStock allow you to upload your photos and earn money every time someone downloads them. Just make sure to have your parent's permission before signing up.

8. Write and Sell eBooks

If you enjoy writing, consider writing and selling eBooks on Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing. This allows you to earn money every time someone purchases your book. Just make sure to have your parent's permission before creating an account.

9. Do Online Tutoring

If you excel in a particular subject, consider doing online tutoring. Websites like Chegg and TutorMe allow you to sign up and start tutoring students online. Just make sure to have your parent's permission before signing up.

10. Start a Blog

Starting a blog is a great way to share your interests and earn money through ads and sponsorships. Use websites like WordPress or Blogger to create your blog and start writing about topics you love. Just make sure to get your parent's permission before creating an account.

Conclusion

There are endless opportunities for 10 year olds to make money online. Whether it's taking surveys, selling your crafts, or offering your services, there's something for everyone. Just make sure to have your parent's permission and always be safe while working online.
