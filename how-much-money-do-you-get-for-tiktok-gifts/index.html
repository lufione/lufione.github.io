How Much Money Do You Get For Tiktok Gifts

Written by Aprilia Apr 11, 2023 · 5 min read

Since its launch in 2016, TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world. With over 1 billion active users, it's no surprise that many people are interested in making money on the platform. One way to do so is by receiving gifts from your followers. But how much money do you actually get for TikTok gifts?

What Are TikTok Gifts?

TikTok gifts are virtual items that users can purchase with real money and send to their favorite creators as a way to show their support. These gifts can be purchased using coins, which users can buy in various denominations. Each gift has a different value in terms of coins, and the amount of money the creator receives depends on the value of the gift.

How Much Money Do Creators Make from TikTok Gifts?

The amount of money creators make from TikTok gifts varies depending on several factors, including the value of the gift, the creator's level, and whether or not they have a Live Gifts feature enabled. Generally speaking, creators receive around $0.01 to $0.02 per coin, and gifts can range in value from 1 coin to 10,000 coins.

Level-Up Bonuses

Creators can also earn bonuses for reaching certain levels on the platform. For example, when a creator reaches level 10, they may receive a bonus of 100 coins. These bonuses can help creators earn more money from gifts over time.

How to Enable the Live Gifts Feature

In order to receive gifts on TikTok, creators must have the Live Gifts feature enabled. This feature allows users to send gifts in real-time during a creator's live stream. To enable this feature, creators must have at least 1,000 followers and meet certain other requirements set by TikTok.

Other Ways to Make Money on TikTok

While receiving gifts from followers is one way to make money on TikTok, it's not the only way. Other ways to monetize your TikTok account include:

  • Sponsored posts
  • Affiliate marketing
  • Merchandise sales
  • Brand deals

How to Get More Followers on TikTok

In order to make money on TikTok, you'll need to have a sizable following. Here are some tips for growing your TikTok following:

  • Post consistently
  • Use trending hashtags
  • Create engaging content
  • Collaborate with other creators
  • Promote your TikTok on other social media platforms

Conclusion

While the amount of money you can make from TikTok gifts may not be substantial, it's one way to earn some extra income on the platform. By enabling the Live Gifts feature and reaching higher levels, you can increase your earnings over time. And don't forget to explore other monetization options on TikTok, as well as strategies for growing your following and engagement.
