How To Make Money As A Photographer In 2020

Written by Aprilia Sep 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Introduction

If you're a photographer, you know how difficult it can be to make a living in this industry. However, there are a variety of ways to monetize your skills and turn your passion into a profitable business. In this article, we'll explore some of the most effective strategies for making money as a photographer in 2020.

1. Sell Your Photos Online

One of the easiest ways to make money as a photographer is to sell your photos online. There are a variety of websites that allow you to upload and sell your photos to customers around the world. Some popular options include Shutterstock, iStockphoto, and Getty Images.

Tip:

To increase your sales, make sure your photos are high-quality and unique. Try to capture images that are in high demand, such as travel photos, food photography, and lifestyle shots.

2. Offer Photography Services

Another way to make money as a photographer is to offer your services to clients. This can include anything from wedding photography to corporate headshots. To attract clients, create a portfolio of your best work and promote your services on social media and local listings.

Tip:

Consider offering package deals or discounts to attract new clients. You can also offer additional services, such as photo editing or printing, to increase your revenue.

3. Teach Photography Classes

If you have extensive knowledge and experience in photography, consider teaching classes to others. This can include anything from beginner-level classes to advanced techniques. You can teach online or in-person, and charge a fee for your services.

Tip:

To attract students, create a detailed syllabus and promote your classes on social media and local listings. You can also offer discounts for referrals and repeat students.

4. Sell Prints of Your Work

If you have a strong following on social media or a website, consider selling prints of your work. This can include anything from fine art prints to custom-designed products, such as phone cases or tote bags.

Tip:

To increase your sales, promote your products on social media and offer limited-edition prints or special discounts to your followers.

5. License Your Photos

Another way to make money as a photographer is to license your photos to publishers and businesses. This can include anything from magazine covers to advertising campaigns.

Tip:

To increase your chances of getting licensed, create a portfolio of your best work and promote your services to potential clients. You can also partner with stock photo agencies to increase your exposure.

6. Participate in Contests

Participating in photography contests can also be a great way to earn money and gain exposure. Many contests offer cash prizes or other rewards for the winners.

Tip:

To increase your chances of winning, choose contests that align with your style and niche. Make sure to read the rules and guidelines carefully before submitting your entries.

7. Write About Photography

If you have a passion for writing, consider creating content about photography. This can include anything from blog posts to ebooks. You can monetize your content by selling it on your website or through online marketplaces.

Tip:

To attract readers, create content that is informative and engaging. You can also partner with other photographers or industry experts to increase your reach.

8. Create Photography Products

If you have a creative streak, consider creating products that incorporate your photography. This can include anything from calendars to greeting cards.

Tip:

To increase your sales, promote your products on social media and offer limited-edition designs or custom orders.

9. Partner with Brands

If you have a strong following on social media or a website, consider partnering with brands to promote their products. This can include sponsored posts or collaborations.

Tip:

To attract brands, create a media kit that showcases your reach and engagement. You can also offer discounts or exclusive content to your followers as part of the partnership.

10. Attend Events and Shows

Finally, attending photography events and shows can be a great way to network and gain exposure. This can include anything from trade shows to gallery openings.

Tip:

To make the most of your attendance, bring promotional materials and business cards to hand out to potential clients. You can also connect with other photographers and industry professionals to expand your network.

Conclusion

Making money as a photographer can be challenging, but with the right strategies and mindset, it's definitely possible. Whether you're selling your photos online or teaching classes, there are plenty of ways to monetize your skills and turn your passion into a profitable business. So, start exploring your options today and see where your photography can take you!
