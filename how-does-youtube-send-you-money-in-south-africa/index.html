How Does Youtube Send You Money In South Africa

Written by Frank Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

YouTube has become one of the most popular platforms for content creators to share their work with the world. Many YouTubers make a living through the platform, earning money from ads, sponsorships, and other sources. But how exactly does YouTube send you money, especially if you're based in South Africa? In this article, we'll explore the different payment options available to South African YouTubers.

Getting Started: Setting Up Your AdSense Account

Before you can start earning money from YouTube, you'll need to set up an AdSense account. AdSense is Google's advertising platform, and it's what YouTube uses to serve ads on your videos. To set up an AdSense account, you'll need to have a Google account and a bank account in your name. Once your AdSense account is set up, you'll be able to link it to your YouTube channel and start earning money.

Payment Options for South African YouTubers

There are several payment options available to South African YouTubers. The most common options are:

Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)

EFT is the most popular payment option for South African YouTubers. With EFT, your earnings will be transferred directly to your bank account. You'll need to provide your bank account details to AdSense in order to receive payments via EFT.

Wire Transfer

Wire transfer is another option for South African YouTubers. With wire transfer, your earnings will be transferred directly to your bank account, but it can take longer than EFT. You'll also need to pay a fee for each wire transfer.

Checks

Checks are a less common payment option for South African YouTubers. With checks, AdSense will mail you a physical check that you can deposit into your bank account. However, checks can take a long time to arrive and clear, and there may be fees associated with depositing them.

How to Set Your Payment Preferences

To set your payment preferences, log in to your AdSense account and click on the "Payments" tab. From there, you'll be able to choose your preferred payment method and enter your bank account details. You can also set a payment threshold, which is the minimum amount you need to earn before AdSense will send you a payment.

Other Things to Consider

When it comes to receiving payments from YouTube, there are a few other things to keep in mind:

  • AdSense payments are made on a monthly basis, but it can take a few days for the payment to process and reach your bank account.
  • If you're earning money from YouTube, you'll need to pay taxes on your earnings. Make sure to keep track of your income and expenses so you can accurately report them on your tax return.
  • If you're not seeing any earnings in your AdSense account, it may be because your channel is not yet eligible for monetization. Make sure you meet all the eligibility requirements before expecting to receive payments.

In Conclusion

Receiving payments from YouTube as a South African content creator is relatively straightforward. By setting up your AdSense account and choosing your preferred payment method, you'll be well on your way to earning money from your videos. Just remember to keep track of your earnings and expenses, and to meet all the eligibility requirements for monetization.
