How To Start Earning Money On Tiktok In 2023

Written by Petter Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world, with over one billion active users worldwide. With its growing popularity, many people are wondering how they can start earning money on TikTok. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to earn money on TikTok.

Step 1: Build Your TikTok Following

The first step in earning money on TikTok is to build your following. You need to have a significant number of followers to be able to monetize your account. To build your following, you need to create high-quality content that is engaging, entertaining, and relevant to your audience.

You can also use hashtags to increase your visibility on the platform. Use relevant hashtags in your posts to attract more followers. You can also collaborate with other TikTok users to increase your reach and exposure.

Step 2: Join the TikTok Creator Fund

If you have over 100,000 followers, you can join the TikTok Creator Fund. The Creator Fund is a program that pays eligible TikTok creators for their content. To join the Creator Fund, you need to meet the eligibility criteria and apply for the program.

The Creator Fund pays creators based on the views and engagement their content receives. The more views and engagement your content gets, the more money you can earn from the Creator Fund.

Step 3: Partner with Brands

Another way to earn money on TikTok is by partnering with brands. Brands are always looking for influencers to promote their products and services on social media. If you have a significant following on TikTok, you can partner with brands and earn money for promoting their products.

To find brand partnerships, you can reach out to brands directly or join influencer marketing platforms that connect influencers with brands.

Step 4: Sell Merchandise

If you have a loyal following on TikTok, you can sell merchandise to your fans. You can create your own merchandise, such as t-shirts, hats, or phone cases, and sell them to your followers. You can also use print-on-demand services to create and sell merchandise without having to invest in inventory.

Step 5: Use Live Streaming

TikTok also has a live streaming feature that allows creators to interact with their followers in real-time. You can use live streaming to earn money by receiving gifts from your followers. Followers can purchase gifts using virtual coins, and you can convert these coins into real money.

FAQs

Q: How many followers do I need to start earning money on TikTok?

A: You need to have a significant following to start earning money on TikTok. The exact number of followers required varies, but generally, you need at least 10,000 followers to be eligible for monetization.

Q: How much money can I earn on TikTok?

A: The amount of money you can earn on TikTok depends on various factors, such as the number of followers you have, the engagement your content receives, and the monetization methods you use. Some TikTok creators earn thousands of dollars per month, while others earn only a few hundred dollars.

Q: Can I earn money on TikTok without a significant following?

A: It is challenging to monetize your TikTok account without a significant following. However, you can still earn money by joining influencer marketing platforms or selling merchandise to your followers.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you earn money on TikTok:

  • Create high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience.
  • Use relevant hashtags to increase your visibility on the platform.
  • Collaborate with other TikTok users to increase your reach and exposure.
  • Join the TikTok Creator Fund if you are eligible.
  • Partner with brands to earn money for promoting their products.
  • Sell merchandise to your followers.
  • Use live streaming to interact with your followers and earn money from gifts.

By following these tips and tricks, you can start earning money on TikTok and turn your passion for creating content into a lucrative career.
