How To Turn Off Comments On Tiktok Live

Written by Eveline May 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a content creator on TikTok, you know how important it is to interact with your audience. One way to do this is through live streams, where your followers can watch you in real-time and leave comments. However, sometimes you may want to turn off comments during a live stream for various reasons. In this article, we'll show you how to turn off comments on TikTok Live.

Step 1: Start a Live Stream

The first step is to start a live stream. To do this, open the TikTok app and click on the plus sign (+) at the bottom of the screen. Then, click on the "Live" option and give your live stream a title.

Step 2: Click on the Three Dots

Once your live stream has started, click on the three dots at the bottom right-hand corner of the screen. This will open up a menu of options.

Step 3: Select "Turn Off Commenting"

Scroll down the menu until you see the option that says "Turn Off Commenting." Click on this option to turn off comments for your live stream.

Step 4: Confirm Your Choice

A pop-up will appear asking you to confirm your choice to turn off commenting. Click "Yes" to confirm.

Step 5: Enjoy Your Comment-Free Live Stream

And that's it! You've successfully turned off comments for your TikTok Live stream. Now you can focus on creating great content without worrying about managing comments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I turn comments back on during my live stream?

Yes, you can turn comments back on during your live stream. Simply follow the same steps as before and select "Turn On Commenting."

Why would I want to turn off comments during a live stream?

There may be various reasons why you want to turn off comments during a live stream. For example, if you're discussing a sensitive topic or if you want to focus solely on the content without any distractions.

Can I turn off comments for a pre-recorded video?

No, you cannot turn off comments for a pre-recorded video on TikTok.

Can I delete comments on my live stream?

Yes, you can delete comments during your live stream by tapping and holding on the comment you want to delete, and then selecting "Delete."

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for using TikTok Live:

  • Choose a good location with good lighting and minimal background noise.
  • Interact with your audience by responding to comments and answering questions.
  • Promote your live stream ahead of time to generate excitement and increase viewership.
  • Consider partnering with other TikTok creators for a joint live stream.
  • Use the TikTok Live feature to showcase behind-the-scenes content or to give your audience a sneak peek of upcoming projects.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create engaging and successful TikTok Live streams that your followers will love.
