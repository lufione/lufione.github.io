How To Get Youtube Money In Your Bank Account

Written by Daniel May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

Are you a YouTuber trying to earn money from your channel? If so, you might be wondering how to get that money into your bank account. In this article, we’ll go over the steps you need to take to make sure you can successfully receive payments from YouTube.

Step 1: Enable Monetization

The first step to getting paid on YouTube is to enable monetization on your channel. This means you’ll be able to run ads on your videos, and you’ll earn a share of the revenue generated by those ads. To enable monetization, go to your YouTube Studio dashboard and click on “Monetization” in the left-hand menu. From there, you can follow the steps to set up your AdSense account and enable monetization for your channel.

Step 2: Set Up Your Payment Details

Once you’ve enabled monetization, you’ll need to set up your payment details. To do this, go to your AdSense account and click on “Payments” in the left-hand menu. From there, you can choose your payment method (e.g. bank transfer, check, etc.) and enter your payment details. Make sure to double-check that all of your information is correct before submitting it.

Step 3: Meet the Payment Threshold

To receive payments from YouTube, you’ll need to meet the payment threshold. This is the minimum amount of money you need to earn before you can receive a payment. As of 2023, the payment threshold for YouTube is $100. Once you’ve earned at least $100, you’ll be eligible to receive a payment in the next payment cycle.

Step 4: Wait for Your Payment

Once you’ve met the payment threshold, you’ll need to wait for your payment to be processed. YouTube pays its creators on a monthly basis, so you can expect to receive your payment within 21 days of the end of the month in which you met the payment threshold. For example, if you earned $100 in January, you can expect to receive your payment by the end of February.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your YouTube earnings:

  • Make sure to regularly check your AdSense account to keep track of your earnings.
  • Consider diversifying your income streams by using other monetization methods, such as sponsorships, merchandise sales, or Patreon.
  • Try to create high-quality, engaging content that will attract more viewers and increase your ad revenue.
  • Engage with your audience and build a community around your channel to encourage viewers to support you in other ways.

FAQ

How often does YouTube pay its creators?

YouTube pays its creators on a monthly basis.

What is the payment threshold for YouTube?

The payment threshold for YouTube is $100.

What payment methods does YouTube offer?

YouTube offers several payment methods, including bank transfer, check, and Western Union Quick Cash.

Can I receive payments if I’m not based in the United States?

Yes, YouTube pays creators in many different countries. However, the payment methods and currencies available may vary depending on your location.

Conclusion

Getting paid on YouTube may seem complicated, but it’s actually a straightforward process once you know the steps. By enabling monetization, setting up your payment details, meeting the payment threshold, and creating engaging content, you can start earning money from your channel and building a successful YouTube career.
