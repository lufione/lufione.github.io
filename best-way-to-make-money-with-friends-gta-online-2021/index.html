Introduction

GTA Online is a fun game to play with friends, and there are many ways to make money. Making money in GTA Online can be challenging, especially if you don't know where to start. In this blog post, we will share the best ways to make money with your friends in GTA Online 2021.

Join a Heist

One of the best ways to make money with friends in GTA Online is by joining a heist. Heists are multiplayer missions that require teamwork and coordination to complete. You can earn a lot of money by completing heists, and the more players you have on your team, the more money you can make. To join a heist, you need to have a high-end apartment and be at least level 12.

Sell Cargo

Another way to make money with friends in GTA Online is by selling cargo. You can buy cargo warehouses and then sell the cargo to make a profit. The more cargo you sell, the more money you can make. You can also team up with your friends to sell cargo, which will make the process easier and faster.

Participate in Races

Participating in races is another way to make money with friends in GTA Online. You can enter races with your friends and bet on the winner. The winner of the race will earn the prize money, which can be split between the participants. You can also earn money by winning races, which can be a fun way to compete with your friends.

Complete Daily Objectives

Completing daily objectives is another way to make money with friends in GTA Online. Daily objectives are small tasks that you can complete every day to earn money and RP. You can team up with your friends to complete these objectives, which will make the process faster and more enjoyable.

Participate in Clubhouse Contracts

Clubhouse contracts are another way to make money with friends in GTA Online. You can join a motorcycle club and participate in clubhouse contracts to earn money. These contracts require teamwork and coordination, which makes them more fun to complete with your friends.

Do Contact Missions

Contact missions are another way to make money with friends in GTA Online. You can team up with your friends to complete these missions, which require you to complete various tasks to earn money. Contact missions can be a fun way to earn money and RP, especially if you have a good team.

Participate in Special Events

Special events are another way to make money with friends in GTA Online. These events are limited-time events that offer special rewards and bonuses. You can team up with your friends to participate in these events, which can be a fun way to earn money and RP.

Invest in the Stock Market

Investing in the stock market is another way to make money with friends in GTA Online. You can buy stocks and then sell them when the price goes up to make a profit. You can also team up with your friends to invest in the stock market, which can be a fun way to make money.

Conclusion

Making money with friends in GTA Online can be a fun and rewarding experience. There are many ways to make money in the game, and by teaming up with your friends, you can earn even more money.
