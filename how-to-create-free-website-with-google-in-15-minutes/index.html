How To Create A Free Website With Google In 15 Minutes

Written by Christine Jun 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Creating a website has never been easier, thanks to Google's free website builder. With this tool, you can create a professional-looking website in just 15 minutes. Here's how to do it.

Step 1: Sign Up for a Google Account

To get started, you'll need a Google account. If you don't have one already, go to https://accounts.google.com/signup and create one. It's free and only takes a few minutes.

Step 2: Go to Google Sites

Once you have a Google account, go to https://sites.google.com/new. This is where you'll create your website.

Step 3: Choose a Template

Google Sites offers a variety of templates to choose from. You can browse through the templates and select one that suits your needs. If you're not sure which one to choose, you can always change it later.

Step 4: Customize Your Website

After you've chosen a template, it's time to customize your website. You can add pages, change the layout, and add content. Google Sites is very user-friendly, so you don't need any coding skills to create a great-looking website.

Step 5: Add Content

When adding content to your website, make sure it's relevant and engaging. You want your visitors to stay on your website and come back for more. You can add text, images, videos, and more.

Step 6: Preview Your Website

Before publishing your website, preview it to make sure everything looks good. You can do this by clicking on the "Preview" button in the top right-hand corner of the screen.

Step 7: Publish Your Website

Once you're happy with your website, it's time to publish it. Click on the "Publish" button in the top right-hand corner of the screen. Your website will be live on the internet in just a few seconds.

Step 8: Share Your Website

Now that your website is published, it's time to share it with the world. You can share it on social media, email it to friends and family, and even use it as your business website.

Step 9: Monitor Your Website

After publishing your website, it's important to monitor it. Check your website analytics to see how many visitors you're getting, which pages are popular, and where your visitors are coming from.

Step 10: Update Your Website

Finally, make sure to update your website regularly. Add new content, update old content, and make sure everything is up-to-date. This will keep your visitors coming back for more.

Conclusion

Creating a free website with Google is quick, easy, and requires no coding skills. Follow these 10 steps, and you'll have a professional-looking website in just 15 minutes.
