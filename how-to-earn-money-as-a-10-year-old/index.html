How To Earn Money As A 10 Year Old

Written by Luffy May 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a 10 year old looking to earn some extra cash, there are plenty of ways to do it. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to make money as a kid.

Sell Crafts

If you're artistic, you can make money by selling your crafts. You can make jewelry, keychains, or even slime to sell. Once you have your products, you can sell them at a local farmer's market or craft fair.

Do Yard Work

If you're willing to put in some hard work, you can make money by doing yard work. You can offer to mow lawns, rake leaves, or shovel snow for your neighbors. Make sure to charge a fair price for your services.

Walk Dogs

If you love dogs, you can make money by walking them. Offer your services to neighbors or family friends who have dogs. You can charge per walk or per day.

Sell Lemonade

One classic way to make money as a kid is by selling lemonade. Set up a stand in your front yard and advertise your lemonade to passing cars and pedestrians. Make sure to have plenty of ice and cups on hand.

Do Chores for Family Members

If you're looking to make some money at home, offer to do chores for your family members. You can clean the house, do laundry, or wash dishes for a fee.

Babysit

If you're responsible and mature, you can make money by babysitting. Offer your services to family friends or neighbors who have young children. Make sure to have references available.

Collect and Sell Items

If you're a collector, you can make money by selling your items. Whether it's stamps, coins, or action figures, there's always someone looking to buy. Consider selling your items online or at a local collector's show.

Do Odd Jobs

If you're willing to do odd jobs, you can make money by helping out your neighbors. You can offer to paint a fence, wash a car, or even run errands for a fee.

Offer Tutoring Services

If you're good at a particular subject, you can make money by offering tutoring services. You can help younger students with their homework or even offer online tutoring services.

Participate in Paid Surveys

If you're looking to make money online, consider participating in paid surveys. There are plenty of websites that will pay you to take surveys and provide feedback. Just make sure to do your research and only participate in legitimate surveys.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of ways to make money as a 10 year old. Whether you're looking to make money at home or in your community, there's a job out there for you. Just make sure to be responsible, work hard, and have fun!
