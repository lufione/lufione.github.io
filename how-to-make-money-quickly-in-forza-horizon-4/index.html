How to Make Money Quickly in Forza Horizon 4

Introduction

Forza Horizon 4 is an open-world racing game that offers players a chance to explore the beautiful British countryside in some of the world's most exotic cars. As players progress through the game, they will need to earn money to buy new cars and upgrade their existing ones. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make money quickly in Forza Horizon 4.

Complete Races and Events

The easiest way to earn money in Forza Horizon 4 is by completing races and events. Each race or event will reward you with a certain amount of money based on your performance. Be sure to choose the races and events that offer the highest payouts to maximize your earnings.

Use the Wheelspin

Forza Horizon 4 features a Wheelspin system that allows players to win prizes such as cars, credits, and other items. One of the prizes you can win is a large sum of credits, which can help you earn money quickly. Be sure to use your Wheelspins regularly to increase your chances of winning.

Invest in Real Estate

One of the best ways to earn passive income in Forza Horizon 4 is by investing in real estate. You can purchase properties throughout the map and earn a steady stream of income from them. Be sure to choose properties that offer the highest payouts to maximize your earnings.

Sell Cars on the Auction House

If you have extra cars in your garage that you don't need, consider selling them on the Auction House. You can set your own price and earn a good amount of money if you have a rare or highly sought-after car.

Participate in Forzathon Live Events

Forzathon Live events are special events that occur regularly throughout the game. These events offer players a chance to earn significant amounts of money and other rewards by completing various challenges. Be sure to participate in these events whenever possible to earn more money.

Complete Horizon Story Chapters

Horizon Story chapters are special missions that offer players a chance to earn money and other rewards. These missions can be completed at any time and offer a significant amount of money upon completion. Be sure to complete as many Horizon Story chapters as possible to earn more money.

Upgrade Your Cars

Upgrading your cars can help you earn more money in races and events. Be sure to invest in upgrades that will give you an edge over your opponents, such as better tires, brakes, and engines. This will help you win more races and earn more money.

Join a Club

Joining a club can help you earn money and other rewards by completing club events and challenges. Be sure to join an active club with other players who are dedicated to earning money and winning races.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many ways to make money quickly in Forza Horizon 4. By completing races and events, using the Wheelspin, investing in real estate, selling cars on the Auction House, participating in Forzathon Live events, completing Horizon Story chapters, upgrading your cars, and joining a club, you can earn a significant amount of money and progress through the game at a faster pace.
