Users can get discounts and cashbacks when they use GCash to pay for their purchases. The amount of cashback you can get varies depending on the merchant and the promo.

GCash Invest Money

GCash Invest Money is a feature in the GCash app that allows users to invest in various funds, such as money market funds and bond funds. Users can earn money through dividends and capital gains when the value of the funds increase. The amount of earnings you can get depends on the performance of the funds.

GCash Lazada

GCash Lazada is a feature in the GCash app that allows users to earn money by shopping in Lazada using their GCash. Users can get discounts and cashbacks when they use GCash to pay for their purchases in Lazada. The amount of cashback you can get varies depending on the promo.

GCash Refer to Earn

GCash Refer to Earn is a feature in the GCash app that allows users to earn money by referring their friends to use GCash. Users can get a referral bonus when their friends sign up for GCash and complete certain tasks, such as linking their bank account or using GCash to pay for their purchases.

GCash Forest Give Back

GCash Forest Give Back is a feature in the GCash app that allows users to donate their green energy to various causes, such as education, health, and environment. Users can earn GCash credits by donating their green energy, and the amount of credits you can get depends on the cause and the amount of green energy you donate.
