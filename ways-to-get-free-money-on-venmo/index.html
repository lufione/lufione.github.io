Ways To Get Free Money On Venmo In 2023

Written by Petter May 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

Venmo, a popular mobile payment application, has become a part of our daily lives. We use it to send and receive money from friends and family, split bills, and even pay for groceries. But did you know that there are ways to earn free money on Venmo? In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks that will help you get some extra cash in your Venmo account.

Sign up for Venmo Rewards

Venmo offers a rewards program that allows you to earn cashback on your purchases. All you have to do is link your Venmo account to the participating merchants and pay with Venmo. You can earn up to 5% cashback on your purchases, which will be credited to your Venmo account.

Refer a Friend

Venmo has a referral program that rewards you and your friends with free money. All you have to do is invite your friends to join Venmo using your referral link. Once your friend signs up and makes their first transaction, both of you will receive $10 in your Venmo account.

Join Venmo Groups

Venmo has a feature called Venmo groups, where you can create or join groups with your friends and split bills. However, some groups offer free money to their members. Some groups may require you to complete certain tasks or challenges to earn the money, but it's worth checking out.

Participate in Venmo Contests

Venmo occasionally hosts contests on their social media platforms, where you can win free money. To participate, follow Venmo on their social media accounts and keep an eye out for their contests. You may be required to complete certain tasks or share posts to enter the contest.

Use Cashback Apps

There are several cashback apps that allow you to earn money when you shop at participating merchants. These apps include Rakuten, Ibotta, and Dosh. You can link your Venmo account to these apps and receive cashback rewards directly to your Venmo account.

Donate Plasma

If you're looking for a more unconventional way to earn free money, consider donating plasma. Many plasma donation centers offer compensation for your donation, and you can receive payment through Venmo.

Ask for Donations

If you're in a financial bind and need some extra cash, consider asking your friends and family for donations on Venmo. You can create a Venmo request and explain your situation. Your loved ones may be willing to help you out.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are several ways to earn free money on Venmo in 2023. Whether it's through Venmo Rewards, referral programs, or participating in contests, there's no shortage of opportunities to earn some extra cash. Just be sure to read the terms and conditions of each program to ensure that you're eligible to participate. Happy earning!
