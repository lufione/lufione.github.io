How Can An 11 Year Old Make Money In The Winter

Written by Aprilia Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Winter is here and with it comes the opportunity for 11-year-olds to earn some extra cash. Whether you need money to buy gifts for your family or want to save up for something special, there are plenty of ways to make money during the colder months. Here are some ideas to get you started.

Sell Hot Beverages

During the winter, people love to warm up with hot beverages. You can take advantage of this by selling hot chocolate, coffee, or tea. Set up a stand outside your home or at a local park and offer your warm drinks to passersby. You can even add some marshmallows or whipped cream to make your drinks extra special.

Shovel Snow

When it snows, many people need help shoveling their driveways and sidewalks. Offer your services to your neighbors or post flyers around your community. You can charge per hour or per job, and you'll get some exercise while making money.

Wrap Gifts

With the holidays approaching, many people need help wrapping gifts. Offer your services to your family, friends, and neighbors. You can charge per gift or per hour, and you'll get to practice your wrapping skills.

Sell Baked Goods

Winter is the perfect time for baking. Make some cookies, brownies, or cupcakes and sell them at a local farmer's market or craft fair. You can also offer to bake goods for your family and friends for a fee.

Walk Dogs

Even in the winter, dogs need exercise. Offer to walk dogs for your neighbors or post flyers around your community. You can charge per walk or per day, and you'll get to spend time with some furry friends.

Do Chores for Seniors

Winter can be tough for seniors who may have trouble getting around. Offer to do chores for seniors in your community, such as shoveling snow, running errands, or cleaning their homes. You can charge per hour or per job, and you'll be doing a good deed while making money.

Wrap Presents at Stores

During the holiday season, many stores offer gift wrapping services. Check with your local retailers to see if they need help wrapping presents. You'll get paid for your time, and you'll get to practice your wrapping skills.

Sell Crafts

Winter is a great time to make crafts. You can make holiday decorations, winter-themed items, or anything else you're good at. Sell your crafts at a local farmer's market or craft fair, or offer them for sale online.

Offer Tutoring Services

If you're good at a particular subject, offer tutoring services to your classmates or younger students. You can charge per hour or per session, and you'll be helping others while making money.

Deliver Food

Many restaurants offer delivery services, and they may need help during the winter months. Check with your local restaurants to see if they need delivery drivers. You'll get paid for your time, and you may even get some free food.

In Conclusion

There are plenty of ways for 11-year-olds to make money during the winter. Whether you choose to shovel snow, sell hot beverages, or offer tutoring services, you can earn some extra cash while having fun. Just be sure to get permission from your parents before starting any business ventures.
