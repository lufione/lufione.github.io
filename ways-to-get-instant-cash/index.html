10 Ways To Get Instant Cash In 2023

Written by Bardi Sep 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Money emergencies happen to everyone. Whether it's a medical emergency or a car repair, unexpected expenses can put a dent in your finances. Here are some ways to get instant cash in 2023.

1. Use a Credit Card Cash Advance

If you have a credit card, you can get a cash advance. However, keep in mind that cash advances are expensive. You'll pay a fee of 3-5% of the amount you withdraw, and the interest rate is usually higher than your regular credit card rate.

2. Sell Items Online

You can sell items you no longer need on websites such as eBay or Craigslist. This is a quick way to get cash, but keep in mind that you may not get as much money as you would if you sold the item in a physical store.

3. Participate in Online Surveys

Some websites pay you to take surveys. While this won't make you rich, it's an easy way to earn some extra cash in your spare time.

4. Drive for a Ride-Sharing Service

If you have a car, you can drive for ride-sharing services such as Uber or Lyft. You can work on your own schedule and make money by giving people rides.

5. Rent Out a Room

If you have a spare room, you can rent it out on websites such as Airbnb. This is a great way to make extra money if you live in a popular tourist destination.

6. Get a Personal Loan

If you need a larger amount of money, you can get a personal loan. However, keep in mind that you'll need good credit to qualify for a loan with a low interest rate.

7. Freelance

If you have a skill such as writing, graphic design, or programming, you can freelance. You can find freelance work on websites such as Upwork or Freelancer.

8. Sell Plasma

You can donate plasma for money. The process takes a few hours, but you can make up to $50 per donation.

9. Participate in Focus Groups

Some companies pay you to participate in focus groups. You'll give your opinion on products or services, and you'll get paid for your time.

10. Do Odd Jobs

You can do odd jobs for people in your community. This can include anything from mowing lawns to cleaning houses. You can advertise your services on websites such as Craigslist.

Conclusion

There are many ways to get instant cash in 2023. Whether you sell items online, participate in online surveys, or drive for a ride-sharing service, there's something for everyone. Keep in mind that some methods may not be suitable for everyone, so choose the one that's right for you.
