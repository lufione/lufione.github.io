What s The Fastest Way To Get Money In Animal Crossing

Written by Oliver Jul 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Animal Crossing is a beloved game that has captured the hearts of people worldwide. One of the most important aspects of the game is earning money, which you can use to buy items and upgrade your island. In this article, we'll explore some of the fastest ways to get money in Animal Crossing.

Sell Your Fruits

One of the easiest ways to earn money in Animal Crossing is by selling your fruits. You can grow different kinds of fruits on your island, and each of them has a different selling price. For example, a single fruit can sell for around 100-500 bells, depending on the type. By planting more fruit trees and harvesting them regularly, you can earn a decent amount of money in no time.

Catch Fish and Bugs

Another way to get money quickly in Animal Crossing is by catching fish and bugs. You can find a variety of fish and bugs in different locations on your island. Each of them has a different selling price, so it's essential to catch as many as you can. You can sell them to Timmy and Tommy at Nook's Cranny or the drop-off box for a good amount of bells.

Participate in Events

Animal Crossing hosts many events throughout the year, which can be a great way to earn a lot of money. These events include fishing tournaments, bug catching competitions, and seasonal events like Halloween and Christmas. By participating in these events, you can earn special items and earn a lot of bells.

Sell Your DIY Items

DIY items are a great way to earn money in Animal Crossing. You can craft various items using materials like wood, iron, and clay. These items can be sold for a good amount of bells or traded with other players. By crafting and selling DIY items, you can earn a lot of money quickly.

Visit Other Islands

Visiting other islands can be a great way to earn money in Animal Crossing. You can trade items with other players, sell your items on other islands, and participate in island tours. By visiting other islands regularly, you can earn a lot of money and make new friends in the process.

Invest in the Stalk Market

The stalk market is a great way to earn money in Animal Crossing, but it's also a risky one. The stalk market is based on the turnip prices, which change every day. You can buy turnips from Daisy Mae on Sundays and sell them when the prices are high. However, if you wait too long, the turnips will spoil, and you'll lose your investment. So, it's essential to keep an eye on the turnip prices and sell them at the right time.

Trade with Other Players

Trading with other players can be a great way to earn money in Animal Crossing. You can trade items, bells, or even villagers with other players. By trading with other players, you can get the items you need and earn a lot of money in the process.

Sell Your Fossils

Fossils are another great way to earn money in Animal Crossing. You can find fossils buried around your island, and each of them has a different selling price. By collecting and selling fossils, you can earn a lot of money and complete your museum collection at the same time.

Use the Money Tree Method

The money tree method is a great way to earn a lot of bells quickly. To do this, you need to plant a bag of bells in the ground, and after a few days, a money tree will grow. The money tree will give you three bags of bells, which can be a significant boost to your funds.

Upgrade Your Island

By upgrading your island, you can earn a lot of money in Animal Crossing. You can build bridges, inclines, and other infrastructure that will make it easier for you to navigate and collect items. By upgrading your island, you can also attract more visitors, which can lead to more sales and more money.

Conclusion

There are many ways to earn money in Animal Crossing, and the methods above are just a few of them. By using these tips and tricks, you can earn a lot of bells quickly and upgrade your island in no time. So, start playing, and have fun!
