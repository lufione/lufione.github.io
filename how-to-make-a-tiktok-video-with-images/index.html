How To Make A Tiktok Video With Images

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create short videos. If you want to make a TikTok video with images, you will need to follow certain steps. In this article, we will discuss how to make a TikTok video with images in a step-by-step manner.

Step 1: Open TikTok App

The first thing you need to do is open the TikTok app on your smartphone. If you don't have the app yet, you can download it from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Step 2: Click on the "+" Button

Once you have opened the app, you will see a "+" button on the bottom of the screen. Click on it to start creating a new video.

Step 3: Select Images

After clicking on the "+" button, you will be taken to the camera screen. To create a video with images, click on the "Upload" button on the bottom left corner of the screen. This will allow you to select images from your phone's gallery.

Step 4: Choose the Images

Once you have selected the images you want to use, click on "Next" to proceed to the next screen. Here, you can rearrange the images by dragging them and also add filters by clicking on the filter icon.

Step 5: Add Music

If you want to add music to your video, click on the "Sounds" button on the bottom of the screen. Here, you can browse through the available music tracks on TikTok and select the one you like.

Step 6: Record Video

Once you have selected the images and music, click on the "Record" button on the bottom of the screen. Hold the record button and move the camera to create a video with the images.

Step 7: Edit Video

After recording the video, you can edit it by adding text, stickers, and effects. Click on the "Effects" button on the bottom left corner of the screen to add effects.

Step 8: Preview Video

Once you have added all the elements to your video, click on the "Preview" button to preview your video. You can make any changes if necessary.

Step 9: Post Video

If you are satisfied with your video, click on the "Next" button to post it. You can also add a caption and hashtags to your video before posting.

Step 10: Share Video

After posting the video, you can share it with your friends and followers on TikTok. You can also share it on other social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Conclusion

Creating a TikTok video with images is a fun and creative way to engage with your audience on social media. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can create a TikTok video with images that is sure to be a hit with your followers. So, go ahead and give it a try!
