How To Claim Money From Youtube

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

YouTube is a platform where people can share their creativity and earn money. It is one of the most popular video sharing platforms in the world. If you are a content creator on YouTube and you have monetized your videos, then you are eligible to earn money. In this article, we will guide you on how to claim money from YouTube.

Step 1: Meet the YouTube Partner Program Requirements

Before you can claim money from YouTube, you need to meet the YouTube Partner Program requirements. To be eligible for the program, you need to have at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the last 12 months on your channel. You also need to comply with YouTube's policies and guidelines.

Step 2: Set up Your AdSense Account

To claim money from YouTube, you need to have an AdSense account. AdSense is a program that Google uses to serve ads on your videos. You can set up your AdSense account by logging into your YouTube account and clicking on the "Monetization" tab. From there, you will be redirected to AdSense to complete the sign-up process.

Step 3: Enable Monetization on Your Videos

After you have set up your AdSense account, you need to enable monetization on your videos. You can do this by going to the "Monetization" tab in your YouTube account settings. From there, you can select the videos you want to monetize and enable ads on them.

Step 4: Track Your Earnings

Once you have enabled monetization on your videos, you can track your earnings in your AdSense account. AdSense provides you with detailed reports on your earnings, including the number of clicks on your ads, the cost per click, and your estimated earnings.

Step 5: Claim Your Money

To claim your money from AdSense, you need to reach the payment threshold. The payment threshold is $100. Once you have reached the threshold, AdSense will send you a payment on the 21st of each month. You can choose to receive your payments via direct deposit, wire transfer, or check.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to get paid from YouTube?

It takes approximately 2-3 weeks for AdSense to process your earnings and send you a payment.

2. Can I claim money from YouTube if I am not a YouTube Partner?

No, you need to be a YouTube Partner to claim money from YouTube.

3. Can I claim money from YouTube if my videos have been demonetized?

No, if your videos have been demonetized, you cannot claim money from YouTube. You need to comply with YouTube's policies and guidelines to be eligible for monetization.

Tips and Tricks

1. Create high-quality content

To earn more money from YouTube, you need to create high-quality content that engages your audience. This will increase your watch time and ad revenue.

2. Collaborate with other YouTubers

Collaborating with other YouTubers can help you grow your audience and increase your earnings.

3. Promote your videos on social media

Promoting your videos on social media can help you reach a wider audience and increase your views and earnings. In conclusion, claiming money from YouTube is a simple and straightforward process. By following the steps mentioned in this article, you can start earning money from your YouTube channel. Remember to comply with YouTube's policies and guidelines to maintain your eligibility for monetization.
