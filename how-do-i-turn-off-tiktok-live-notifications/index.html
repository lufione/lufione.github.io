How To Turn Off Tiktok Live Notifications

Written by Bardi Oct 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in recent years. With millions of users worldwide, TikTok provides a space for people to share their creativity and connect with others. One of the features that TikTok offers is live streaming, where users can broadcast their videos in real-time. While this feature can be a lot of fun, it can also be overwhelming to receive constant notifications about live streams. Here's how you can turn off TikTok live notifications:

Step 1: Open TikTok

To turn off TikTok live notifications, you'll need to open the app on your mobile device. You can do this by tapping on the TikTok icon on your home screen or app drawer.

Step 2: Go to Your Profile

Once you've opened the TikTok app, go to your profile by tapping on the "Me" icon in the bottom right-hand corner of the screen.

Step 3: Tap on the Three Dots

Next, tap on the three dots in the top right-hand corner of the screen. This will open a menu with several options.

Step 4: Go to Settings and Privacy

In the menu, scroll down until you see "Settings and Privacy." Tap on this option to continue.

Step 5: Tap on Notifications

Once you're in the "Settings and Privacy" menu, tap on "Notifications." This will open a list of notification settings that you can customize.

Step 6: Tap on Live

In the list of notification settings, find "Live" and tap on it. This will open the live notification settings.

Step 7: Turn Off the Toggle Switch

To turn off TikTok live notifications, simply toggle the switch next to "Live" to the off position. The switch will turn gray when it's off.

Step 8: Confirm Your Choice

After turning off the live notification switch, you'll be asked to confirm your choice. Tap "Turn Off" to confirm that you want to turn off live notifications.

Step 9: Exit the Settings Menu

Once you've turned off live notifications, you can exit the settings menu by tapping on the back arrow in the top left-hand corner of the screen. This will take you back to your profile.

Step 10: Enjoy a Less Cluttered Notification Feed

Now that you've turned off TikTok live notifications, you can enjoy a less cluttered notification feed. You'll no longer receive notifications every time someone goes live on TikTok.

Conclusion

TikTok live notifications can be overwhelming, especially if you follow a lot of users. By following these simple steps, you can turn off live notifications and enjoy a less cluttered notification feed. Now you can focus on the content that matters most to you on TikTok.
