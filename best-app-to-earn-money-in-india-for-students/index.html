Best Apps To Earn Money In India For Students

Written by Daniel May 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

As a student, it's always a good idea to have some extra cash in your pocket. Fortunately, there are a variety of apps available that allow you to earn money from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will take a look at some of the best apps to earn money in India for students.

1. Swagbucks

Swagbucks is a popular app that allows you to earn money by completing surveys, watching videos, and even shopping online. You can redeem your points for cash or gift cards, making it a great way to earn some extra income.

2. Roz Dhan

Roz Dhan is an app that pays you for completing various tasks, such as reading news articles, playing games, and inviting friends. You can withdraw your earnings via Paytm or bank transfer.

3. Google Opinion Rewards

Google Opinion Rewards is an app that rewards you for sharing your opinions. You will receive surveys from Google, and for each completed survey, you will earn Google Play credits that can be used for app purchases or in-app purchases.

4. MPL

MPL is a gaming app that allows you to earn money by playing games. You can participate in various tournaments and win real cash prizes. MPL has a variety of games available, including popular games like PUBG and Free Fire.

5. Meesho

Meesho is an app that allows you to earn money by reselling products. You can choose from a variety of products, including fashion, beauty, and home decor, and earn a commission on each sale. Meesho is a great way to start your own online business without any investment.

6. TaskBucks

TaskBucks is an app that pays you for completing simple tasks like downloading apps, watching videos, and taking surveys. You can redeem your earnings via Paytm or mobile recharge.

7. CashKaro

CashKaro is a cashback app that allows you to earn money by shopping online. You can earn cashback on purchases from a variety of retailers, including Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra. CashKaro also has a referral program that allows you to earn money by inviting friends.

8. Dream11

Dream11 is a fantasy sports app that allows you to earn money by creating your own virtual teams and participating in tournaments. You can win real cash prizes by competing against other users.

9. Paytm First Games

Paytm First Games is a gaming app that allows you to earn money by playing games. You can participate in various tournaments and win real cash prizes. Paytm First Games has a variety of games available, including popular games like Rummy and Ludo.

10. Udemy

Udemy is an online learning platform that allows you to earn money by creating and selling your own courses. If you have expertise in a particular subject, you can create a course and earn passive income from it.

Conclusion

There are a variety of apps available that allow you to earn money as a student. Whether you prefer completing surveys, playing games, or shopping online, there is an app out there for you. Be sure to check out the apps listed above and start earning some extra cash today!
