How To Make Money As A 10 Year Old In 2021

Written by Oliver Sep 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a 10 year old looking for ways to earn some extra cash? Well, you're in luck! There are plenty of ways for kids your age to make money. Here are some of the best ways to get started:

1. Start a Lemonade Stand

One of the most classic ways for kids to make money is by setting up a lemonade stand. All you need is some lemonade mix, cups, and a table to set up your stand. Make sure to advertise your stand with signs and flyers around your neighborhood.

2. Walk Dogs or Pet Sit

If you love animals, consider offering your services as a dog walker or pet sitter. Many busy families would love to have someone trustworthy take care of their pets while they're away. Make sure to ask for references and charge a fair rate for your services.

3. Do Chores for Neighbors

Another way to make money is by doing chores for your neighbors. Offer to mow lawns, rake leaves, or shovel snow in the winter. You can also offer to clean houses or help with errands. Make sure to negotiate a fair price for your services.

4. Sell Crafts or Artwork

If you're creative, consider selling your crafts or artwork. You can set up a booth at a local craft fair or sell your items online. Make sure to price your items appropriately and promote your products on social media.

5. Offer Tutoring Services

If you're good at a particular subject, consider offering tutoring services to younger kids. You can advertise your services at school or on social media. Make sure to charge a fair rate and be patient with your students.

6. Participate in Online Surveys

There are several websites that pay kids to take online surveys. This is a great way to make some extra money in your spare time. Just make sure to only sign up for reputable survey sites.

7. Sell Items Online

If you have items you no longer need, consider selling them online. You can use sites like eBay, Amazon, or Craigslist to sell items like clothes, toys, or electronics. Just make sure to get your parents' permission before selling anything.

8. Babysit

Babysitting is a great way to make money if you're responsible and enjoy spending time with kids. Make sure to get certified in CPR and first aid, and ask for references from families you've babysat for in the past.

9. Make and Sell Jewelry

If you're crafty, consider making and selling jewelry. You can use sites like Etsy to sell your items online, or set up a booth at local craft fairs. Make sure to price your items appropriately and promote your products on social media.

10. Wash Cars

Finally, you can make money by washing cars. Offer your services to your neighbors or set up a car wash in your driveway. Make sure to have all the necessary supplies, like soap, sponges, and towels.

Conclusion

Making money as a 10 year old may seem daunting, but there are plenty of ways to get started. Whether you're selling lemonade, walking dogs, or making crafts, there's a way for every kid to earn some extra cash. Just make sure to be responsible and get your parents' permission before starting any money-making ventures.
