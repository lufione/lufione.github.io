How Can I Make Money As A Stay At Home Mum In The Uk

Written by Frank May 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Being a stay at home mum is a full-time job, but it doesn't mean you can't earn an income. With the rise of technology and the internet, there are now more opportunities than ever for stay at home mums to make money from home. In this article, we'll explore 10 ways you can earn money while still being there for your family.

1. Freelance Writing

If you have a talent for writing, you can make money as a freelance writer. Many websites and blogs require content, and they're willing to pay for it. You can start by creating a portfolio of your work and reaching out to potential clients.

2. Online Tutoring

If you have expertise in a particular subject, you can offer your services as an online tutor. Many parents are looking for tutors to help their children with their studies, and you can do it from the comfort of your own home.

3. Virtual Assistant

Many businesses need help with administrative tasks, and they're willing to pay for it. As a virtual assistant, you can help with tasks such as email management, social media, and data entry.

4. Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay you to take surveys. While it won't make you rich, it's an easy way to earn some extra cash in your spare time.

5. Sell Products Online

If you're crafty, you can create products to sell online. You can set up a shop on websites such as Etsy or Amazon and sell your products to a worldwide audience.

6. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is when you promote someone else's product and earn a commission for every sale made through your unique link. You can promote products on your blog or social media and earn money while you sleep.

7. Dropshipping

Dropshipping is when you sell products without holding any inventory. You simply set up an online store and when someone makes a purchase, you order the product from a supplier who ships it directly to the customer.

8. Pet Sitting

If you love animals, you can offer your services as a pet sitter. Many pet owners need someone to look after their pets while they're away, and you can earn money by providing that service.

9. Online Coaching

If you have expertise in a particular area, you can offer your services as an online coach. You can coach people on anything from fitness to business, and you can do it all from the comfort of your own home.

10. Transcription

If you have good typing skills, you can earn money as a transcriptionist. Many businesses need audio or video files transcribed, and they're willing to pay for it.

Conclusion

Being a stay at home mum doesn't mean you can't earn an income. With the many opportunities available online, you can make money while still being there for your family. Whether you choose to freelance write or start your own online store, there's an option out there for everyone.
