How Many Views to Start Making Money on TikTok

If you're like many people, you've probably wondered how many views you need to start making money on TikTok. While the answer isn't straightforward, there are a few things you should know if you're hoping to monetize your TikTok account in 2023.

Understanding TikTok's Monetization Policies

Before we dive into the specifics of how many views you need to start making money on TikTok, it's important to understand TikTok's monetization policies. Essentially, TikTok pays creators for their content through a program called the TikTok Creator Fund. However, not every creator is eligible to participate in the Creator Fund, and even those who are eligible won't necessarily make a lot of money.

Eligibility Requirements for the TikTok Creator Fund

To be eligible for the TikTok Creator Fund, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You must have at least 100,000 followers
  • You must have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days
  • You must meet TikTok's community guidelines and terms of service

If you meet these requirements, you can apply to join the Creator Fund. However, just because you're eligible doesn't mean you'll automatically start making money.

How Much Money Can You Make on TikTok?

The amount of money you can make on TikTok varies depending on a few factors, including:

  • The number of views your videos get
  • The engagement rate of your videos (likes, comments, shares)
  • The type of content you create
  • Your location and audience demographics

While it's difficult to predict exactly how much money you'll make on TikTok, most estimates suggest that creators can expect to earn anywhere from $0.01 to $0.02 per view. That means if you have a video that gets 10,000 views, you could earn between $100 and $200.

How Many Views Do You Need to Make Money on TikTok?

Now that you have a better understanding of how TikTok's monetization policies work and how much money you can expect to make, let's answer the question you came here for: how many views do you need to start making money on TikTok?

The answer is that it depends. As we mentioned earlier, you need at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days to be eligible for the Creator Fund. However, just because you're eligible doesn't mean you'll start making money right away.

Ultimately, the number of views you need to start making money on TikTok will depend on a variety of factors, including your engagement rate, the type of content you create, and your location and audience demographics. However, as a general rule of thumb, you'll likely need to have at least 500,000 to 1 million views per month to start making a meaningful amount of money on TikTok.

Tips for Growing Your TikTok Account

If you're hoping to monetize your TikTok account in 2023, there are a few things you can do to increase your chances of success:

  • Create high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your target audience
  • Use relevant hashtags to increase the visibility of your videos
  • Promote your TikTok account on other social media platforms
  • Engage with your followers and respond to comments
  • Collaborate with other TikTok creators to reach new audiences

By following these tips and consistently creating great content, you'll be well on your way to building a successful TikTok account and potentially earning money through the Creator Fund.

Conclusion

While there's no magic number of views you need to start making money on TikTok, it's clear that you'll need to have a significant following and engagement rate to be successful. By following the eligibility requirements for the Creator Fund, creating high-quality content, and engaging with your followers, you'll be well on your way to monetizing your TikTok account in 2023.
