How To Make Money As A 10 Year Old During Quarantine

Written by Bardi May 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

With the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, many people are struggling financially. This includes adults who have lost their jobs and are having a hard time making ends meet. However, children are not exempt from the financial struggles that come with a pandemic. If you're a 10 year old looking to make some extra money during quarantine, you're in luck! There are several ways you can earn some cash from the comfort of your own home.

Sell Homemade Crafts

One way to make money as a 10 year old during quarantine is to sell homemade crafts. This can include things like friendship bracelets, painted rocks, or even homemade slime. You can sell your crafts on websites like Etsy or even create your own website to showcase and sell your creations. Make sure to price your items fairly and include shipping costs if necessary.

Offer Pet Services

If you love animals, offering pet services can be a great way to make money. This can include walking dogs, pet sitting, or even offering to clean up after pets. Make sure to get permission from your parents before offering these services and make sure you're comfortable with the animals you'll be working with.

Start a YouTube Channel

If you're comfortable in front of a camera, starting a YouTube channel can be a great way to make money. You can create videos about things you're passionate about, like gaming, cooking, or even just vlogging about your daily life. Once you have a certain amount of subscribers and views, you can start making money through ads and sponsorships.

Do Chores for Family and Neighbors

Another way to make money during quarantine is to do chores for family and neighbors. This can include things like mowing lawns, raking leaves, or even doing laundry. Make sure to negotiate a fair price for the work you'll be doing and make sure you're capable of completing the task before agreeing to do it.

Offer Online Tutoring Services

If you're a straight-A student, offering online tutoring services can be a great way to make money. You can offer your services to classmates or even advertise on social media. Make sure you're comfortable with the subject you'll be tutoring in and make sure to set a fair price for your services.

Start a Blog

If you love writing, starting a blog can be a great way to make money. You can write about things you're passionate about, like cooking, fashion, or even just your daily life. Once you have a certain amount of traffic to your blog, you can start making money through ads and sponsorships.

Sell Items Online

If you have items you no longer need or use, selling them online can be a great way to make some extra cash. You can sell things like clothes, toys, or even electronics on websites like eBay or Craigslist. Make sure to clean and price your items fairly before listing them for sale.

Offer Lawn Care Services

If you're comfortable with outdoor work, offering lawn care services can be a great way to make money. This can include things like mowing lawns, trimming hedges, or even planting flowers. Make sure you have the necessary tools and equipment before offering these services and make sure to price them fairly.

Complete Online Surveys

Completing online surveys can be a great way to make money from home. There are several websites that pay users to complete surveys or watch videos. Make sure to research the website before signing up and make sure it's a legitimate way to make money.

Offer Babysitting Services

If you're comfortable with children, offering babysitting services can be a great way to make money. Make sure to get permission from your parents before offering these services and make sure you're comfortable with the age range of the children you'll be watching. Set a fair price for your services and make sure the parents are comfortable with your experience level.

Conclusion

There are several ways to make money as a 10 year old during quarantine. From selling homemade crafts to offering online tutoring services, there's something for everyone. Make sure to do your research and negotiate fair prices for your services. With a little hard work and determination, you can earn some extra cash from the comfort of your own home.
