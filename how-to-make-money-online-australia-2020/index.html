How To Make Money Online In Australia In 2020

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

With the rise of technology and the internet, making money online has become a popular way to earn extra income. In Australia, there are various ways to make money online, whether it's through freelancing, selling products, or completing surveys. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make money online in Australia in 2020.

Freelancing

Freelancing is a popular way to make money online, especially if you have skills in graphic design, writing, programming, or marketing. Websites such as Upwork, Freelancer, and Fiverr are great platforms to find freelance work. To get started, create a profile, highlight your skills and experience, and apply for relevant jobs. Be sure to set reasonable rates and deliver high-quality work to build a positive reputation.

Selling Products

If you have a talent for creating products, such as crafts, jewelry, or artwork, selling them online can be a profitable venture. Websites such as Etsy and Redbubble are popular marketplaces for handmade and unique products. Consider creating a brand or niche for your products to stand out, and be sure to price them competitively.

Completing Surveys

Completing surveys is a simple way to make money online in Australia. Websites such as Swagbucks, Toluna, and Survey Junkie offer paid surveys and focus groups. While the pay may not be substantial, it's an easy way to earn extra income in your spare time. Be sure to check the legitimacy of the website before providing any personal information.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing involves promoting other people's products and earning a commission for each sale. You can promote products through your website, social media, or email marketing. Websites such as Amazon Associates and ClickBank offer affiliate programs for a wide range of products. Be sure to disclose your affiliate relationships and provide honest reviews to build trust with your audience.

Blogging

Blogging can be a profitable way to make money online in Australia. You can monetize your blog through advertising, sponsored content, and affiliate marketing. Choose a niche that you're passionate about and create high-quality content to attract readers. Be sure to promote your blog through social media and engage with your audience to build a loyal following.

Virtual Assistant

If you have administrative skills, becoming a virtual assistant can be a lucrative way to make money online. Websites such as Zirtual and Fancy Hands offer virtual assistant services to businesses and individuals. Tasks may include managing emails, scheduling appointments, and social media management. Be sure to highlight your skills and experience in your application.

Online Tutoring

If you have expertise in a particular subject, online tutoring can be a great way to make money online in Australia. Websites such as Chegg and Tutor.com offer online tutoring services for a range of subjects. Be sure to have a reliable internet connection and a quiet workspace to provide quality tutoring sessions.

Dropshipping

Dropshipping involves selling products without holding inventory. You can create an online store through platforms such as Shopify and Oberlo and source products from suppliers. The supplier ships the product directly to the customer, and you earn a profit from the difference in price. Be sure to research suppliers and choose products with high demand to maximize profits.

Stock Photography

If you have a talent for photography, selling stock photos online can be a passive way to earn money. Websites such as Shutterstock and iStock offer stock photo marketplaces for photographers. Be sure to take high-quality photos and choose popular themes to increase sales.

Conclusion

Making money online in Australia in 2020 is possible with various options available. Whether it's freelancing, selling products, or completing surveys, there's a way to earn extra income online. Be sure to choose a method that suits your skills and interests and deliver high-quality work to build a positive reputation.
