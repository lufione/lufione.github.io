How Much Do Filipino Youtubers Earn

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

With the rise of social media and the internet, it's not surprising that many people are turning to YouTube as a means of earning an income. In the Philippines, YouTube has become a popular platform for content creators to showcase their talent and creativity. But just how much do Filipino YouTubers earn?

Factors Affecting Earnings

The amount of money a YouTuber earns depends on various factors, such as the number of views, the number of subscribers, and the type of content they produce. Advertisers pay YouTube for ad space, and YouTube, in turn, pays a portion of that revenue to the content creator. The more views and subscribers a YouTuber has, the more money they can earn.

Average Earnings of Filipino YouTubers

There is no fixed amount that Filipino YouTubers earn, as it varies depending on the factors mentioned earlier. However, according to a survey conducted by Social Blade, the average earnings of a Filipino YouTuber with 1 million subscribers range from $1,000 to $5,000 per month. This is just an estimate, and some YouTubers may earn more or less depending on their content and audience.

Top Earning Filipino YouTubers

Some Filipino YouTubers have become so popular that they are now considered as celebrities. Here are some of the top-earning Filipino YouTubers:

1. Ranz Kyle

With over 14 million subscribers, Ranz Kyle is considered one of the most popular Filipino YouTubers. He earns an estimated $50,000 to $75,000 per month from YouTube alone.

2. Niana Guerrero

Niana Guerrero, who is also Ranz Kyle's sister, has over 15 million subscribers. She earns an estimated $30,000 to $45,000 per month from YouTube.

3. Michelle Dy

Michelle Dy is a beauty and lifestyle YouTuber with over 8 million subscribers. She earns an estimated $10,000 to $15,000 per month from YouTube.

4. Cong TV

Cong TV is a comedy YouTuber with over 10 million subscribers. He earns an estimated $15,000 to $20,000 per month from YouTube.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Filipino YouTubers can earn a substantial amount of money depending on their content and audience. While it may seem like an easy way to earn money, creating quality content and building a loyal audience takes a lot of hard work and dedication. So, if you're thinking of starting a YouTube channel, make sure you're passionate about it and willing to put in the effort to make it successful.
