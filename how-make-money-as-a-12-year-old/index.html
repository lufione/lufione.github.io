How To Make Money As A 12 Year Old

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Introduction

Making money at a young age is a great way to learn important skills and gain financial independence. As a 12 year old, there are many ways to earn money and have fun at the same time. In this article, we'll explore some of the best strategies for making money as a pre-teen.

1. Babysitting

Babysitting is a classic way for young people to earn money. As a 12 year old, you can start by offering your services to friends and family. Once you have some experience, you can create flyers or use apps like Care.com to find new clients. Make sure to get proper training and certification before taking on any jobs.

2. Dog Walking

If you love dogs and enjoy spending time outside, dog walking can be a great way to make money. You can start by offering your services to neighbors or using apps like Rover to find clients. Make sure to be responsible and reliable, and always clean up after the dogs.

3. Yard Work

Yard work is another classic way to earn money. As a 12 year old, you can offer services like mowing lawns, raking leaves, and pulling weeds. Make sure to have proper equipment and safety gear, and always ask for permission from the homeowners before starting any work.

4. Lemonade Stand

A lemonade stand is a fun way to make money and meet new people. You can set up your stand in a busy area like a park or a street corner. Make sure to have a good recipe, a catchy sign, and reasonable prices. You can also sell other items like cookies or snacks.

5. Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay people to take online surveys. As a 12 year old, you can sign up for sites like Swagbucks or Survey Junkie to earn extra cash. Make sure to read the terms and conditions carefully, and never give out personal information.

6. Sell Crafts

If you enjoy making crafts like jewelry or artwork, you can sell them online or at local markets. Websites like Etsy are great for showcasing your work and reaching a wider audience. Make sure to price your items fairly and promote them on social media.

7. House Sitting

House sitting is a great way to earn money and gain responsibility. As a 12 year old, you can offer your services to neighbors or use apps like TrustedHousesitters to find clients. Make sure to be respectful of the homeowners' property and follow any instructions they give you.

8. Tutoring

If you excel in a certain subject like math or science, you can offer your tutoring services to classmates or younger students. Make sure to have a clear plan and set reasonable rates. You can also use websites like Tutor.com to find clients.

9. Garage Sale

A garage sale is a great way to declutter your home and make some extra money. As a 12 year old, you can involve your family and friends and sell items like toys, clothes, and books. Make sure to advertise your sale on social media and put up signs in your neighborhood.

10. Recycling

Recycling is not only good for the environment, but it can also be a source of income. As a 12 year old, you can collect cans and bottles and take them to a recycling center for cash. Make sure to wear gloves and be careful when handling sharp objects.

Conclusion

Making money as a 12 year old is a great way to learn important skills and gain financial independence. From babysitting to recycling, there are many ways to earn money and have fun at the same time. Make sure to be responsible, reliable, and always ask for permission from your parents or guardians.
