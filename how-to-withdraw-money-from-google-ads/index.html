How To Withdraw Money From Google Ads In 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Google Ads is undoubtedly one of the most popular advertising platforms used by businesses across the globe. As a business owner, you may have been using Google Ads to promote your products or services and generate revenue. However, at some point, you may want to withdraw the money from your Google Ads account.

Understanding Google Ads Payment Cycle

Before we dive into the steps of withdrawing money from Google Ads, it's essential to understand the payment cycle. Google Ads has a monthly billing cycle, and you'll receive an invoice at the end of each billing cycle. The billing cycle starts on the first of every month and ends on the last day of the month.

How to Withdraw Money from Google Ads

Step 1: Sign in to Your Google Ads Account

The first step in withdrawing money from Google Ads is to sign in to your account. Click on the "Tools & Settings" icon in the top right corner of your dashboard and select "Billing & Payments" from the dropdown menu.

Step 2: Click on "Transaction History"

Next, click on "Transaction History" to view your billing transactions. You'll see a list of all your transactions, including payments and refunds.

Step 3: Select the Transaction You Want to Withdraw

Identify the transaction you want to withdraw and click on the "Withdraw" button next to it. You'll be prompted to enter your bank account details, including your account number and routing number.

Step 4: Confirm Your Withdrawal Amount

Confirm the withdrawal amount and click on "Submit." Google Ads will process your withdrawal request, and the money will be transferred to your bank account within a few business days.

Tips and Tricks for Withdrawing Money from Google Ads

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when withdrawing money from Google Ads:

  • Make sure you have sufficient funds in your account to cover the withdrawal amount.
  • Double-check your bank account details to avoid any errors.
  • Withdrawals can only be made to a bank account with the same name as the Google Ads account holder.
  • Withdrawals are subject to a $1 processing fee.

FAQs

Can I withdraw money from Google Ads at any time?

Yes, you can withdraw money from Google Ads at any time, as long as you have sufficient funds in your account.

How long does it take for the money to be transferred to my bank account?

The money will be transferred to your bank account within a few business days.

Is there a fee for withdrawing money from Google Ads?

Yes, there is a $1 processing fee for every withdrawal.

Can I withdraw money to a bank account with a different name?

No, withdrawals can only be made to a bank account with the same name as the Google Ads account holder.

What happens if my withdrawal request is declined?

If your withdrawal request is declined, Google Ads will notify you of the reason why. You can then take the necessary steps to resolve the issue and resubmit your withdrawal request.

Conclusion

Withdrawing money from Google Ads is a simple process that can be completed in just a few steps. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can withdraw your funds and transfer them to your bank account with ease.
