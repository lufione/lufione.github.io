Earn Money By Watching Youtube Videos A Guide For Filipinos In 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 · 6 min read

With the rise of online content consumption, it's no secret that YouTube has become a go-to platform for entertainment, education, and even income. In this article, we'll explore how you can earn money by watching YouTube videos in the Philippines in 2023.

How Does It Work?

There are several ways you can earn money by watching YouTube videos. The most common method is through ads. YouTube pays content creators based on the number of views and clicks their ads receive. As a viewer, you can contribute to this revenue by watching the ads in their entirety.

Another way to earn money is through sponsored content. Influencers and brands often collaborate to create sponsored videos, where the influencer promotes the product or service in exchange for payment. By watching these videos and engaging with them, you're supporting the influencer and helping them earn money.

How Much Can You Earn?

The amount of money you can earn by watching YouTube videos varies depending on several factors. The most significant factor is your location. In the Philippines, the average CPM (cost per thousand views) for YouTube ads is around $0.50. This means that for every 1,000 views, the content creator earns around $0.50.

However, keep in mind that not all content creators receive the same CPM. Factors such as the type of content, audience demographics, and ad placement can affect how much money they earn. Additionally, sponsored content payments vary depending on the influencer's reach and the brand's budget.

What Are Some Tips to Maximize Earnings?

If you're serious about earning money by watching YouTube videos, here are some tips to help you maximize your earnings:

1. Find High-Paying Videos

Some content creators earn more money per view than others. To maximize your earnings, look for videos with a high CPM. These are usually videos that target a specific audience and have engaged viewers.

2. Engage with the Videos

Engaging with the videos you watch can help content creators earn more money. Leave a like, comment, or share the video to help it reach a wider audience. Additionally, engaging with sponsored content can help influencers secure more sponsorships in the future.

3. Watch Longer Videos

Longer videos tend to have more ads, which means more revenue for the content creator. If you're looking to maximize your earnings, consider watching longer videos instead of short ones.

FAQs

Can Anyone Earn Money by Watching YouTube Videos?

Yes, anyone can earn money by watching YouTube videos. However, the amount you can earn varies depending on your location, the type of content you watch, and how engaged you are with the content.

Do I Need to Create Content to Earn Money?

No, you don't need to create content to earn money by watching YouTube videos. You can earn money by simply watching the ads or engaging with sponsored content.

Is This a Sustainable Source of Income?

Earning money by watching YouTube videos is not a sustainable source of income. It's best to view it as a side hustle or a way to earn some extra cash.

Conclusion

Earning money by watching YouTube videos is a viable option for Filipinos in 2023. By following the tips outlined in this article and engaging with the content you watch, you can help content creators earn more money and earn some extra cash for yourself.
