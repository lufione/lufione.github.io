How To Make A Tiktok With Both Videos And Pictures In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create and share short videos. While it was originally designed for videos, TikTok now allows users to create TikToks with both videos and pictures. In this article, we’ll show you how to make a TikTok with both videos and pictures in the year 2023.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Open the TikTok app

To create a TikTok with both videos and pictures, you must first open the TikTok app on your mobile device. Once you’ve opened the app, tap on the plus sign at the bottom of the screen to start creating a new TikTok.

Step 2: Choose your media

After tapping on the plus sign, you’ll be taken to the TikTok camera screen. From here, you can choose to either record a video or add a photo from your camera roll by tapping on the “Upload” button.

Step 3: Add effects and filters

Once you’ve chosen your media, you can add effects and filters to your TikTok. TikTok offers a wide range of effects and filters that you can use to enhance your TikTok and make it more engaging.

Step 4: Add music

TikTok is known for its music, and adding music to your TikTok is an essential step. You can either choose a song from the TikTok music library or use a song from your own device.

Step 5: Edit your TikTok

After adding your media, effects, filters, and music, you can edit your TikTok by adjusting the length of your video, adding text, and adding stickers.

Step 6: Post your TikTok

Once you’ve finished editing your TikTok, you can post it to your TikTok profile by tapping on the “Post” button. Your TikTok will be visible to all of your followers and may even go viral if it’s engaging enough.

Tips and Tricks

- Use a mix of videos and pictures to make your TikTok more engaging - Choose music that fits the mood of your TikTok - Use effects and filters to make your TikTok stand out - Keep your TikTok short and sweet, ideally under 60 seconds - Use hashtags to increase the visibility of your TikTok

FAQs

Can I use copyrighted music in my TikTok?

No, you cannot use copyrighted music in your TikTok without permission from the copyright owner. TikTok offers a wide range of licensed music that you can use without worrying about copyright issues.

How long can my TikTok be?

TikToks can be up to 60 seconds long, but many users prefer to keep their TikToks shorter to keep their audience engaged.

Can I edit my TikTok after posting it?

Yes, you can edit your TikTok after posting it by tapping on the “Edit” button on your profile page.

Conclusion

Creating a TikTok with both videos and pictures is a great way to make your TikTok more engaging and increase its visibility. By following these simple steps, you can create a TikTok that is sure to impress your followers and maybe even go viral. So, grab your camera and start creating!
