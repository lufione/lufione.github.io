Can You Still Receive Money On Gcash Without Verification

Written by Robby Apr 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

GCash is a popular mobile wallet in the Philippines that allows users to transfer money, pay bills, buy load, and more. However, some people may wonder if it's still possible to receive money on GCash without verification. The short answer is yes, but there are some limitations.

What is GCash Verification?

GCash verification is a process that confirms your identity and links your account to your mobile number. This is necessary if you want to unlock all the features of GCash, such as cashing in and cashing out, increasing your wallet limit, and using GCredit. To verify your account, you need to submit valid IDs and take a selfie.

Can You Receive Money Without Verification?

Yes, you can still receive money on GCash without verification. However, there are some restrictions. For example, you can only receive up to PHP 8,000 per month and your wallet limit is only PHP 50,000.

How to Receive Money on GCash Without Verification

To receive money on GCash without verification, you need to follow these steps:

  1. Open your GCash app and log in to your account.
  2. Tap "Cash-In" on the home screen.
  3. Select "Remittance" as the source of funds.
  4. Enter the amount you want to receive and the name of the sender.
  5. Choose the remittance center where the sender will send the money.
  6. Ask the sender to provide the reference number and the name of the remittance center.
  7. Wait for the money to arrive in your GCash wallet.

What are the Advantages of GCash Verification?

While you can still receive money on GCash without verification, it's recommended to verify your account to enjoy the full benefits of the app. Here are some advantages of GCash verification:

  • You can cash in and cash out at more than 30,000 GCash partner outlets nationwide.
  • You can increase your wallet limit up to PHP 500,000.
  • You can use GCredit, which is a virtual credit line that you can use for online and offline purchases.
  • You can enjoy exclusive perks and discounts from GCash partners.

What are the Requirements for GCash Verification?

To verify your GCash account, you need to prepare the following requirements:

  • One valid ID such as passport, driver's license, UMID, SSS, PRC, or Voter's ID.
  • A clear selfie.

Conclusion

In summary, you can still receive money on GCash without verification, but there are some limitations. If you want to enjoy the full benefits of GCash, it's recommended to verify your account. The process is simple and only requires a valid ID and a selfie. With GCash, you can enjoy cashless transactions and convenient financial services anytime, anywhere.
