How Can A 9 Year Old Make Money Fast In Quarantine

Written by Bardi Oct 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

2023 has been a challenging year for most people, especially for kids who have been stuck at home due to the quarantine. However, this situation can also be an opportunity for children to learn new skills and make some money. Here are some ideas on how a 9 year old can make money fast in quarantine:

1. Sell Crafts Online

If your child loves to make crafts, they can sell them online. They can create their own online store on platforms like Etsy or eBay and sell their handmade items. They can also use social media platforms like Instagram or Facebook to showcase their products and attract potential customers.

2. Offer Pet-Sitting Services

If your child loves animals, they can offer pet-sitting services for neighbors or family friends who are away or busy during the day. They can take care of pets, feed them, and take them for walks. This is a great way to earn some extra cash while doing something they love.

3. Do Chores for Family Members

Household chores can be an easy way for kids to earn money. They can offer to do chores like washing dishes, folding laundry, or vacuuming for family members or neighbors. This teaches responsibility and helps them earn some extra cash.

4. Sell Items Online

If your child has outgrown their clothes or toys, they can sell them online on platforms like eBay or Facebook Marketplace. This is a great way to declutter their space and earn some money in the process.

5. Offer Tutoring Services

If your child excels in a particular subject, they can offer tutoring services to younger children. They can help with homework or offer lessons in their area of expertise. This is a great way to earn some extra cash while sharing their knowledge.

6. Participate in Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay people to participate in online surveys. Children can earn money by signing up for these websites and sharing their opinions on various topics.

7. Sell Baked Goods

If your child loves to bake, they can sell their baked goods online or to neighbors. They can create their own online store or advertise their products on social media platforms.

8. Offer Lawn Care Services

If your child is physically active, they can offer lawn care services to neighbors. They can mow lawns, rake leaves, and do other yard work for a fee.

9. Create and Sell Digital Art

If your child is artistic, they can create digital art and sell it online. They can use platforms like Etsy or Redbubble to sell their designs on t-shirts, phone cases, and other items.

10. Offer Babysitting Services

If your child is responsible and loves children, they can offer babysitting services to neighbors or family friends. They can take care of children while parents are away or busy.

Conclusion

There are many ways for a 9 year old to make money fast in quarantine. From selling crafts online to offering pet-sitting services, there are many opportunities for children to learn new skills and earn some extra cash. It is important to remember that any money earned should be managed responsibly and with the guidance of a parent or guardian.
