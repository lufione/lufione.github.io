How To Earn 200 As A Kid In 2023

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a kid looking to earn some extra cash? Whether you want to save up for a new toy or just have some spending money, there are plenty of ways for kids to make money in 2023. Here are some tips and tricks for earning $200 as a kid.

Sell Your Stuff

One of the easiest ways to make money as a kid is to sell things you no longer need or want. Go through your toys, clothes, and other belongings and see what you can sell. You can have a garage sale, sell items online, or even sell items to friends and family.

Start a Business

If you're feeling entrepreneurial, consider starting your own business. You could start a dog-walking or pet-sitting service, mow lawns, or offer tutoring services. Think about what you're good at and what people might be willing to pay for.

Do Chores for Others

Offer to do chores for your neighbors or family members. You could offer to wash cars, clean houses, or do yard work. Make sure to set a fair price for your services.

Participate in Paid Surveys

There are plenty of websites that offer paid surveys for kids. You can earn money by giving your opinion on products and services. Just make sure to do your research and only sign up for legitimate survey sites.

Babysit

If you're responsible and good with kids, consider offering babysitting services. You could offer to babysit for your neighbors or family members. Make sure to get certified in CPR and first aid, and set a fair rate for your services.

Sell Crafts or Artwork

If you're creative, consider selling your crafts or artwork. You could sell items online or at local craft fairs. Make sure to set a fair price for your items and market them effectively.

Offer Tech Support

If you're good with technology, consider offering tech support to others. You could help people set up their computers, fix software issues, or even teach them how to use new software.

Work as a Pet Sitter

If you love animals, consider offering pet-sitting services. You could offer to walk dogs, play with cats, or even feed and care for smaller pets like hamsters or guinea pigs.

Do Odd Jobs

Offer to do odd jobs for your neighbors or family members. You could help with moving furniture, running errands, or even washing cars. Make sure to set a fair price for your services.

Conclusion

Earning $200 as a kid in 2023 is definitely possible. Whether you choose to sell your stuff, start a business, or offer services to others, there are plenty of ways to make money. Just be sure to be responsible, set fair prices, and have fun!
