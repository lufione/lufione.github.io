What Is The Easiest Way To Make Money In Red Dead Online

Written by Bardi Sep 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Red Dead Online is a massive multiplayer game where players can take on various roles and tasks to earn money. While there are no shortcuts to earn money in the game, there are some tips and tricks that can help you make money faster and easier. Here are some of the easiest ways to make money in Red Dead Online:

1. Hunting and Fishing

Hunting and fishing are two of the easiest and most lucrative ways to make money in Red Dead Online. You can sell your catch and skins to butchers and traders, and earn a good amount of money. You can find a variety of animals and fish across the map, and some can be sold for more than others. It is also a good idea to invest in a fishing rod and hunting bow to increase your chances of catching rare animals and fish.

2. Completing Stranger Missions

Stranger missions are tasks that can be completed for non-playable characters (NPCs) in the game. These missions are usually short and can be completed quickly, and they offer a good amount of money as a reward. You can find these missions on the map, and they are marked with a question mark icon. Completing these missions will also increase your experience and reputation in the game.

3. Collecting Items

Collecting items is another easy and fun way to make money in Red Dead Online. You can collect various items such as herbs, flowers, and antique bottles, and sell them to collectors for a good price. You can find these items scattered around the map, and they are marked with a magnifying glass icon. It is a good idea to invest in a metal detector and shovel to increase your chances of finding rare items.

4. Participating in Events and Races

Events and races are special activities that take place in the game, and they offer a good amount of money and rewards. You can participate in various events such as shootouts, horse races, and treasure hunts, and earn money based on your performance. You can find these events on the map, and they are marked with a star icon.

5. Selling Stolen Items

While stealing is not encouraged in the game, you can earn a good amount of money by selling stolen items to fences. You can steal items such as jewelry, watches, and other valuables from NPCs and sell them to fences for a good price. However, be careful not to get caught by the law, as they will arrest you and take away your stolen items.

6. Investing in a Business

Investing in a business is a long-term strategy to make money in Red Dead Online. You can purchase a business such as a moonshine shack or a bounty hunter license, and earn money by completing tasks and missions related to the business. These businesses require an initial investment, but they offer a good return on investment in the long run.

7. Joining a Posse

Joining a posse is a great way to earn money and experience in Red Dead Online. You can join a group of players and complete tasks and missions together, and share the rewards. This will not only help you earn money faster, but it will also make the game more fun and social.

8. Selling Horses

If you have a good horse, you can sell it for a good price in Red Dead Online. You can find horses across the map, and some of them are rare and valuable. It is a good idea to invest in a lasso and horse brush to increase your chances of finding and taming rare horses.

9. Completing Daily Challenges

Daily challenges are tasks that can be completed every day, and they offer a good amount of money and rewards. You can find these challenges in the game menu, and they are marked with a star icon. Completing these challenges every day will help you earn money consistently and increase your experience and reputation.

10. Selling Treasure Maps

Treasure maps are special items that can be found across the map, and they offer a good amount of money and rewards. You can find these maps by looting NPCs or as a reward for completing missions and challenges. You can sell these maps to collectors for a good price, and earn a good return on investment.

Conclusion

These are some of the easiest ways to make money in Red Dead Online. While there are no shortcuts to earn money in the game, these tips and tricks will help you make money faster and easier. Remember to have fun while playing the game, and enjoy the various activities and roles that the game offers.
