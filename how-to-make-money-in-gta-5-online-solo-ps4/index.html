How To Make Money In Gta 5 Online Solo Ps4

Written by Oliver May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

GTA 5 Online has been one of the most popular online games for years, and many players are always looking for ways to make more money in the game. While there are many ways to make money in GTA 5 Online, it can be challenging to find ways to do it solo. In this article, we will be discussing how to make money in GTA 5 Online solo on PS4.

1. Start with Contact Missions

Contact missions are a great way to start making money in GTA 5 Online solo. They are relatively easy to complete, and you can earn up to $25,000 per mission. To start a contact mission, go to your phone and select “Job List” and then “Contact Missions.”

Tip:

If you complete contact missions with a friend, you can earn more money. The more players in the mission, the higher the payout.

2. Invest in a CEO Office

Investing in a CEO office is one of the best ways to make money in GTA 5 Online solo. Once you have an office, you can start doing CEO missions, which can earn you up to $150,000 per mission. To buy an office, go to the Dynasty 8 Executive website on your phone and select “Office.”

Tip:

Investing in a vehicle warehouse is also a great way to make money as a CEO. You can steal and sell high-end vehicles for a profit.

3. Participate in Heists

Heists are some of the most challenging but rewarding missions in GTA 5 Online. While it can be difficult to complete heists solo, it is still possible. You can earn up to $1 million per heist, and you can replay them to earn more money.

Tip:

If you have a friend to play with, heists can be much easier to complete.

4. Sell Stolen Cars

Another way to make money in GTA 5 Online solo is to sell stolen cars. You can steal cars and sell them to the Los Santos Customs store for a profit. To steal cars, look for high-end vehicles driving around the city.

Tip:

Selling cars to the Los Santos Customs store is not the most profitable way to make money, but it can be a good way to earn some extra cash.

5. Participate in Double Money and RP Events

Occasionally, Rockstar Games will hold events where players can earn double money and RP for completing missions. Keep an eye out for these events, as they can be a great way to make more money in GTA 5 Online.

Tip:

To stay updated on events, follow Rockstar Games on social media or check the GTA 5 Online news section on your phone.

Conclusion

Making money in GTA 5 Online solo on PS4 can be challenging, but with the right strategies, it is possible. Contact missions, investing in a CEO office, participating in heists, selling stolen cars, and participating in double money and RP events are all great ways to make more money in the game. Keep in mind that some of these strategies may take time to pay off, but with persistence, you can become a wealthy player in GTA 5 Online.
