How To Make Money Fast For 12 Year Olds In The Uk

Written by Oliver Oct 04, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

Are you a 12-year-old living in the UK and looking to make some extra cash? Whether you want to save up for a new gadget or just want to have money to spend on your hobbies, there are plenty of ways to make money fast. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best ways for 12-year-olds to earn money in the UK.

Sell Your Old Stuff

Do you have old toys, clothes, or electronics lying around your house? Consider selling them online on platforms like eBay or Depop. Take clear photos of your items and write a detailed description to attract potential buyers. This is a great way to earn some quick cash and declutter your space at the same time.

Offer Your Services

Are you good at baking, babysitting, or dog walking? Offer your services to friends, family, and neighbors. Let them know that you’re available to help and charge a reasonable fee for your time. This is a great way to build up your experience and reputation in your community.

Participate in Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay people to participate in online surveys. While the pay may not be very high, it’s an easy way to earn some extra cash in your free time. Some popular survey websites in the UK include Toluna, Swagbucks, and YouGov.

Start a YouTube Channel

Do you have a talent or hobby that you’re passionate about? Consider starting a YouTube channel to share your skills with the world. With dedication and hard work, you can earn money through ad revenue and sponsorships. Just make sure to follow YouTube’s guidelines and keep your content appropriate for all ages.

Do Odd Jobs

Ask your parents or neighbors if they need help with odd jobs around the house, such as cleaning, gardening, or organizing. Offer your time and skills for a reasonable fee. This is a great way to earn some extra money and build up your work experience.

Host a Garage Sale

If you have a lot of items to sell, consider hosting a garage sale. This is a great way to attract local buyers and get rid of unwanted items. Make sure to advertise your sale in advance and price your items fairly.

Become a Freelance Writer

If you enjoy writing, consider becoming a freelance writer. Many websites and blogs are looking for writers to create content for them. You can start by creating a portfolio of your work and reaching out to potential clients.

Deliver Newspapers

Many local newspapers are looking for young people to deliver their papers. This is a great way to earn some extra money and get some exercise at the same time. Contact your local newspaper to see if they have any openings.

Conclusion

Making money as a 12-year-old in the UK is possible with some creativity and hard work. Consider trying out some of these ideas to earn some extra cash and gain valuable experience. Remember to always get permission from your parents and prioritize your safety. Good luck!
