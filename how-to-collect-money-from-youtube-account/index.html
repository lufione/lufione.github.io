How To Collect Money From Your Youtube Account

Written by Frank Jun 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

YouTube is one of the most popular video-sharing platforms in the world. With over 2 billion monthly active users, the platform has become a great way for content creators to earn money. If you're one of those creators and you want to know how to collect money from your YouTube account, then you're in the right place.

Step 1: Meet YouTube's Partner Program Requirements

To be eligible for monetization on YouTube, you must meet the platform's Partner Program requirements. These include:

  • Having at least 1,000 subscribers
  • Having at least 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months
  • Complying with YouTube's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
  • Having an AdSense account

Step 2: Enable Monetization on Your Videos

Once you meet the Partner Program requirements, you can enable monetization on your videos. To do this:

  1. Sign in to your YouTube account
  2. Click on your profile picture and select "YouTube Studio"
  3. Click on "Monetization" in the left-hand menu
  4. Click on "Start" under "Monetization"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your AdSense account (if you haven't already)

Step 3: Choose Your Monetization Settings

After you've enabled monetization on your videos, you can choose your monetization settings. These include:

  • Ad formats: You can choose which ad formats to show on your videos, such as display ads, overlay ads, and skippable video ads.
  • Ad placement: You can choose where to place ads on your videos, such as before, during, or after the video.
  • Ad targeting: You can choose which audience to target with your ads, such as people based on their location or interests.

Step 4: Monitor Your Earnings

Once you've enabled monetization on your videos and chosen your monetization settings, you can monitor your earnings. To do this:

  1. Sign in to your YouTube account
  2. Click on your profile picture and select "YouTube Studio"
  3. Click on "Analytics" in the left-hand menu
  4. Click on "Revenue" in the top menu

Step 5: Get Paid

YouTube pays its content creators through AdSense. To get paid:

  1. Sign in to your AdSense account
  2. Check your payment threshold, which is the minimum amount you must earn before you can receive payment
  3. Choose your payment method, such as direct deposit or wire transfer
  4. Wait for your payment to be processed (payments are usually made on the 21st of each month)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much money can you make from YouTube?

The amount of money you can make from YouTube depends on various factors, such as the number of views, the ad format, and the audience targeting. However, on average, YouTube pays content creators $0.01 to $0.03 per view.

When can you monetize your YouTube channel?

You can monetize your YouTube channel once you meet the Partner Program requirements, which include having at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months.

How often do you get paid from YouTube?

You get paid from YouTube once a month, usually on the 21st of each month. However, you must reach the payment threshold (which is $100) before you can receive payment.

Can you monetize copyrighted content on YouTube?

No, you cannot monetize copyrighted content on YouTube without permission from the copyright owner. Doing so can result in your account being terminated or legal action being taken against you.

Tips and Tricks

  • Create high-quality, engaging content that people want to watch
  • Promote your videos on social media and other platforms
  • Engage with your audience by responding to comments and messages
  • Experiment with different ad formats and placements to see what works best for your content
  • Stay up-to-date with YouTube's policies and guidelines to avoid any issues
