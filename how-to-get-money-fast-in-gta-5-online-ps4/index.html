Selling Cars

One of the quickest ways to earn money in GTA 5 Online PS4 is by selling cars. You can steal cars on the street and take them to the Los Santos Customs to sell them. The value of the car depends on its condition, model, and rarity. You can earn up to $10,000 for a high-end car.

Robbing Stores

Another way to earn money fast is by robbing stores. You can find convenience stores, gas stations, and liquor stores on the map. Enter the store with a weapon and aim at the cashier to make them open the cash register. You can earn up to $2,000 by robbing a store.

Completing Heists

Heists are a great way to earn a lot of money in a short period. You will need to complete some setup missions before the actual heist. The payout depends on the difficulty and the number of players involved. You can earn up to $1 million by completing a heist.

Participating in Races

You can participate in races to earn money in GTA 5 Online PS4. The payout depends on your position and the number of players involved. You can earn up to $50,000 by winning a race.

Investing in the Stock Market

You can invest in the stock market in GTA 5 Online PS4 to earn money. The stock market is affected by the events in the game. You can buy stocks when they are low and sell them when they are high. You can earn up to $2 million by investing in the stock market.

Using Money Glitches

Selling Cargo

You can buy and sell cargo to earn money in GTA 5 Online PS4. You can buy cargo from the SecuroServ app on your phone and deliver it to the destination. The payout depends on the type and amount of cargo you deliver. You can earn up to $2 million by selling cargo.

Participating in Contact Missions

Contact missions are missions given by the NPCs in the game. You can participate in contact missions to earn money. The payout depends on the difficulty and the number of players involved. You can earn up to $50,000 by completing a contact mission.

Using Shark Cards

If you want to earn money quickly without doing any missions, you can buy Shark Cards from the game store. Shark Cards are virtual currency that you can use to buy in-game items. You can buy Shark Cards with real money.

Conclusion
