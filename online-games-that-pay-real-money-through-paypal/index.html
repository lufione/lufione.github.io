Online Games That Pay Real Money Through Paypal

Are you a gaming enthusiast who wants to earn some extra cash? If yes, then you're in luck! There are various online games that pay real money through PayPal. In this article, we'll list down some of the best games that you can play to earn money.

How Does It Work?

Before we dive into the list of games, let's understand how this works. These games allow you to earn points or virtual currency by playing them. Once you accumulate a certain number of points or virtual currency, you can convert them into real money and withdraw it through PayPal.

Top Online Games That Pay Real Money Through Paypal

1. Swagbucks

Swagbucks is a popular website that offers various ways to earn money, including playing games. The website has a wide range of games that you can play to earn Swagbucks points. You can redeem these points for real money through PayPal or gift cards.

2. InboxDollars

InboxDollars is another website that pays you to play games. You can earn cash by playing games such as solitaire, spider solitaire, and mahjong. You can also earn money by completing surveys, watching videos, and reading emails. The website pays through PayPal or check.

3. Lucktastic

Lucktastic is a mobile app that offers scratch-off cards and other games to earn money. You can earn tokens by playing games, which can be redeemed for real money through PayPal or gift cards. The app is available for both Android and iOS devices.

4. Skillz

Skillz is a platform that offers various skill-based games, such as solitaire, bubble shooter, and 21 Blitz. You can compete with other players to win real money. The platform charges a small fee, but you can earn much more than that if you win. The money can be withdrawn through PayPal.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to increase your chances of earning money through these games:

  • Choose games that you're good at.
  • Read the rules and regulations of the game before playing.
  • Play during off-peak hours to increase your chances of winning.
  • Don't spend too much money on playing the games.
  • Set a budget and stick to it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about online games that pay real money through PayPal:

  • Is it legal to earn money through these games?
  • Yes, it's legal to earn money through these games as long as the platform is legitimate.
  • How much money can I earn?
  • It depends on the game and your skills. Some games offer a few cents per game, while others offer much more.
  • What are the payment options?
  • Most platforms offer payment through PayPal, but some may also offer gift cards, checks, or other payment methods.
