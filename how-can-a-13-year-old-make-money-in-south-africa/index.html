How Can A 13 Year Old Make Money In South Africa

Written by Oliver Sep 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As a 13 year old, it can be challenging to make money in South Africa. However, with the right mindset and approach, it is possible to earn some extra cash. In this article, we will explore various ways in which a 13 year old can make money in South Africa.

Sell Crafts and Products Online

If you are creative and enjoy making crafts, you can sell your products online. There are various platforms available, such as Etsy, where you can showcase your work and sell your products. You can also use social media to promote your products and reach a wider audience.

Offer Babysitting Services

If you enjoy spending time with children, babysitting can be a great way to earn money. You can offer your services to family and friends or advertise your services online. Make sure to get the necessary training and certifications before offering your services.

Do Yard Work

If you don't mind getting your hands dirty, doing yard work can be a great way to earn money. You can offer services such as mowing lawns, trimming hedges, and watering plants. Advertise your services in your neighbourhood or online.

Complete Online Surveys

There are various websites that pay you to complete online surveys. Although the pay is not significant, it can be a good way to earn some extra cash in your free time.

Offer Pet Sitting Services

If you love animals, pet sitting can be a great way to earn money. You can offer your services to friends and family or advertise your services online. Make sure to get the necessary training and certifications before offering your services.

Sell Second-Hand Items

If you have items that you no longer need, you can sell them online or at a garage sale. This can be a great way to declutter your space and earn some extra cash.

Offer Cleaning Services

If you enjoy cleaning, you can offer your services to friends and family or advertise your services online. Make sure to get the necessary training and certifications before offering your services.

Create a YouTube Channel

If you have a talent or skill that you want to share, you can create a YouTube channel. You can monetize your channel and earn money through advertising revenue.

Offer Tutoring Services

If you excel in a particular subject, you can offer tutoring services to your peers or younger students. Advertise your services in your neighbourhood or online.

Do Odd Jobs

You can offer your services for odd jobs such as running errands, assembling furniture, or painting. Advertise your services in your neighbourhood or online.

Conclusion

As a 13 year old, there are various ways in which you can make money in South Africa. Whether it's selling crafts online, offering pet sitting services, or doing odd jobs, there are many opportunities available. The key is to find something that you enjoy doing and that you are good at. With hard work and dedication, you can earn some extra cash and develop valuable skills.
