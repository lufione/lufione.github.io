How To Make Easy Money As A Minor In 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As a minor, it can be difficult to make money without a traditional job. However, there are several ways to earn money without having to leave your house or get a work permit. In this article, we will explore ten different ways you can make easy money as a minor in 2023.

Sell Items Online

One way to make money as a minor is to sell items online. You can sell gently used clothing, toys, electronics, or other items on websites like eBay, Poshmark, or Mercari. You can also create your own online store using websites like Etsy or Shopify.

Freelance Services

If you have a skill like writing, graphic design, or video editing, you can offer your services as a freelancer. Websites like Fiverr or Upwork allow you to create a profile and offer your services to clients around the world. As a minor, you may need to get your parent's permission and help with setting up your account.

Participate In Surveys

There are several websites that will pay you to participate in surveys. These surveys can range from product feedback to political opinions. Websites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, or Toluna are great places to start.

Babysitting

If you enjoy spending time with kids, babysitting can be a great way to make money. You can advertise your services to friends, family, or neighbors. You may need to get certified in CPR or first aid before you start babysitting.

Do Odd Jobs

You can make money by doing odd jobs for people in your community. This can include tasks like dog walking, lawn mowing, or snow shoveling. You can advertise your services using flyers or social media.

Start A Blog

If you enjoy writing, starting a blog can be a great way to make money. You can write about a topic you are passionate about and monetize your blog with ads or affiliate marketing. Websites like WordPress or Blogger are great places to start.

Virtual Tutoring

If you excel in a particular subject, you can offer your services as a virtual tutor. Websites like Chegg or TutorMe allow you to create a profile and offer your services to students around the world.

Online Gigs

You can make money by completing online gigs on websites like Amazon Mechanical Turk or Clickworker. These gigs can range from data entry to website testing.

Create Digital Products

If you have a talent for creating digital products like ebooks, printables, or designs, you can sell them online. Websites like Gumroad or Payhip allow you to create a storefront and sell your digital products.

Investing

If you have some extra money, you can start investing in stocks or cryptocurrency. You will need to do your research and understand the risks involved before you start investing.

Conclusion

As a minor, there are several ways to make easy money without having to get a traditional job. Whether you sell items online, freelance your services, or do odd jobs, there are plenty of opportunities to earn money from home. Just remember to get your parent's permission and help before you start any money-making ventures.
