How To Make Money Online In The Uk Under 18

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Introduction

Making money online as a teenager in the UK might seem like a challenging task, but it is entirely possible. With the rise of the internet, there are now more opportunities than ever before for young people to earn money online. In this article, we will explore some of the best ways to make money online in the UK if you are under 18.

1. Online Surveys

One of the easiest ways to make money online is by completing online surveys. Many companies are looking for feedback on their products and services, and they are willing to pay for it. You can sign up for survey websites such as Swagbucks, Toluna, and Ipsos i-Say, and start earning money by sharing your opinions.

2. Freelance Work

If you have a specific skill, such as writing, design, or programming, you can offer your services as a freelancer. Websites such as Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer allow you to create a profile and start bidding on jobs that match your skills. This is an excellent way to make money online while developing your skills and building your portfolio.

3. Online Tutoring

If you are good at a particular subject, you can offer online tutoring services. Websites such as Tutorful and MyTutor allow you to create a profile and start offering your services as a tutor. This is an excellent way to make money while helping others and improving your own knowledge.

4. Selling Products Online

If you have a talent for making crafts or creating products, you can sell them online. Websites such as Etsy and eBay allow you to create a store and start selling your products. This is an excellent way to make money while doing something you enjoy.

5. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing involves promoting products or services and earning a commission for each sale that you make. You can sign up for affiliate programs such as Amazon Associates or Clickbank and start promoting products on your website or social media channels.

6. Social Media Management

If you are good at social media, you can offer your services as a social media manager. Many businesses are looking for help managing their social media accounts, and they are willing to pay for it. You can offer your services on websites such as PeoplePerHour or Freelancer.

7. Online Writing

If you enjoy writing, you can earn money by creating content for websites or blogs. Many websites are looking for freelance writers, and they are willing to pay for high-quality content. You can find writing jobs on websites such as ProBlogger or Freelance Writing.

8. Online Gaming

If you enjoy playing games, you can make money by streaming your gameplay on platforms such as Twitch or YouTube. You can earn money through advertising, sponsorships, and donations from your viewers.

9. Online Store

If you have a passion for a particular niche, you can create an online store and start selling products related to that niche. You can use platforms such as Shopify or WooCommerce to create your store and start selling your products.

10. Online Investing

If you have some money to invest, you can use online platforms such as eToro or Trading 212 to invest in stocks, cryptocurrencies, or other assets. This is an excellent way to make money online if you are willing to take some risks and do your research.

Conclusion

Making money online in the UK as a teenager is entirely possible if you are willing to put in the time and effort. Whether you choose to complete online surveys, offer your services as a freelancer or tutor, sell products online, or invest in stocks, there are many opportunities available. With a bit of creativity and hard work, you can start making money online today.
