How To Receive Money Using Qr Code In Gcash

Written by Petter Jul 10, 2023 · 5 min read

GCash is a popular mobile wallet app in the Philippines that allows users to pay bills, buy load, transfer money, and even invest. One of its convenient features is the ability to receive money using QR code. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

Step 1: Open GCash App

Open the GCash app on your smartphone. Make sure you are logged in and have a stable internet connection.

Step 2: Go to Cash-In Tab

Tap the “Cash-In” tab on the bottom right corner of the screen. This will show you different options on how to add money to your GCash account.

Step 3: Choose QR Code

Select “QR Code” as your preferred cash-in method. This will generate a unique QR code that you can use to receive money.

Step 4: Show QR Code to Sender

Show the QR code to the person who will send you money. They can scan it using their GCash app or any other QR code scanner app.

Step 5: Wait for Confirmation

Once the sender has successfully scanned your QR code, you will receive a confirmation message from GCash. The money will be instantly credited to your GCash account.

Step 6: Check Your Balance

To check if you have received the money, go to the “My Wallet” tab and check your balance. You should see the amount credited to your account.

Step 7: Use Your GCash Balance

You can now use your GCash balance to pay bills, buy load, transfer money, and even invest. GCash also has a Mastercard that you can use for online and offline transactions.

FAQs

1. Is there a fee for receiving money using QR code in GCash?

No, there is no fee for receiving money using QR code in GCash.

2. Can I receive money using QR code even if I don’t have a GCash account?

No, you need to have a GCash account to receive money using QR code.

3. Is it safe to receive money using QR code in GCash?

Yes, it is safe to receive money using QR code in GCash. The app uses encryption technology to protect your account and transactions.

Tips and Tricks

1. Make sure your QR code is visible and not blurred to avoid errors in scanning.

2. Double-check the sender’s details before accepting the money to avoid fraud or scams.

3. Always keep your GCash app and phone updated to ensure smooth transactions.

Receiving money using QR code in GCash is a convenient and hassle-free way to get paid. Follow these simple steps and enjoy the benefits of mobile banking.
