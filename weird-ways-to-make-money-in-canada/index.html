Weird Ways To Make Money In Canada

Written by Bardi May 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the traditional 9-5 job? Do you want to make some extra cash on the side? Look no further! Here are some weird ways to make money in Canada:

Become a Professional Cuddler

Believe it or not, you can get paid to cuddle with strangers. Professional cuddlers offer their services to people who are looking for physical touch and connection. It may sound strange, but it’s a growing industry in Canada.

Participate in Medical Studies

Medical studies are always looking for participants to test out new drugs or treatments. While it may not be the most glamorous job, it can pay well and you’re helping advance medical research.

Virtual Game Currency Trader

Do you love playing video games? You can make money by trading virtual game currency. Some games have their own economies where players can buy and sell virtual goods. You can make a profit by buying low and selling high.

Professional Line Sitter

Do you hate waiting in line? You can hire a professional line sitter to stand in line for you. People will pay good money to avoid waiting in long lines for popular events or products.

Professional Snuggler

Similar to professional cuddlers, professional snugglers offer their services to people who are looking for non-sexual physical touch. It may seem strange, but it’s a legitimate way to make money.

Virtual Jury Member

Some online legal services will pay you to be a virtual jury member. You’ll be asked to review cases and provide feedback. It’s a unique way to make money and learn about the legal system.

Human Billboard

Companies will pay you to wear their logo or promote their product. You can become a walking advertisement by wearing branded clothing or carrying a sign around a busy area.

Professional Mourner

Funeral homes will hire professional mourners to attend funerals and provide emotional support to the family. It may seem strange, but it’s a common practice in some cultures.

Personal Shopper for Seniors

Many seniors have difficulty with shopping and may need help with grocery shopping or running errands. You can offer your services as a personal shopper and make some extra money while helping others.

Professional Whistler

Believe it or not, you can get paid to whistle. Some musicians and composers will hire professional whistlers to record for their songs. It’s a unique talent that can pay off.

Conclusion

These are just a few of the weird ways to make money in Canada. While they may not be for everyone, they’re a fun and unique way to make some extra cash.
