Best Way To Get Money In Simcity Buildit

Introduction

SimCity BuildIt is a popular simulation game where players create and manage their own city. One of the key aspects of the game is managing finances and earning money to grow the city. In this article, we will discuss the best ways to get money in SimCity BuildIt.

1. Keep Your City Happy

Keeping your citizens happy is crucial to earning money in SimCity BuildIt. Happy citizens pay higher taxes and generate more revenue for your city. To keep your citizens happy, build parks, entertainment venues, and provide basic services like police and fire departments.

2. Sell Extra Items in Trade Depot

The Trade Depot is a marketplace where players can sell and buy items from other players. If you have extra items like building materials or rare items, you can sell them in the Trade Depot for a profit. Keep an eye on the market demand and set competitive prices to earn more money.

3. Complete Mayor's Challenges

Mayor's Challenges are time-limited events that offer various rewards for completing tasks. These challenges can offer a significant amount of Simoleons (in-game currency) and other valuable items. Participate in these challenges and complete tasks to earn more money.

4. Upgrade and Expand Your City

Upgrading and expanding your city is important to attract more citizens and generate more revenue. Build new residential areas, upgrade roads and transportation, and add new services like hospitals and universities. A larger and more developed city will generate more money.

5. Collect Taxes Regularly

Collecting taxes from your citizens is the primary source of revenue in SimCity BuildIt. Make sure to collect taxes regularly and keep an eye on the tax rate. A higher tax rate will generate more revenue, but it may also make your citizens unhappy.

6. Participate in Contest of Mayors

Contest of Mayors is a weekly competition where players compete against each other to earn the highest score. Completing tasks and earning a high score will offer various rewards, including Simoleons and other valuable items. Participate in this competition to earn more money.

7. Use SimCash Wisely

SimCash is a premium currency that can be used to speed up construction or purchase rare items. Use SimCash wisely and only spend it on essential items. Avoid spending SimCash on unnecessary items or speeding up construction that can be completed with regular currency.

8. Connect with Friends

Connecting with friends in SimCity BuildIt can offer various benefits. You can visit your friend's city and earn rewards, trade items, or even borrow resources. Make sure to add friends and build a strong community to earn more money.

9. Watch Ads for Rewards

Watching ads in SimCity BuildIt can offer various rewards like Simoleons, building materials, or rare items. Make sure to watch ads regularly to earn more rewards and save money on purchasing these items.

10. Be Patient and Persistent

Earning money in SimCity BuildIt requires patience and persistence. Keep building your city, completing tasks, and participating in events to earn more money. It may take time, but a well-developed city will generate a significant amount of revenue.

Conclusion

In conclusion, earning money in SimCity BuildIt requires a combination of strategy, patience, and persistence. Keep your citizens happy, participate in events, and upgrade your city to attract more citizens and generate more revenue. With these tips, you can earn more money and build a thriving city.
