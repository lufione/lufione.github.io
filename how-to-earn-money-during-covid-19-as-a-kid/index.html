How To Earn Money During Covid 19 As A Kid

Written by Eveline Apr 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has affected everyone in different ways. Kids are not exempted from these effects as they have been forced to stay at home due to the pandemic. This has created an opportunity for kids to explore different ways to earn money during the pandemic. This article will provide you with tips and tricks on how to earn money during COVID-19 as a kid.

Online Tutoring

One of the best ways to earn money as a kid is by offering online tutoring services. With schools closed and kids studying from home, there is a high demand for online tutors. You can offer tutoring services in subjects you are good at, and charge a reasonable fee. You can use platforms such as Zoom, Skype or Google Meet to offer your services.

Sell Crafts Online

You can also earn money by selling your crafts online. If you are good at making crafts, such as bracelets, necklaces or even paintings, you can sell them on online marketplaces such as Etsy or Amazon. These platforms offer a wide audience, and you can make a significant amount of money selling your crafts.

Virtual Garage Sale

You can also sell items you no longer need by hosting a virtual garage sale. You can use platforms such as Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist to sell items such as clothes, shoes, toys or books. This will not only help you earn money, but it will also help you declutter your space.

Freelance Writing

If you enjoy writing, you can offer freelance writing services to individuals and businesses. You can offer services such as blog writing, copywriting, or editing. You can use platforms such as Upwork or Fiverr to offer your services.

Virtual Assistant

You can also offer virtual assistant services to individuals or businesses. You can offer services such as data entry, email management, or social media management. You can use platforms such as Freelancer or Indeed to offer your services.

Start a Youtube Channel

If you are good at creating content, you can start a Youtube channel. You can create videos on topics you are passionate about, such as gaming, cooking or sports. You can earn money through advertising revenue, sponsorships or product placements.

Babysitting

You can also offer babysitting services to parents who are working from home. With parents working from home, they may need someone to look after their kids while they work. You can charge a reasonable fee, and use platforms such as Care.com to find clients.

Conclusion

COVID-19 has created an opportunity for kids to explore different ways to earn money. The tips and tricks provided in this article can help you earn money during the pandemic. Remember to always offer services that you are passionate about and charge a reasonable fee. Good luck!
