How To Make Instagram Posts Shareable On Facebook In 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Social media marketing is an essential part of any brand's marketing strategy, and Instagram and Facebook are two of the most popular platforms. Instagram is a great platform to showcase your brand's personality and aesthetic, while Facebook is a powerful tool for connecting with your audience and driving traffic to your website. In this article, we'll show you how to make your Instagram posts shareable on Facebook in 2023.

Step 1: Connect Your Instagram Account to Your Facebook Page

The first step to making your Instagram posts shareable on Facebook is to connect your Instagram account to your Facebook page. To do this, go to your Facebook page and click on the "Settings" tab. From there, click on "Instagram" in the left-hand menu and follow the prompts to connect your Instagram account.

Step 2: Set Your Instagram Posts to Automatically Share on Facebook

Once your Instagram account is connected to your Facebook page, you can set your Instagram posts to automatically share on Facebook. To do this, go to your Instagram profile and click on the three lines in the top right-hand corner. From there, click on "Settings" and then "Account." Scroll down to "Sharing to Other Apps" and select "Facebook." From there, you can choose to share your Instagram posts to your Facebook page, your Facebook profile, or both.

Step 3: Edit Your Instagram Post Before Sharing on Facebook

If you prefer to manually share your Instagram posts on Facebook, you can do so by editing your post before sharing. To do this, click on the three dots on your Instagram post and select "Share." From there, select "Facebook" and edit the post as desired. You can add a caption, location, and tag people before sharing.

Step 4: Make Sure Your Instagram Post is Public

In order for your Instagram post to be shareable on Facebook, it must be set to "public." If your Instagram profile is set to "private," your posts will not be shareable on Facebook. To change your Instagram profile to public, go to your Instagram profile and click on "Edit Profile." From there, toggle the "Private Account" switch to "Off."

Step 5: Use Hashtags and Location Tags

Using hashtags and location tags on your Instagram posts can help increase visibility and make your posts more shareable on Facebook. When you use hashtags and location tags, your posts are more likely to be seen by people who are interested in your brand or in the location you are posting from.

Step 6: Encourage Engagement on Instagram

The more engagement your Instagram posts receive, the more likely they are to be shared on Facebook. Encourage your followers to like, comment, and share your posts on Instagram by asking questions, running contests, and hosting giveaways.

Step 7: Share Your Instagram Posts on Your Facebook Page

In addition to automatically sharing your Instagram posts on Facebook, you can also manually share your Instagram posts on your Facebook page. To do this, simply open your Instagram post and click on the "Share" button. From there, select "Facebook" and choose whether you want to share the post on your Facebook page or your personal profile.

Step 8: Use Eye-Catching Visuals

In order to make your Instagram posts more shareable on Facebook, it's important to use eye-catching visuals. Use high-quality images and videos that showcase your brand's personality and aesthetic. When your posts look great on Instagram, they are more likely to be shared on Facebook.

Step 9: Post Consistently on Instagram

The more consistently you post on Instagram, the more likely your posts are to be shared on Facebook. Make sure you are posting high-quality content on a regular basis to keep your followers engaged and interested in your brand.

Step 10: Analyze Your Results

Finally, make sure you are analyzing your results to see what is working and what isn't. Use Instagram and Facebook analytics tools to track your engagement and see which posts are being shared the most. Use this information to adjust your strategy and make your Instagram posts more shareable on Facebook.

Conclusion

Making your Instagram posts shareable on Facebook is a great way to increase visibility and drive traffic to your website. Follow these steps to connect your Instagram account to your Facebook page, set your posts to automatically share, and manually share your posts on Facebook. Use hashtags, location tags, and eye-catching visuals to make your posts more shareable, and encourage engagement on Instagram to increase your reach. By following these tips, you can make your Instagram posts more shareable on Facebook and take your social media marketing to the next level in 2023.
