How Much Money Do We Get From Youtube In Indian Rupees

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Introduction

YouTube has become a popular platform for content creators to showcase their skills and earn money in return. It has given birth to a new breed of professionals known as YouTubers. These individuals create content ranging from educational to entertaining to informative in nature. But how much money do they make from YouTube in Indian Rupees? Let's find out.

Factors Affecting Earnings

The amount of money a YouTuber makes depends on several factors such as the number of views, clicks on ads, engagement, and the type of content. YouTube channels with a large number of subscribers and viewers are more likely to earn higher revenues than those with fewer subscribers. Additionally, the type of content plays an important role in determining the earnings.

Monetization of YouTube Channels

To monetize your YouTube channel, you need to have at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the last 12 months. Once you meet these requirements, you can apply for the YouTube Partner Program (YPP). Once approved, you can earn money from ads displayed on your videos.

Types of Ads

There are several types of ads that can be displayed on YouTube videos, such as display ads, overlay ads, skippable video ads, non-skippable video ads, and bumper ads. Each ad has its own revenue model, and the earnings vary depending on the ad format.

CPC and CPM Model

The earnings from YouTube ads are calculated using two models, Cost per Click (CPC) and Cost per Impression (CPM). In the CPC model, you earn money when a viewer clicks on the ad displayed on your video. In the CPM model, you earn money for every 1,000 ad impressions.

Average Earnings

On average, a YouTuber can earn between INR 0.25 to INR 4 per view, depending on the type of content and the engagement rate. However, this is not a fixed amount, and the earnings can vary significantly depending on the factors mentioned earlier.

YouTube Premium

YouTube Premium is a subscription-based service that allows users to watch videos without ads. If your video is played by a YouTube Premium user, you can earn a share of the subscription revenue.

Conclusion

Earning money from YouTube is not an easy task. It requires consistent effort and quality content to attract viewers and advertisers. The amount of money you can earn varies depending on several factors, making it a challenging task to predict earnings accurately. However, if you have a passion for creating content and are willing to put in the effort, YouTube can be a lucrative platform to earn money in Indian Rupees.
