How To Enjoy Youtube Without Ads In 2023

Written by Petter Apr 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Introduction

YouTube has become an essential part of our lives, and we spend hours daily watching videos on this platform. However, the only downside is the ads that disrupt our viewing experience. In 2023, there are ways to watch YouTube without ads, and we will show you how.

Use YouTube Premium

YouTube Premium is a subscription-based service that offers ad-free YouTube experience. You can enjoy your favorite videos without any interruptions, and the best part is that it is available on all devices. Moreover, you can also download videos for offline viewing, and the subscription includes access to YouTube Music.

Install Ad-Blockers

Ad-blockers are browser extensions that block all ads on websites, including YouTube. You can install ad-blockers like AdBlock Plus, uBlock Origin, or AdGuard on your browser and enjoy an ad-free YouTube experience. However, some ad-blockers may block YouTube videos themselves, and you may need to tweak the settings to allow YouTube videos.

Use Third-Party Apps

There are several third-party apps that allow you to watch YouTube without ads. Apps like YouTube Vanced, NewPipe, and YouTube Go provide an ad-free experience and additional features like background playback, video downloads, and more. However, these apps may not be available on the official app stores, and you may need to download them from third-party sources.

Disable Personalized Ads

YouTube shows personalized ads based on your search history, watch history, and other data. You can disable personalized ads by going to your Google Account settings and turning off the "Ads Personalization" option. However, you may still see some generic ads on YouTube.

Watch Videos on Alternative Platforms

Several alternative platforms like Vimeo, Dailymotion, and Twitch offer video streaming services without ads. You can explore these platforms and find your favorite content creators and videos. However, these platforms may not have the same user base as YouTube, and you may not find all the videos you are looking for.

Conclusion

In conclusion, watching YouTube without ads is possible in 2023, and you can choose from various options like YouTube Premium, ad-blockers, third-party apps, and alternative platforms. You can choose the option that suits your needs and preferences and enjoy an uninterrupted viewing experience. However, it is essential to remember that content creators rely on ads for revenue, and if you enjoy their content, consider supporting them by watching their ads.
