How To Make Money Living In Bali

Written by Oliver May 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Bali is a beautiful island paradise that attracts millions of tourists every year. However, did you know that you can also make a living in Bali? Whether you want to start a business or work for someone else, there are plenty of opportunities to earn a living on this tropical island. Here are some tips on how to make money living in Bali.

Start a Business

Starting a business in Bali is a great way to make money. With the rise of tourism, there are plenty of opportunities to start a business that caters to tourists. For example, you could open a restaurant, café, or bar. Alternatively, you could start a tour company or a surf school. The possibilities are endless.

Work for Someone Else

If you don’t want to start your own business, you can still make a living in Bali by working for someone else. There are plenty of job opportunities in Bali, especially in the hospitality and tourism industries. You could work as a bartender, waiter, or tour guide. You could also work in a hotel or resort.

Become a Digital Nomad

Bali is a popular destination for digital nomads, who work remotely while traveling the world. If you have skills that can be done online, such as writing, graphic design, or programming, you could become a digital nomad and work from Bali. There are plenty of co-working spaces and cafes with fast internet connections.

Teach English

Teaching English is another way to make money in Bali. There are plenty of language schools and private tutoring opportunities. You don’t need to be a certified teacher, but it helps if you have a TEFL or TESOL certification. You could also work as an au pair for a family who wants their children to learn English.

Freelance

If you have skills that can be done remotely, such as writing, graphic design, or programming, you could freelance and work from Bali. There are plenty of websites that connect freelancers with clients, such as Upwork and Fiverr. You could also network with other freelancers in Bali.

Invest in Property

Investing in property is a great way to make money in Bali. With the rise of tourism, property prices have been increasing steadily. You could buy a villa or apartment and rent it out to tourists. Alternatively, you could buy land and develop it yourself.

Start a Blog or YouTube Channel

If you have a passion for writing or video, you could start a blog or YouTube channel about Bali. There are plenty of topics to cover, such as travel, food, culture, and lifestyle. You could monetize your blog or channel through advertising, sponsorships, or affiliate marketing.

Work in the Yoga or Wellness Industry

Bali is known for its yoga and wellness scene. If you have experience as a yoga teacher, massage therapist, or wellness coach, you could work in this industry in Bali. There are plenty of retreat centers, spas, and yoga studios that are always looking for qualified professionals.

Learn a New Skill

If you want to make money in Bali, it helps to have a unique skill that sets you apart from others. For example, you could learn how to make Balinese handicrafts, such as batik or woodcarving. You could also learn how to cook Balinese cuisine or play traditional Balinese music. These skills could be used to start a business or offer services to tourists.

Network and Build Relationships

Finally, to make money in Bali, it’s important to network and build relationships with other people. Attend events, join groups, and meet new people. Bali is a friendly and welcoming place, so don’t be afraid to put yourself out there.

Conclusion

Living in Bali is a dream for many people. But it’s not just a place for relaxation and leisure. You can also make a living in Bali by following these tips. Whether you start a business, work for someone else, or become a digital nomad, there are plenty of opportunities to earn a living in this tropical paradise.
