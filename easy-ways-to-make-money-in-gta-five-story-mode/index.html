Easy Ways to Make Money in GTA Five Story Mode

GTA Five Story Mode is one of the most popular games in the world. It is an action-packed game that involves a lot of shooting, racing, and heists. One of the most important aspects of the game is making money. In this article, we will give you some tips on how to make money in GTA Five Story Mode.

Selling Cars

One of the easiest ways to make money in GTA Five Story Mode is by selling cars. You can steal cars and sell them to the Los Santos Customs. You can also sell high-end cars to the specialist dealer, Simeon Yetarian, for more money.

Investing in the Stock Market

The stock market is another way to make money in GTA Five Story Mode. You can invest in the stock market through the LCN or BAWSAQ websites. You can use the in-game internet to access these websites. Keep an eye on the stock prices and invest in the right companies to make a profit.

Completing Missions

Completing missions is the main way to progress in GTA Five Story Mode. You can also make a lot of money by completing missions. The payouts for missions vary depending on the difficulty level. You can also replay missions to earn more money.

Robbing Stores

You can also rob stores to make money in GTA Five Story Mode. You can rob gas stations, convenience stores, and liquor stores. You can use a gun or a melee weapon to rob the store. Make sure to escape the cops after the robbery.

Collecting Hidden Packages

There are 12 hidden packages in GTA Five Story Mode. You can find them scattered around the map. Each package contains $7,000. Collecting all 12 packages will give you a total of $84,000.

Playing the Stock Market with Lester's Assassination Missions

Lester's Assassination Missions are a great way to make money in GTA Five Story Mode. You can manipulate the stock market by assassinating certain targets. Before doing the missions, invest in the stocks of the targetâs rival companies. After the mission, the stock prices of the rival companies will increase, and you can sell your stocks for a profit.

Participating in Races

Races are a fun way to make money in GTA Five Story Mode. You can participate in street races, off-road races, and sea races. The payouts for races vary depending on the difficulty level. You can also bet on yourself to win the race and earn more money.

Robbing Armored Trucks

You can also rob armored trucks to make money in GTA Five Story Mode. You can find armored trucks on the map. Use a sticky bomb or a grenade to blow up the truck and collect the money. Make sure to escape the cops after the robbery.

Collecting Epsilon Tracts

There are 10 Epsilon Tracts in GTA Five Story Mode. You can find them scattered around the map. Each tract contains $25,000. Collecting all 10 tracts will give you a total of $250,000.

Buying Properties

You can also make money by buying properties in GTA Five Story Mode. You can buy businesses and properties, such as the Los Santos Golf Club, the Vanilla Unicorn Strip Club, and the Downtown Cab Co. You can earn a steady stream of income from these properties.

Conclusion

These were some of the easy ways to make money in GTA Five Story Mode. Use these tips wisely to become a rich player in the game. Remember, the key to making money is patience and perseverance.
