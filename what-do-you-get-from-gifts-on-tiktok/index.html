What Do You Get From Gifts On Tiktok

Written by William May 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're an avid TikTok user, you might have noticed that some creators receive gifts from their followers. These gifts, which can vary from virtual stickers to real money, are a way for fans to show their appreciation and support for their favorite content creators. But what do you actually get from gifts on TikTok?

What Are TikTok Gifts?

TikTok gifts are virtual items that you can send to your favorite creators as a form of appreciation. These gifts can be purchased using real money or TikTok coins, which you can earn by watching videos on the app or by buying them with real money. The value of these gifts ranges from a few coins to hundreds of dollars, depending on the type of gift.

What Do Creators Get from Gifts?

When a creator receives a gift on TikTok, they earn "beans," which are the virtual currency used on the app. These beans can be exchanged for real money, which creators can withdraw once they reach a certain threshold. The amount of beans earned from a gift depends on the value of the gift, with more expensive gifts earning more beans.

What Can Creators Do with Beans?

Creators can use their beans to purchase gifts for other creators or to exchange them for real money. TikTok takes a percentage of the beans exchanged for real money, but creators can still earn a decent amount if they receive a lot of gifts. Some creators use their earnings to invest in better equipment or to fund their content creation.

Why Do Fans Send Gifts?

Fans send gifts to their favorite creators as a way to show their support and appreciation. It's a way to say "thank you" for the content that the creator provides and to encourage them to keep creating. Some fans also send gifts to get the attention of their favorite creators and to show that they're a dedicated fan.

What Are the Different Types of Gifts?

TikTok offers a variety of gifts that fans can send to their favorite creators. Some of the most popular gifts include:

  • Stickers - These are the cheapest gifts and can be bought for a few coins. They're virtual stickers that appear on the creator's video.
  • Flowers - These are more expensive gifts and can be bought for a few hundred coins. They're virtual flowers that appear on the creator's profile.
  • Gifts - These are more expensive gifts that can be bought for thousands of coins. They're virtual gifts that appear on the creator's profile and can include things like a car or a yacht.
  • Money - Fans can also send real money to their favorite creators as a gift. This is the most expensive gift and can be a way to show extra support.

How Do You Send Gifts?

To send a gift on TikTok, you need to have TikTok coins in your account. You can purchase coins using real money or earn them by watching videos on the app. Once you have coins, you can go to a creator's profile and select the gift you want to send. You'll be prompted to enter the number of the gift you want to send, and the cost in coins will be deducted from your account.

What Are the Benefits of Receiving Gifts?

Receiving gifts on TikTok can be a big boost for creators. Not only do they earn beans, which can be exchanged for real money, but they also get a sense of validation and appreciation from their fans. This can encourage them to keep creating and to improve the quality of their content. Creators who receive a lot of gifts can also gain more visibility on the app, as their profile will be more prominent.

Conclusion

Sending gifts on TikTok is a way for fans to show their support for their favorite creators, while also giving them a boost in earnings and visibility. Creators who receive gifts can use the beans to buy more gifts or exchange them for real money, which can help fund their content creation. Whether you're a fan or a creator, TikTok gifts can be a fun and rewarding way to interact with the app and its community.
