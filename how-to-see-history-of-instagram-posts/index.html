How to See History of Instagram Posts

Instagram is a popular social media platform that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers. If you're an avid Instagram user, you may want to see your post history to keep track of your activity. In this article, we'll show you how to see your history of Instagram posts.

Step 1: Open Instagram App

The first step to see your history of Instagram posts is to open the Instagram app on your smartphone. You can download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store if you haven't already.

Step 2: Go to Your Profile

Once you've opened the app, tap on your profile picture in the bottom right corner of the screen. This will take you to your profile page where you can see all your photos and videos.

Step 3: Tap on the Three Lines

Next, tap on the three horizontal lines in the top right corner of the screen. This will open a menu where you can access different features of the app.

Step 4: Select Settings

From the menu, select the "Settings" option. This will take you to a new page where you can adjust your account settings.

Step 5: Tap on Account

On the Settings page, tap on the "Account" option. This will take you to a new page where you can manage your account settings.

Step 6: Select Posts You've Liked

Scroll down on the Account page until you see the "Posts You've Liked" option. Tap on this option to see all the posts you've liked on Instagram.

Step 7: Tap on the Three Dots

To see your history of Instagram posts, tap on the three dots in the top right corner of the screen. This will open a menu where you can select different options.

Step 8: Select Archive

From the menu, select the "Archive" option. This will take you to a new page where you can see all your archived posts.

Step 9: View Archived Posts

To see your history of Instagram posts, tap on the "Posts" option on the Archive page. This will show you all the posts you've archived since you started using Instagram.

Step 10: Scroll Through Your History

Finally, you can scroll through your history of Instagram posts to see all the photos and videos you've shared on the app. You can also unarchive any posts that you want to bring back to your profile.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to see your history of Instagram posts, you can keep track of your activity on the app. Whether you're a casual user or a social media influencer, it's always helpful to know what you've shared on Instagram.
