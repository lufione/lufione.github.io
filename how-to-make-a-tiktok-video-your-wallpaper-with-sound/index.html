How To Make A Tiktok Video Your Wallpaper With Sound In 2023

Written by Petter Sep 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a fan of TikTok and want to personalize your phone’s wallpaper with a TikTok video and sound? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of making a TikTok video your wallpaper with sound, step by step.

Step 1: Download the TikTok Video

The first thing you need to do is download the TikTok video you want to use as your wallpaper. To do this, open the TikTok app and find the video you want. Click on the share icon and select “Save video” to save the video to your camera roll.

Step 2: Download a Video to Live Wallpaper App

Next, you need to download a video to live wallpaper app from the app store. There are several options available, but we recommend using “Video Live Wallpaper – HD Backgrounds and Lock Screens” for its user-friendly interface and numerous features.

Step 3: Select Your TikTok Video

Open the video to live wallpaper app and select “Video Live Wallpaper” from the menu. Then, select “Choose Video” and find the TikTok video you saved in step 1.

Step 4: Edit the Video Settings

Once you’ve selected your TikTok video, you’ll be taken to the video settings screen. Here, you can edit the video’s position, size, and sound settings. You can also choose whether to loop the video or play it only once.

Step 5: Set the Video as Your Wallpaper

After you’ve edited the video settings to your liking, select “Set Wallpaper” to make the TikTok video your phone’s wallpaper. You’ll be asked to choose whether to set the video as your home screen wallpaper, lock screen wallpaper, or both.

Step 6: Enjoy Your New Wallpaper

And there you have it! Your TikTok video is now your phone’s wallpaper with sound. You can enjoy watching it whenever you use your phone, and you can even show it off to your friends.

FAQs

Q: Can I use any TikTok video as my wallpaper?

A: Yes, you can use any TikTok video you like as your wallpaper, as long as you download it to your camera roll first.

Q: Can I use a TikTok video as my wallpaper on an iPhone or Android?

A: Yes, you can use a TikTok video as your wallpaper on both iPhones and Androids.

Q: Can I change the settings of the wallpaper once I’ve set it?

A: Yes, you can change the settings of your TikTok wallpaper at any time by going back to the video to live wallpaper app and selecting the video you want to edit.

Tips and Tricks

  • Choose a TikTok video with bright and vibrant colors to make your wallpaper pop.
  • Experiment with different video settings to find the perfect look for your wallpaper.
  • Consider using a TikTok video of your favorite artist or celebrity to show off your fandom.
  • Make sure to download a video to live wallpaper app with good reviews and frequent updates to ensure it works properly.

Now that you know how to make a TikTok video your wallpaper with sound, you’re ready to personalize your phone like a TikTok pro. Happy wallpapering!
