How To Make Money Online In Kenya 2022

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

With the rise of technology and the internet, making money online has become a popular interest for many Kenyans. In this article, we will explore several ways to make money online in Kenya in 2022.

Online Freelancing

One of the best ways to make money online in Kenya is through online freelancing. Freelancing involves working for different clients on a project basis. Websites such as Upwork, Freelancer, and Fiverr offer a wide range of freelancing opportunities. You can offer services such as writing, graphic design, video editing, and programming.

Online Tutoring

If you have expertise in a particular field, you can make money online by tutoring students. Websites such as Chegg and TutorMe offer online tutoring opportunities. You can teach subjects such as math, science, and English.

Online Surveys

You can also make money online by participating in online surveys. Companies such as Survey Junkie, Swagbucks, and Toluna pay users for sharing their opinions. While you may not make a lot of money, it can be a good way to earn some extra cash.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing involves promoting products and earning a commission for every sale made through your unique referral link. Websites such as Amazon Associates and ClickBank offer affiliate marketing programs. You can promote products such as books, electronics, and software.

Blog Writing

If you have a passion for writing, you can make money online by starting a blog. You can write about topics such as food, fashion, and travel. Once you have built a following, you can monetize your blog through advertising and sponsored content.

Online Trading

You can also make money online by trading stocks, forex, and cryptocurrencies. Websites such as eToro and Plus500 offer online trading platforms. While online trading can be risky, it can also be a lucrative way to make money.

Online Store

If you have a product to sell, you can make money online by setting up an online store. Websites such as Shopify and WooCommerce offer easy-to-use e-commerce platforms. You can sell products such as clothing, jewelry, and electronics.

Conclusion

In conclusion, making money online in Kenya in 2022 is possible through various methods such as online freelancing, tutoring, surveys, affiliate marketing, blog writing, online trading, and setting up an online store. With determination and hard work, anyone can make money online.

Disclaimer:

It is important to note that making money online requires effort and dedication. While these methods may work for some, there is no guarantee of success. It is important to do your research and only invest what you can afford to lose.
