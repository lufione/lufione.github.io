How Google Adsense Pays In Pakistan

Written by Bardi May 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Introduction

Google AdSense is one of the most popular monetization methods used by bloggers and website owners. It allows you to earn money by displaying ads on your website. But how does Google AdSense pay in Pakistan?

Payment Methods

Google AdSense offers several payment methods for publishers in Pakistan. The most commonly used payment methods are bank transfer and Western Union. You can also choose to receive payments through a check, but it is not the recommended payment method due to the longer processing time.

Payment Threshold

The payment threshold for Google AdSense in Pakistan is $100. This means that you need to earn at least $100 in order to receive payment. Once you reach the payment threshold, Google AdSense will automatically send your payment on the next payment date.

Payment Dates

Google AdSense has a monthly payment cycle. Payments are sent on the 21st of every month. However, it is important to note that the payment date may vary depending on your payment method. For example, bank transfer payments may take longer to process.

Payment Currency

Google AdSense offers payment in US dollars (USD). This means that if you are receiving payment through bank transfer or Western Union, you will need to convert the payment into Pakistani rupees (PKR) by yourself. It is important to keep in mind the exchange rate when converting your payment.

Taxes

In Pakistan, all Google AdSense earnings are subject to income tax. You will need to file your income tax returns and declare your earnings from Google AdSense. It is important to consult with a tax professional to ensure that you are compliant with the tax laws in Pakistan.

Payment Issues

If you are facing issues with receiving payment from Google AdSense, you can contact their support team. They will be able to assist you with any payment-related issues that you may be facing.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Google AdSense is a great way to earn money from your website. In Pakistan, you can receive payment through bank transfer or Western Union. However, it is important to keep in mind the payment threshold, payment dates, payment currency, and taxes. By following these guidelines, you can ensure that you receive your payment from Google AdSense without any issues.
