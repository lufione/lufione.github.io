Easy Ways To Make Money As A Kid In Quarantine

Written by Christine May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has changed our lives in many ways, including the way we earn money. With schools closed and social distancing measures in place, it can be challenging for kids to find ways to make money. However, with a little creativity and effort, there are still plenty of opportunities to earn some extra cash. In this article, we'll explore some easy ways for kids to make money while in quarantine.

Sell Crafts Online

Do you have a talent for making crafts? If so, you can turn that talent into cash by selling your creations online. There are many platforms, such as Etsy and eBay, where you can set up an online store and sell your handmade goods. Whether you're good at knitting, crocheting, painting, or making jewelry, there's a market for your craft.

Offer Online Tutoring Services

With schools closed, many kids are struggling to keep up with their studies. If you're good at a particular subject, you can offer online tutoring services to other kids. You can advertise your services on social media or tutoring websites, such as TutorMe or Chegg, and set your own rates.

Do Yard Work for Neighbors

Just because you're in quarantine doesn't mean you can't help your neighbors. Offer to do yard work, such as mowing lawns or pulling weeds, for a small fee. You can advertise your services on social media or put up flyers in your neighborhood.

Sell Items Online

Do you have items you no longer need or want? You can sell them online for some extra cash. Whether it's clothes, toys, or electronics, there's a market for used items. You can use platforms like eBay or Facebook Marketplace to sell your items.

Deliver Groceries

Many people are avoiding going to the grocery store due to the pandemic. If you're old enough to ride a bike or drive a car, you can offer to deliver groceries for your neighbors. You can advertise your services on social media or put up flyers in your neighborhood.

Offer Pet Sitting Services

With people working from home, there's a greater need for pet sitting services. If you love animals, you can offer to pet sit for your neighbors. You can advertise your services on social media or put up flyers in your neighborhood.

Create and Sell Digital Products

Do you have a talent for graphic design or writing? You can create and sell digital products, such as logos, social media posts, or e-books. Platforms like Fiverr or Upwork are great places to advertise your services and find clients.

Complete Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay you to complete online surveys. While you won't get rich doing this, it's an easy way to make some extra cash. Some popular survey websites include Swagbucks and Survey Junkie.

Offer House Cleaning Services

Many people are spending more time at home and are looking for help with house cleaning. If you're good at cleaning, you can offer your services to your neighbors. You can advertise your services on social media or put up flyers in your neighborhood.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are still plenty of opportunities for kids to make money during quarantine. Whether it's selling crafts online, offering tutoring services, or delivering groceries, there's something for everyone. With a little effort and creativity, you can turn your skills and talents into cash. So, what are you waiting for? Start making money today!
