How to Make TikTok Videos into Live Photos

If you're a TikTok user, you know how addictive it can be to watch and create videos on the platform. But have you ever wanted to turn your favorite TikTok videos into live photos? It's easier than you might think! In this article, we'll show you how to do it step by step.

What are Live Photos?

Live Photos are a feature on iPhones that capture a few seconds of video before and after a photo is taken. When you press and hold on the photo, it comes to life and plays the video. This feature is perfect for capturing moments in motion, such as a jumping dog or a child blowing out candles on a birthday cake.

Step 1: Find the TikTok Video You Want to Use

The first step in making a TikTok video into a Live Photo is to find the video you want to use. Open the TikTok app and find the video in your feed or search for it using the search bar.

Step 2: Download the Video

Once you've found the video, you need to download it to your phone. To do this, tap the "Share" icon on the right side of the screen and select "Save Video." The video will be saved to your camera roll.

Step 3: Convert the Video to a Live Photo

Now that you have the video saved to your camera roll, it's time to turn it into a Live Photo. There are several apps available for this, but we recommend using the "IntoLive" app, which is available for free on the App Store.

Step 4: Open the IntoLive App

Open the IntoLive app and select the video you want to turn into a Live Photo. You can select videos from your camera roll or from other sources such as Google Drive or Dropbox.

Step 5: Edit the Live Photo

Once you've selected the video, you can edit the Live Photo to your liking. You can choose the starting point of the video, add filters, and even add music. When you're happy with the Live Photo, tap "Save" to save it to your camera roll.

Step 6: Set the Live Photo as Your Wallpaper

Now that you have your Live Photo, it's time to set it as your wallpaper. To do this, go to your phone's settings and select "Wallpaper." Choose the Live Photo you just created and set it as your wallpaper.

Step 7: Enjoy Your New Live Photo!

That's it! You've successfully turned your favorite TikTok video into a Live Photo. Now you can enjoy it every time you unlock your phone.

FAQs

Q: Can I make any TikTok video into a Live Photo?

A: Yes, as long as you can download the video to your phone, you can turn it into a Live Photo.

Q: Do I need an iPhone to make Live Photos?

A: Yes, Live Photos are a feature exclusive to iPhones.

Q: Can I share Live Photos with my friends?

A: Yes, you can share Live Photos with other iPhone users by sending them through iMessage or AirDrop.

Q: Can I use a different app to make Live Photos?

A: Yes, there are several apps available for making Live Photos, but we recommend using IntoLive for its ease of use and features.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for making the best Live Photos:

  • Choose videos with interesting motion or action.
  • Experiment with different filters and music to make your Live Photo unique.
  • Try using Live Photos as your lock screen wallpaper for a fun and interactive experience.
  • Share your Live Photos with friends and family to show off your creativity.
