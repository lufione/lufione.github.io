How To Withdraw Money From Your Youtube Account

Written by William May 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

Creating content and uploading it to YouTube can be a lucrative way to make money. If you have been monetizing your videos, you might be wondering how you can withdraw the earnings from your YouTube account. In this article, we will guide you through the process of withdrawing money from your YouTube account.

Step 1: Meet the Payment Threshold

Before you can withdraw your earnings from your YouTube account, you need to meet the payment threshold. The payment threshold is $100 for most countries. This means that you need to have at least $100 in your AdSense account before you can withdraw your earnings.

Step 2: Link Your AdSense Account

In order to withdraw your earnings from your YouTube account, you need to link your AdSense account to your YouTube account. If you haven't already done so, you can follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your YouTube account.
  2. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Select "YouTube Studio" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click on "Monetization" in the left sidebar.
  5. Click on "Settings" in the "Monetization" tab.
  6. Click on "Connect AdSense account".
  7. Follow the prompts to create a new AdSense account or link an existing one.

Step 3: Set Up Payment Method

Once your AdSense account is linked to your YouTube account, you need to set up your payment method. You can choose from several payment methods, including:

  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)
  • Wire Transfer
  • Check
  • Rapida

Select the payment method that is most convenient for you and follow the prompts to set it up.

Step 4: Request a Payment

Once you have met the payment threshold and set up your payment method, you can request a payment from your AdSense account. Here's how:

  1. Log in to your AdSense account.
  2. Click on "Payments" in the left sidebar.
  3. Click on "Add payment method" if you haven't already set one up.
  4. Click on "Request payment".
  5. Select the amount you want to withdraw.
  6. Select your payment method.
  7. Follow the prompts to complete the payment request.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to receive payment?

The time it takes to receive payment depends on your payment method. EFT and Rapida payments typically take 1-5 business days, while wire transfers and checks can take up to several weeks.

2. What if I don't meet the payment threshold?

If you don't meet the payment threshold, your earnings will roll over to the next month until you reach the threshold. Once you reach the threshold, you can follow the steps above to withdraw your earnings.

3. Can I change my payment method?

Yes, you can change your payment method at any time by following the prompts in your AdSense account.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips to help you maximize your earnings on YouTube:

  • Upload high-quality, engaging content on a regular basis.
  • Promote your videos on social media and other platforms.
  • Collaborate with other YouTubers to reach new audiences.
  • Engage with your viewers by responding to comments and creating a community.

Conclusion

Withdrawing your earnings from your YouTube account is a straightforward process once you have met the payment threshold and linked your AdSense account. By following the steps above and implementing the tips and tricks, you can increase your earnings and build a successful YouTube channel.
