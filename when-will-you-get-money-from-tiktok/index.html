When Will You Get Money From Tiktok

Written by Christine Sep 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in recent years. With its vast user base and creative content, it has become a hub for influencers and entrepreneurs to showcase their talent and make money. If you are wondering when you will get money from TikTok, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the various ways in which you can make money on TikTok and when you can expect to receive your earnings.

How to Make Money on TikTok

Before we delve into when you can expect to receive your earnings, let us first discuss the various ways in which you can make money on TikTok. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Brand Deals and Sponsorships

One of the most common ways to make money on TikTok is through brand deals and sponsorships. This involves partnering with a brand or company to promote their products or services on your TikTok account. The payment for such deals can range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars, depending on your follower count and engagement rate.

2. Livestreaming

TikTok also allows you to earn money through its live streaming feature. During your live streams, your followers can send you virtual gifts, which can be converted into real money. The amount you earn per gift depends on your level on the platform, with higher-level users earning more per gift.

3. Creator Fund

The Creator Fund is a program launched by TikTok to support its content creators. It allows eligible users to earn money based on their video views and engagement rate. The payment can range from a few cents to a few dollars per 1,000 views.

When Will You Receive Your Money?

The answer to this question depends on the payment method you choose and the payment threshold set by the platform. Here are the payment details for each of the methods mentioned above:

1. Brand Deals and Sponsorships

The payment for brand deals and sponsorships is usually made directly to your bank account or PayPal. The payment terms and conditions are decided between you and the brand, so it can vary. Typically, the payment is made after the completion of the campaign or after a specified period.

2. Livestreaming

The payment for virtual gifts received during your live streams is made every week, directly to your PayPal account. The minimum payment threshold is $100.

3. Creator Fund

The payment for the Creator Fund is made every month, directly to your PayPal account. The minimum payment threshold is $100.

Conclusion

TikTok offers multiple opportunities to make money and monetize your content. Whether it is through brand deals, livestreaming, or the Creator Fund, you can earn a considerable amount of money on the platform. However, it is essential to remember that the payment terms and conditions vary for each method, and you may have to wait for a certain period to receive your earnings. We hope this article helped you understand when you can expect to get money from TikTok.
