How To Earn Money By Watching Videos On Youtube

Written by Robby May 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

YouTube is a platform that has gained immense popularity over the years. People have made a fortune by creating content and uploading it on the platform. However, did you know that you can also earn money by watching videos on YouTube? Yes, you heard it right. In this article, we will show you how to earn money by watching videos on YouTube.

What is YouTube?

YouTube is a video-sharing platform that was created in 2005. It allows users to upload, share, and view videos. The platform has over 2 billion monthly active users and is the second most visited website in the world after Google. YouTube has become an important source of entertainment and information for people across the globe.

How to Earn Money by Watching Videos on YouTube?

There are several ways to earn money by watching videos on YouTube. One of the most popular ways is by becoming a YouTube Partner. To become a YouTube Partner, you need to have a YouTube channel that has at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months. Once you meet these requirements, you can apply to become a YouTube Partner.

Becoming a YouTube Partner

To become a YouTube Partner, follow these steps:

  1. Create a YouTube channel
  2. Upload original videos
  3. Enable monetization on your videos
  4. Apply for the YouTube Partner Program

Once you become a YouTube Partner, you can earn money through advertising revenue. YouTube displays ads on your videos, and you get a share of the revenue generated from those ads.

Other Ways to Earn Money by Watching Videos on YouTube

Aside from becoming a YouTube Partner, there are other ways to earn money by watching videos on YouTube. Some companies pay people to watch their ads on YouTube. You can sign up for these programs and get paid for watching ads.

Things to Keep in Mind

While it is possible to earn money by watching videos on YouTube, it is not a get-rich-quick scheme. You need to put in time and effort to build your YouTube channel and attract viewers. You also need to create original content that people want to watch.

Moreover, you need to be careful of scams that promise to pay you for watching videos on YouTube. Always do your research before signing up for any program that promises to pay you for watching videos.

Conclusion

In conclusion, earning money by watching videos on YouTube is a real possibility. However, it requires time, effort, and dedication. Becoming a YouTube Partner is one of the most popular ways to earn money on the platform. Other ways include signing up for programs that pay you to watch ads. Remember to be careful of scams and always do your research before signing up for any program.
