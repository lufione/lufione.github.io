How To Get Money Fast In Forza Horizon 4

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Introduction

Forza Horizon 4 is an open-world racing game developed by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios. The game is set in a fictionalized version of the United Kingdom, and players can explore the world, participate in races, and customize their cars. One of the key aspects of the game is earning money, which can be used to buy new cars, upgrade existing ones, and purchase properties. In this article, we will provide tips and tricks on how to get money fast in Forza Horizon 4.

Complete Races and Challenges

The easiest way to earn money in Forza Horizon 4 is by completing races and challenges. There are different types of races and challenges available, such as street races, dirt races, and cross-country races. Completing these races will earn you money, and the amount of money you earn depends on the difficulty of the race and your performance. To maximize your earnings, try to finish in first place and complete any bonus objectives.

Use Wheelspins and Super Wheelspins

Wheelspins and Super Wheelspins are rewards that you can earn in Forza Horizon 4. You can use them to earn money, cars, and other prizes. Wheelspins are earned by leveling up, while Super Wheelspins are earned by completing certain challenges. To use a Wheelspin or Super Wheelspin, go to the Wheelspin menu and select the item you want to spin. You can earn up to 1 million credits with a Super Wheelspin, so it's worth saving them up for a big payout.

Sell Cars in the Auction House

If you have cars that you don't need or want, you can sell them in the Auction House. To sell a car, go to the Cars menu and select the car you want to sell. Choose the Auction House option and set a starting price and a buyout price. Other players can bid on your car, and if someone meets the buyout price, the car will be sold to them. Selling rare or high-performance cars can earn you a lot of money, so keep an eye out for valuable cars to sell.

Invest in Properties

Properties are a great way to earn money passively in Forza Horizon 4. You can buy different properties, such as car dealerships and businesses, and they will generate income for you over time. The amount of money you earn depends on the value of the property and the upgrades you purchase. To maximize your earnings, invest in properties that generate the most income and upgrade them as much as possible.

Participate in Forzathon Live Events

Forzathon Live Events are multiplayer events that take place in Forza Horizon 4. They are held at specific times and locations and involve completing challenges with other players. Participating in Forzathon Live Events will earn you money, cars, and other prizes. To join a Forzathon Live Event, go to the Horizon Life menu and select the Forzathon Live option. Make sure to complete as many challenges as possible to earn the most rewards.

Complete Daily and Weekly Challenges

In addition to regular races and challenges, Forza Horizon 4 also features daily and weekly challenges. These challenges offer unique rewards and are a great way to earn money and other prizes. To access daily and weekly challenges, go to the Festival Playlist menu and select the challenges tab. Make sure to complete as many challenges as possible to maximize your earnings.

Use the Auction House to Buy Low and Sell High

In addition to selling cars in the Auction House, you can also buy cars from other players and resell them for a profit. Look for cars that are undervalued or in high demand and purchase them at a low price. Then, resell them for a higher price to earn a profit. This strategy requires some knowledge of the car market in Forza Horizon 4, but it can be a lucrative way to earn money.

Complete Horizon Stories and Showcases

Horizon Stories and Showcases are unique challenges that involve completing special tasks, such as jumping over a certain distance or racing against a train. Completing these challenges will earn you money and other rewards. To access Horizon Stories and Showcases, go to the Horizon Life menu and select the Stories and Showcases option. Make sure to complete as many as possible to earn the most rewards.

Conclusion

Earning money in Forza Horizon 4 can take some time and effort, but with the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can maximize your earnings and get the cars and upgrades you want. Remember to complete races and challenges, use Wheelspins and Super Wheelspins, sell cars in the Auction House, invest in properties, participate in Forzathon Live Events, complete daily and weekly challenges, and use the Auction House to buy low and sell high. With these strategies, you'll be raking in the credits in no time.
