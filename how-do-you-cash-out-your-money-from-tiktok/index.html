How To Cash Out Your Money From Tiktok In 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 02, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a TikTok creator, you're probably wondering how you can cash out your earnings from the platform. While TikTok doesn't have a direct cash out option, there are still several ways you can get paid for your content. In this article, we'll go over some of the most popular methods for cashing out on TikTok.

Method 1: Creator Fund

The TikTok Creator Fund is a program that pays eligible creators for their content. To be eligible, you need to meet certain criteria, such as having at least 100,000 followers and a certain amount of engagement on your videos. If you're eligible, you'll receive a monthly payment based on the number of views your videos receive.

To apply for the Creator Fund, go to your profile settings and select "Creator Fund." From there, you'll need to submit an application and wait for approval. Once you're approved, you'll start receiving payments directly to your account.

Method 2: Brand Deals

Another way to make money on TikTok is through brand deals. Brands are always looking for influencers to promote their products, and TikTok is no exception. If you have a large following and create engaging content, you may be able to secure brand deals with companies.

To find brand deals, you can reach out to companies directly or join influencer marketing platforms like TikTok Creator Marketplace or Tribe. These platforms connect brands with influencers, making it easier to find paid partnerships.

Method 3: Merchandise

If you have a loyal following on TikTok, you may be able to sell merchandise to your fans. This could include anything from t-shirts to stickers to phone cases. To sell merchandise, you'll need to set up an online store and promote your products on your TikTok videos.

There are several platforms you can use to sell merchandise, such as Teespring, Redbubble, or Shopify. Once you've set up your store, you can link to it in your TikTok bio or add it to your videos.

Method 4: Live Streams

TikTok also has a live streaming feature that allows creators to earn money from their streams. During a live stream, viewers can send virtual gifts to the creator, which can be converted into real money.

To enable live streaming, you'll need to have at least 1,000 followers and be at least 16 years old. Once you've met these requirements, you can start live streaming and earning money from your viewers.

Method 5: Donations

Finally, you can also receive donations from your fans on TikTok. This is similar to the live streaming feature, but it allows viewers to donate money to the creator at any time, not just during a live stream.

To enable donations, you'll need to have a PayPal account linked to your TikTok account. Once you have a PayPal account set up, viewers can send you money directly through the app.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are several ways to cash out your money from TikTok. Whether you're eligible for the Creator Fund, secure brand deals, sell merchandise, or earn money from live streams and donations, there are plenty of opportunities to make money on the platform. So keep creating great content, and you could be on your way to earning a living from TikTok.
