Easy Ways For Stay At Home Moms To Make Money In 2023

Written by Petter Sep 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Being a stay-at-home mom is a full-time job. But what if you want to make some extra money without leaving your home? Fortunately, there are many ways for stay-at-home moms to earn money in 2023. Here are some easy and legitimate ways to boost your income:

1. Start a Blog or Vlog

If you have a passion or expertise, you can start a blog or vlog and monetize it through ads, sponsored content, affiliate marketing, and product reviews. It may take time and effort to build an audience and generate income, but it can be a rewarding and flexible way to make money from home.

2. Sell Products Online

You can sell handmade crafts, vintage items, or new products on online marketplaces like Etsy, eBay, Amazon, or Facebook Marketplace. You can also create your own online store using platforms like Shopify or WooCommerce. You may need to invest in materials or inventory, but you can set your own prices and work on your own schedule.

3. Provide Services Online

You can offer your skills or knowledge as a freelancer or consultant on platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, or LinkedIn. You can offer services like writing, editing, graphic design, social media management, virtual assistance, tutoring, or coaching. You can set your own rates and clients, and work remotely from anywhere.

4. Participate in Surveys or Rewards Programs

You can sign up for online survey or rewards programs like Swagbucks, InboxDollars, or Survey Junkie, and earn points or cash for completing surveys, watching videos, playing games, or shopping online. You may not earn a lot of money, but it can be a fun and easy way to earn some extra cash or gift cards.

5. Rent Out Your Property or Space

If you have a spare room, garage, or backyard, you can rent it out on platforms like Airbnb, VRBO, or Turo. You can also rent out your car, bike, or other equipment on platforms like Getaround or Spinlister. You can earn money while sharing your assets with others.

6. Teach or Tutor Online

If you have teaching experience or expertise in a subject, you can teach or tutor online on platforms like VIPKid, Teachable, or Udemy. You can create your own courses or curriculum, or work for an online school or program. You can earn money while sharing your knowledge with students around the world.

7. Do Microtasks or Gig Jobs

You can do microtasks or gig jobs on platforms like TaskRabbit, Gigwalk, or Amazon Mechanical Turk. You can do small tasks like data entry, research, transcription, or delivery, and earn money per task or hour. You can work on your own schedule and choose the tasks that suit your skills and interests.

8. Invest or Trade Online

If you have some money to spare, you can invest or trade online on platforms like Robinhood, Acorns, or E*TRADE. You can buy stocks, bonds, or cryptocurrencies, and earn money through dividends, capital gains, or appreciation. You need to be aware of the risks and fees involved, and do your research before investing.

9. Drive or Deliver for Apps

If you have a car and a driver's license, you can drive or deliver for apps like Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, or Postmates. You can earn money by driving passengers or delivering food, groceries, or packages. You can work on your own schedule and choose the rides or deliveries that suit your preferences.

10. Network or Referral Online

If you have a large social network or good referral skills, you can earn money through network or referral programs like Amway, Avon, or Mary Kay. You can sell products or recruit others to sell products, and earn commissions or bonuses based on your sales or referrals. You need to be aware of the requirements and regulations involved, and choose a program that suits your values and goals.

Conclusion

These are just some of the easy ways for stay-at-home moms to make money in 2023. You can choose one or more of these options based on your skills, interests, and availability. You need to be proactive, persistent, and patient to succeed, but you can also enjoy the freedom, flexibility, and fulfillment of working from home.
