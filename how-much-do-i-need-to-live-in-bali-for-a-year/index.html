How Much Do I Need To Live In Bali For A Year

Written by Daniel Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Bali is a beautiful island in Indonesia that attracts millions of tourists every year. But have you ever wondered how much it would cost to live in Bali for a year? In this article, we will break down the expenses and give you an idea of how much you need to live in Bali for a year.

Housing Costs

The cost of housing in Bali varies depending on where you choose to live. If you want to live in a luxury villa, you will need to spend around $1,500 to $2,500 per month. But if you are on a budget, you can find a decent apartment for $300 to $500 per month. Keep in mind that prices may vary depending on the location and the amenities provided.

Food and Drinks

Bali has a variety of food options, from street food to high-end restaurants. If you are on a tight budget, you can survive on street food for as low as $1 per meal. But if you want to indulge in fine dining, expect to spend around $20 to $50 per meal. As for drinks, a bottle of beer costs around $1 to $3, while a cocktail costs around $5 to $10.

Transportation Costs

Getting around Bali can be cheap or expensive depending on your mode of transportation. If you want to explore the island, renting a scooter is the most cost-effective option, costing around $50 to $80 per month. But if you prefer to hire a driver, expect to pay around $30 to $50 per day.

Visa and Immigration Costs

If you plan to stay in Bali for a year, you will need a long-stay visa. The cost of the visa varies depending on your nationality, but it usually ranges from $300 to $500. You will also need to pay for a few other immigration fees, such as the visa extension fee and the exit permit fee.

Healthcare Costs

Bali has a good healthcare system, and there are a few international clinics and hospitals on the island. If you have health insurance, you can expect to pay around $30 to $50 for a doctor's visit. But if you do not have insurance, the cost can go up to $100 or more.

Entertainment and Activities

Bali offers a lot of activities and entertainment options, from yoga classes to surfing lessons. The cost of these activities varies depending on what you choose to do. For example, a yoga class can cost anywhere from $5 to $20, while a surfing lesson can cost around $30 to $50.

Utilities and Internet Costs

The cost of utilities and internet in Bali is relatively cheap. You can expect to pay around $30 to $50 per month for electricity, water, and internet. However, if you plan to use air conditioning, your bill may go up to $100 or more during the hot season.

Conclusion

Living in Bali for a year can be affordable or expensive, depending on your lifestyle choices. If you are on a tight budget, you can survive on $800 to $1,000 per month. But if you want to live comfortably and indulge in luxury, you will need to spend around $2,500 to $3,500 per month. Keep in mind that these prices are estimates and can vary depending on your lifestyle and preferences.

FAQs:

1. Can I find a job in Bali?

Yes, it is possible to find a job in Bali, but it can be challenging. Most jobs in Bali are related to tourism, such as hospitality, food, and beverage, or entertainment. However, the pay is relatively low compared to western standards.

2. Can I live in Bali without knowing the local language?

Yes, you can live in Bali without knowing the local language. Most Balinese people speak English, and it is the second language on the island. However, it is always recommended to learn a few words of Bahasa Indonesia to communicate with the locals.

3. Is Bali safe for foreigners?

Yes, Bali is generally safe for foreigners. However, like any other tourist destination, there is always a risk of petty crime, such as theft or pickpocketing. It is always recommended to be cautious and keep your valuables safe.
