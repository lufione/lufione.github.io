How To Make Money On Youtube With Google Adsense

Written by Petter Aug 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

YouTube is a platform that has become increasingly popular over the years, with many people using it to watch videos, learn, and be entertained. However, did you know that you can make money on YouTube with Google AdSense? In this article, we will guide you through the steps you need to take to start earning money on YouTube through AdSense.

First Things First: Create a YouTube Channel

Before you can start earning money on YouTube, you need to have a channel. If you already have a channel, great! Skip to the next section. If not, create one by going to the YouTube website and clicking on the "Create a Channel" button.

Once you have created your channel, start uploading videos that you think will resonate with your audience. You can create videos on a wide range of topics, from makeup tutorials to cooking demonstrations, to gaming videos.

Build Your Audience

Once you have started uploading videos, the next step is to build your audience. This is a crucial step, as you need to have a certain number of subscribers and views before you can start earning money through AdSense.

To build your audience, make sure that your videos are high-quality and engaging. You can also promote your videos on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Apply for Google AdSense

Once you have built your audience, it's time to apply for Google AdSense. Google Adsense is a program that allows you to earn money by displaying ads on your videos.

To apply for AdSense, go to the AdSense website and sign up for an account. Once you have signed up, link your YouTube channel to your AdSense account by following the instructions provided.

Create and Upload High-Quality Videos

To earn money on YouTube, you need to create and upload high-quality videos that people want to watch. Make sure that your videos are well-produced, engaging, and informative.

You can also optimize your videos for search engines by using keywords in your titles, descriptions, and tags. This will help your videos rank higher in search results, which will increase your views and subscribers.

Promote Your Videos

To increase your views and subscribers, you need to promote your videos. You can do this by sharing your videos on social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. You can also collaborate with other YouTubers in your niche to reach a wider audience.

Interact with Your Audience

To build a loyal audience, you need to interact with them. Respond to comments, ask for feedback, and engage with your viewers. This will help you build a community of loyal fans who will keep coming back to your channel.

Monitor Your Analytics

To improve your videos and increase your earnings, you need to monitor your analytics. This will give you insights into which videos are performing well, which ones are not, and where your viewers are coming from.

Use this information to make improvements to your videos and to target your audience more effectively.

Stay Consistent

To continue earning money on YouTube, you need to stay consistent. Upload videos regularly, engage with your audience, and keep improving your content. This will help you build a loyal audience and increase your earnings over time.

Conclusion

Making money on YouTube with Google AdSense is not easy, but it is possible. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can start earning money on YouTube and build a successful career as a YouTuber.
