How Does Youtube Pay Money In India

Written by Aprilia Oct 09, 2023 · 6 min read

YouTube is one of the most popular video-sharing platforms in the world. It has allowed content creators to showcase their talents and reach a vast audience. With the increasing popularity of the platform, many people are curious about how YouTube pays its creators in India. In this article, we will explore the different ways YouTube pays money to its creators in India.

Monetization on YouTube

The first step to earning money on YouTube is to monetize your channel. This means that you will allow YouTube to place advertisements on your videos. You can monetize your channel by joining the YouTube Partner Program (YPP). Once you join the YPP, you will be eligible to earn money from advertisements on your videos.

Ad Revenue Sharing

When you monetize your channel, YouTube will start placing ads on your videos. You will earn a share of the revenue generated by these ads. The amount you earn will depend on factors such as the number of views, the length of the video, and the type of ads displayed. In India, YouTube pays creators 55% of the revenue generated by ads on their videos.

Sponsorships and Brand Deals

Another way to earn money on YouTube is through sponsorships and brand deals. This involves partnering with a brand to promote their products or services in your videos. You can negotiate the terms of the sponsorship or brand deal, including the amount you will be paid. Many creators in India have earned significant amounts of money through sponsorships and brand deals.

Payment Methods

YouTube pays its creators in India through different payment methods. These include:

AdSense Account

YouTube pays its creators through an AdSense account. You will need to create an AdSense account and link it to your YouTube channel. AdSense is a program that allows you to earn money by displaying ads on your website or YouTube channel. Once you have linked your AdSense account to your YouTube channel, you will be able to receive payments from YouTube.

Wire Transfer

Creators in India can also receive payments through wire transfer. This involves transferring funds directly to your bank account. To receive payments through wire transfer, you will need to provide your bank account details to YouTube. Once your earnings reach the minimum payout threshold, YouTube will initiate the wire transfer.

PayPal

YouTube also offers PayPal as a payment method for creators in India. PayPal is an online payment system that allows you to send and receive money. To receive payments through PayPal, you will need to link your PayPal account to your AdSense account. Once your earnings reach the minimum payout threshold, YouTube will initiate the payment through PayPal.

Conclusion

Earning money on YouTube in India requires hard work, dedication, and patience. You need to create high-quality content that engages your audience and attracts advertisers. Monetizing your channel through the YouTube Partner Program is the first step to earning money on the platform. You can also explore other revenue streams, such as sponsorships and brand deals. With the right strategies and techniques, you can earn a significant amount of money on YouTube in India.
