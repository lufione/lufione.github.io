How To Make Money From Youtube Views In 2023

Written by Robby May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

YouTube has become one of the most popular platforms for sharing videos online. With over 2 billion active users, it is no surprise that many people are looking for ways to make money from their YouTube views. In this article, we will explore the different ways that you can use to monetize your YouTube channel and earn money from your video views.

1. Join the YouTube Partner Program

The YouTube Partner Program is the most common way to monetize your YouTube channel. To join the program, you need to have at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months. Once you are accepted, you can start earning money from ads that appear on your videos.

How to join the YouTube Partner Program:

To join the program, follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your YouTube account
  2. Click on your profile picture and select “YouTube Studio”
  3. On the left-hand side, click “Monetization”
  4. Follow the prompts to accept the terms of the YouTube Partner Program

2. Sell Merchandise

If you have a loyal fan base, you can sell merchandise related to your channel. You can sell t-shirts, mugs, stickers, and other products with your branding. You can use platforms like Teespring, Spreadshirt, or Redbubble to create and sell your merchandise.

How to sell merchandise on YouTube:

To sell merchandise on YouTube, follow these steps:

  1. Create your merchandise on a platform like Teespring or Redbubble
  2. Add your merchandise to your YouTube channel
  3. Promote your merchandise in your videos and on your social media channels

3. Get Sponsored

If you have a large following, you can get sponsored by brands that want to reach your audience. You can promote their products in your videos or create sponsored content for them. You can use platforms like FameBit or Grapevine to find sponsorships.

How to get sponsored on YouTube:

To get sponsored on YouTube, follow these steps:

  1. Create a media kit that includes your channel statistics, demographics, and pricing
  2. Sign up for a platform like FameBit or Grapevine
  3. Browse available sponsorships and apply for the ones that fit your channel
  4. Create sponsored content for the brands that sponsor you

4. Offer Consulting or Coaching Services

If you have expertise in a specific area, you can offer consulting or coaching services to your viewers. For example, if you have a fitness channel, you can offer personal training services. You can use platforms like Clarity.fm or Coach.me to offer your services.

How to offer consulting or coaching services on YouTube:

To offer consulting or coaching services on YouTube, follow these steps:

  1. Create a media kit that includes your expertise and pricing
  2. Sign up for a platform like Clarity.fm or Coach.me
  3. Promote your services in your videos and on your social media channels

5. Crowdfunding

Crowdfunding is a way to fund your projects by getting support from your viewers. You can use platforms like Patreon or Kickstarter to set up a crowdfunding campaign. In exchange for their support, you can offer rewards like exclusive content or merchandise.

How to set up a crowdfunding campaign on YouTube:

To set up a crowdfunding campaign on YouTube, follow these steps:

  1. Create a crowdfunding campaign on a platform like Patreon or Kickstarter
  2. Add your campaign to your YouTube channel and promote it in your videos
  3. Offer rewards for supporters, like exclusive content or merchandise

Conclusion

These are the five ways that you can use to make money from your YouTube views in 2023. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced YouTuber, there is a way for you to monetize your channel and earn money from your videos.
