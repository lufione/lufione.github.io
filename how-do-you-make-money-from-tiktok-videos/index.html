How To Make Money From Tiktok Videos In 2023

Written by Oliver Sep 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Introduction

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world. With over a billion active users, it has become a great way for people to share their creativity and make a name for themselves. But did you know that you can also make money from TikTok? In this article, we will guide you through the process of making money from TikTok videos in 2023.

1. Build Your Following

The first step to making money on TikTok is to build a following. You need to create high-quality content that resonates with your audience. You can use trending hashtags, collaborate with other TikTokers, and engage with your followers to increase your reach. The more followers you have, the more money you can make.

2. Use TikTok Creator Fund

The TikTok Creator Fund is a program that pays creators for their content. To be eligible for the Creator Fund, you must be at least 18 years old, have at least 100,000 followers, and have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days. If you meet these requirements, you can apply for the Creator Fund and start earning money from your videos.

3. Partner with Brands

Another way to make money on TikTok is by partnering with brands. If you have a large following, brands may reach out to you to promote their products or services. You can also reach out to brands that align with your niche and pitch your services to them. Make sure to disclose any sponsored content to your followers.

4. Sell Merchandise

If you have built a loyal following on TikTok, you can sell merchandise to your fans. You can create products that align with your brand and sell them through your TikTok profile or other e-commerce platforms. Merchandise can range from t-shirts and stickers to digital products like e-books and courses.

5. Join the TikTok Affiliate Program

The TikTok Affiliate Program is a new way to make money on the platform. It allows creators to earn commissions by promoting products from participating brands. To join the program, you must be at least 18 years old and have at least 100,000 followers. You can apply for the program through the TikTok app.

6. Offer Your Services

If you have a particular skill or talent, you can offer your services on TikTok. For example, if you are a makeup artist, you can offer makeup tutorials and consultations to your followers. You can charge for your services through your TikTok profile or through other platforms like Fiverr or Upwork.

7. Create Sponsored Content

Sponsored content is another way to make money on TikTok. Brands may pay you to create a video that promotes their product or service. Make sure to disclose any sponsored content to your followers to maintain their trust.

8. Participate in TikTok Challenges

TikTok challenges are a great way to gain exposure on the platform. You can participate in challenges that align with your niche and create high-quality content. If your video goes viral, you can gain more followers and potentially make money from sponsored content or merchandise sales.

9. Use TikTok Live

TikTok Live is a feature that allows you to stream live videos to your followers. You can use TikTok Live to interact with your followers, showcase your skills, or promote your merchandise. You can also receive gifts from your followers during your live streams, which can be converted into real money.

10. Conclusion

In conclusion, TikTok has become a great platform for creators to make money. By building a following, using the Creator Fund, partnering with brands, selling merchandise, joining the TikTok Affiliate Program, offering services, creating sponsored content, participating in challenges, and using TikTok Live, you can monetize your TikTok videos in 2023. Remember to create high-quality content, engage with your followers, and be transparent about any sponsored content to maintain your credibility on the platform.
