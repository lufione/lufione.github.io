How To Make Money As A Teenager In Quarantine

Written by Oliver May 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, many teenagers find themselves with extra time on their hands due to quarantine measures. While it may seem like a challenging time, there are actually plenty of opportunities to make money from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways for teenagers to make money during quarantine.

Sell Items Online

If you have unwanted items lying around your home, consider selling them online. Websites like eBay and Amazon make it easy to sell everything from clothes to electronics. You can also try selling items on specialized platforms like Etsy for handmade goods or Poshmark for clothing.

Start a Blog or Vlog

If you have a talent for writing or a passion for video, consider starting a blog or vlog. You can create content on a wide range of topics, from beauty and fashion to gaming and music. Once you have a following, you can monetize your blog or vlog through advertising and sponsorships.

Offer Online Services

If you have a particular skill or talent, consider offering your services online. For example, if you're a talented writer, you can offer freelance writing services. If you're good at graphic design, you can offer logo design services. Websites like Fiverr and Upwork make it easy to find clients and get paid for your work.

Complete Online Surveys

There are many websites that offer cash or gift cards in exchange for completing online surveys. While the pay is usually low, it's an easy way to make some extra money in your spare time. Some popular survey websites include Swagbucks and Survey Junkie.

Sell Crafts or Artwork

If you're artistic or crafty, consider selling your creations online. You can sell everything from paintings and sculptures to handmade jewelry and home decor. Websites like Etsy and Redbubble make it easy to set up a shop and start selling your creations.

Babysit or Pet-Sit

If you're good with kids or animals, consider offering babysitting or pet-sitting services. Many parents and pet owners are looking for help during quarantine, and you can make some extra money while providing a valuable service. Websites like Care.com and Rover make it easy to find clients in your area.

Deliver Food and Groceries

With many people staying home during quarantine, there's a high demand for delivery drivers. You can sign up to deliver food through apps like Uber Eats or DoorDash, or sign up to deliver groceries through services like Instacart.

Tutor Online

If you're good at a particular subject, consider offering online tutoring services. Many students are struggling with online learning during quarantine, and you can help them succeed while making some extra money. Websites like Chegg and Tutor.com make it easy to find clients and get paid for your work.

Participate in Online Contests

There are many online contests that offer cash prizes or gift cards. You can participate in everything from writing contests to photography contests. While the odds of winning may be low, it's a fun way to make some extra money and showcase your talents. Websites like Contests2Win and Creative Writing Contests make it easy to find contests to participate in.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are many ways for teenagers to make money during quarantine. Whether you choose to sell items online, offer online services, or participate in online contests, there are plenty of opportunities to earn some extra cash. With a little creativity and hard work, you can turn quarantine into a profitable opportunity.
