How To Get A Live Wallpaper From Tiktok

Written by Oliver Jul 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Live wallpapers have become a popular trend among smartphone users, and TikTok users are not left behind. TikTok's community of talented creators has produced a vast collection of live wallpapers that can make your phone screen look stunning. In this article, we will show you how to get a live wallpaper from TikTok in just a few simple steps.

Step 1: Find a Live Wallpaper on TikTok

The first step is to browse through TikTok and find a live wallpaper that you like. You can use the search bar or explore page to find live wallpapers related to your interests. Once you find a live wallpaper that you like, click on the share button located at the bottom right of the video.

Step 2: Save the Live Wallpaper Video

After clicking on the share button, you will see several options. Click on the "Save Video" option to download the live wallpaper video to your phone. The video will be saved in your phone's gallery.

Step 3: Download a Live Wallpaper App

Now that you have the live wallpaper video saved on your phone, you need to download a live wallpaper app. There are several live wallpaper apps available on the Google Play Store and App Store, such as Live Wallpaper 4K and Walloop. Choose the app that you prefer and download it to your phone.

Step 4: Open the Live Wallpaper App

Once you have downloaded the live wallpaper app, open it and navigate to the "Settings" or "Preferences" section. Here, you will find an option to select a video as your live wallpaper. Click on this option and select the live wallpaper video that you saved earlier from your phone's gallery.

Step 5: Set the Live Wallpaper

After selecting the live wallpaper video, you can customize it by adjusting settings such as the speed and size. Once you are satisfied with the customizations, click on the "Set as Wallpaper" option to make the live wallpaper your phone's background.

FAQ

Can I use any video on TikTok as a live wallpaper?

No, not all videos on TikTok can be used as live wallpapers. Only videos that are shot in a vertical format and have a resolution of at least 1080p can be used as live wallpapers.

Do I need to root my phone to use a live wallpaper?

No, you do not need to root your phone to use a live wallpaper. Most live wallpaper apps are compatible with non-rooted phones.

Can I create my own live wallpaper on TikTok?

Yes, you can create your own live wallpaper on TikTok by shooting a video in a vertical format and with a resolution of at least 1080p. Once you have saved the video, follow the steps mentioned in this article to turn it into a live wallpaper.

Tips and Tricks

If you want to make your live wallpaper even more personalized, you can use editing tools such as Canva or Adobe Spark to add text, stickers, or filters to the video before setting it as your live wallpaper.

Make sure to choose a live wallpaper that is not too distracting or bright, as it can drain your phone's battery faster. Also, avoid using live wallpapers on phones with low processing power, as it can slow down your device.

Finally, if you have any issues with the live wallpaper app or the live wallpaper itself, try restarting your phone or clearing the app's cache to see if it resolves the issue.
