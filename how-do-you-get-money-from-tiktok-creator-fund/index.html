How To Get Money From Tiktok Creator Fund In 2023

Written by Robby Sep 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

TikTok has become a popular platform for creators to showcase their talents and reach a massive audience. With 1 billion active users, it is no surprise that many creators are looking to monetize their content. One of the ways to do this is through the TikTok Creator Fund. Here's how you can get money from it.

What is TikTok Creator Fund?

TikTok Creator Fund is a program that pays eligible creators for their content. The fund was launched in 2020 to support creators and encourage them to continue creating content. TikTok allocates a portion of its advertising revenue to the fund, which is then distributed to creators who meet certain criteria.

Who is Eligible for TikTok Creator Fund?

To be eligible for the TikTok Creator Fund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You must have at least 100,000 followers
  • You must have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days
  • You must comply with TikTok's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service

How to Apply for TikTok Creator Fund?

If you meet the eligibility criteria, you can apply for the TikTok Creator Fund through the app. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to your profile page
  2. Tap on the three dots in the top right corner
  3. Select "Creator Fund"
  4. Review the terms and conditions
  5. Tap "Sign up"

Once you have signed up, TikTok will review your application and notify you of your acceptance status within a few weeks.

How Much Money Can You Make from TikTok Creator Fund?

The amount of money you can make from the TikTok Creator Fund varies based on several factors, such as the number of views, engagement, and location. TikTok pays creators based on their performance and the amount of money allocated to the fund. While there is no guarantee of a specific amount, some creators have reported earning thousands of dollars per month.

How to Maximize Your Earnings from TikTok Creator Fund?

If you want to maximize your earnings from the TikTok Creator Fund, here are some tips:

  • Create engaging content that resonates with your audience
  • Post consistently
  • Use trending hashtags and challenges
  • Collaborate with other creators
  • Promote your TikTok account on other social media platforms

When Will You Get Paid from TikTok Creator Fund?

TikTok pays creators on a monthly basis. The payments are processed around the 21st of each month for the previous month's earnings. For example, if you earned $500 in January, you will receive the payment around the 21st of February.

What Else Should You Know about TikTok Creator Fund?

Here are some additional things you should know about the TikTok Creator Fund:

  • The fund is not available in all countries
  • Creators can earn money from both the Creator Fund and brand partnerships
  • TikTok reserves the right to change the eligibility criteria and payment structure

Conclusion

The TikTok Creator Fund is a great way for eligible creators to monetize their content and earn money. By following the eligibility criteria and best practices, you can increase your chances of getting accepted and maximizing your earnings. Remember to create engaging content and stay consistent with your posts to keep your audience engaged.
