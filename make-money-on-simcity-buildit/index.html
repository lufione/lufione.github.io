How To Make Money On Simcity Buildit

Written by Eveline Jul 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

SimCity BuildIt is a popular city-building game that allows players to create and manage their own virtual cities. While the game is free to play, players can spend real money to purchase in-game currency or items. However, there are ways to make money in the game without spending a dime. Here are some tips and tricks to help you make money on SimCity BuildIt.

1. Build and Upgrade Residential Buildings

Residential buildings generate income for your city, so it's important to build and upgrade them regularly. The more residents you have, the more money you'll make. Build a variety of different types of housing to attract different types of residents and maximize your income.

2. Sell Excess Items on the Global Trade HQ

If you have excess items like building materials or factory products, you can sell them on the Global Trade HQ to other players. This is a great way to make some extra money and clear out your inventory. Keep an eye on the market to see what items are in demand and sell accordingly.

3. Complete Cargo Shipments

The cargo shipments that arrive at your dock can be completed for a reward. These rewards can include cash, items, or even special buildings. Make sure to complete these shipments as often as possible to maximize your income.

4. Participate in Contests and Events

SimCity BuildIt regularly hosts contests and events that offer rewards for participating. These rewards can include cash, items, or even premium currency. Make sure to participate in these events to earn some extra money and exclusive items.

5. Build and Upgrade Commercial Buildings

Commercial buildings generate income in the form of taxes. Make sure to build and upgrade a variety of different commercial buildings to attract different types of businesses and maximize your income. Keep an eye on the demand for goods and services in your city to know which types of businesses to build.

6. Connect with Friends

Connecting with friends in the game can offer benefits like receiving gifts or borrowing items. Make sure to connect with other players to take advantage of these benefits and save some money.

7. Save and Invest

It's important to save your money and invest in your city wisely. Don't spend money on unnecessary items or upgrades. Instead, focus on building a sustainable and profitable city that will generate income in the long run.

8. Use Specializations

Specializations like education, transportation, and entertainment can offer bonuses to your city's income. Make sure to build and upgrade these specializations to take advantage of their benefits.

9. Watch Ads for Rewards

SimCity BuildIt offers rewards for watching ads in the game. These rewards can include cash, items, or even premium currency. Make sure to take advantage of this feature to earn some extra money.

10. Don't Give Up

Making money in SimCity BuildIt takes time and patience. Don't give up if you're not seeing results right away. Keep building and upgrading your city, and eventually, you'll start to see the rewards of your hard work.
