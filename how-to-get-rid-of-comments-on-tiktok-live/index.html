How To Get Rid Of Comments On Tiktok Live

Written by Daniel Aug 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

TikTok is one of the most popular social media platforms today, allowing users to create and share short videos. However, some users may find themselves overwhelmed by the comments they receive during their TikTok live streams. If you're looking for a way to get rid of comments on TikTok live, read on for some tips and tricks.

Why Get Rid of Comments?

While comments can be a great way to interact with your audience and build a following, they can also be a source of stress and negativity. If you find yourself struggling to keep up with comments during your live streams, or if you're dealing with trolls and haters, getting rid of comments may be the best option for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps you can take to get rid of comments on TikTok live:

Step 1: Open the TikTok App

First, open the TikTok app on your mobile device.

Step 2: Go to the Live Stream

Go to the live stream where you want to turn off comments.

Step 3: Tap on the Three Dots

Tap on the three dots in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Step 4: Select "Turn Off Commenting"

Select "Turn Off Commenting" from the options that appear.

Step 5: Confirm the Action

Confirm that you want to turn off commenting for the live stream.

Step 6: Enjoy Your Comment-Free Live Stream

Once you've turned off commenting, you can enjoy your live stream without having to worry about dealing with comments.

FAQ

Can I Turn Comments Back On?

Yes, you can turn comments back on at any time during your live stream. Simply follow the same steps outlined above and select "Turn On Commenting" instead.

What Happens to Existing Comments?

Existing comments will still be visible to you and other viewers, but no new comments will be allowed.

Will I Lose Followers?

It's unlikely that you'll lose followers simply for turning off comments. If anything, it may make your live streams more enjoyable for your audience, which could lead to more followers in the long run.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for dealing with comments on TikTok live:

1. Use Moderators

Consider adding moderators to your live stream to help you manage comments and keep things positive.

2. Set Ground Rules

Make it clear to your audience what kind of comments are acceptable and what kind are not. Encourage positivity and discourage negativity.

3. Take Breaks

If you're feeling overwhelmed by comments, take a break from the live stream to regroup and refresh.

4. Report Inappropriate Comments

If you encounter comments that are inappropriate or violate TikTok's community guidelines, report them to the platform.

Conclusion

Getting rid of comments on TikTok live can be a great way to reduce stress and negativity during your live streams. By following the steps outlined above and using the tips and tricks provided, you can create a more enjoyable live streaming experience for yourself and your audience.
