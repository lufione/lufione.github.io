How to Make Money During Covid as a Teenager

The Covid-19 pandemic has impacted everyone in many ways, especially financially. As a teenager, it may seem difficult to make money during these challenging times. However, there are several ways to earn money and make the most of your time. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks for how to make money during Covid as a teenager.

1. Online Tutoring

With schools being closed due to the pandemic, many students are struggling to keep up with their studies. As a teenager, you can offer your services as an online tutor. You can help younger students with their homework or even offer test preparation services. There are many online tutoring platforms available, such as TutorMe and Chegg, that can help you find students.

2. Freelance Writing

If you enjoy writing, you can offer your services as a freelance writer. Many businesses are in need of content for their websites and social media platforms. You can also write blog posts or articles for websites such as Medium or HubPages. You can find freelance writing opportunities on websites such as Upwork and Fiverr.

3. Virtual Assistant

Many businesses are in need of virtual assistants to help with administrative tasks such as data entry, email management, and scheduling appointments. As a teenager, you can offer your services as a virtual assistant. You can find virtual assistant opportunities on websites such as Indeed and Freelancer.

4. Online Surveys

Completing online surveys is an easy way to make money as a teenager. There are several websites that pay you to complete surveys, such as Swagbucks and Survey Junkie. While the pay may not be substantial, it can be a good way to earn some extra cash in your free time.

5. Sell Handmade Items

If you are crafty, you can make handmade items such as jewelry or clothing and sell them online. Websites such as Etsy and Handmade by Amazon are great places to sell handmade items. You can also sell your items on social media platforms such as Instagram and Facebook.

6. Pet Sitting or Dog Walking

If you love animals, you can offer your services as a pet sitter or dog walker. Many pet owners are in need of someone to care for their pets while they are away or to take their dogs for walks. You can find pet sitting and dog walking opportunities on websites such as Rover and Care.com.

7. Social Media Management

If you are familiar with social media platforms such as Instagram and Twitter, you can offer your services as a social media manager. Many businesses are in need of someone to manage their social media accounts and create content. You can find social media management opportunities on websites such as Freelancer and Guru.

8. Sell Unused Items

If you have unused items such as clothes, electronics, or books, you can sell them online. Websites such as eBay and Amazon are great places to sell unused items. You can also sell your items on social media platforms such as Facebook Marketplace.

9. Babysitting

If you enjoy spending time with children, you can offer your services as a babysitter. Many parents are in need of someone to care for their children while they are away. You can find babysitting opportunities on websites such as Care.com and Sittercity.

10. Delivery Services

With more people staying at home due to the pandemic, there is an increased demand for delivery services. You can offer your services as a delivery driver for companies such as DoorDash and Uber Eats. You can also offer your services as a courier for local businesses.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money during Covid as a teenager. By offering your services online, selling unused items, or providing services such as pet sitting or babysitting, you can earn money and make the most of your time during these challenging times.
