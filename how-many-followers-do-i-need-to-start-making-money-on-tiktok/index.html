How Many Followers Do I Need To Start Making Money On Tiktok

Written by Robby May 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms around the world, attracting millions of users every day. Many people have started to make money from TikTok, and it has become a lucrative platform for content creators. But the question that many people ask is, how many followers do I need to start making money on TikTok?

Understanding TikTok Monetization

To make money on TikTok, you need to participate in the TikTok Creator Fund. The Creator Fund is a program that pays content creators for their videos, and it is available to users who meet specific eligibility criteria. To be eligible for the Creator Fund, you need to have at least 100,000 followers, and your videos must have received at least 100,000 views in the last 30 days.

Getting Started on TikTok

When you first start on TikTok, it can be challenging to grow your following, but it's not impossible. To grow your following, you need to create engaging content that resonates with your target audience. It would help if you also used popular hashtags and collaborated with other TikTok users to increase your visibility.

How Many Followers Do You Need?

As mentioned earlier, you need at least 100,000 followers to be eligible for the TikTok Creator Fund. However, this does not mean that you cannot make money on TikTok if you have fewer followers. Many TikTok users with smaller followings have found ways to monetize their content, such as sponsored posts, affiliate marketing, and merchandise sales.

Ways to Monetize Your TikTok Account

Aside from the TikTok Creator Fund, there are many ways to monetize your TikTok account. One of the most popular ways is through sponsored posts. Brands and businesses are always looking for influencers to promote their products and services on social media platforms, and TikTok is no exception.

Affiliate Marketing

Another way to monetize your TikTok account is through affiliate marketing. Affiliate marketing involves promoting a product or service and getting a commission for every sale made through your unique link. You can promote products on your TikTok account and include your affiliate link in the description.

Merchandise Sales

You can also make money on TikTok by selling merchandise. If you have a loyal following, you can create your own merchandise and sell it on your TikTok account. You can sell anything from t-shirts to phone cases, and your followers can purchase them directly from your account.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the number of followers you need to start making money on TikTok is 100,000. However, this does not mean that you cannot monetize your TikTok account if you have fewer followers. There are many ways to make money on TikTok, such as sponsored posts, affiliate marketing, and merchandise sales. The key to success on TikTok is creating engaging content and building a loyal following.
