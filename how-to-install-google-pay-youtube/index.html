How To Install Google Pay On Youtube In 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Google Pay is a payment service that allows you to make payments and send money to other people. It is also integrated with YouTube, which means you can use it to make purchases within the platform. In this article, we will show you how to install Google Pay on YouTube in 2023.

Step 1: Check Compatibility

Before you install Google Pay on YouTube, you need to check if your device is compatible. Google Pay is available on Android and iOS devices, but it may not be available in your country. To check compatibility, go to the Google Pay website and select your device and country.

Step 2: Download the Google Pay App

To install Google Pay on YouTube, you need to download the app first. You can download the app from the Google Play Store for Android devices or the App Store for iOS devices. Once you have downloaded the app, you need to open it and sign in with your Google account.

Step 3: Link Your Payment Method

After signing in to the Google Pay app, you need to link your payment method. You can link your credit or debit card or choose to link your bank account. To link your payment method, follow the instructions in the app.

Step 4: Open YouTube

Once you have linked your payment method, you need to open YouTube. If you have the YouTube app installed on your device, open it. If not, you can open YouTube in your web browser. Sign in to your YouTube account if you haven't already.

Step 5: Select a Video to Purchase

After opening YouTube, select a video that you want to purchase. You will see a "Buy" button next to the video. Click on the "Buy" button to proceed with the purchase.

Step 6: Select Google Pay as Your Payment Method

When you click on the "Buy" button, you will be prompted to select a payment method. Choose Google Pay as your payment method. You will be redirected to the Google Pay app to complete the payment process.

Step 7: Confirm Your Purchase

After you are redirected to the Google Pay app, you need to confirm your purchase. Verify the details of your purchase, including the amount and payment method. If everything is correct, click on the "Confirm" button to complete the purchase.

Step 8: Enjoy Your Video

After completing the purchase, you will be redirected back to YouTube. You can now enjoy your video without any interruptions. The purchase will also be saved to your account, so you can watch the video again later if you want.

FAQs

Can I use Google Pay on YouTube without installing the app?

No, you need to install the Google Pay app to use it on YouTube. The app is required to complete the payment process.

Is Google Pay available in my country?

You need to check the Google Pay website to see if it is available in your country. If it is not available, you won't be able to use it on YouTube.

Can I use Google Pay to purchase any video on YouTube?

No, not all videos on YouTube are available for purchase. Only certain videos are available for purchase, and you will see a "Buy" button next to them.

Is it safe to use Google Pay on YouTube?

Yes, Google Pay is a secure payment method, and your payment information is encrypted and protected.

Tips and Tricks

Make sure you have a stable internet connection when using Google Pay on YouTube. If the connection is unstable, the payment process may be interrupted.

Always check the details of your purchase before confirming it. Make sure the amount and payment method are correct.

Keep your payment information up to date to avoid any payment issues.
