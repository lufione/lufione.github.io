How To Earn Money In Gcash By Watching Tiktok Videos

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

Introduction

Do you love scrolling through TikTok? Did you know that you can earn money while doing it? Gcash, a mobile wallet in the Philippines, has partnered with TikTok to give users the opportunity to earn cash by watching videos. In this article, we'll show you how to make money on Gcash by watching TikTok videos.

What is Gcash?

Gcash is a mobile wallet app in the Philippines that allows its users to pay for goods and services, transfer money, and even invest. It's a convenient way to do financial transactions without the need for physical cash.

What is TikTok?

TikTok is a social media app that allows users to create and share short videos, ranging from 15 to 60 seconds. It has become a popular platform for entertainment, especially for the younger generation.

How to Start Earning Money in Gcash by Watching TikTok Videos

To start earning money in Gcash by watching TikTok videos, follow these steps:

Step 1: Download the Gcash app

If you haven't already, download the Gcash app on your phone. It's available on both the App Store and Google Play.

Step 2: Sign up or log in to your Gcash account

If you're a new user, sign up for a Gcash account. If you already have an account, log in.

Step 3: Link your TikTok account to Gcash

In the Gcash app, go to the "Cashing In" tab and select "TikTok." Follow the instructions to link your TikTok account to Gcash.

Step 4: Watch TikTok videos

Once your TikTok account is linked to Gcash, you can start watching videos. For every minute you watch, you'll earn a certain amount of cash.

Step 5: Cash out your earnings

When you've earned enough cash, you can cash out your earnings through the Gcash app. You can transfer it to your bank account or use it to pay for goods and services.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to maximize your earnings in Gcash by watching TikTok videos: - Watch as many videos as you can to earn more cash. - Take breaks in between watching videos to avoid eye strain. - Make sure your TikTok account is public so Gcash can track your viewing time. - Follow popular TikTok creators to increase your chances of earning more cash.

FAQ

How much can I earn by watching TikTok videos on Gcash?

The amount you can earn depends on how long you watch the videos. Gcash pays a certain amount for every minute of video watched.

Do I need a TikTok account to earn money on Gcash?

Yes, you need a TikTok account linked to your Gcash account to start earning money.

Can I cash out my earnings immediately?

No, you need to earn a certain amount before you can cash out your earnings. Check the Gcash app for the minimum cash out amount.

Conclusion

Earning money while watching TikTok videos is a great way to make use of your free time. With Gcash, it's easy and convenient to earn cash while enjoying entertaining content. Follow our steps and tips to start earning money in Gcash by watching TikTok videos today!
