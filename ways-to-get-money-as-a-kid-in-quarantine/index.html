Ways To Get Money As A Kid In Quarantine

Written by Luffy Apr 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought many changes to our lives, especially for kids who are now stuck at home due to quarantine measures. While this may seem like a boring or unproductive time, there are actually many ways for kids to make money while staying safe at home. Here are some tips on how to earn some extra cash during quarantine.

Sell Unwanted Items

One of the easiest ways to make money is by selling items that you no longer need or use. This could be clothes, toys, books, or other items that are in good condition. You can sell these items online through websites like eBay or Amazon, or even on social media platforms like Facebook or Instagram.

Offer Online Tutoring

With schools closed, many kids are struggling to keep up with their studies. If you excel in a particular subject, you can offer online tutoring services to other students. You can advertise your services on social media or tutoring websites like TutorMe or Chegg.

Do Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay you to complete surveys or participate in market research. While the pay may not be high, it can add up over time. Some popular survey websites include Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, and Vindale Research.

Start a YouTube Channel

If you have a talent or interest that you're passionate about, consider starting a YouTube channel. You can make money through ad revenue, sponsorships, or by selling merchandise related to your channel. It's important to remember that building a successful YouTube channel takes time and effort, but it can be a fun and rewarding experience.

Offer Pet-Sitting Services

Many people are still working during quarantine and may need someone to take care of their pets while they're away. If you love animals, consider offering pet-sitting services. You can advertise your services on social media or through local pet stores.

Create and Sell Crafts

If you're artistic or crafty, you can create and sell your own crafts online. This could include jewelry, clothing, or home decor items. You can sell your creations on websites like Etsy or even on your own website or social media page.

Offer Lawn Care Services

With spring approaching, many people will need help with lawn care. If you have access to a lawnmower and other lawn care equipment, you can offer your services to neighbors or advertise online.

Sell Homemade Baked Goods

If you love to bake, you can sell your homemade goods to friends, family, and neighbors. You can also advertise your services online or through social media. Just make sure to follow all local health and safety regulations when selling food items.

Offer Virtual Babysitting

With parents working from home, they may need someone to keep their kids occupied and entertained. You can offer virtual babysitting services through video chat platforms like Zoom or Skype.

Participate in Paid Focus Groups

Many companies conduct focus groups to get feedback on their products or services. You can participate in these focus groups online and get paid for your time. Websites like UserTesting and TryMyUI offer paid focus group opportunities.

Conclusion

Quarantine doesn't have to be a time of boredom and unproductivity. By using these tips, kids can earn some extra cash while staying safe at home. Whether you're selling unwanted items or starting your own YouTube channel, there are many ways to make money during quarantine.
