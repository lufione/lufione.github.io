Can You Get Youtube Without Ads

Written by Luffy Jun 03, 2023 · 6 min read

YouTube is one of the most popular platforms for watching videos online. However, one of the biggest drawbacks of using YouTube is the presence of ads. Ads can be frustrating, especially when you're in the middle of watching a video. So, is it possible to get YouTube without ads? The answer is yes! In this article, we'll show you how to enjoy your favorite videos on YouTube without ads.

Option 1: YouTube Premium

The easiest way to get YouTube without ads is to subscribe to YouTube Premium. YouTube Premium is a paid subscription service that offers several benefits, including ad-free videos, background play, and offline viewing. You can sign up for YouTube Premium on the YouTube website or app. The cost of YouTube Premium varies depending on your location, but it's typically around $11.99 per month.

Option 2: Use an Ad Blocker

If you don't want to pay for YouTube Premium, you can use an ad blocker to remove ads from YouTube. An ad blocker is a browser extension that blocks ads from appearing on websites. There are several ad blockers available, such as Adblock Plus, uBlock Origin, and AdGuard. To use an ad blocker, simply install the extension on your browser and enable it. Keep in mind that some ad blockers may not work on YouTube, so you may need to try a few before finding one that works for you.

Option 3: Watch YouTube Videos on Other Platforms

If you're not a fan of YouTube's ads, you can always watch videos on other platforms. There are several websites and apps that offer video content without ads, such as Vimeo, Dailymotion, and Twitch. Keep in mind that these platforms may not have the same amount of content as YouTube, but they offer an ad-free experience.

FAQs

Why does YouTube have ads?

YouTube has ads because it's a free platform. Ads help YouTube generate revenue, which allows them to provide free video content to users. Without ads, YouTube would have to charge users for access to videos.

Can I skip ads on YouTube?

Some ads on YouTube allow you to skip them after a few seconds. However, not all ads have this option. If you have YouTube Premium or an ad blocker, you won't see any ads at all.

Is it legal to use an ad blocker on YouTube?

Using an ad blocker on YouTube is not illegal. However, it's against YouTube's terms of service. If YouTube catches you using an ad blocker, they may restrict your access to the platform.

Tips and Tricks

If you're using an ad blocker, make sure to keep it updated. Ad blockers need to be updated regularly to ensure they're blocking the latest ads. Additionally, if you're using an ad blocker on YouTube, make sure to whitelist channels and creators you want to support. Ad blockers can hurt content creators by blocking their ads and reducing their revenue.

In conclusion, there are several ways to get YouTube without ads. You can subscribe to YouTube Premium, use an ad blocker, or watch videos on other platforms. Whatever option you choose, make sure to support the creators you love by whitelisting their channels or subscribing to their content.
