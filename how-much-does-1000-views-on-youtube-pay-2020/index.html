How Much Does 1000 Views On Youtube Pay In 2020

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a content creator on YouTube and wondering how much money you can make from 1000 views? Well, the answer isn't as simple as you might think. In this article, we'll delve into the various factors that determine your YouTube earnings and give you an estimate of how much you can expect to earn from 1000 views.

The Factors That Affect Your Earnings

Before we get into the numbers, it's important to understand the factors that affect your YouTube earnings. Here are some of the most important ones:

Ad Formats

There are different ad formats on YouTube, such as skippable and non-skippable ads, display ads, and overlay ads. Each ad format pays differently, so the more ads you have on your videos, the more money you can make.

Ad Engagement

Your earnings also depend on how engaged your viewers are with the ads. If they skip the ads or use ad-blockers, you won't make as much money as you would if they watch the ads in full.

Geography

The location of your viewers also affects your earnings. Ads in some countries pay more than in others, so if you have a large audience in a high-paying country like the US, you can expect to make more money.

Industry

The industry that your content belongs to also affects your earnings. Some industries like finance, technology, and health pay more for ads, so if your content falls under these categories, you can expect to earn more.

How Much Do YouTubers Make per 1000 Views?

The average earnings per 1000 views on YouTube is around $1 to $5. However, this can vary widely depending on the factors we mentioned earlier. Some YouTubers have reported earning as little as $0.10 per 1000 views, while others have earned up to $10.

It's also important to note that YouTube takes a cut of your earnings. They keep 45% of the revenue generated from ads, while you get 55%. This means that if you earn $2 per 1000 views, you'll only get to keep $1.10.

How to Increase Your YouTube Earnings

If you want to make more money from your YouTube videos, here are some tips and tricks:

Create Engaging Content

The more engaged your viewers are with your content, the more likely they are to watch the ads and help you make money. Make sure your content is entertaining, informative, and valuable to your audience.

Promote Your Videos

The more views you have on your videos, the more money you can make. Promote your videos on social media, collaborate with other YouTubers, and use SEO to get more views.

Diversify Your Income Streams

Don't rely solely on ad revenue to make money from your YouTube channel. You can also earn money through sponsorships, merchandise sales, and crowdfunding.

Conclusion

Earning money from YouTube isn't as straightforward as you might think. Your earnings depend on various factors such as ad formats, engagement, geography, and industry. However, if you create engaging content, promote your videos, and diversify your income streams, you can increase your earnings and make a living from your YouTube channel.
