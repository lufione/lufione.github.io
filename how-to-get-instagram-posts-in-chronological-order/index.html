How to Get Instagram Posts in Chronological Order

Instagram has undergone numerous changes since its inception in 2010. One of the most significant changes was the introduction of the algorithmic feed in 2016. This change meant that Instagram no longer displayed posts in chronological order, which caused a lot of frustration for users who wanted to see the latest posts from their favorite accounts. In this article, we will show you how to get Instagram posts in chronological order in 2023.

Why Instagram Stopped Showing Posts in Chronological Order?

Before we dive into how to get Instagram posts in chronological order, it is essential to understand why Instagram stopped displaying posts in chronological order. Instagram's algorithmic feed was introduced to improve user experience by showing them the posts that they are most likely to engage with. This change was made to ensure that users spend more time on the platform.

How to Get Instagram Posts in Chronological Order?

Step 1: Turn on Post Notifications

The first step to getting Instagram posts in chronological order is to turn on post notifications for the accounts you want to see the latest posts from. To do this, go to the profile page of the account you want to receive notifications from, click on the three dots in the top right corner, and select "Turn on Post Notifications."

Step 2: Create a List of Accounts to Follow

The second step to getting Instagram posts in chronological order is to create a list of accounts to follow. This list should consist of the accounts you want to see the latest posts from. You can create a list by clicking on the three lines in the top right corner of your Instagram profile, selecting "Settings," and then selecting "Followed Accounts." From there, you can create a list of accounts that you want to follow.

Step 3: Use the Instagram Chronological Order Tool

The third step to getting Instagram posts in chronological order is to use the Instagram Chronological Order Tool. This tool is a third-party app that allows you to see your Instagram feed in chronological order. The tool works by analyzing your Instagram activity and displaying posts in the order they were posted. You can download the Instagram Chronological Order Tool from the App Store or Google Play Store.

FAQs

Q: Can I Get All Posts in Chronological Order?

A: No, it is not possible to get all posts in chronological order. Instagram's algorithmic feed is designed to show users the posts that they are most likely to engage with. However, by following the steps outlined in this article, you can see the latest posts from the accounts you want to follow.

Q: Is the Instagram Chronological Order Tool Safe?

A: Yes, the Instagram Chronological Order Tool is safe to use. However, it is essential to download the tool from a reliable source to avoid any security issues.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get Instagram posts in chronological order:

  • Turn on post notifications for the accounts you want to see the latest posts from
  • Create a list of accounts to follow
  • Use the Instagram Chronological Order Tool
  • Engage with the posts you want to see more of

In Conclusion

Getting Instagram posts in chronological order may seem challenging, but it is possible. By following the steps outlined in this article and using the tips and tricks provided, you can see the latest posts from the accounts you want to follow. Remember to engage with the posts you want to see more of to improve your Instagram experience.
