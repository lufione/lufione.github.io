What is TikTok Creator Fund?

TikTok Creator Fund is a program initiated by TikTok to encourage creators to produce quality videos and earn money. Creators who meet certain criteria can apply to join the program and receive a payment for their work. The fund is aimed at supporting creators and encouraging them to continue producing quality content.

To join TikTok Creator Fund, you need to meet certain criteria. Firstly, you need to have a TikTok account with at least 100,000 followers. The amount you earn depends on several factors such as the number of views, engagement, and region. The payment ranges from $0.01 to $0.02 per view, and you can earn up to $200 per day. However, this amount is subject to change based on TikTok's discretion.
