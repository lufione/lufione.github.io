How To Make Money In Quarantine As An 11 Year Old

Written by Bardi Jun 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

With the ongoing pandemic and quarantine, many people are struggling financially. As an 11 year old, you may not have a lot of options when it comes to making money, but there are still ways to earn some extra cash while staying safe at home. In this article, we will explore some tips and tricks for making money during quarantine.

Sell Your Stuff Online

One way to make money during quarantine is to sell items you no longer need or use. Consider selling old toys, clothes, or books online through platforms like eBay or Facebook Marketplace. Take clear pictures of your items and write detailed descriptions to attract potential buyers.

Offer Your Services

Another way to make money is to offer your services to others. You could offer to walk dogs in your neighborhood, do yard work for your neighbors, or even offer virtual tutoring services for younger students. Make posters or flyers advertising your services and post them in your community.

Take Online Surveys

You can also make money by taking online surveys. Many companies are looking for feedback on their products and services, and they are willing to pay for it. Look for reputable survey sites such as Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, or Vindale Research.

Start a YouTube Channel

If you enjoy making videos, consider starting a YouTube channel. You can create content about your hobbies or interests and monetize your videos through ads. Just be sure to follow the site's guidelines and create content that is appropriate for your age group.

Sell Homemade Crafts

If you enjoy crafting, you could also make money by selling your creations. Make items such as jewelry, candles, or soap, and sell them online through platforms like Etsy. Be sure to promote your products on social media to attract customers.

Participate in Online Contests

Another way to make money is to participate in online contests. Look for writing, art, or photography contests that offer cash prizes. Just be sure to read the rules and guidelines carefully before entering.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money during quarantine as an 11 year old. Whether you choose to sell your stuff online, offer your services, take online surveys, start a YouTube channel, sell homemade crafts, or participate in online contests, be sure to stay safe and follow all guidelines and regulations. With a little creativity and hard work, you can earn some extra cash while staying safe at home.
