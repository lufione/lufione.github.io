How Can You Make Money As A 10 Year Old

Written by Eveline Sep 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a 10 year old looking to make some extra cash? Or are you a parent looking for ways to teach your child the value of money and hard work? Either way, there are plenty of ways for a 10 year old to earn money. Here are some ideas:

1. Lemonade Stand

A classic way to make money as a kid is to set up a lemonade stand. You can sell lemonade, cookies, or other snacks to people passing by your house. Make sure to advertise your stand with signs and on social media.

2. Dog Walking

If you love dogs, why not offer to walk your neighbors' dogs for a fee? This is a great way to get some exercise and make money at the same time.

3. Yard Work

You can offer to do yard work for your neighbors, such as mowing lawns, raking leaves, or weeding gardens. Make sure to charge a fair price and do a good job so you can get repeat customers.

4. Babysitting

If you're responsible and enjoy spending time with younger kids, you can offer to babysit for families in your community. Make sure to get certified in CPR and first aid, and ask your parents for help with setting up a safe and fun environment for the kids.

5. Garage Sale

If you have a lot of old toys, clothes, or other items that you no longer use, you can have a garage sale to sell them. Make sure to price your items fairly and advertise the sale in your neighborhood.

6. Sell Crafts

If you're creative, you can make and sell crafts such as friendship bracelets, painted rocks, or homemade soap. You can sell your crafts online or at local craft fairs.

7. Online Surveys

There are websites that pay you to take online surveys. While you won't make a lot of money, it's an easy way to earn some pocket change in your spare time.

8. Recycling

You can collect cans and bottles and take them to a recycling center for cash. Make sure to wear gloves and ask your parents for help with transportation.

9. Tutoring

If you excel in a certain subject, you can offer to tutor younger students who need help. Make sure to set a fair price and create a lesson plan.

10. Car Washing

You can offer to wash your neighbors' cars for a fee. Make sure to use the right materials and do a good job so you can get repeat customers.

Conclusion

As a 10 year old, there are plenty of ways to earn money and learn valuable skills. Whether it's through traditional methods such as lemonade stands and yard work, or through more modern methods such as online surveys and selling crafts, there's no shortage of opportunities. With hard work and determination, you can make some extra cash and have fun doing it!
