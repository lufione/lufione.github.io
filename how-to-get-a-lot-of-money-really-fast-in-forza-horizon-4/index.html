Forza Horizon 4 is an open-world racing game that allows players to explore the beautiful countryside of Great Britain while competing in a variety of races and challenges. One of the key elements of the game is earning money, which can be used to buy new cars, upgrades, and other items. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for getting a lot of money really fast in Forza Horizon 4.

Complete Races and Challenges

The easiest way to earn money in Forza Horizon 4 is by completing races and challenges. There are a variety of different races and challenges available, each with their own rewards. Some races offer large cash prizes, while others offer rare cars or other valuable items. You can also earn money by completing skill challenges, such as drifting or jumping, which will reward you with influence points that can be used to unlock new events.

Invest in Real Estate

In Forza Horizon 4, you can invest in real estate by buying and upgrading properties. Each property you own will generate a steady stream of income, which can add up quickly if you own multiple properties. To get started, look for properties that are located in high-traffic areas, such as near popular race tracks or tourist destinations. Upgrading your properties will also increase their income potential.

Sell Cars and Parts

If you have a lot of cars or parts that you don’t need, you can sell them for cash. In the game, you can visit the auction house to buy and sell cars and parts. To get the most money for your items, make sure they are in good condition and are in high demand. You can also try to buy low and sell high, by looking for undervalued items and reselling them for a profit.

Join a Club

Joining a club in Forza Horizon 4 can be a great way to earn money and other rewards. Clubs allow you to compete in special events and challenges, and you can earn rewards based on your performance. You can also earn bonuses for completing club objectives, such as winning a certain number of races or earning a certain amount of influence points.

Use Wheelspins

Wheelspins are a special type of reward in Forza Horizon 4 that can earn you a lot of money and other valuable items. You can earn wheelspins by completing races and challenges, or by leveling up. When you spin the wheel, you can win cash prizes, rare cars, or other valuable items. Make sure to use your wheelspins whenever you can to maximize your rewards.

Complete the Festival Playlist

The Festival Playlist is a series of events and challenges that are updated each week in Forza Horizon 4. By completing the Playlist, you can earn a variety of rewards, including cash prizes, rare cars, and other valuable items. Make sure to check the Playlist regularly and complete as many events as you can to earn the most rewards.

Participate in Live Events

Live Events are special events that take place in Forza Horizon 4 at specific times. These events allow you to compete against other players in real-time races and challenges. By participating in Live Events, you can earn cash prizes, rare cars, and other valuable items. Make sure to check the schedule for Live Events and participate whenever you can to earn the most rewards.

Upgrade Your Cars

Upgrading your cars can be a great way to earn more money in Forza Horizon 4. By upgrading your cars, you can improve their performance and win more races and challenges. You can also sell your old parts to earn some extra cash. Make sure to choose upgrades that are suited to your playstyle and the type of races you enjoy.

Complete Daily and Weekly Challenges

Forza Horizon 4 offers a variety of daily and weekly challenges that can earn you money and other rewards. These challenges can range from simple tasks, such as completing a race or drifting for a certain distance, to more difficult challenges that require skill and strategy. Make sure to check the challenges regularly and complete as many as you can to earn the most rewards.

Play with Friends

Finally, playing with friends can be a great way to earn money and other rewards in Forza Horizon 4. By playing with friends, you can compete in races and challenges together, and earn rewards based on your performance. You can also work together to complete challenges and objectives, which can earn you even more rewards. Make sure to invite your friends to join you in the game and have fun!
