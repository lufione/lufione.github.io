How to Make Money During Covid as a 12-Year-Old

The Covid pandemic has affected people of all ages, including children. With schools closed and parents working from home, it can be challenging to find ways to make money. However, there are still opportunities for 12-year-olds to earn some extra cash.

1. Sell Crafts Online

If you're artistic, you can make and sell crafts online. Websites like Etsy and Redbubble allow you to create and sell your own designs on products like stickers, t-shirts, and phone cases. You can also sell your crafts on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

2. Do Chores for Neighbors

Offer to do chores for your neighbors, such as mowing their lawn, walking their dog, or cleaning their pool. You can also offer to do small tasks like watering plants or taking out the trash. Make sure to charge a fair price and be reliable.

3. Start a YouTube Channel

If you're comfortable in front of the camera, you can start a YouTube channel. You can create content around your hobbies or interests, or even vlog about your daily life. Once you have enough subscribers, you can earn money through ads and sponsorships.

4. Tutor Younger Students

If you're good at a certain subject, you can offer to tutor younger students. You can advertise your services on social media or through flyers in your neighborhood. Make sure to charge a fair price and be patient with your students.

5. Sell Baked Goods

If you enjoy baking, you can sell your baked goods to friends and family or at a local farmer's market. Make sure to follow food safety guidelines and price your products competitively.

6. Participate in Online Surveys

There are many websites that pay you to participate in online surveys. While you won't make a lot of money, it's a simple way to earn some extra cash in your free time.

7. Offer Babysitting Services

If you're responsible and enjoy spending time with kids, you can offer babysitting services to families in your neighborhood. Make sure to get certified in CPR and first aid, and never leave children unsupervised.

8. Walk Dogs

If you love dogs, you can offer dog walking services to busy pet owners in your neighborhood. Make sure to be reliable and follow any instructions given by the owner.

9. Sell Items Online

You can sell items you no longer need or want on websites like eBay or Craigslist. This is a great way to declutter your space and make some extra cash at the same time.

10. Create an Online Course

If you're knowledgeable in a certain subject, you can create an online course and sell it on websites like Udemy or Skillshare. Make sure to create high-quality content and market your course to reach a wider audience.

Conclusion

While Covid has brought many challenges, there are still ways for 12-year-olds to make money. Whether it's through selling crafts online or offering babysitting services, there are opportunities for everyone. Get creative and don't be afraid to try new things!
