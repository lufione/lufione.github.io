How To Earn Money By Selling Your Internet Data

Written by Aprilia Jul 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

It's no secret that companies are willing to pay big bucks for access to your internet data. But did you know that you can actually make money by selling your data? Here's how:

Step 1: Sign Up for a Data Brokerage Service

There are a number of data brokerage services out there that will pay you for access to your internet data. Some of the most popular include:

  • Nielsen DMP
  • Acxiom
  • Oracle Data Cloud

Do your research to find the best option for you.

Step 2: Install a Data Collection App

Once you've signed up for a data brokerage service, you'll need to install a data collection app on your devices. This app will track your internet usage and collect data to be sold to the brokerage service.

Make sure to read through the app's terms and conditions carefully to ensure that you're comfortable with the data being collected.

Step 3: Start Earning Money

Once the app is installed and collecting data, you can sit back and start earning money. The amount you earn will depend on the brokerage service and the amount of data you're willing to share.

Some services will pay you a flat fee for access to your data, while others will pay you based on how much data you share.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is selling my data safe?

Generally speaking, selling your data is safe as long as you're using a reputable data brokerage service and reading through the terms and conditions of the data collection app carefully.

How much money can I make?

The amount of money you can make will depend on the data brokerage service and the amount of data you're willing to share. Some users report earning anywhere from $10 to $100 per month.

Can I stop sharing my data at any time?

Yes, most data brokerage services will allow you to stop sharing your data at any time. Simply uninstall the data collection app and notify the brokerage service.

Tips and Tricks

  • Do your research to find the best data brokerage service for you.
  • Read through the terms and conditions of the data collection app carefully before installing.
  • Consider using a separate device for data collection if you're concerned about your privacy.
  • Be honest about the amount of data you're willing to share to avoid any surprises when it comes to payment.

By following these steps, you can start earning money by selling your internet data. Just remember to do your research, read through the terms and conditions carefully, and be honest about the amount of data you're willing to share.
