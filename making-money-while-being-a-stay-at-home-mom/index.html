How To Make Money As A Stay At Home Mom In 2023

Written by Robby Oct 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Being a stay-at-home mom can be a fulfilling and rewarding experience, but it can also be tough financially. However, with the right mindset and tools, it is possible to make money from home while still being able to take care of your family. Here are some tips and tricks on how to make money as a stay-at-home mom in 2023.

1. Start a Blog

Blogging is a popular way to make money from home, and it can be a great outlet for sharing your experiences as a stay-at-home mom. You can monetize your blog through ads, sponsored content, and affiliate marketing. It takes time and effort to build up a following, but with dedication, it can be a lucrative source of income.

2. Sell Your Crafts

If you have a talent for crafting, you can turn it into a business. With online marketplaces like Etsy, it's easier than ever to sell handmade goods. From jewelry to home decor, there's a market for almost any type of craft. You can also sell your crafts at local craft fairs and markets.

3. Offer Freelance Services

If you have skills in writing, graphic design, social media management, or other areas, you can offer your services as a freelancer. Websites like Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer.com connect freelancers with clients who need their services. You can set your own rates and work on your own schedule.

4. Become a Virtual Assistant

Virtual assistants are in high demand, and it's a job that can be done from home. You can help businesses with tasks like email management, social media management, scheduling, and more. Websites like Zirtual and Fancy Hands connect virtual assistants with clients who need their services.

5. Participate in Online Surveys

While online surveys won't make you rich, they can provide some extra income. Companies like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, and InboxDollars pay users for taking surveys, watching videos, and completing other tasks. It's not a full-time job, but it's an easy way to make some extra cash.

6. Offer Childcare Services

If you're already a stay-at-home mom, offering childcare services to other families can be a way to make money while still being able to take care of your own children. You can advertise your services on local parenting groups or websites like Care.com.

7. Write an Ebook

If you have expertise in a certain area, you can write an ebook and sell it on Amazon or other platforms. It can be about parenting, cooking, fitness, or any other topic you know well. With a little marketing effort, your ebook can become a passive source of income.

8. Rent Out Your Home on Airbnb

If you have a spare room or a vacation home, you can rent it out on Airbnb. It's a great way to make money while still being able to stay home with your children. You can set your own rates and availability, and Airbnb takes care of the booking process and payment.

9. Sell Products Through Direct Sales

Direct sales companies like Avon, Tupperware, and Mary Kay allow you to sell products from home. You can earn commission on sales and build a team of other salespeople to earn even more. It's a flexible way to make money while still being able to take care of your family.

10. Become a YouTuber

If you have a passion for creating video content, you can start a YouTube channel and monetize it through ads and sponsorships. It takes time and effort to build up a following, but with dedication, it can be a lucrative source of income.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money as a stay-at-home mom in 2023. From blogging to direct sales to offering freelance services, there's an option for almost any skill set. It takes time and effort to build up a successful business, but with perseverance, you can achieve financial independence while still being able to take care of your family.
