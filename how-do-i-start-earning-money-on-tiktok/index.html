How To Start Earning Money On Tiktok In 2023

Written by Christine May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Introduction

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world, with over a billion active users. It's a platform where people can share short-form videos, and it's quickly becoming a popular way to earn money. In this article, we'll be discussing how you can start earning money on TikTok in 2023.

Step 1: Build Your Following

The first step to making money on TikTok is building a following. You need a substantial following to make money on the platform. You can build your following by consistently creating high-quality content and engaging with your audience. Make sure to use relevant hashtags, collaborate with other creators, and participate in challenges to increase your visibility.

Step 2: Join the TikTok Creator Fund

The TikTok Creator Fund is a program that pays creators for their content. To be eligible for the program, you need to have at least 100,000 followers, be 18 years or older, and have a certain level of engagement on your videos. The Creator Fund pays creators based on the views they receive on their videos. If you're eligible, you can apply to join the Creator Fund through the TikTok app.

Step 3: Work with Brands

Another way to make money on TikTok is by working with brands. Brands are always looking for influencers to promote their products or services. If you have a substantial following, you can reach out to brands and offer your services. Alternatively, you can sign up for influencer marketing platforms like AspireIQ, Upfluence, and Influencer.co to connect with brands.

Step 4: Sell Your Own Products

If you have a product to sell, you can promote it on TikTok. You can create short videos showcasing your product and encouraging people to make a purchase. You can also use TikTok's live streaming feature to sell your products in real-time. This approach is best for creators who have built a loyal following and have a product that appeals to their audience.

Step 5: Participate in Challenges

TikTok challenges are a great way to increase your visibility on the platform. You can participate in challenges to attract new followers and engage with your existing audience. Some challenges also offer cash prizes, so make sure to participate in those as well.

FAQs

How many followers do I need to start making money on TikTok?

You need a substantial following to make money on TikTok. While there's no specific number, having at least 100,000 followers is a good starting point.

How much can I earn from the TikTok Creator Fund?

The amount you earn from the Creator Fund depends on the views your videos receive. The exact amount is not disclosed by TikTok, but creators have reported earning anywhere from $0.01 to $0.02 per view.

How do I find brands to work with on TikTok?

You can reach out to brands directly or sign up for influencer marketing platforms like AspireIQ, Upfluence, and Influencer.co to connect with brands.

Tips and Tricks

- Consistency is key when it comes to building a following on TikTok. Make sure to post regularly and engage with your audience. - Use relevant hashtags to increase your visibility on the platform. - Collaborate with other creators to reach new audiences. - Participate in challenges to increase your visibility and engagement. - Be authentic and showcase your personality in your videos. This will help you build a loyal following.

Conclusion

Making money on TikTok requires time, effort, and dedication. You need to build a substantial following, participate in challenges, work with brands, and sell your own products. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can start earning money on TikTok in 2023. Remember to be consistent, authentic, and engage with your audience to build a loyal following.
