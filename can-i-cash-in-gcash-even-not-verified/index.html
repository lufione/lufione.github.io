However, some users wonder if they can use GCash even if they are not verified. The answer is yes, but there are some limitations.

What Is GCash Verification?

GCash verification is a process that confirms a user's identity and links their mobile number to their GCash account. It involves submitting personal information and valid IDs, as well as taking a selfie. Once verified, a user can enjoy higher transaction limits, access to more features, and better security.

What Can I Do With An Unverified GCash Account?

Even if you have not verified your GCash account, you can still use it to cash in, buy load, and pay bills up to a certain amount. The daily limit for cash in and cash out is Php 8,000, while the monthly limit is Php 100,000. The limit for buying load and paying bills is Php 2,000 per day.

How Do I Cash In To An Unverified GCash Account?

You can cash in to your unverified GCash account through several channels, such as online banking, over-the-counter, and remittance centers. Some of the partner banks that allow cash in to GCash are BDO, BPI, Metrobank, and Unionbank. You can also go to any 7-Eleven, SM Business Center, or Cebuana Lhuillier branch to cash in.

What Are The Fees For Cashing In To An Unverified GCash Account?

The fees for cashing in to an unverified GCash account may vary depending on the channel and the amount. Some channels, such as online banking and remittance centers, may charge a service fee or a convenience fee. Meanwhile, some partner merchants, such as 7-Eleven and SM Business Center, may offer cash in for free or with a minimal fee.

What Are The Risks Of Using An Unverified GCash Account?

While using an unverified GCash account may be convenient, it also poses some risks. Since your identity is not verified, you may be more vulnerable to fraud and scams. You should also be careful in sharing your GCash account details, such as your mobile number and MPIN, to avoid unauthorized transactions.

How Do I Verify My GCash Account?

If you want to enjoy higher limits and more features in GCash, you can verify your account by following these steps:

  1. Open your GCash app and go to the menu.
  2. Select "Verify Now" and choose the type of verification you want to do.
  3. Fill out the necessary information and upload your valid IDs and selfie.
  4. Wait for the confirmation of your verification, which may take up to 24 hours.

Conclusion

Using GCash without verification is possible, but it has some limitations and risks. If you want to maximize the benefits of GCash, it is recommended to verify your account and enjoy higher limits and better security. Nevertheless, you can still use GCash to transact online even if you are not verified, as long as you follow the guidelines and precautions.
