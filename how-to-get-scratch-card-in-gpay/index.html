How To Get Scratch Card In Gpay

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

Introduction

Google Pay, commonly known as GPay, is a popular digital payment platform that allows you to make payments and transfer money from one account to another. Apart from making payments, GPay also offers various rewards and cashback offers to its users. In this article, we will discuss how to get a scratch card in GPay and what benefits you can avail of by using it.

What is a Scratch Card in GPay?

A scratch card is a reward offered by GPay to its users when they make transactions using the app. The reward can be in the form of cashback or a chance to win some exciting prizes. To get a scratch card, you need to use GPay for making transactions, and the card will be credited to your account automatically.

How to Get a Scratch Card in GPay?

To get a scratch card in GPay, follow the steps given below:

Step 1: Install and Set Up GPay

If you have not installed GPay on your mobile phone, go to the Play Store or App Store and download the app. Once downloaded, set it up by linking your bank account.

Step 2: Make Transactions Using GPay

To get a scratch card, you need to make transactions using GPay. You can make transactions by scanning QR codes, entering the UPI ID, or entering the bank account details of the recipient.

Step 3: Check for Scratch Card in Rewards Section

Once you make a transaction, check the rewards section of the app to see if you have received a scratch card. If you have received a scratch card, click on it to reveal the reward.

Benefits of Scratch Card in GPay

Using scratch cards in GPay has many benefits. Some of them are:

1. Cashback

Scratch cards offer cashback to users, which can be credited to their GPay account or bank account. The cashback percentage varies depending on the transaction amount and the offer available at the time.

2. Chance to Win Prizes

Apart from cashback, scratch cards also offer a chance to win prizes such as smartphones, laptops, and other gadgets. The probability of winning a prize depends on the number of users participating in the offer.

3. Encourages Digital Transactions

Scratch cards encourage users to make digital transactions through GPay, which is a safe, secure, and convenient way of making payments.

FAQs

1. Can I Get More than One Scratch Card per Transaction?

No, you can get only one scratch card per transaction, irrespective of the transaction amount.

2. How Long is a Scratch Card Valid?

The validity of a scratch card is mentioned in the rewards section of the app. Generally, scratch cards are valid for a few days or weeks, depending on the offer.

3. Is There Any Limit on the Number of Scratch Cards I Can Get?

Yes, there is a limit on the number of scratch cards you can get per week. The limit varies depending on the offer available at the time.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to maximize your chances of getting scratch cards in GPay:

1. Check for Offers

GPay offers various scratch card offers from time to time. Keep checking the app for offers and participate in them to get more scratch cards.

2. Use GPay for All Transactions

To increase your chances of getting scratch cards, use GPay for all your transactions, whether it is paying bills, shopping online, or sending money to friends and family.

3. Make Transactions During Offer Period

Most of the scratch card offers have a limited period. Make transactions during the offer period to avail of the benefits.

Conclusion

Scratch cards in GPay are a great way to get cashback and win prizes while making transactions. By following the steps mentioned in this article and using the tips and tricks, you can increase your chances of getting scratch cards and avail of the benefits offered by GPay.
