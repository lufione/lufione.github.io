How to Withdraw Money from TikTok Malaysia

TikTok has become a popular platform for people to showcase their talents and earn money. If you are an influencer on TikTok Malaysia and want to withdraw your earnings, this article will guide you through the process.

Step 1: Connect your Account to Payment Method

The first step is to connect your TikTok account to a payment method. You can do this by going to your profile and clicking on the three dots on the top right corner. From there, select "Balance" and then "Withdraw." You will be asked to connect your account to a payment method such as PayPal or a bank account.

Step 2: Verify Your Account

Before you can withdraw your earnings, you need to verify your account. TikTok Malaysia requires users to provide their personal information such as their name, address, and identification number. Once you have provided this information, your account will be verified.

Step 3: Reach the Minimum Withdrawal Limit

TikTok Malaysia has a minimum withdrawal limit of RM50. You need to have at least RM50 in your account to be able to withdraw your earnings.

Step 4: Request for Withdrawal

Once you have reached the minimum withdrawal limit, you can request for withdrawal by going to "Balance" and then "Withdraw." Enter the amount you want to withdraw and select your payment method. TikTok Malaysia processes withdrawal requests within 7-10 working days.

Step 5: Check Your Payment Method

After 7-10 working days, check your payment method for the transferred amount. If you have connected your account to PayPal, the amount will be reflected in your PayPal account. If you have connected your account to a bank account, the amount will be reflected in your bank account.

FAQs

1. How often can I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your earnings as often as you want as long as you have reached the minimum withdrawal limit of RM50.

2. Is there a fee for withdrawing my earnings?

No, TikTok Malaysia does not charge any fee for withdrawing your earnings. However, your payment method may charge a fee for receiving the transferred amount.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips to increase your earnings on TikTok Malaysia:

  • Post regularly and engage with your followers
  • Collaborate with other influencers
  • Use popular hashtags
  • Create engaging content
  • Promote your TikTok account on other social media platforms

By following these tips, you can increase your followers and engagement, which will ultimately lead to higher earnings.
