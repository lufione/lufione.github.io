Can You Make Money Online Writing Poetry?

If you have a passion for poetry and love to write, you might wonder if it's possible to make money by writing poetry. The truth is that, with the rise of digital technology and the internet, it's now easier than ever to sell your poetry online and earn some extra income. In this article, we will explore some ways you can make money online by writing poetry.

Write and Sell Poems Online

One of the most direct ways to make money from your poetry is by writing and selling individual poems online. There are many websites and marketplaces out there that allow you to sell your poems to interested buyers. Some of these sites include:

  • PoetrySoup.com
  • The Poetry Foundation
  • PoemHunter.com
  • Poetry.com
  • PoetryNation.com

These websites allow you to upload your poems and set a price for them, which interested buyers can purchase and use as they wish. While you may not earn a lot from each sale, it's an excellent way to build up a portfolio and gain exposure as a poet.

Enter Poetry Contests

Another way to make money from your poetry is by entering poetry contests. Many organizations, foundations, and publications hold poetry contests regularly, offering cash prizes and publication opportunities for the winners. Some of the popular poetry contests include:

  • The National Poetry Competition
  • The T.S. Eliot Prize
  • The Poetry Society of America's Annual Chapbook Fellowship
  • The Ruth Lilly and Dorothy Sargent Rosenberg Poetry Fellowships
  • The Bridport Prize

Entering poetry contests can be a great way to showcase your work, gain recognition, and earn some extra income. However, keep in mind that most poetry contests have entry fees, and the competition can be stiff.

Create a Poetry Blog

A blog is an excellent platform for showcasing your poetry and building a following. By creating a poetry blog and regularly posting your work, you can gain a loyal audience and monetize your blog through ads, sponsorships, and affiliate marketing. Some popular poetry blogs include:

  • The Poetry Foundation's Blog
  • Poetry School's Blog
  • Poetry News' Blog
  • Poetry International's Blog
  • Verse Daily

Creating a successful poetry blog takes time and effort, but it can be a rewarding way to earn money doing what you love.

Conclusion

So, can you make money online writing poetry? The answer is yes, but it takes hard work, dedication, and perseverance. By selling your poems online, entering poetry contests, and creating a poetry blog, you can turn your passion into a profitable side hustle. Keep honing your craft, building your portfolio, and exploring new opportunities, and who knows? You might just become the next famous poet.
