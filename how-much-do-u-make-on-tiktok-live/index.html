How Much Do You Make On Tiktok Live

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 · 6 min read

Since the launch of TikTok Live, many people have been wondering how much they can earn from going live on the platform. In this article, we will explore the different ways TikTok pays its users and how much you can expect to make from TikTok Live.

How Does TikTok Pay its Users?

TikTok pays its users through its Creator Fund program, which was launched in 2020. The Creator Fund is a $1 billion program set up by TikTok to support its creators monetarily. The fund pays eligible creators based on the views and engagement their content generates.

However, not all TikTok users are eligible for the Creator Fund. To be eligible, you must meet certain requirements, such as having at least 100,000 followers and having generated at least 100,000 views in the last 30 days.

How Much Can You Make from TikTok Live?

Currently, TikTok does not pay its users directly for going live on the platform. However, creators can earn money through live streaming by receiving gifts from their viewers.

Gifts are virtual items that viewers can purchase using real money and send to their favorite creators during live streams. The gifts have different values, and creators earn a percentage of the value of the gifts they receive.

The percentage of the gift value that creators earn depends on their level. TikTok has five levels for its creators, and the higher the level, the higher the percentage of the gift value earned. The levels are determined by the creator's engagement, content quality, and consistency.

How to Increase Your Earnings from TikTok Live?

If you want to earn more from TikTok Live, you need to focus on increasing your engagement, content quality, and consistency. This will help you grow your audience, increase your views, and attract more gifts from your viewers.

You can also promote your live streams on your other social media platforms, such as Instagram and Twitter, to attract more viewers and increase your chances of receiving gifts.

FAQs About Earning on TikTok Live

1. Can you make a living from going live on TikTok?

It is possible to make a living from going live on TikTok, but it requires a lot of hard work, dedication, and consistency. You need to have a large following, high engagement, and great content to attract gifts from your viewers.

2. How much do TikTok stars make per live stream?

There is no fixed amount that TikTok stars make per live stream, as it depends on the number and value of gifts they receive. However, some TikTok stars have reported earning hundreds or even thousands of dollars per live stream.

3. Can you get paid for live streaming on TikTok without the Creator Fund?

Yes, you can earn money from live streaming on TikTok without the Creator Fund by receiving gifts from your viewers. You can also collaborate with brands and earn money through sponsored live streams.

Tips and Tricks for Earning on TikTok Live

1. Engage with your viewers during your live streams by responding to their comments and questions.

2. Use popular hashtags to attract more viewers to your live streams.

3. Collaborate with other TikTok creators to increase your reach and attract more viewers.

4. Experiment with different types of content to see what works best for your audience.

5. Promote your live streams on your other social media platforms to attract more viewers.

By following these tips and tricks, you can increase your earnings from TikTok Live and grow your following on the platform.
