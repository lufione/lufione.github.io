How To Make Money On Tiktok Videos In 2023

Written by Daniel May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

With the rise of social media, TikTok has become one of the most popular platforms for creating and sharing short videos. But did you know that you can also make money from your TikTok videos? In this article, we’ll show you how to monetize your TikTok account and turn your passion into profit.

1. Build a Following

The first step to making money on TikTok is to build a following. You need to have a significant number of followers to attract brands and potential sponsors. To grow your following, create high-quality and engaging content that resonates with your audience. Use trending hashtags and participate in challenges to increase your visibility on the platform.

2. Join the TikTok Creator Fund

The TikTok Creator Fund is a program that pays eligible creators for their content. To join, you need to have at least 100,000 followers, be 18 years or older, and meet certain other criteria. Once you’re accepted, you can earn money based on the views and engagement your videos receive.

3. Partner with Brands

Brands are always looking for influencers to promote their products and services. If you have a sizable following, you can partner with brands to create sponsored content. Make sure the products or services you promote align with your niche and brand values to maintain authenticity and credibility.

4. Sell Merchandise

If you have a loyal fanbase, you can sell merchandise related to your brand. This can include anything from t-shirts and hats to phone cases and stickers. Use TikTok’s “link in bio” feature to direct your followers to your online store.

5. Offer Services

If you have a particular skill or talent, you can offer your services to your followers. For example, if you’re a makeup artist, you can offer virtual consultations or tutorials. If you’re a musician, you can offer personalized songs or performances. Get creative and think outside the box to monetize your skills.

6. Participate in Contests and Challenges

TikTok often hosts contests and challenges with cash prizes. Participate in these events to potentially win money and gain exposure. Even if you don’t win, participating in these events can help you grow your following and improve your content creation skills.

7. Use Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a popular way to make money online. You can promote products or services you believe in and earn a commission for every sale made through your unique affiliate link. Use your TikTok account to promote products related to your niche and include your affiliate link in your bio or video descriptions.

8. Crowdfund Your Projects

If you have a creative project you want to pursue, you can use crowdfunding platforms like Kickstarter or Patreon to raise funds. Use your TikTok account to promote your project and encourage your followers to support you.

9. Join TikTok Live Gifting

TikTok Live Gifting is a feature that allows your followers to send you virtual gifts during a live stream. These gifts can be converted into real money. To become eligible, you need to have at least 1,000 followers and be 18 years or older.

10. Stay Authentic and Consistent

Lastly, it’s important to stay true to yourself and your brand. Don’t compromise your values or style to make money. Consistently create high-quality and engaging content that resonates with your audience. With dedication and hard work, you can turn your passion for TikTok into a lucrative career.
