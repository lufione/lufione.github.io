Can You Monetize Shorts On Youtube

Written by Oliver May 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Youtube Shorts is the newest feature of Youtube that allows creators to upload short-form videos of up to 60 seconds. With the growing popularity of TikTok and Instagram Reels, creators are now wondering if they can monetize their Shorts on Youtube. In this article, we will explore whether or not monetization is possible for Youtube Shorts.

What is Monetization on Youtube?

Monetization on Youtube refers to the process of earning money from your Youtube content. This can be done through various methods such as ads, sponsorships, merchandise sales, and Youtube Premium revenue. To be eligible for monetization on Youtube, you need to meet certain criteria such as having 1000 subscribers and 4000 watch hours within the past 12 months.

Can You Monetize Shorts on Youtube?

As of 2023, Youtube does not offer monetization for Shorts. This means that you cannot earn money directly from the Shorts feature. However, you can still monetize your Youtube channel as a whole by meeting the eligibility criteria for Youtube Partner Program (YPP). Once you are accepted into YPP, you can earn money through ads, sponsorships, and Youtube Premium revenue.

How to Monetize Your Youtube Channel?

To monetize your Youtube channel, you need to meet the following eligibility criteria:

1. 1000 Subscribers

You need to have at least 1000 subscribers on your Youtube channel. This shows that you have a dedicated audience that is interested in your content.

2. 4000 Watch Hours

Your videos need to have a total watch time of at least 4000 hours within the past 12 months. This shows that your audience is engaged and spends a significant amount of time watching your content.

3. Advertiser-Friendly Content

Your videos need to comply with Youtube's community guidelines and be advertiser-friendly. This means that your content should not contain any inappropriate language, violence, or controversial topics that may harm the reputation of advertisers.

4. Youtube Partner Program Agreement

You need to agree to the terms and conditions of the Youtube Partner Program agreement. This includes following Youtube's monetization policies and not engaging in any fraudulent activities.

Tips for Monetizing Your Youtube Channel

Here are some tips to help you monetize your Youtube channel:

1. Consistency

Upload videos on a regular basis to keep your audience engaged and attract new viewers.

2. Engaging Content

Create content that is interesting, informative, and entertaining to keep your audience engaged and coming back for more.

3. Collaborations

Collaborate with other creators in your niche to reach a wider audience and attract new subscribers.

4. Promote Your Channel

Promote your channel on social media platforms and other websites to reach new viewers and increase your subscribers.

Conclusion

While monetization is not currently available for Youtube Shorts, you can still monetize your Youtube channel as a whole by meeting the eligibility criteria for Youtube Partner Program. By creating engaging content, being consistent, collaborating with others, and promoting your channel, you can increase your subscribers and earn money from your Youtube content.
