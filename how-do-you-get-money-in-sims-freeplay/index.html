How To Earn Money In Sims Freeplay

Written by Daniel Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

Sims FreePlay is a popular life simulation game that allows players to create their own virtual world. In this game, players need to earn money to buy items and build houses. However, earning money in Sims FreePlay can be a challenge, especially for new players. In this article, we will share some tips on how to earn money in Sims FreePlay.

Completing Tasks

One of the easiest ways to earn money in Sims FreePlay is by completing tasks. Tasks can be found on the left side of the screen and can be completed by your Sims. Completing tasks will earn you money, XP, and lifestyle points.

Tip: Focus on completing the main quests as they offer higher rewards.

Growing Crops

Growing crops is another way to earn money in Sims FreePlay. Crops can be grown in the garden and can be harvested after a certain amount of time. The longer the crop takes to grow, the more money it will yield.

Tip: Invest in higher-level crops to earn more money.

Selling Items

In Sims FreePlay, you can sell items that you no longer need. This includes furniture, decorations, and even clothes. Selling items can be done by tapping on the item and selecting the sell option.

Tip: If you have duplicate items, sell the duplicates to earn more money.

Completing Hobbies

Hobbies are activities that can be completed by your Sims to earn money and XP. Each hobby has a different set of tasks and rewards. Some hobbies, such as cooking and writing, can even lead to a career for your Sims.

Tip: Choose hobbies that your Sims enjoy to make it more fun and rewarding.

Getting a Job

In Sims FreePlay, your Sims can get a job to earn a steady income. Jobs can be found in the career tab and can be applied for by your Sims. Each job has a different set of tasks and rewards.

Tip: Choose a job that matches your Sim's skills and interests to earn more money.

Participating in Events

Events are limited-time activities that offer special rewards. Some events offer money as a reward for completing tasks.

Tip: Participate in events as much as possible to earn extra money and rewards.

Watching Ads

In Sims FreePlay, you can earn money by watching ads. Ads can be found in the rewards tab and can be watched in exchange for money and other rewards.

Tip: Watch ads as often as possible to earn extra money.

Conclusion

Earning money in Sims FreePlay can be a challenge, but by using these tips, you can quickly accumulate wealth and build your dream world. Remember to focus on completing tasks, growing crops, selling items, participating in events, and getting a job to earn a steady income. Happy gaming!
