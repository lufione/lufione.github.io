How To Make Tiktok Live Wallpaper In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having the same boring wallpaper on your phone? Why not switch it up with a TikTok live wallpaper? In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to make your very own TikTok live wallpaper.

Step 1: Download TikTok Video

The first step to making a TikTok live wallpaper is to download the video you want to use as your wallpaper. To do this, open the TikTok app and find the video you want to use. Click on the "Share" button and select "Save Video". The video will now be saved to your camera roll.

Step 2: Download Live Wallpaper App

Next, you will need to download a live wallpaper app. There are many options available on both the App Store and Google Play Store. Some popular options include Live Wallpapers 4K and Live Wallpaper HD 3D.

Step 3: Select Video as Wallpaper

Open the live wallpaper app and select "Choose Live Wallpaper". From there, select "Video Live Wallpaper". Choose the TikTok video you downloaded in step 1 as the video source.

Step 4: Adjust Settings

You can now adjust the settings of your TikTok live wallpaper. This includes options such as video playback speed, video quality, and sound settings. Play around with these settings until you are happy with the result.

Step 5: Set as Wallpaper

Finally, set your new TikTok live wallpaper as your phone's wallpaper. To do this, go to your phone's settings and select "Wallpaper". Choose "Live Wallpaper" and select the TikTok live wallpaper you just created.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use any TikTok video as a live wallpaper?

Yes, you can use any TikTok video you have saved to your camera roll as a live wallpaper.

Do I need to pay for a live wallpaper app?

There are both free and paid live wallpaper apps available. It is up to you to decide which one you want to use.

Can I adjust the sound settings of my TikTok live wallpaper?

Yes, you can adjust the sound settings of your TikTok live wallpaper. This includes options such as mute, play sound on loop, and adjust volume.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you create the best TikTok live wallpaper:

  • Choose a TikTok video with vibrant colors and interesting visuals to make your live wallpaper more eye-catching.
  • Experiment with different playback speeds to create a unique effect.
  • Consider using a live wallpaper app that allows you to add special effects to your video, such as sparkles or fire.
  • Make sure your phone's battery is fully charged before setting your TikTok live wallpaper, as it may drain your battery faster than regular wallpaper.

By following these steps and tips, you can create a stunning TikTok live wallpaper that will make your phone stand out from the rest.
