How To Get Free Money On Simcity Buildit Iphone In 2023

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 · 6 min read

SimCity BuildIt is a popular game among iPhone users who enjoy building and managing their own virtual cities. However, as with any game, it can be challenging to progress without spending real money. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to get free money on SimCity BuildIt iPhone in 2023.

1. Take Advantage of Daily Login Rewards

One of the easiest ways to earn free money in SimCity BuildIt is by logging in every day. The game rewards players with cash and other items just for logging in consecutively for several days. Make sure to log in every day to collect your rewards.

2. Complete City Achievements

Another way to earn free money is by completing city achievements. These are goals that you can achieve by building certain structures or reaching certain population milestones. Once you achieve these goals, the game rewards you with cash and other items.

3. Participate in Contest of Mayors

The Contest of Mayors is a weekly competition where players compete against each other to earn the most points by completing certain tasks. The top players are rewarded with cash and other prizes. Participate in the Contest of Mayors to earn free money and improve your city.

4. Sell Extra Items in Trade Depot

If you have extra items in your inventory that you don't need, consider selling them in the Trade Depot. Other players may be looking for those items and will pay you for them. This is a great way to earn free money on SimCity BuildIt.

5. Keep Your Citizens Happy

Keeping your citizens happy is important for your city's growth and prosperity. Happy citizens pay more taxes, which means more money for you. Make sure to provide them with the services they need and keep their happiness level high to earn more money.

6. Upgrade Your Buildings

Upgrading your buildings is another way to earn more money in SimCity BuildIt. Upgraded buildings pay more taxes and generate more income for you. Make sure to upgrade your buildings whenever you can to increase your revenue.

7. Watch Ads for Free Money

SimCity BuildIt offers players the opportunity to watch ads in exchange for free money. Take advantage of this feature by watching ads whenever they are available. This is an easy way to earn free money without spending any real money.

8. Connect Your Facebook Account

Connecting your Facebook account to SimCity BuildIt can also earn you free money. The game often offers rewards to players who connect their Facebook account, so make sure to do so to take advantage of these rewards.

9. Use the Daniel's City App

The Daniel's City App is a third-party app that can help you earn free money in SimCity BuildIt. The app provides you with daily goals to achieve and rewards you with cash and other items once you complete them.

10. Use SimCity BuildIt Hack Tools

Using SimCity BuildIt hack tools is not recommended, as it goes against the game's terms of service and can result in your account being banned. However, some players choose to use these tools to earn free money. Use them at your own risk.

Conclusion

There you have it, 10 tips and tricks on how to get free money on SimCity BuildIt iPhone in 2023. By following these tips, you can progress in the game without spending any real money. Remember to play the game responsibly and have fun!
