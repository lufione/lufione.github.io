How To Earn Money As A Student In Bangladesh

Written by Luffy Aug 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Being a student in Bangladesh can be tough, especially when it comes to finances. With the cost of living increasing every year and the limited job opportunities for students, it can be challenging to make ends meet. However, there are ways to earn money as a student in Bangladesh that can help you pay for your expenses and even save up for the future.

1. Freelancing

Freelancing is a great way for students to earn money in Bangladesh. With the rise of the gig economy, there are plenty of online platforms that offer freelance opportunities. You can offer your skills in writing, graphic design, programming, and other fields. The best part is that you can work from anywhere, as long as you have an internet connection.

How to Start Freelancing

To start freelancing, you need to identify your skills and find a platform that matches your interests. Some popular freelancing platforms in Bangladesh include Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer. You'll need to create a profile, showcase your skills, and start bidding on projects. Remember to set realistic rates and deliver high-quality work to build your reputation as a freelancer.

2. Tutoring

If you're good at a particular subject, you can offer tutoring services to other students. Tutoring can be a lucrative way to earn money, especially during exam season. You can set your own rates and schedule, and you'll be helping other students succeed academically.

How to Start Tutoring

To start tutoring, you need to identify the subject(s) you're good at and find students who need help. You can advertise your services on social media, online forums, or through word of mouth. Make sure you have a clear understanding of the subject matter and can explain it in a way that's easy to understand.

3. Online Surveys

Online surveys are a simple way to earn money as a student in Bangladesh. Many companies and organizations are willing to pay for feedback on their products and services. You can sign up for online survey sites and start filling out surveys in your spare time.

How to Start Taking Online Surveys

To start taking online surveys, you need to find legitimate survey sites that pay. Some popular survey sites in Bangladesh include Toluna, Swagbucks, and Survey Junkie. You'll need to create a profile and provide some basic information about yourself. You can then start taking surveys and earning rewards, which can be redeemed for cash or gift cards.

4. Blogging

Blogging is a great way to earn money as a student in Bangladesh, especially if you're passionate about a particular topic. You can start a blog and share your thoughts, opinions, and experiences with others. As your blog grows, you can monetize it through advertising, sponsored posts, and affiliate marketing.

How to Start Blogging

To start blogging, you need to choose a blogging platform and create a blog. Some popular blogging platforms include WordPress, Blogger, and Wix. You'll need to choose a niche, create content, and promote your blog through social media and other channels. Remember to focus on quality content and engage with your readers to build a loyal following.

5. Selling Online

If you're good at making crafts or have items to sell, you can start selling online. You can create an online store on platforms like Etsy or Facebook Marketplace and sell your products to a wider audience. You can also sell items you no longer need or want.

How to Start Selling Online

To start selling online, you need to identify the products you want to sell and choose a platform to sell on. Some popular platforms in Bangladesh include Daraz, Ajkerdeal, and Bikroy. You'll need to create a profile, list your products, and start promoting them through social media and other channels. Remember to offer competitive prices and excellent customer service to build a loyal customer base.

Conclusion

Earning money as a student in Bangladesh can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By taking advantage of the opportunities available online and offline, you can earn money while still focusing on your studies. Whether you choose to freelance, tutor, take online surveys, blog, or sell online, remember to stay focused, work hard, and deliver high-quality work to succeed.
