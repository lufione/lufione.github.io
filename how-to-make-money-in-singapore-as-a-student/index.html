How To Make Money In Singapore As A Student

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

Being a student in Singapore can be expensive, with the high cost of living and tuition fees. However, there are many ways to make money as a student in Singapore, and this article will provide you with some tips and tricks to help you earn some extra cash.

Part-Time Jobs

One of the most common ways to make money as a student in Singapore is to get a part-time job. There are many opportunities available, such as working in retail, food and beverage, or tutoring. You can search for part-time jobs on job portals such as Indeed or JobStreet.

Sell Your Skills

If you have a particular skill or talent, such as photography, graphic design, or writing, you can offer your services to others for a fee. You can advertise your services on social media or freelance job platforms such as Upwork or Fiverr.

Online Surveys

Another way to make money as a student in Singapore is to participate in online surveys. Many companies pay people to take surveys and provide feedback on their products or services. You can register for survey websites such as Toluna or Swagbucks to start earning.

Participate in Focus Groups

Similar to online surveys, focus groups are a way for companies to gather feedback on their products or services. You can participate in focus groups and earn money for providing your opinion. You can find focus group opportunities on platforms such as FocusGroup.com.

Sell Your Stuff

If you have items that you no longer need, such as clothes or gadgets, you can sell them online on platforms such as Carousell or Shopee. This is a great way to declutter your space while earning some extra cash.

Rent Your Space

If you have a spare room or a parking space, you can rent it out on platforms such as Airbnb or Parkable. This is a great way to earn passive income without much effort.

Blogging or Vlogging

If you enjoy writing or creating videos, you can start a blog or YouTube channel and monetize it through ads or sponsorships. This is a great way to showcase your creativity and earn money at the same time.

Freelance Writing

If you enjoy writing, you can offer your services as a freelance writer. Many companies and individuals require content for their websites or social media, and you can earn money by providing high-quality content. You can find freelance writing opportunities on platforms such as ProBlogger or Freelancer.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money as a student in Singapore, and the options listed in this article are just a few of them. With a little creativity and effort, you can earn some extra cash to help with your expenses. Remember to always prioritize your studies and not let your side hustle interfere with your academic performance.
