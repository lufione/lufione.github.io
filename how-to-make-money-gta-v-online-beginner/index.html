How To Make Money In Gta V Online For Beginners

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read
If you're just starting out in GTA V Online, you might be wondering how to make money quickly. After all, you'll need cash to buy weapons, vehicles, and properties. In this article, we'll provide some tips and tricks to help you earn money in the game.

Complete Jobs and Heists

One of the easiest ways to make money in GTA V Online is to complete jobs and heists. These can be accessed by opening your phone and selecting the "Jobs" tab. From there, you can choose from a variety of missions, including races, deathmatches, and more. Heists are more complex missions that require a team of players to complete, but they offer a larger payout.

Invest in Properties

Another way to make money in GTA V Online is to invest in properties. You can purchase businesses, apartments, and garages to generate passive income. Some properties even offer missions and challenges that can earn you additional cash.

Sell Stolen Vehicles

If you're looking for a quick cash injection, consider stealing and selling vehicles. You can steal cars, bikes, and boats and sell them to the chop shop or to other players. Just be careful not to get caught by the police!

Participate in Events

GTA V Online offers a variety of events that can earn you money and other rewards. These include races, survival challenges, and more. Keep an eye on the in-game calendar to see when events are happening.

Complete Daily Objectives

Each day, you'll receive a set of three objectives to complete in GTA V Online. These can include anything from killing a certain number of players to completing a specific mission. If you complete all three objectives, you'll receive a cash reward.

Invest in the Stock Market

If you're looking for a more advanced way to make money in GTA V Online, consider investing in the stock market. You can access the stock market through your phone and purchase stocks in various companies. Keep an eye on the news and trends to make informed investments.

Join a Crew

Joining a crew can offer a variety of benefits in GTA V Online. Not only can you team up with other players to complete missions and heists, but you can also earn bonuses and rewards for working together.

Rob Stores and Armored Trucks

If you're feeling daring, consider robbing stores and armored trucks for a quick cash boost. Just be prepared for a high level of police attention!

Become a VIP or CEO

Once you have enough money, consider becoming a VIP or CEO. This will unlock a variety of missions and challenges that offer large payouts. Plus, you'll have access to special abilities and bonuses.

Use Shark Cards

If all else fails, you can always purchase Shark Cards with real money to get a large amount of in-game cash. Just be aware that this can become expensive quickly.

Conclusion

Making money in GTA V Online can be a challenge, but there are plenty of ways to earn cash. Whether you're completing missions, investing in properties, or stealing vehicles, be sure to have fun and stay safe out there!
