How Do Youtubers Make Money From Youtube Premium

Written by Petter Apr 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

YouTube has become a popular platform for many individuals to showcase their talents, share their knowledge, and create content that their audience loves. However, creating quality content requires time and effort, and many YouTubers rely on monetizing their channels to earn a living. YouTube Premium is one of the many ways YouTubers make money from their channels.

What is YouTube Premium?

YouTube Premium is a subscription-based service that provides additional benefits to viewers who subscribe to it. Some of the benefits of YouTube Premium include ad-free viewing, background play, and access to YouTube Originals. Viewers who subscribe to YouTube Premium pay a monthly fee, and a portion of this fee goes to the creators whose content they watch.

How Do YouTubers Earn Money from YouTube Premium?

YouTubers earn money from YouTube Premium in two ways:

1. Revenue Share

YouTube shares a portion of the revenue it earns from YouTube Premium subscriptions with the creators whose content is watched by subscribers. The amount that a creator earns depends on factors such as the number of views their videos receive and the time viewers spend watching their content. The more subscribers and views a creator has, the more money they are likely to earn from YouTube Premium.

2. YouTube Originals

YouTube Originals are exclusive shows and movies that are produced by YouTube. These shows are only available to YouTube Premium subscribers, and creators who produce them earn a share of the revenue that YouTube earns from subscriptions. YouTube may also provide funding to creators to produce original content for the platform.

How Can YouTubers Increase Their Earnings from YouTube Premium?

There are several ways that YouTubers can increase their earnings from YouTube Premium:

1. Produce High-Quality Content

Creating high-quality content that engages viewers is essential to building a loyal audience. The more viewers a creator has, the more likely they are to earn money from YouTube Premium.

2. Encourage Viewers to Subscribe to YouTube Premium

YouTubers can encourage their viewers to subscribe to YouTube Premium by mentioning the benefits of the service in their videos or providing links to subscribe in their video descriptions. The more subscribers a creator has, the more money they are likely to earn from YouTube Premium.

3. Participate in YouTube Originals

Participating in YouTube Originals is an excellent way for YouTubers to earn money from YouTube Premium. By producing original content for the platform, creators can earn a share of the revenue that YouTube earns from subscriptions.

Conclusion

YouTubers can earn money from YouTube Premium through revenue sharing and participating in YouTube Originals. To increase their earnings, creators must produce high-quality content, encourage viewers to subscribe to YouTube Premium, and participate in YouTube Originals. With the right strategies, YouTubers can earn a sustainable income from their channels.
