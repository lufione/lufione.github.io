Does Money Tree Grow Fast

Written by Christine Jul 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Money tree, also known as Pachira aquatica, is a popular houseplant that is believed to bring good luck and prosperity to its owner. One of the common questions that people ask about this plant is whether it grows fast or not. In this article, we will explore the growth rate of the money tree and provide some tips on how to make it grow faster.

How Fast Does Money Tree Grow?

The growth rate of the money tree depends on various factors such as the age of the plant, the environment, and the care it receives. Generally, young money trees grow faster than mature ones. It can grow up to 1-2 feet per year under the right conditions. However, the growth rate may slow down if the plant is not getting enough sunlight, water, and nutrients.

Factors Affecting the Growth of Money Tree

Light

Money tree requires bright, indirect sunlight to grow. If the plant is not getting enough light, it may grow slowly or not at all. You can place it near a window that receives bright but filtered sunlight or use artificial grow lights to supplement the light.

Water

Overwatering or underwatering can affect the growth rate of the money tree. It is important to water the plant thoroughly but allow the soil to dry out slightly before the next watering. Too much water can lead to root rot, while too little can cause the leaves to droop and fall off.

Nutrients

Money tree requires fertilization to grow faster. You can use a balanced fertilizer once a month during the growing season to provide the necessary nutrients. However, avoid overfertilizing as it can burn the roots and damage the plant.

Tips to Make Money Tree Grow Faster

Pruning

Pruning the money tree can help promote faster growth by stimulating new growth and removing dead or damaged branches. You can prune the plant in the spring or early summer when it is actively growing.

Repotting

Money tree may outgrow its pot, which can stunt its growth. Repotting it into a larger pot with fresh soil can provide more space and nutrients for the plant to grow faster. You can repot the plant every 2-3 years or when you notice the roots are coming out of the drainage holes.

Misting

Misting the leaves of the money tree can help increase the humidity around the plant and promote faster growth. You can use a spray bottle to mist the leaves once a week or more often during the dry winter months.

Conclusion

Money tree can grow fast under the right conditions of light, water, and nutrients. By providing the plant with the necessary care and following the tips mentioned above, you can help promote faster growth and enjoy a lush and healthy money tree in your home.
