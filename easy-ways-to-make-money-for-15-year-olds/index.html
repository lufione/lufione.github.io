Easy Ways To Make Money For 15 Year Olds

Written by Bardi Apr 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're 15 years old and looking to make some extra cash, there are numerous ways you can do so. Here are some easy ways to make money for 15 year olds:

Sell Your Stuff Online

If you have items that you no longer need, consider selling them online. You can use websites like eBay and Amazon to sell your items. Take clear photos of your items and write detailed descriptions. This will help your items sell faster.

Babysitting

Babysitting is a great way to make money, especially if you enjoy spending time with kids. Spread the word to family, friends, and neighbors that you're available to babysit. You can also create a profile on websites like Care.com to find babysitting gigs.

Part-Time Job

Many businesses hire 15 year olds for part-time jobs. Some examples include working at a grocery store, restaurant, or retail store. Check with your local businesses to see if they're hiring.

Pet Sitting

If you love animals, consider pet sitting. You can offer to take care of people's pets while they're away on vacation. Spread the word to family, friends, and neighbors that you're available for pet sitting.

Tutoring

If you excel in a particular subject, consider tutoring. You can offer your services to other students who need help with that subject. Spread the word to your classmates and teachers that you're available for tutoring.

Online Surveys

There are websites that pay you to take surveys. While you won't make a lot of money doing this, it's an easy way to make some extra cash. Some websites to consider include Swagbucks and Survey Junkie.

Yard Work

Offer to do yard work for your neighbors. This can include mowing lawns, raking leaves, and pulling weeds. Spread the word to your neighbors that you're available for yard work.

Sell Crafts

If you're crafty, consider selling your creations. You can use websites like Etsy to sell your handmade items. Take clear photos of your items and write detailed descriptions. This will help your items sell faster.

Freelance Work

If you have a particular skill, such as writing, graphic design, or programming, consider freelancing. You can offer your services to businesses and individuals who need them. Websites like Upwork and Fiverr are great places to find freelance work.

Car Washing

Offer to wash your neighbors' cars for a fee. You can also offer to detail their cars for an additional fee. Spread the word to your neighbors that you're available for car washing.

These are just a few ways that 15 year olds can make money. Remember to always get your parents' permission before pursuing any of these opportunities.
