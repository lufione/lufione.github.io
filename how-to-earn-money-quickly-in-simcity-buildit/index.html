How To Earn Money Quickly In Simcity Buildit

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

SimCity BuildIt is a popular city-building simulation game where players can create and manage their own virtual city. One of the most important aspects of the game is earning money, which can be used to build and improve your city. In this article, we will provide some tips and tricks on how to earn money quickly in SimCity BuildIt.

1. Increase Your Population

One of the best ways to earn money quickly in SimCity BuildIt is to increase your population. The more people you have in your city, the more taxes you can collect. To increase your population, you need to build residential buildings and provide them with services such as water, electricity, and sewage. You can also upgrade your residential buildings to increase their capacity and earn more taxes.

2. Sell Your Excess Items

If you have excess items such as raw materials or finished goods, you can sell them to other players. You can advertise your items in the Global Trade HQ or join a trade group to find buyers. Selling your excess items can be a quick way to earn money, especially if you have a high demand item.

3. Complete Mayor's Tasks

The Mayor's Tasks are a series of challenges that you can complete to earn rewards such as Simoleons, SimCash, and Golden Keys. Some of the tasks require you to build certain buildings or upgrade existing ones, while others require you to collect a certain amount of resources. Completing the Mayor's Tasks can be a great way to earn money quickly in SimCity BuildIt.

4. Participate in Contest of Mayors

The Contest of Mayors is a weekly competition where you can compete against other players to earn rewards. The competition consists of several challenges that require you to build and upgrade certain buildings, collect resources, and complete tasks. The rewards for the Contest of Mayors can be substantial, and participating in the competition can be a great way to earn money quickly.

5. Build Profitable Buildings

Some buildings in SimCity BuildIt are more profitable than others. For example, the Omega Labs and NeoMall are both high-profit buildings that can earn you a lot of money. If you have the resources to build these buildings, they can be a great investment that will pay off in the long run.

6. Use SimCash Wisely

SimCash is a premium currency in SimCity BuildIt that can be used to speed up construction times and purchase special items. While it can be tempting to spend SimCash on everything, it's important to use it wisely. Save your SimCash for important purchases such as expansion items or rare buildings that can't be obtained through regular gameplay.

7. Upgrade Your Services

Upgrading your city services such as water, electricity, and sewage can increase your city's efficiency and earn you more money. For example, upgrading your water tower can increase your water capacity and allow you to build more residential buildings. Upgrading your sewage treatment plant can reduce the amount of pollution in your city and increase your land value.

8. Plan Your Layout Carefully

The layout of your city can have a big impact on your income. For example, placing residential buildings near commercial buildings can increase your land value and earn you more taxes. Placing industrial buildings far away from residential areas can reduce pollution and increase your overall efficiency. Plan your layout carefully to maximize your income.

9. Join a Club

Joining a club in SimCity BuildIt can provide you with several benefits. You can trade items with other club members, participate in club challenges, and earn rewards. Club rewards can include Simoleons, SimCash, and Golden Keys, which can all help you earn money quickly.

10. Be Patient

Earning money in SimCity BuildIt takes time and patience. Don't expect to become a millionaire overnight. Take your time, plan your strategy, and focus on building a sustainable and profitable city. With patience and persistence, you can earn money quickly and build the city of your dreams.
