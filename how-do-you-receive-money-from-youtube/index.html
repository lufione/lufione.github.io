How Do You Receive Money From Youtube In 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

YouTube has become a popular platform for content creators to showcase their skills and earn money. It is the go-to platform for millions of people worldwide. If you are a YouTuber, you can monetize your content and make money from it. But the question is, how do you receive money from YouTube? In this article, we will discuss various ways to receive payment from YouTube.

Create a Google AdSense Account

The first step to earning money from YouTube is to sign up for a Google AdSense account. AdSense is a program that allows content creators to earn money by displaying ads on their videos. It is easy to set up an AdSense account, and you can do it in a few steps.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating an AdSense Account

1. Go to the AdSense website and sign up for an account. 2. Fill out the required information, including your name, email address, and website. 3. Submit your application and wait for approval. 4. Once approved, set up your payment information.

Enable Monetization on Your YouTube Channel

After creating an AdSense account, you need to enable monetization on your YouTube channel. This allows ads to be displayed on your videos, and you earn money based on the number of clicks or views on the ads. To enable monetization, follow these steps: 1. Go to your YouTube account settings. 2. Click on the "Monetization" tab. 3. Enable monetization by clicking on the "Enable My Account" button. 4. Link your YouTube account to your AdSense account.

Meet the Minimum Monetization Requirements

To start earning money from YouTube, you must meet the minimum monetization requirements. These requirements include: 1. 1,000 subscribers on your YouTube channel. 2. 4,000 watch hours in the last 12 months. 3. Compliance with YouTube's terms of service and community guidelines.

Get Paid Through AdSense

Once you have met the minimum monetization requirements, you can start earning money through AdSense. AdSense payments are made monthly, and you can receive your payment via direct deposit or check. You can also check your earnings and payment history on the AdSense dashboard.

Conclusion

Earning money from YouTube is a great way to showcase your skills and earn some extra cash. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can start earning money from YouTube in no time. Remember to create quality content, follow YouTube's guidelines, and meet the minimum monetization requirements to maximize your earnings. Good luck!
