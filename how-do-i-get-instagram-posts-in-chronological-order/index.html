How To Get Instagram Posts In Chronological Order In 2023

Written by Aprilia Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Instagram is a popular social media platform that has evolved over the years. One of the changes that many users have been unhappy about is the algorithmic feed. This means that Instagram doesn't show posts in chronological order. Instead, it decides which posts to show you based on your usage patterns and engagement. This can make it difficult to keep up with the accounts you follow. In this article, we will show you how to get Instagram posts in chronological order in 2023.

Why Instagram Changed to an Algorithmic Feed

Instagram changed to an algorithmic feed to improve user engagement. The idea was that by showing users the most relevant posts, they would spend more time on the app. This, in turn, would increase ad revenue for Instagram. However, many users have been unhappy with the change as they feel like they are missing out on posts from their favorite accounts.

How to Get Instagram Posts in Chronological Order

Step 1: Turn on Notifications

One way to get Instagram posts in chronological order is to turn on notifications for the accounts you follow. This way, you will be notified when they post something new. To turn on notifications, go to the account's profile page and tap the three dots in the top right corner. Then select "Turn on Post Notifications."

Step 2: Use Third-Party Apps

Another way to get Instagram posts in chronological order is to use third-party apps. These apps allow you to view your feed in chronological order. Some popular options include Hootsuite, Later, and Planoly. However, keep in mind that using third-party apps can be risky as it goes against Instagram's terms of service.

Step 3: Create a List

You can also create a list of accounts that you want to see posts from in chronological order. This way, you can quickly access their posts without having to scroll through your entire feed. To create a list, go to your profile and tap the three lines in the top right corner. Then select "Lists" and "Create List."

FAQ

Can I Change Back to Chronological Order on Instagram?

No, Instagram does not allow users to change back to chronological order. However, by following the steps outlined above, you can get posts in chronological order.

Why Did Instagram Change to an Algorithmic Feed?

Instagram changed to an algorithmic feed to improve user engagement and increase ad revenue.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Engage With Accounts You Want to See Posts From

Instagram's algorithm is designed to show you posts from accounts you engage with regularly. So, if you want to see posts from a particular account, make sure to like and comment on their posts.

Tip 2: Use Hashtags and Location Tags

Using hashtags and location tags can help your posts get seen by more people. This, in turn, can increase engagement and improve your chances of showing up in your followers' feeds.

Tip 3: Post Consistently

Posting consistently can help you stay top of mind for your followers. This, in turn, can increase engagement and improve your chances of showing up in your followers' feeds.

Conclusion

Getting Instagram posts in chronological order can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By following the steps outlined above, you can make sure you're not missing out on posts from your favorite accounts. Remember to engage with accounts you want to see posts from, use hashtags and location tags, and post consistently to improve your chances of showing up in your followers' feeds.
