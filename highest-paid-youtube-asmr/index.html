Highest Paid Youtube Asmr A Comprehensive Guide In 2023

Written by Petter Sep 15, 2023 · 6 min read

The world of ASMR has taken the internet by storm, and YouTube is the hub of this phenomenon. ASMR, or Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response, is a tingling sensation that some people experience in response to certain sounds or stimuli. With millions of ASMR videos on YouTube, it has become a lucrative industry, with some YouTubers making millions of dollars. In this article, we will take a deep dive into the highest paid YouTube ASMR artists in 2023.

Who are the Highest Paid YouTube ASMR Artists?

ASMR artists come in all shapes and sizes, and they all have their unique styles and techniques. However, some have managed to stand out from the crowd and attract massive audiences, which have translated into hefty paychecks. Here are some of the highest paid YouTube ASMR artists:

1. ASMR Darling

ASMR Darling, whose real name is Taylor Darling, is one of the most popular ASMR artists on YouTube. She has over 7 million subscribers and has been making ASMR videos since 2014. Her videos range from roleplays to tapping and scratching sounds, and she has collaborated with other YouTubers and brands. According to reports, ASMR Darling makes over $1 million annually from her YouTube channel.

2. Gibi ASMR

Gibi ASMR, whose real name is Gibi Hanna, is another popular ASMR artist on YouTube. She has over 5 million subscribers and has been making ASMR videos since 2016. Gibi's videos are known for their high production value and attention to detail, and she often collaborates with other ASMR artists. Reports suggest that Gibi ASMR makes over $500,000 annually from her YouTube channel.

3. ASMR Glow

ASMR Glow, whose real name is Sharon Dubois, is a French-Canadian ASMR artist with over 3 million subscribers. She has been making ASMR videos since 2016 and is known for her soothing voice and gentle mannerisms. ASMR Glow often incorporates makeup and skincare routines into her videos, making her channel a favorite among beauty enthusiasts. Reports suggest that ASMR Glow makes over $300,000 annually from her YouTube channel.

How do YouTubers Make Money from ASMR Videos?

YouTube pays YouTubers based on the number of views and engagement their videos receive. However, ASMR artists can also make money from sponsorships, merchandise sales, and other revenue streams. Some ASMR artists also create exclusive content for their Patreon subscribers, which is a membership platform that allows creators to earn money directly from their fans.

Can Anyone Become a Successful YouTube ASMR Artist?

While anyone can create ASMR videos and upload them to YouTube, becoming a successful YouTube ASMR artist takes time, dedication, and talent. To stand out from the crowd, you need to have a unique style and personality, create high-quality content, and engage with your audience. It also helps to collaborate with other ASMR artists and brands to expand your reach and monetization opportunities.

Conclusion

ASMR has become a massive industry, and YouTube is at the forefront of this phenomenon. While there are many talented ASMR artists on the platform, some have managed to attract massive audiences and earn millions of dollars. ASMR Darling, Gibi ASMR, and ASMR Glow are among the highest paid YouTube ASMR artists in 2023, but there are many more out there. If you're interested in becoming a YouTube ASMR artist, remember that success takes time, dedication, and talent. Happy tingles!
