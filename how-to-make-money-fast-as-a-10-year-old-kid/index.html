How To Make Money Fast As A 10 Year Old Kid

Written by Daniel Apr 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a 10 year old kid looking to make some extra cash? Well, look no further! With a little creativity and hard work, there are plenty of ways for you to earn some money.

1. Offer Your Services

There are plenty of services you can offer to your neighbors and family members. Some ideas include:

  • Walking dogs
  • Mowing lawns
  • Babysitting
  • Washing cars

Make flyers and post them around your neighborhood to let people know about your services.

2. Sell Your Crafts

If you're crafty, consider making and selling your creations. Some ideas include:

  • Bracelets
  • Paintings
  • Knitted items
  • Candles

You can sell your crafts online or set up a table at a local craft fair.

3. Offer Your Skills

Do you have a particular skill that others might be interested in? Consider offering lessons or tutoring services. Some ideas include:

  • Teaching a language
  • Tutoring in math or science
  • Teaching a musical instrument
  • Teaching art or crafting

Advertise your services online or through flyers.

4. Participate in Online Surveys

There are a number of websites that will pay you to take surveys. While you won't make a ton of money, it can be a good way to earn some extra cash in your free time.

5. Sell Your Old Items

Do you have old toys or games you no longer use? Consider selling them online or in a yard sale. You can also sell clothes you've outgrown.

6. Offer Yard Work Services

During the fall and winter months, offer to rake leaves or shovel snow for your neighbors. This can be a great way to earn some extra cash.

7. Sell Refreshments

If you live in a hot climate, consider selling lemonade or other refreshments to people passing by. You can also sell hot cocoa or coffee during the colder months.

8. Walk Pets

If you love animals, consider offering pet walking services. You can advertise your services online or through flyers in your neighborhood.

9. Offer House Cleaning Services

If you're good at cleaning, consider offering house cleaning services to your neighbors. You can advertise your services online or through flyers in your neighborhood.

10. Do Odd Jobs

Offer to do odd jobs for your neighbors or family members. Some ideas include:

  • Running errands
  • Helping with yard work
  • Cleaning cars
  • Helping with household chores

With a little hard work and creativity, you can make money as a 10 year old kid. Good luck!
