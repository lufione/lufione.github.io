How To Make Money On Youtube Without Adsense

Written by Aprilia Sep 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

YouTube is a platform that has the potential to help you make a lot of money. However, one of the most common ways to earn money on YouTube is through AdSense. Unfortunately, not everyone is eligible for AdSense or may not want to rely solely on it. So, what can you do? Here are some ways to make money on YouTube without AdSense.

1. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a popular strategy that involves promoting other people's products or services and earning a commission for every sale made through your unique affiliate link. To make money on YouTube with affiliate marketing, you can create videos showcasing products or services that your audience would be interested in and include your affiliate link in the video description.

2. Sell Digital Products

If you have a skill or expertise in a particular area, you can create and sell digital products such as e-books, courses, or templates. You can promote these products on your YouTube channel and include a link to purchase them in the video description. Make sure that your products align with your audience's interests and needs.

3. Crowdfunding

Crowdfunding is a great way to get financial support from your audience. You can use platforms like Patreon, Kickstarter, or GoFundMe to ask your viewers to support your work. In return, you can offer rewards such as exclusive content, merchandise, or early access to your videos.

4. Brand Sponsorships

If you have a large and engaged audience, you can approach brands for sponsorships. Brands may pay you to feature their products or services in your videos, or to create sponsored content around a particular theme. Make sure that the brands you partner with align with your values and your audience's interests.

5. Merchandising

If you have a loyal fan base, you can create and sell merchandise such as T-shirts, mugs, or stickers. You can use platforms like Teespring or Spreadshirt to create and sell your products. Make sure that your merchandise reflects your brand and your audience's interests.

6. Consulting Services

If you have expertise in a particular field, you can offer consulting services to your audience. You can create videos showcasing your knowledge and skills, and offer consulting services to those who need more personalized advice. This can be a lucrative way to make money on YouTube while helping others.

7. Fan Funding

You can use fan funding platforms like Tipeee or PayPal to receive donations from your viewers. Make sure that you provide value to your viewers and that you are transparent about how you will use their donations. This can be a great way to get financial support from your most loyal fans.

8. Sell Physical Products

If you have a physical product that you want to sell, you can use YouTube to promote it. You can create videos showcasing your product and include a link to purchase it in the video description. Make sure that your product aligns with your audience's interests and needs.

9. Sell Services

If you offer a service, such as photography or writing, you can use YouTube to promote it. You can create videos showcasing your work and include a link to your website or portfolio in the video description. Make sure that your services align with your audience's interests and needs.

10. Donations

If you want to receive donations directly from your viewers, you can use platforms like PayPal or Cash App. Make sure that you provide value to your viewers and that you are transparent about how you will use their donations. This can be a great way to get financial support from your most loyal fans.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money on YouTube without AdSense. The key is to provide value to your audience and to be transparent about your income streams. By diversifying your income, you can create a sustainable and profitable YouTube channel.
