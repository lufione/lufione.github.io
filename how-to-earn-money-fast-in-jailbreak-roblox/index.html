How To Earn Money Fast In Jailbreak Roblox

Written by Luffy Jul 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Jailbreak is one of the most popular Roblox games, where players can choose to be a police officer or a criminal. The objective of the game is simple: as a criminal, players must escape from prison and commit crimes to earn money, while as a police officer, they must stop the criminals and maintain peace in the city. In this article, we will guide you on how to earn money fast in Jailbreak Roblox.

1. Choose the Right Vehicle

One of the best ways to earn money fast in Jailbreak is by using the right vehicle. Some vehicles are faster and more durable than others, which means you can escape from the police and commit crimes more efficiently. The Bugatti Veyron and the Lamborghini are two of the best vehicles in the game, but they are also the most expensive. If you're just starting, consider using the Camaro or the Mustang, which are cheaper but still fast.

2. Rob Stores

Robbing stores is one of the most common ways to earn money in Jailbreak. You can rob gas stations, convenience stores, and banks. To rob a store, you need to have a keycard, which you can find inside the prison or buy from other players. Once you have the keycard, go to a store and hold down the E key to start the robbery. The amount of money you earn depends on the type of store, the number of players in the game, and the amount of time you spend robbing the store.

3. Participate in Heists

Heists are another way to earn money in Jailbreak. Heists are organized crimes that require a group of players to work together to rob a bank or a museum. To participate in a heist, you need to have a keycard and a gun. Find a group of players who are planning a heist and ask if you can join. Once the heist starts, follow the instructions of the leader and help rob the targeted location. The amount of money you earn from a heist depends on how much money is in the location and how many players are involved.

4. Sell Crates

Crates are special items that you can find around the map. They contain random items, such as guns, money, or keys. You can sell crates to other players or use them to get items that you need. To sell crates, go to the prison parking lot and find the white van. Hold down the E key to sell your crates. The amount of money you earn depends on the type and number of crates you sell.

5. Complete Quests

Completing quests is a great way to earn money and XP in Jailbreak. Quests are challenges that you can find around the map. They usually require you to do something specific, such as drive a certain distance or rob a specific store. Once you complete a quest, you will earn money and XP. The amount of money and XP you earn depends on the difficulty of the quest.

6. Use the ATM

The ATM is a useful tool in Jailbreak. You can use it to deposit or withdraw money. If you have money in the bank, you can earn interest on it, which means you can earn money even when you're not playing the game. To use the ATM, go to the bank and walk up to the machine. Hold down the E key to access the ATM.

7. Stay Alive

Staying alive is crucial in Jailbreak. If you die, you will lose all the money and items you have on you. To avoid losing everything, be careful when driving, and avoid getting into fights with other players. If you see a police officer, run away or hide until they pass.

8. Buy Gamepasses

Gamepasses are special items that you can buy with Robux, the in-game currency. Gamepasses offer various benefits, such as faster vehicles, more storage space, or special items. If you have some spare Robux, consider buying a gamepass to help you earn money faster in Jailbreak.

9. Use the Jetpack

The Jetpack is a special item that you can use to fly around the map. The Jetpack is a great way to escape from the police or reach high places that you couldn't reach otherwise. To use the Jetpack, find it inside the prison or buy it from other players.

10. Play Often

The more you play Jailbreak, the more money you will earn. Try to play the game as often as possible to maximize your earnings. You can also join a group or a clan to play with other players and earn more money together.

Conclusion

Earning money fast in Jailbreak Roblox requires some strategy and patience. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can increase your earnings and become a successful criminal in the game. Remember to always be careful and avoid getting caught by the police. Happy robbing!
