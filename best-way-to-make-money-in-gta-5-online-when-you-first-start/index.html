Best Way To Make Money In Gta 5 Online When You First Start

Written by Petter Aug 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Grand Theft Auto V has become one of the most popular games worldwide, with millions of players from all over the globe. The game's online counterpart, GTA 5 Online, has been the go-to for gamers who want to make money and have fun at the same time. However, making money in GTA 5 Online can be challenging, especially for new players. In this article, we will explore the best ways to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start.

Complete the Tutorial

Before you start making money in GTA 5 Online, make sure to complete the tutorial. The tutorial is an essential part of the game, and it will teach you the basics of the game, including how to make money. It is a short tutorial, and it will only take a few minutes to complete. Completing the tutorial will also give you some starter cash, which you can use to start your journey to becoming a millionaire in the game.

Participate in Jobs

One of the best ways to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start is by participating in jobs. Jobs are activities that you can do with other players or alone, and they pay you for completing them. Jobs can be accessed through the pause menu, and there are different types of jobs, including races, heists, and missions. Completing jobs will not only earn you cash, but it will also help you level up, which will unlock more activities and rewards.

Invest in the Stock Market

The stock market is another way to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start. The game's stock market is based on the real-life stock market, and it can be accessed through your phone's browser. You can invest in different companies, and the stock prices will fluctuate based on the in-game events. Investing in the stock market requires some research and patience, but it can be a lucrative way to make money in the game.

Sell Stolen Vehicles

If you want to make quick cash in GTA 5 Online, consider stealing and selling vehicles. You can steal cars, bikes, and other vehicles and sell them to the chop shop or the Los Santos Customs. The amount of money you can make depends on the vehicle's condition and the demand for it in the game. Selling stolen vehicles is a quick way to make money, but it can also attract the attention of the police.

Complete Daily Objectives

GTA 5 Online offers daily objectives that you can complete to earn extra cash and RP. Daily objectives are simple tasks that you can complete in the game, and they refresh every 24 hours. Completing daily objectives will not only earn you cash, but it will also help you level up faster and unlock more activities and rewards.

Join a Crew

Joining a crew is another way to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start. A crew is a group of players who work together to complete activities and earn rewards. Joining a crew will give you access to more activities, and you can also earn bonuses for completing activities with your crew members. You can join a crew by searching for them in the pause menu or through social media.

Rob Stores

Robbing stores is another quick way to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start. You can rob convenience stores, gas stations, and other stores by pointing a gun at the cashier and taking the money from the register. Robbing stores can attract the attention of the police, so make sure to have a getaway vehicle ready.

Participate in Races

Races are another way to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start. You can participate in different types of races, including car races, bike races, and boat races. Races pay you for completing them, and they also give you a chance to show off your driving skills. Participating in races will also help you level up faster, which will unlock more activities and rewards.

Buy a Business

If you have enough money, consider buying a business in GTA 5 Online. Businesses, such as nightclubs, garages, and warehouses, can generate passive income over time. You can also use your business to participate in missions and sell goods to other players. Buying a business requires a significant investment, but it can be a lucrative way to make money in the game.

Sell Goods

You can also sell goods in GTA 5 Online when you first start. You can buy goods from your businesses or steal them from other players and sell them for a profit. Selling goods requires some strategy and patience, but it can be a lucrative way to make money in the game.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money in GTA 5 Online when you first start, and the options are endless. Whether you choose to participate in jobs, invest in the stock market, or rob stores, make sure to have fun while doing it. GTA 5 Online is a game, and it's important to remember that the primary goal is to have fun. So, grab your controller and start your journey to becoming a millionaire in the game.
