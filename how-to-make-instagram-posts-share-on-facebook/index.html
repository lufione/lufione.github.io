How To Make Instagram Posts Share On Facebook

Written by Daniel Oct 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Instagram and Facebook are two of the most popular social media platforms in the world, and it's no surprise that users want to share their Instagram posts on Facebook. In this article, we'll go through the steps on how to make Instagram posts share on Facebook.

Step 1: Connect Your Instagram and Facebook Accounts

The first step is to connect your Instagram and Facebook accounts. To do this, go to your Instagram profile and tap the three lines in the top right corner. From there, select Settings, then Account, and then Linked Accounts. Tap Facebook and enter your login information to link your accounts.

Step 2: Choose Which Posts to Share on Facebook

Once your accounts are linked, you can choose which Instagram posts you want to share on Facebook. To do this, go to the post you want to share and tap the three dots in the top right corner. From there, select Share to Facebook and choose whether you want to share the post on your profile or a page you manage.

Step 3: Customize Your Facebook Post

After selecting which posts to share, you can customize the Facebook post to your liking. You can add a caption, tag friends, and choose the audience you want to share the post with. You can also choose to share the post on your Facebook story or as a regular post.

Step 4: Check Your Facebook Page

After sharing your Instagram post on Facebook, it's important to check your Facebook page to make sure the post has been shared successfully. If you encounter any issues, double-check your linked accounts and make sure you have the correct permissions.

Tips and Tricks

Use Hashtags

When sharing your Instagram posts on Facebook, consider using hashtags in your Facebook post to increase the post's reach. This will help more people discover your post and potentially gain new followers.

Post at the Right Time

Consider posting your Instagram posts on Facebook at a time when your audience is most active. This will ensure that your post is seen by as many people as possible.

FAQs

Can I Share Instagram Posts on Facebook Automatically?

Yes, you can share your Instagram posts on Facebook automatically by enabling the feature in your Instagram settings. To do this, go to Settings, then Account, and then Linked Accounts. Tap Facebook and enable the option to Share Your Posts to Facebook.

What Happens to My Instagram Hashtags When I Share on Facebook?

When you share your Instagram posts on Facebook, your hashtags will be included in the Facebook post. However, Facebook's algorithm may not give as much weight to hashtags as Instagram does.

Can I Share Instagram Stories on Facebook?

Yes, you can share your Instagram stories on Facebook by tapping the Share button at the bottom of your story and selecting Facebook. This will create a new Facebook story with your Instagram content.

Conclusion

Sharing your Instagram posts on Facebook is a great way to increase your reach and gain new followers. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily share your Instagram content on Facebook and customize your post to your liking. Remember to use hashtags and post at the right time to maximize your post's reach.
