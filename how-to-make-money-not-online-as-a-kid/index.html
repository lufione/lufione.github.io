How To Make Money Not Online As A Kid

Written by Oliver May 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of scrolling endlessly through the internet trying to find ways to make money? Do you want to step away from your computer and earn some cash in the real world? As a kid, there are plenty of opportunities to make money without relying on the internet. Here are some simple and effective ways to make money in 2023:

Sell Your Old Stuff

One of the easiest ways to make money as a kid is to sell your old stuff. Whether it's clothes, toys, or electronics, there's always someone who's willing to buy it. You can have a garage sale, sell your items on social media, or use a website like eBay to reach a wider audience.

Babysitting

If you enjoy spending time with kids, then babysitting is a great way to make money. You can advertise your services to friends and family, or use a website like Care.com to find potential clients. Make sure to get certified in CPR and first aid to give parents peace of mind.

Walking Dogs

If you love dogs, then why not get paid to walk them? Many busy pet owners would be happy to pay someone to take their furry friends on a walk. You can advertise your services in your neighborhood, or use a website like Rover to find potential clients.

Mowing Lawns

During the summer months, many people are in need of someone to mow their lawn. If you have access to a lawn mower and are willing to put in some physical work, then you can make a decent amount of money doing this. Advertise your services in your neighborhood or use a website like TaskRabbit to find potential clients.

Selling Homemade Crafts

If you're crafty, then you can make money by selling your creations. Whether it's jewelry, pottery, or knitted items, there's always a market for handmade goods. You can sell your items at local craft fairs, on Etsy, or even on social media.

Washing Cars

Another way to make money is to wash cars. Many people don't have the time or desire to wash their own car, so they're willing to pay someone to do it for them. Advertise your services in your neighborhood or use a website like TaskRabbit to find potential clients.

House Cleaning

If you're willing to do some cleaning, then you can make money by offering house cleaning services. Many people are too busy to keep their homes clean, so they're willing to pay someone to do it for them. Advertise your services in your neighborhood or use a website like Care.com to find potential clients.

Tutoring

If you excel in a particular subject, then you can make money by tutoring others. You can offer your services to classmates, friends, or use a website like Tutor.com to find potential clients. Make sure to have a strong understanding of the subject matter and be patient with your students.

Selling Lemonade

One classic way to make money as a kid is to sell lemonade. All you need is some lemonade mix, cups, and a table to set up shop. Make sure to choose a busy location and advertise your stand with signs to attract customers.

Helping with Yard Work

Finally, you can make money by helping with yard work. Whether it's raking leaves, pulling weeds, or planting flowers, there's always someone who could use an extra hand. Advertise your services in your neighborhood or use a website like TaskRabbit to find potential clients.

In Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of ways for kids to make money without relying on the internet. Whether you choose to sell your old stuff, offer services like babysitting or dog walking, or get creative with handmade crafts or lemonade stands, there are plenty of opportunities to earn some extra cash in 2023.
