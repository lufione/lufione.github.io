How Much Is Each Tiktok Live Gift Worth

Since its launch in 2016, TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms worldwide. One of the unique features of TikTok is the ability to send virtual gifts to content creators during live streams. These gifts not only support the creators financially but also show appreciation for their content. In this article, we will explore the value of each TikTok live gift.

What Are TikTok Live Gifts?

TikTok live gifts are virtual items that viewers can purchase and send to their favorite content creators during live streams. These gifts range from basic to premium, and each one is worth a different value in terms of monetary compensation for the creator. To send a gift, viewers must purchase TikTok coins, which can be bought in-app through the TikTok wallet or via third-party websites.

How Much Is Each TikTok Live Gift Worth?

The value of each TikTok live gift varies depending on the gift's level and the country you are in. Currently, there are five levels of TikTok live gifts, which are:

Level 1 Gifts

Level 1 gifts are the most basic TikTok live gifts and cost 1 TikTok coin. In terms of monetary compensation for the creator, each Level 1 gift is worth $0.01 USD. Examples of Level 1 gifts include Panda, Sun Cream, and Rainbow Puke.

Level 2 Gifts

Level 2 gifts cost 5 TikTok coins and are worth $0.05 USD each. These gifts are slightly more expensive than Level 1 gifts and include items such as Drama Queen, Concert, and Cute Bunny.

Level 3 Gifts

Level 3 gifts are worth $0.10 USD each and cost 10 TikTok coins. These gifts include items such as Firework, Drama King, and Sunflower.

Level 4 Gifts

Level 4 gifts are the second-highest tier of TikTok live gifts and cost 50 TikTok coins. Each Level 4 gift is worth $0.50 USD and includes items such as Rainbow Cloud and Skateboard.

Level 5 Gifts

Lastly, Level 5 gifts are the most expensive TikTok live gifts and cost 100 TikTok coins. Each Level 5 gift is worth $1 USD and includes items such as Super Panda and Love Bang.

How Do Creators Cash Out TikTok Live Gifts?

Once a creator has received TikTok live gifts during a live stream, they can cash out their earnings by converting the gifts into diamonds. Diamonds are in-app currency that creators can use to withdraw their earnings. The conversion rate of TikTok live gifts to diamonds varies depending on the country, but in the United States, one diamond is worth $0.50 USD.

Conclusion

TikTok live gifts are a great way for viewers to support their favorite creators financially and show appreciation for their content. With each gift ranging from $0.01 USD to $1 USD, it's important to consider the value of each gift before sending. As TikTok continues to grow in popularity, the value of live gifts may change, but for now, this guide provides a helpful overview of each gift's worth.
