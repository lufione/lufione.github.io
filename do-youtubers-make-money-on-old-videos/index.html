Do Youtubers Make Money On Old Videos

Written by Frank May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

YouTube has become a major platform for content creators to share their videos with the world. Many YouTubers have built successful careers and even become millionaires from their channels. But what happens to their old videos? Do they still make money from them?

The Short Answer is Yes

YouTube's monetization system allows creators to earn money from their videos through ads, sponsorships, and other means. This means that even if a video is several years old, it can still generate revenue for the creator.

However, the amount of money earned from an old video will depend on several factors, including the video's views, engagement, and the advertiser demand for ads in that particular niche.

How Do YouTubers Make Money from Old Videos?

There are a few ways YouTubers can continue to earn money from their old videos:

1. Adsense Revenue

Adsense is a program provided by Google that allows YouTubers to earn money from their videos through ads. The revenue earned from Adsense is based on the number of impressions and clicks the ads receive. This means that even if a video is old, it can still generate revenue from ads that are displayed on it.

2. Affiliate Marketing

Many YouTubers use affiliate marketing to monetize their videos. This involves promoting products or services in their videos and earning a commission on any sales made through their unique affiliate link. Since old videos can still generate views and clicks, they can also generate affiliate sales and revenue.

3. Brand Deals

Some YouTubers may also receive brand deals or sponsorships for their old videos. Brands are always looking for ways to reach new audiences, and if an old video has a large number of views, it can still be an attractive option for brands to advertise on.

How Can You Maximize Revenue from Old Videos?

There are a few things YouTubers can do to maximize revenue from their old videos:

1. Optimize Video Titles and Descriptions

By optimizing the titles and descriptions of old videos, YouTubers can make them more discoverable to new audiences. This can lead to increased views and engagement, which can in turn lead to more revenue from ads and affiliate sales.

2. Promote Old Videos on Social Media

YouTubers can also promote their old videos on social media platforms to increase their visibility and reach new audiences. By sharing links to old videos on Twitter, Facebook, or Instagram, YouTubers can attract new viewers and generate more revenue from ads and affiliate sales.

3. Update Old Videos with New Information

If an old video is still relevant to the creator's niche, they can update it with new information or add an annotation to promote their latest content. This can encourage viewers to watch more videos and increase the creator's overall revenue.

Conclusion

While it may seem like old videos are no longer valuable, they can still generate revenue for YouTubers through ads, affiliate marketing, and brand deals. By optimizing old videos and promoting them on social media, YouTubers can continue to earn money from their old content for years to come.
