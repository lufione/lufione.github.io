How Can I Earn Money From Gcash Easily

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 · 9 min read

Gcash is a popular digital wallet in the Philippines that allows you to pay bills, buy load, and transfer money to other Gcash users. But did you know that you can also earn money from Gcash? In this article, we’ll show you some easy ways to make money with Gcash.

1. Join Gcash’s Referral Program

Gcash’s referral program is a great way to earn money by inviting your friends to use Gcash. To join, simply open your Gcash app and go to the “Promos” tab. You’ll see a section that says “Invite Friends, Get Rewarded.” Click on that and share your referral code with your friends.

For every successful referral, you’ll earn Php 50. Plus, your friend will also get Php 50 as a reward. The more friends you invite, the more money you can earn!

2. Use Gcash to Shop Online

Gcash has a feature called “Gcash Forest” where you can earn “green energy” by using Gcash to pay for your online purchases. Green energy can be converted to Gcash credits, which you can use to pay for your bills or buy load.

To participate in Gcash Forest, simply go to the “Forest” tab in your Gcash app and plant a virtual tree. The more you use Gcash to pay for your online purchases, the more your tree will grow and the more green energy you’ll earn.

3. Participate in Gcash’s Surveys

Gcash occasionally sends out surveys to its users. By participating in these surveys, you can earn Gcash credits that you can use to pay for your bills or buy load.

To receive these surveys, make sure that you have enabled push notifications for the Gcash app. You can do this by going to your phone’s settings and turning on push notifications for the Gcash app.

4. Sell Products or Services Using Gcash

If you have a business, you can use Gcash to accept payments from your customers. Gcash has a feature called “Gcash QR” where you can generate a QR code that your customers can scan to pay you.

To use Gcash QR, simply go to the “QR” tab in your Gcash app and generate your QR code. You can then display your QR code in your store or share it with your customers.

5. Invest in Gcash’s Money Market Fund

Gcash has a feature called “GInvest” where you can invest in their money market fund. This is a low-risk investment that can earn you higher returns than a regular savings account.

To invest in GInvest, simply go to the “GInvest” tab in your Gcash app and choose the amount you want to invest. Your money will be invested in the money market fund and you can earn returns daily.

6. Join Gcash’s Raffle Promos

Gcash occasionally holds raffle promos where you can win cash prizes or other rewards. To join these promos, simply go to the “Promos” tab in your Gcash app and look for the current raffle promos.

Make sure to read the mechanics of each promo carefully to know how to join and win.

7. Cash In and Cash Out for Other People

If you have friends or family members who need to cash in or cash out from their Gcash accounts, you can offer to do it for them for a small fee. This is a great way to earn extra money without leaving your house.

To cash in or cash out for other people, simply go to the “Cash In” or “Cash Out” tab in your Gcash app and choose “Others.” You’ll need to enter the other person’s Gcash number and the amount they want to cash in or cash out.

8. Use Gcash for Your Bills Payment

Gcash has a wide range of billers that you can pay using your Gcash wallet. By using Gcash to pay for your bills, you can earn rebates or cashbacks that you can use for your future bills payment.

To pay for your bills using Gcash, simply go to the “Pay Bills” tab in your Gcash app and choose your biller. Enter the amount you want to pay and confirm your payment.

9. Play Gcash Games

Gcash has a feature called “Gcash Games” where you can play fun games and earn Gcash credits. These games are free to play and you can earn rewards by completing certain tasks or achieving certain levels.

To play Gcash Games, simply go to the “Gcash Games” tab in your Gcash app and choose the game you want to play.

10. Refer Gcash to Businesses

If you know of any businesses that could benefit from using Gcash, you can refer them to Gcash and earn commissions. Gcash has a business referral program where you can earn Php 500 for every successful referral.

To refer a business to Gcash, simply go to the “Business Solutions” tab in your Gcash app and choose “Refer a Business.” Enter the business’s details and wait for Gcash to contact them.

Now that you know how to earn money from Gcash, start exploring these features and see how much you can earn. With Gcash, earning money has never been this easy!
