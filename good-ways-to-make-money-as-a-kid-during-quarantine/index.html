Good Ways To Make Money As A Kid During Quarantine

Written by Christine May 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

With the current global pandemic, many people have been forced to stay at home. This has resulted in a lot of free time for kids who are not able to attend school or participate in extracurricular activities. However, there are still many ways for kids to make money during quarantine. In this article, we will explore some good ways for kids to make money while staying safe at home.

1. Online Tutoring

Now that many schools have shifted to online learning, there is a huge demand for online tutors. If you are good at a particular subject, you can offer your services as an online tutor. You can advertise your services on social media platforms such as Facebook or Instagram, or you can sign up to online tutoring websites.

2. Virtual Assistant

Many businesses are now operating remotely, which means that they are in need of virtual assistants. As a virtual assistant, you can help with tasks such as email management, data entry, and social media management. You can find virtual assistant jobs on websites such as Upwork or Freelancer.

3. Online Surveys

There are many websites that offer paid online surveys. These surveys are designed to gather information about consumer preferences, and companies are willing to pay for this information. Some popular survey websites include Swagbucks and Survey Junkie.

4. Freelance Writing

If you enjoy writing, you can offer your services as a freelance writer. Many businesses are in need of content for their websites and social media platforms. You can find freelance writing jobs on websites such as Fiverr or Freelancer.

5. Social Media Management

If you are social media savvy, you can offer your services as a social media manager. Many businesses are in need of someone to manage their social media platforms. You can find social media management jobs on websites such as Upwork or Freelancer.

6. Sell Crafts

If you are creative, you can sell your crafts online. There are many websites that allow you to sell handmade items, such as Etsy or ArtFire. You can also sell your crafts on social media platforms such as Facebook or Instagram.

7. Pet Sitting

If you love animals, you can offer your services as a pet sitter. Many people are in need of someone to take care of their pets while they are away. You can advertise your services on social media platforms such as Facebook or Instagram.

8. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a way to earn money by promoting other people's products. You can sign up to affiliate marketing programs and promote products on your website or social media platforms. You earn a commission for every sale that is made through your affiliate link.

9. Online Gaming

If you are good at online gaming, you can earn money by participating in tournaments or streaming your gameplay. There are many websites that offer cash prizes for online gaming tournaments, such as WorldGaming or GamerSaloon.

10. Sell Unused Items

If you have items that you no longer use, you can sell them online. There are many websites that allow you to sell items, such as eBay or Craigslist. You can also sell items on social media platforms such as Facebook or Instagram.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are many good ways for kids to make money during quarantine. Whether you are good at tutoring, writing, or social media management, there are many opportunities available. By taking advantage of these opportunities, you can earn money while staying safe at home.
