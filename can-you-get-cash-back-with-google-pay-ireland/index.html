Can You Get Cash Back With Google Pay Ireland

Written by Aprilia Aug 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Google Pay is a popular payment app that allows users to pay for their purchases without the need for cash or physical cards. But can you get cash back with Google Pay in Ireland? The short answer is yes, but there are some limitations and requirements that you need to know.

What is Google Pay?

Google Pay is a digital wallet and payment app developed by Google. It allows users to store their credit and debit cards, loyalty cards, and other payment methods in one place. You can use Google Pay to make purchases online, in stores, and even send money to friends and family.

How to Get Cash Back with Google Pay in Ireland

If you want to get cash back with Google Pay in Ireland, you need to have a debit card linked to your Google Pay account. Not all banks in Ireland support Google Pay, so make sure to check with your bank first.

Once your debit card is linked to your Google Pay account, you can start using it to make purchases at participating merchants. Some merchants offer cash back as a reward for using Google Pay. The cash back amount varies depending on the merchant and the promotion.

To get cash back, simply select your linked debit card in the Google Pay app when making a purchase. After completing the transaction, the cash back amount will be credited to your linked debit card account.

Limitations and Requirements

While it is possible to get cash back with Google Pay in Ireland, there are some limitations and requirements that you need to be aware of. First, not all merchants support Google Pay, so make sure to check the app for participating merchants before making a purchase.

Second, the cash back amount is usually limited to a certain amount per transaction or per day. This means that you may not be able to get cash back for larger purchases.

Third, you need to have a debit card linked to your Google Pay account to get cash back. Credit cards and other payment methods are not eligible for cash back rewards.

Tips and Tricks

If you want to maximize your cash back rewards with Google Pay, here are some tips and tricks:

  • Check the app for participating merchants before making a purchase.
  • Look for promotions and special offers that offer higher cash back rewards.
  • Use Google Pay for everyday purchases like groceries and gas to maximize your rewards.
  • Link multiple debit cards to your Google Pay account to maximize your cash back rewards.

Conclusion

Getting cash back with Google Pay in Ireland is possible, but it requires a linked debit card and some limitations apply. Make sure to check with your bank and the app for participating merchants and promotions to maximize your rewards.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make the most out of your Google Pay account and get cash back rewards for your purchases.
