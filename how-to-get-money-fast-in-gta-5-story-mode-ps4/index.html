1. Complete the Story Missions

One of the easiest and most straightforward ways to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4 is to complete the story missions. As you progress through the game, you'll earn money for completing each mission, and the amount you earn will increase as you get further into the game.

Tip:

If you're having trouble completing a mission, try replaying it a few times. You'll get better at it each time, and you'll also earn more money.

2. Rob Stores

Another great way to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4 is to rob stores. Simply walk into a store, point your gun at the cashier, and demand that they give you all the money in the register. You can rob a store multiple times, but be careful not to get caught by the police.

Tip:

If you want to rob a store without getting caught, try wearing a mask or changing your clothes before you do it. This will make it harder for the police to identify you.

3. Invest in the Stock Market

If you're looking for a more long-term way to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4, consider investing in the stock market. Pay attention to the in-game news and invest in companies that are doing well. As their stock prices increase, so will your profits.

Tip:

Be patient when investing in the stock market. It can take some time for your investments to pay off, but the payoff can be significant.

4. Complete Side Missions

In addition to the main story missions, there are also a number of side missions that you can complete to earn money. These missions range from delivering packages to stealing cars, and they can be a great way to earn some extra cash.

Tip:

Make sure to check your phone regularly for new side missions. They'll pop up as you progress through the game.

5. Sell Cars

If you're looking for a quick way to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4, consider selling cars. Simply steal a car and take it to a garage that accepts stolen vehicles. You'll earn money for each car you sell.

Tip:

Make sure to choose a garage that pays well for stolen vehicles. Some garages pay more than others.

6. Complete Heists

Heists are some of the most exciting missions in GTA 5 story mode on PS4, and they can also be a great way to make money. During a heist, you'll need to plan and execute a robbery, and you'll earn a large sum of money if you're successful.

Tip:

Make sure to choose your crew carefully when planning a heist. Each member of your crew has different skills, and choosing the right crew can make all the difference.

7. Hunt for Bounties

If you're looking for a fun way to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4, consider hunting for bounties. Bounties are placed on players who have committed crimes, and you can earn money by tracking them down and capturing them.

Tip:

Bounties can be difficult to find, so make sure to check your phone regularly for new bounties.

8. Participate in Races

Racing is another fun way to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4. Simply enter a race and try to finish in first place. You'll earn money for each race you win.

Tip:

Make sure to choose the right vehicle for each race. Some vehicles are better suited for certain types of races than others.

9. Play the Stock Market

In addition to investing in the stock market, you can also play the stock market in GTA 5 story mode on PS4. Simply buy and sell stocks on a regular basis, and you'll earn money as the prices fluctuate.

Tip:

Pay attention to the in-game news and invest in companies that are likely to do well in the future.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money in GTA 5 story mode on PS4, and the methods you choose will depend on your play style and preferences. Whether you prefer to complete story missions, rob stores, or invest in the stock market, there are plenty of opportunities to earn money in the game. So get out there and start making some cash!
