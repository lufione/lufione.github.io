How To Make Money From Instagram Filters In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read

As social media continues to dominate our lives, Instagram has become one of the most popular platforms for sharing photos and videos. But did you know that you can also make money from Instagram filters? In this article, we’ll show you how to create and sell your own filters on Instagram.

What are Instagram Filters?

Instagram filters are visual effects that can be applied to photos and videos on the platform. They can change the color, tone, and overall look of your content. Instagram filters are created using Spark AR Studio, which is a free software developed by Facebook.

How to Create Instagram Filters?

To create Instagram filters, you will need to download Spark AR Studio and learn the basics of 3D modeling and programming. There are plenty of tutorials available online that can help you get started. Once you’ve created your filter, you can submit it to Instagram for approval.

How to Sell Instagram Filters?

Once your filter is approved, you can sell it on the Instagram Filter Store. The store is a marketplace where users can purchase filters created by other users. To sell your filter, you will need to create a developer account on Facebook and link it to your Instagram account.

How Much Money Can You Make from Instagram Filters?

The amount of money you can make from Instagram filters depends on how popular your filter is. Instagram takes a cut of 30% from each sale, and the rest goes to the filter creator. Some filters have sold for thousands of dollars, while others may only make a few hundred dollars.

How to Promote Your Instagram Filters?

To promote your Instagram filters, you can share them on your Instagram profile and encourage your followers to try them out. You can also reach out to influencers and ask them to promote your filters to their audience. Another option is to run Instagram ads to promote your filters to a wider audience.

What are Some Tips and Tricks for Creating Instagram Filters?

Here are some tips and tricks to help you create Instagram filters that stand out:

1. Keep it simple:

Make sure your filter is easy to use and doesn’t require too much effort from the user.

2. Be creative:

Experiment with different colors, shapes, and textures to create a unique filter.

3. Test it out:

Before submitting your filter, test it out on different devices to make sure it works properly.

4. Have fun:

Creating Instagram filters should be a fun and creative process, so don’t be afraid to experiment and try new things.

FAQs

1. Do I need to be a professional programmer to create Instagram filters?

No, you don’t need to be a professional programmer to create Instagram filters. There are plenty of tutorials available online that can help you learn the basics of 3D modeling and programming.

2. Can I sell my Instagram filters on other platforms?

No, Instagram filters can only be sold on the Instagram Filter Store.

3. How long does it take for Instagram to approve my filter?

The approval process can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on the complexity of your filter.

4. Can I make changes to my filter after it has been approved?

Yes, you can make changes to your filter after it has been approved, but you will need to resubmit it for approval.

5. Do I need to pay to submit my filter for approval?

No, submitting your filter for approval is free.

Now that you know how to create and sell Instagram filters, it’s time to get started. With a bit of creativity and hard work, you could be on your way to making money from your own filters on Instagram.
