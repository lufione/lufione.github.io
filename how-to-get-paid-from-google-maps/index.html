How To Get Paid From Google Maps

Written by Christine Apr 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Google Maps is one of the most popular navigation apps. It has become an essential tool for people to find their way around the city, discover new places, and explore different neighborhoods. However, did you know that you can also earn money from Google Maps?

What is Google Local Guide?

Google Local Guide is a program that allows you to contribute to Google Maps by writing reviews, adding photos, and updating information about local businesses. As a local guide, you can earn points for your contributions, which can be redeemed for rewards such as free Google Drive storage, early access to new Google products, and even cash.

How to Become a Google Local Guide?

To become a Google Local Guide, you need to sign up for the program on the Google Maps app or website. Once you have signed up, you can start contributing to Google Maps by writing reviews, adding photos, and updating information about local businesses.

The more you contribute, the higher your level will be, and the more rewards you will receive. For example, if you reach level 4, you will get early access to new Google products, and if you reach level 5, you will get a badge and a chance to be featured on Google’s official channels.

How to Earn Money from Google Maps?

Google Local Guides can earn money by participating in Google surveys. Google surveys are short questionnaires that ask you about your recent experiences at local businesses. The surveys are designed to help Google improve its services and provide better recommendations to its users.

For each survey, you complete you will earn Google Play credits, which can be used to purchase apps, games, and other digital content from the Google Play Store. You can also earn cash rewards for completing surveys, which will be paid into your PayPal account.

Conclusion

If you enjoy exploring your local neighborhood and want to earn some extra cash, becoming a Google Local Guide is an excellent way to do so. By contributing to Google Maps, you can help others discover new places and earn rewards for yourself. So, what are you waiting for? Sign up for the program today and start exploring!
