Can You Buy Shares In Ufc Fighters

Written by Eveline Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

If you're a fan of the UFC, you might be wondering if you can invest in your favorite fighters. After all, investing in sports has become increasingly popular in recent years. The good news is that you can buy shares in UFC fighters, but the process is not as straightforward as it might seem. In this article, we'll explore the ins and outs of investing in UFC fighters.

What Are UFC Fighter Shares?

UFC fighter shares are essentially investments in the future earnings of a fighter. These shares are typically sold by a promotion or management company, and investors can buy a percentage of a fighter's future earnings. The idea is that if the fighter becomes successful, the investor will receive a return on their investment.

How Do You Buy UFC Fighter Shares?

Unfortunately, buying UFC fighter shares is not as easy as buying stocks on the stock market. Currently, there is no centralized platform for buying and selling fighter shares. Instead, investors must work directly with the promotion or management company that is offering the shares.

Who Can Buy UFC Fighter Shares?

In order to buy UFC fighter shares, you typically need to be an accredited investor. This means that you must meet certain financial criteria, such as having a net worth of at least $1 million or an annual income of at least $200,000. This requirement is in place to protect investors from making risky investments that they cannot afford.

What Are the Risks of Buying UFC Fighter Shares?

Like any investment, buying UFC fighter shares comes with risks. Fighters can get injured or lose matches, which can greatly impact their future earnings potential. Additionally, the UFC is a relatively new sport compared to more established sports like football or basketball, which means that there is a higher level of uncertainty when it comes to predicting the future success of fighters.

What Are the Benefits of Buying UFC Fighter Shares?

The biggest benefit of buying UFC fighter shares is the potential for high returns. If a fighter becomes successful and earns a lot of money, investors can see significant returns on their investment. Additionally, investing in UFC fighter shares can be a fun and exciting way to get involved in the sport.

How Do You Evaluate Fighters for Investment?

When evaluating fighters for investment, it's important to consider their current level of success, their age, and their potential for future success. You should also consider any injuries or other factors that could impact their future earnings potential.

What Are Some Companies That Offer UFC Fighter Shares?

Currently, there are a few companies that offer UFC fighter shares, including Fighters Share and MMA Shares. These companies work directly with fighters to offer shares to investors.

Conclusion

Investing in UFC fighter shares can be a risky but potentially lucrative venture. While there are currently no centralized platforms for buying and selling fighter shares, investors can work directly with promotion or management companies to invest in their favorite fighters. As with any investment, it's important to do your research and evaluate the potential risks and rewards before making a decision.
