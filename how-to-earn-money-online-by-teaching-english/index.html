How To Earn Money Online By Teaching English

Written by Petter May 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Introduction

Teaching English online has become increasingly popular over the years, especially since the pandemic has forced many people to work from home. If you're a native English speaker or have a strong command of the language, you can earn money by teaching English online.

Requirements

Before you start teaching English online, you need to have a few things in place. Firstly, you need to have a reliable internet connection and a computer/laptop. You'll also need to have a webcam and microphone, as most online classes are conducted via video conferencing software like Skype or Zoom.

Choose the Right Platform

There are many platforms available for teaching English online, including VIPKid, Cambly, and iTalki. Each platform has its own requirements and pay rates, so it's important to do your research and choose the one that works best for you.

Create a Profile

Once you've chosen a platform, you'll need to create a profile. This is where you'll showcase your skills and experience, and potential students will be able to view your profile and decide if they want to take classes with you. Make sure to include a professional-looking photo and a clear and concise description of your teaching experience.

Set Your Rates

Most online teaching platforms allow you to set your own rates. It's important to do some research and see what other teachers are charging for their services. You don't want to charge too high and scare away potential students, but at the same time, you don't want to undervalue your services.

Prepare Your Lessons

Before you start teaching, you'll need to prepare your lessons. Make sure to tailor your lessons to your students' needs and skill levels. You can use textbooks, online resources, or create your own materials. It's important to be flexible and willing to adapt your lessons based on your students' progress.

Teach Your Classes

Once you've prepared your lessons, it's time to start teaching. Make sure to be punctual and professional, and create a positive and engaging learning environment. Encourage your students to ask questions and provide feedback.

Get Feedback

After each class, ask your students for feedback. This will help you improve your teaching skills and make necessary changes to your lessons.

Promote Your Services

To attract more students, it's important to promote your services. You can use social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram, or create a website promoting your services. Make sure to include testimonials from satisfied students.

Conclusion

Teaching English online is a great way to earn money from the comfort of your own home. With the right platform, preparation, and teaching skills, you can make a decent income while helping others learn the English language.
