7. Play Mini-Games

Adopt Me has a variety of mini-games that players can play for rewards. These games may include racing, fishing, and more. By playing mini-games, you can earn money and other rewards.

8. Host a Lemonade Stand

9. Join a Group

10. Be Patient

Finally, it is important to be patient when trying to earn money in Adopt Me. Rome wasn't built in a day, and neither is your virtual fortune. Keep playing the game, and eventually, you will start to see your bank account grow.
