How To Receive Money From Youtube In Pakistan

Written by Bardi May 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

YouTube has become a popular platform for content creators to showcase their skills and earn money. However, receiving money from YouTube in Pakistan can be challenging due to various restrictions. In this article, we will guide you through the process of receiving money from YouTube in Pakistan.

Eligibility Criteria

To receive money from YouTube, you need to meet certain eligibility criteria. Firstly, you must have a YouTube channel with at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the last 12 months. Secondly, you must have an AdSense account linked to your YouTube channel. Lastly, you must comply with YouTube's community guidelines and terms of service.

Setting Up an AdSense Account

To receive money from YouTube, you need to have an AdSense account. To set up an AdSense account, go to the AdSense website and sign up. You will need to provide your personal and payment information. Once your account is approved, link it to your YouTube channel by going to the YouTube Studio and clicking on the Monetization tab.

Monetizing Your Videos

To monetize your videos, go to the YouTube Studio and click on the Monetization tab. Enable monetization for your videos by clicking on the switch button. You can also choose the types of ads you want to display on your videos.

Ad Revenue Sharing

YouTube shares a portion of the ad revenue generated from your videos. The revenue sharing percentage depends on various factors such as the type of ad, the advertiser, and the country. In Pakistan, the revenue sharing percentage is around 55% for standard ads and 45% for non-skippable ads.

Payment Threshold

To receive payment from YouTube, you need to reach the payment threshold. The payment threshold is $100 for Pakistan. Once you reach the payment threshold, YouTube will send the payment to your AdSense account. You can then withdraw the money through your preferred payment method.

Taxation

In Pakistan, YouTube revenue is subject to taxation. You need to declare your YouTube earnings in your income tax return and pay taxes accordingly. Failure to do so can result in penalties and legal consequences.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I receive money from YouTube without an AdSense account?
  • No, you need to have an AdSense account linked to your YouTube channel to receive money.
  • What is the minimum payment threshold for Pakistan?
  • The minimum payment threshold for Pakistan is $100.
  • Do I need to pay taxes on my YouTube earnings?
  • Yes, YouTube revenue is subject to taxation in Pakistan.

Tips and Tricks

  • Focus on creating quality content to increase your subscribers and watch hours.
  • Promote your videos on social media to reach a wider audience.
  • Engage with your viewers by responding to comments and feedback.
  • Stay up-to-date with YouTube's policies and guidelines to avoid any violations.

Conclusion

Receiving money from YouTube in Pakistan requires meeting certain eligibility criteria and following the monetization process. With the right strategies and dedication, you can earn a significant income from your YouTube channel. Remember to comply with YouTube's policies and guidelines and pay taxes on your earnings to avoid any legal consequences.
