How Can I Make Money As A 12 Year Old During Quarantine

Written by Robby Jul 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

The COVID-19 pandemic has forced many people to stay indoors, and for 12-year-olds, this means no more weekend activities or hanging out with friends. However, this doesn't mean that you can't make money while staying at home. There are many opportunities available to you, and here are some tips on how you can make money during the quarantine.

Sell Your Old Stuff Online

One easy way to make money during the quarantine is by selling your old stuff online. You can sell anything from old toys, clothes, and books to electronics and furniture. Websites like eBay, Amazon, and Craigslist are great platforms to sell your items. Make sure you take good photos and provide accurate descriptions of the items you're selling.

Start a YouTube Channel

If you have a passion for something, consider starting a YouTube channel. You can create videos about anything from cooking and gaming to fashion and beauty. If you get enough subscribers, you can monetize your channel and make money from ads. Just make sure you're following YouTube's guidelines and creating high-quality content.

Take Online Surveys

Another way to make money during the quarantine is by taking online surveys. Companies are always looking for feedback on their products and services, and they're willing to pay for it. Websites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, and Vindale Research offer paid surveys that you can take in your free time.

Offer Your Services

If you're good at something, offer your services to others. For example, if you're good at graphic design, you can create logos and designs for businesses. If you're good at writing, you can offer your services as a freelance writer. Websites like Fiverr and Upwork are great platforms to offer your services.

Start a Blog

If you enjoy writing, consider starting a blog. You can write about anything from your hobbies and interests to current events and news. If you get enough traffic to your blog, you can monetize it and make money from ads and sponsored content.

Participate in Online Contests

Many companies and organizations are hosting online contests during the quarantine. You can participate in these contests and win prizes or cash. Just make sure you're following the rules and regulations of the contest.

Offer Yard Work Services

If you're physically able, offer your yard work services to your neighbors. You can mow lawns, rake leaves, and weed gardens. Make sure you're following social distancing guidelines and wearing a mask.

Create and Sell Crafts

If you're artistic, consider creating and selling crafts. You can make anything from jewelry and paintings to pottery and sculptures. Websites like Etsy and Handmade at Amazon are great platforms to sell your crafts.

Walk Dogs or Pet Sit

If you're an animal lover, offer your services as a dog walker or pet sitter. Many people are working from home and need someone to take care of their pets while they're busy. Make sure you're following social distancing guidelines and wearing a mask.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make money as a 12-year-old during the quarantine. You can sell your old stuff online, start a YouTube channel, take online surveys, offer your services, start a blog, participate in online contests, offer yard work services, create and sell crafts, or walk dogs and pet sit. Just make sure you're following social distancing guidelines and wearing a mask when necessary.
