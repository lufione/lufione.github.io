How To Make Money Online By Watching Videos On Youtube

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

YouTube is the world's most popular video-sharing platform. With more than two billion monthly active users, it's a great place to make money online. One of the ways you can earn money on YouTube is by watching videos. In this article, we'll guide you on how to make money online by watching videos on YouTube.

What is YouTube?

YouTube is a video-sharing platform where people can upload, view, and share videos. It was founded in 2005 by three former PayPal employees and later acquired by Google in 2006. YouTube is free to use and has become a popular platform for people to share their thoughts, interests, and experiences.

How Do People Make Money on YouTube?

There are several ways people can make money on YouTube. One of the most popular ways is through advertising revenue. YouTube pays a percentage of the advertising revenue to creators who have monetized their videos. Creators can also make money through sponsorships, merchandise sales, and crowdfunding.

How Can You Make Money on YouTube by Watching Videos?

There are several ways you can make money on YouTube by watching videos. One of the most popular ways is through paid surveys. Companies pay people to watch videos and provide feedback. You can sign up for survey websites such as Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, and Vindale Research to start earning money by watching videos.

Step 1: Sign Up for Survey Websites

The first step to making money online by watching videos is to sign up for survey websites. You'll need to provide your personal information, such as your name, email address, and demographic information. Once you've signed up, you'll receive surveys that match your profile.

Step 2: Watch Videos and Provide Feedback

Once you've received a survey, you'll be asked to watch a video and provide feedback. The feedback can be in the form of multiple-choice questions, written responses, or video responses. You'll earn money for each survey you complete.

Step 3: Cash Out Your Earnings

Once you've earned enough money, you can cash out your earnings. Most survey websites pay through PayPal, gift cards, or bank transfer. You can use your earnings to buy things you need or save for a rainy day.

Tips and Tricks to Make More Money on YouTube

Here are some tips and tricks to make more money on YouTube:

  • Sign up for multiple survey websites to increase your chances of getting more surveys.
  • Complete surveys as soon as you receive them to avoid missing out on opportunities.
  • Provide detailed and honest feedback to increase your chances of getting more surveys in the future.
  • Share your referral link with friends and family to earn extra money.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making money online by watching videos on YouTube:

Is it really possible to make money by watching videos on YouTube?

Yes, it's possible to make money by watching videos on YouTube. You can sign up for survey websites and get paid to watch videos and provide feedback.

How much money can you make by watching videos on YouTube?

The amount of money you can make by watching videos on YouTube varies depending on the survey website and the number of surveys you complete. However, you can expect to earn between $0.50 to $5 per survey.

Is it safe to sign up for survey websites?

Yes, it's safe to sign up for survey websites. However, you should always read the terms and conditions before signing up and providing your personal information.

Conclusion

YouTube is a great platform to make money online. By watching videos and providing feedback, you can earn money and save for a rainy day. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to start making money on YouTube today!
