How To Make Money Online In Kenya With Your Phone

Written by William Apr 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

With the rise of smartphones and mobile data, making money online has become more accessible than ever. In Kenya, where mobile penetration is high, there are numerous opportunities for anyone with a phone to earn some extra cash. In this article, we will explore some of the ways you can make money online in Kenya using your phone.

1. Online Surveys

Online surveys are a popular way to make money online in Kenya. Companies are always looking for consumer feedback, and they are willing to pay for it. You can sign up for online survey sites like Toluna, Triaba, and SurveySavvy and start taking surveys. The pay for surveys varies, but you can earn anywhere from KES 50 to KES 500 per survey.

2. Freelancing

If you have a skill like writing, graphic design, or programming, you can offer your services on freelancing platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer. These platforms allow you to work with clients from all over the world and earn money from the comfort of your phone.

3. Online Tutoring

If you're knowledgeable in a particular subject, you can offer your services as an online tutor. There are numerous online tutoring platforms like Chegg, TutorMe, and Skooli that allow you to connect with students who need help with their studies. You can earn anywhere from KES 500 to KES 5000 per hour as an online tutor.

4. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a popular way to make money online in Kenya. You can sign up for affiliate programs like Jumia, Kilimall, and Amazon and promote their products on your social media platforms or blog. You earn a commission for every sale made through your unique affiliate link.

5. Online Trading

You can also make money online in Kenya by trading stocks, forex, and cryptocurrencies. There are numerous online trading platforms like eToro, Olymp Trade, and IQ Option that allow you to trade using your phone. However, trading is a high-risk activity, and you should only invest what you can afford to lose.

6. Online Writing

If you enjoy writing, you can make money online in Kenya by writing for blogs and websites. There are numerous online platforms like iWriter, HireWriters, and Textbroker that allow you to earn money for writing articles. The pay varies, but you can earn anywhere from KES 50 to KES 500 per article.

7. Online Selling

You can also make money online in Kenya by selling products on online marketplaces like Jumia, Kilimall, and Masoko. You can sell anything from clothes to electronics to food items. You can also sell your own handmade products on platforms like Etsy and Afrikrea.

8. Online Gaming

If you enjoy playing games, you can make money online in Kenya by playing games on platforms like SportPesa, Betika, and Betway. These platforms offer various games like sports betting, casino games, and virtual games. However, gambling can be addictive, and you should only play with what you can afford to lose.

9. Social Media Management

If you're good at social media, you can offer your services as a social media manager. You can manage social media accounts for businesses and individuals and earn money for it. You can find social media management jobs on freelancing platforms like Upwork and Fiverr.

10. Virtual Assistant

You can also make money online in Kenya by offering your services as a virtual assistant. Virtual assistants provide administrative support to businesses and individuals remotely. You can find virtual assistant jobs on freelancing platforms like Upwork and Fiverr.

Conclusion

Making money online in Kenya with your phone is possible, and there are numerous opportunities available. However, you should be careful and do your research before getting involved in any online activity. Always remember to only invest what you can afford to lose and never give out personal information to anyone online.
