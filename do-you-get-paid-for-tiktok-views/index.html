Do You Get Paid For Tiktok Views

Written by Daniel May 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Since its launch in 2016, TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world. With over 1 billion active users, it has become a hub for content creators and influencers. One question that many people have is whether or not they can get paid for their TikTok views. In this article, we’ll explore the answer to that question.

How TikTok Pays Its Creators

TikTok has a Creator Fund that pays eligible creators for their content. The fund was created to support and reward creators for their hard work and creativity. TikTok pays its creators based on their engagement, views, and followers. The more engagement a creator has, the more money they can make.

Eligibility for the Creator Fund

To be eligible for the Creator Fund, creators must meet certain requirements. They must be 18 years or older, have at least 100,000 followers, and have at least 100,000 video views in the past 30 days. Additionally, creators must follow TikTok’s Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

How Much Money Can You Make on TikTok?

The amount of money a creator can make on TikTok varies. TikTok does not disclose the exact amount they pay their creators, but it is reported to be around $0.01 to $0.02 per 1,000 views. So, if a video gets 100,000 views, a creator could make around $10 to $20.

Other Ways to Make Money on TikTok

Aside from the Creator Fund, there are other ways for creators to make money on TikTok. One way is through brand partnerships. Brands will pay creators to promote their products on their TikTok account. The amount of money paid for brand partnerships varies depending on the brand, the creator’s following, and the type of content that is being created.

Another way to make money on TikTok is through affiliate marketing. Creators can earn a commission by promoting products or services and including an affiliate link in their bio or video description. When a follower clicks on the link and makes a purchase, the creator earns a commission.

Conclusion

While TikTok does offer a way for creators to make money through the Creator Fund, it is not the only way. Creators can also make money through brand partnerships and affiliate marketing. It’s important to remember that making money on TikTok requires hard work, dedication, and creativity. By consistently creating engaging content and growing your following, you can increase your chances of making money on the platform.
