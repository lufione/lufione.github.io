How To Collect Your Money From Youtube In 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

YouTube is a popular video-sharing platform that allows content creators to monetize their videos and earn money. If you are a YouTuber, you may be wondering how to collect your earnings from the platform. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to collect your money from YouTube in 2023.

Step 1: Set up Your AdSense Account

The first step to collecting your earnings from YouTube is to set up your AdSense account. AdSense is a Google-owned platform that allows you to monetize your YouTube videos. To set up your AdSense account, go to the AdSense website and sign up using your Google account. Once your account is set up, link it to your YouTube channel by going to the "Monetization" tab in your YouTube Studio.

Step 2: Meet the Payment Threshold

Before you can collect your earnings from YouTube, you need to meet the payment threshold. The payment threshold is the minimum amount of money you need to earn before you can receive a payout. In 2023, the payment threshold for YouTube is $100.

Step 3: Enable Monetization for Your Videos

To start earning money on YouTube, you need to enable monetization for your videos. To do this, go to the "Monetization" tab in your YouTube Studio and turn on the monetization option for your videos. You can choose different types of ads to display on your videos, including display ads, overlay ads, and sponsored cards.

Step 4: Keep Track of Your Earnings

It is important to keep track of your earnings on YouTube to ensure that you are meeting the payment threshold and to monitor your progress. You can view your earnings in your AdSense account or in your YouTube Studio under the "Analytics" tab.

Step 5: Set Up Your Payment Method

Once you have met the payment threshold, you can set up your payment method to receive your earnings. YouTube offers several payment options, including direct deposit, wire transfer, and checks. Choose the payment method that works best for you and enter your payment details in your AdSense account.

Step 6: Wait for Your Payment to Arrive

After you have set up your payment method, you just need to wait for your payment to arrive. YouTube pays its creators monthly, so you can expect to receive your payment at the end of the month following the month in which you reached the payment threshold.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much money can I make on YouTube?

The amount of money you can make on YouTube depends on several factors, including the number of views on your videos, the type of ads you display, and the engagement of your audience. Some YouTubers make millions of dollars a year, while others make only a few hundred dollars.

What is the best type of ad to display on my videos?

The best type of ad to display on your videos depends on your audience and the type of content you create. Experiment with different types of ads, such as display ads, overlay ads, and sponsored cards, to see which ones perform best with your audience.

What should I do if I have not received my payment?

If you have not received your payment from YouTube, check your AdSense account to ensure that your payment details are correct. If your payment details are correct and you still have not received your payment, contact YouTube support for assistance.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you maximize your earnings on YouTube:

  • Create high-quality content that engages your audience.
  • Promote your videos on social media and other platforms.
  • Collaborate with other YouTubers to expand your audience.
  • Experiment with different types of ads to see which ones perform best.
  • Engage with your audience by responding to comments and creating community posts.

By following these tips and tricks and using the steps outlined in this article, you can collect your money from YouTube and start earning from your videos in 2023.
