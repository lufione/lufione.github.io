How to Earn Credits in Forza Horizon 4

Forza Horizon 4 is an open-world racing game that allows players to explore a vast map of the United Kingdom. As you progress through the game, you'll need to earn credits to purchase new cars and upgrade your existing ones. In this article, we'll show you some tips and tricks to help you earn more credits in Forza Horizon 4.

Complete Races and Championships

The easiest way to earn credits in Forza Horizon 4 is by completing races and championships. As you progress through the game, you'll unlock more races and championships that offer higher payouts. Be sure to check the rewards before starting a race or championship, as some offer more credits than others.

Participate in Events

Throughout the map, you'll come across various events that offer credit rewards. These events can range from speed traps and drift zones to danger signs and treasure hunts. Be sure to participate in as many events as possible to earn more credits.

Use Wheelspins and Super Wheelspins

Wheelspins and Super Wheelspins are a great way to earn credits in Forza Horizon 4. You can earn these spins by leveling up, completing certain challenges, or by purchasing them with in-game currency. These spins can reward you with credits, cars, or other prizes.

Sell Cars and Parts

If you have any cars or parts that you no longer need, you can sell them for credits. To do this, go to the Autoshow or Garage and select the car or part you wish to sell. You'll receive a percentage of the original purchase price in credits.

Complete Forzathon Challenges

Forzathon Challenges are daily and weekly challenges that offer credit rewards. These challenges can range from completing a certain number of races to reaching a certain speed in a specific car. Be sure to check the Forzathon menu regularly to see what challenges are available.

Join a Club

Joining a Club in Forza Horizon 4 can offer a variety of benefits, including credit rewards. Clubs can participate in events and challenges together, which can earn them club points. These points can be used to unlock rewards, including credits.

Invest in the Festival Site

The Festival Site is the main hub for Forza Horizon 4, and it offers various upgrades that can increase your credit earnings. These upgrades include increasing the number of wheelspins you can earn, increasing the credit payout for races, and unlocking new events. Be sure to invest in the Festival Site as soon as possible to maximize your credit earnings.

Complete Horizon Stories

Horizon Stories are a series of missions that offer credit rewards upon completion. These stories can range from delivering pizzas to racing a hovercraft. Be sure to complete as many Horizon Stories as possible to earn more credits.

Use the Auction House

The Auction House is a great way to earn credits by selling cars to other players. If you have a rare or highly sought-after car, you can sell it for a substantial amount of credits. Be sure to check the Auction House regularly to see what cars are in demand.

Conclusion

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to earning more credits in Forza Horizon 4. Remember to participate in races and events, use wheelspins and Forzathon challenges, and invest in the Festival Site to maximize your credit earnings. With a little bit of effort, you'll be able to purchase the cars of your dreams and dominate the open roads of Forza Horizon 4.
