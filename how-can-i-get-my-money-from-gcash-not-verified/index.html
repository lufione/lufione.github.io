How To Get Your Money From Gcash If Your Account Is Not Verified

Written by Frank May 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're using GCash as your mobile wallet, you know how convenient it is to pay bills, buy load, and transfer money to other GCash users. However, if your account is not verified, you might encounter some limitations when it comes to cashing out your money. In this article, we'll guide you on how to get your money from GCash if your account is not verified.

What is GCash Verification?

GCash verification is the process of confirming your identity and linking your GCash account to your personal details. To verify your account, you need to submit valid identification documents and fill out a form on the GCash app or website. Once your account is verified, you can enjoy higher transaction limits and access to all GCash services.

What are the Limitations of an Unverified GCash Account?

If your GCash account is not verified, you can still use GCash for transactions, but you will have some limitations:

  • You can only cash in up to Php 8,000 per month
  • You can only transact up to Php 40,000 per month
  • You cannot cash out your money

How to Cash Out Your Money from GCash if Your Account is Not Verified

If you need to get your money from GCash, but your account is not verified, you have two options:

Option 1: Send Money to Another GCash User

You can transfer your money to another GCash user who has a verified account. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Open your GCash app
  2. Tap "Send Money"
  3. Enter the GCash number of the user you want to send money to
  4. Enter the amount you want to send
  5. Tap "Send"

The recipient will receive the money instantly, and they can cash it out from their verified GCash account.

Option 2: Use GCash Mastercard

If you have a GCash Mastercard, you can withdraw your money from any BancNet or Mastercard ATM. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Insert your GCash Mastercard into the ATM
  2. Enter your PIN
  3. Select "Withdraw"
  4. Enter the amount you want to withdraw
  5. Take your cash

Note that using GCash Mastercard has fees and transaction limits. Make sure to check the terms and conditions before using it.

How to Verify Your GCash Account

If you want to avoid the limitations of an unverified GCash account, you can verify your account by following these steps:

  1. Open your GCash app
  2. Tap "Verify Now"
  3. Fill out the form with your personal details
  4. Upload a photo of your valid ID
  5. Submit the form

GCash verification usually takes up to 24 hours. Once your account is verified, you can enjoy higher transaction limits and access to all GCash services.

Conclusion

If your GCash account is not verified, you still have options to get your money. You can transfer it to another GCash user or use your GCash Mastercard to withdraw it from an ATM. However, if you want to avoid limitations and enjoy all GCash services, make sure to verify your account by submitting valid identification documents and filling out the verification form.
