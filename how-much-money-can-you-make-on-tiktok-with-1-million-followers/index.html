How Much Money Can You Make On Tiktok With 1 Million Followers In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in recent years. With over 1 billion active users, it has created a new wave of content creators, many of whom have amassed millions of followers. One of the most common questions among TikTok creators is how much money they can make with 1 million followers. In this article, we’ll explore the potential earnings of TikTok creators with 1 million followers in 2023.

How TikTok Creators Make Money

TikTok creators can make money in a variety of ways. One of the most common ways is through brand partnerships. Brands pay creators to promote their products or services on their TikTok account. Another way is through the TikTok Creator Fund, which pays creators based on the views and engagement of their videos. Additionally, creators can earn money through merchandise sales and live streaming.

Brand Partnerships

Brand partnerships are one of the most lucrative ways for TikTok creators to make money. Brands are willing to pay creators with large followings to promote their products or services on their TikTok account. The amount of money a creator can make from a brand partnership depends on the brand, the creator’s niche, and their audience.

TikTok Creator Fund

The TikTok Creator Fund is a program that pays eligible creators based on their video views and engagement. The program is open to creators with at least 100,000 followers and 100,000 video views in the last 30 days. The amount of money a creator can earn from the Creator Fund varies based on their location and the engagement of their videos.

Merchandise Sales

Many TikTok creators have started selling merchandise to their followers. This can include anything from t-shirts to phone cases. Creators can use services like Teespring or Shopify to create and sell their merchandise. The amount of money a creator can make from merchandise sales depends on the cost of the product and the number of sales.

Live Streaming

Live streaming is another way for TikTok creators to make money. Creators can receive virtual gifts from their viewers during live streams. The cost of the virtual gifts varies, but creators can earn a percentage of the revenue from the gifts.

How Much Money Can You Make with 1 Million Followers?

The amount of money a TikTok creator can make with 1 million followers varies. According to Influencer Marketing Hub, creators with 1 million followers can earn between $10,000 and $20,000 per sponsored post. However, this number can vary based on the brand and the creator’s niche. Additionally, creators can earn money through the TikTok Creator Fund, merchandise sales, and live streaming, which can add to their overall earnings.

Factors that Impact Earnings

There are several factors that can impact a TikTok creator’s earnings with 1 million followers. These include their niche, engagement rate, location, and the brands they work with. Creators in popular niches like fashion and beauty may earn more than creators in smaller niches. Additionally, creators with high engagement rates may earn more from the TikTok Creator Fund and brand partnerships.

Conclusion

In conclusion, TikTok creators with 1 million followers have the potential to earn a significant amount of money through brand partnerships, the TikTok Creator Fund, merchandise sales, and live streaming. The amount of money a creator can make varies based on several factors, but they can expect to earn between $10,000 and $20,000 per sponsored post. As TikTok continues to grow in popularity, the earnings of creators with large followings are likely to increase as well.
