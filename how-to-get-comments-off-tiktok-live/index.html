How To Get Comments Off Tiktok Live In 2023

Written by Frank May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

If you're an avid TikTok user, you might have tried going live on the platform. When you go live, your followers can interact with you through comments. However, sometimes it's overwhelming to keep track of all the comments. In this article, we'll show you how to get comments off TikTok live in 2023.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start Your Live Video

To get comments off TikTok live, you first need to start your live video. Tap the "+" icon in the middle of the bottom navigation bar, then select "Live."

Step 2: Enable Comment Moderation

Before you go live, you can enable comment moderation. This will allow you to filter out comments that contain certain keywords or phrases. To do this, go to your profile and tap the three-dot icon in the top right corner. Then, select "Settings and Privacy"> "Privacy"> "Comment Filters." Turn on "Enable Comment Filters" and add the keywords or phrases you want to filter out.

Step 3: View Comments

During your live video, you'll see comments popping up on the right side of the screen. To view all the comments, swipe up on the comments section.

Step 4: Pin Comments

If you want to highlight a specific comment, you can pin it to the top of the comments section. To do this, swipe left on the comment you want to pin, then tap the "Pin" button.

Step 5: Reply to Comments

To reply to a comment, tap on it and type your response. Your reply will appear as a comment under the original comment.

Step 6: Remove Comments

If you see a comment that you don't want on your live video, you can remove it. Swipe left on the comment, then tap the "Remove" button. You can also report the comment if it violates TikTok's community guidelines.

Step 7: End Your Live Video

When you're ready to end your live video, tap the "X" icon in the top right corner.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Use Comment Filters

Comment filters can help you manage the comments on your live video. You can filter out keywords or phrases that you don't want to appear in the comments section.

Tip 2: Pin Important Comments

If someone leaves a particularly insightful or funny comment, consider pinning it to the top of the comments section. This will highlight the comment and show your appreciation for the user's engagement.

Tip 3: Respond to Comments

Engaging with your followers is key to building a strong TikTok community. Try to respond to as many comments as you can during your live video.

FAQs

Q: Can I turn off comments during my live video?

A: No, you can't turn off comments completely during your live video. However, you can use comment filters to filter out certain keywords or phrases.

Q: Can I block a user from commenting on my live video?

A: Yes, you can block a user from commenting on your live video. Swipe left on their comment, then tap the "Block" button.

Q: Can I report a comment that violates TikTok's community guidelines?

A: Yes, you can report a comment that violates TikTok's community guidelines. Swipe left on the comment, then tap the "Report" button.

Conclusion

Managing comments on your TikTok live video can be overwhelming, but with these tips and tricks, you can easily get comments off TikTok live in 2023. Remember to engage with your followers and use comment filters to make your live video experience more enjoyable.
