How To Turn Off Comments On A Tiktok

Written by Frank May 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Introduction

TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to create and share short videos. While the app is known for its creative content, some users may want to turn off comments on their videos to avoid negativity or spam. In this article, we’ll provide step-by-step instructions on how to turn off comments on a TikTok.

Step 1: Open the TikTok App

To turn off comments on a TikTok video, open the app on your mobile device and navigate to your profile page. From there, select the video you want to turn off comments for.

Step 2: Tap on the Video

Once you have selected the video, tap on it to open it in full screen mode. You should see a series of icons at the bottom of the screen, including a heart, speech bubble, and arrow.

Step 3: Tap on the Speech Bubble Icon

The speech bubble icon represents the comments section. Tap on it to open the comments section for the video.

Step 4: Tap on the Three Dots Icon

Once you have opened the comments section, tap on the three dots icon located at the top right corner of the screen. This will open a menu of options for the video.

Step 5: Select Privacy Settings

From the menu of options, select “Privacy Settings”. This will take you to a new screen where you can adjust the privacy settings for the video.

Step 6: Turn Off Comments

To turn off comments for the video, toggle the “Turn Off Commenting” switch to the on position. This will disable comments for the video and prevent users from leaving comments.

Step 7: Save Changes

Once you have turned off comments, be sure to save your changes by tapping the “Save” button located at the top right corner of the screen.

Step 8: Verify Comments Are Disabled

To verify that comments are disabled for the video, go back to the video and tap on the speech bubble icon. You should see a message that says “Comments are disabled for this video”.

FAQs

1. Can I turn off comments for all my TikTok videos?

Yes, you can turn off comments for all your TikTok videos by adjusting your account’s privacy settings.

2. Can I turn off comments for a specific group of users?

No, you can only turn off comments for individual videos.

3. Can I turn off comments for a video after it has been posted?

Yes, you can turn off comments for a video at any time, even after it has been posted.

Tips and Tricks

1. Consider turning off comments for sensitive or controversial content

If you are sharing sensitive or controversial content, consider turning off comments to prevent negative or inappropriate feedback.

2. Use the “Friends” privacy setting to limit comments to people you know

If you want to limit comments to people you know, use the “Friends” privacy setting. This will allow only your friends to leave comments on your videos.

3. Remember to turn comments back on if desired

If you turn off comments for a video, remember to turn them back on if you want to allow feedback or interaction from other users. In conclusion, turning off comments on a TikTok video is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. By following the instructions in this article, you can prevent negative or spam comments and maintain control over your content.
