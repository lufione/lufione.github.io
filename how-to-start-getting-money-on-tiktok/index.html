How To Start Getting Money On Tiktok In 2023

Written by William Jun 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Introduction

TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world, with millions of active users. It has also become a source of income for many people who have used the platform to showcase their talents and build a following. In this article, we will discuss how you can start earning money on TikTok in 2023.

Understanding TikTok

Before you can start making money on TikTok, you need to understand how the platform works. TikTok is a short-form video platform that allows users to create and share 15 to 60-second videos. The videos can be about anything, from lip-syncing to dancing, comedy, or educational content.

Step 1: Create a TikTok Account

The first step to making money on TikTok is to create an account. You can do this by downloading the TikTok app on your phone and signing up with your email or phone number. Once you have created an account, you can start creating and sharing videos.

Step 2: Build a Following

To start earning money on TikTok, you need to have a following. The more followers you have, the more likely you are to get noticed by brands and advertisers. To build a following, you need to create engaging content that people will enjoy watching and share.

Step 3: Join the TikTok Creator Fund

In 2023, TikTok will continue to offer its Creator Fund to eligible creators. The Creator Fund is a program that pays creators for their content. To join the Creator Fund, you need to have at least 100,000 followers and meet other eligibility criteria.

Step 4: Participate in Brand Deals

Another way to make money on TikTok is to participate in brand deals. Brands and advertisers are always looking for influencers to promote their products and services. If you have a large following, you can reach out to brands or participate in influencer marketing platforms to find brand deals.

Step 5: Sell Merchandise

If you have a strong brand and following on TikTok, you can sell merchandise to your fans. You can create and sell merchandise such as t-shirts, hats, or mugs with your brand name or logo on them.

Conclusion

In conclusion, TikTok offers many opportunities for people to make money. By creating engaging content, building a following, joining the Creator Fund, participating in brand deals, and selling merchandise, you can start earning money on TikTok in 2023. It takes time and effort to build a following, but with dedication and hard work, you can achieve success on the platform.
