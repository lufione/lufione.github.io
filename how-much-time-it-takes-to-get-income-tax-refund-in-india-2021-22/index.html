How Much Time It Takes To Get Income Tax Refund In India 2021 22

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Getting an income tax refund is always a happy moment for taxpayers in India. It means you have paid more tax than you were required to for the financial year. However, the waiting period for receiving the refund can be a bit of a hassle. In this article, we will discuss how much time it takes to get an income tax refund in India for the financial year 2021-22.

What is an Income Tax Refund?

An income tax refund is a reimbursement of excess tax paid by the taxpayer during the financial year. The refund is issued by the Income Tax Department after verifying your income tax return (ITR) with the details submitted by your employer or other sources of income.

How to claim an Income Tax Refund?

To claim an income tax refund, you need to file your income tax return (ITR) for the financial year in which the excess tax was paid. If you have paid more tax than what was required, the IT department will process your ITR and issue a refund. You can claim an income tax refund online or offline, depending on your convenience.

How much time it takes to get an Income Tax Refund?

The time taken to get an income tax refund in India depends on various factors, such as the accuracy of your ITR, the number of refunds being processed by the IT department, and the mode of refund. Typically, it takes around 20-45 days to get an income tax refund in India.

Factors affecting the time taken to get an Income Tax Refund

1. Accuracy of ITR – If there are any errors or discrepancies in your ITR, it can delay the processing of your refund.

2. Number of refunds being processed – During the peak tax filing season, the IT department receives a large number of ITRs, which can delay the processing of refunds.

3. Mode of refund – Refunds can be issued through various modes such as NEFT, RTGS, or cheque. The time taken to receive the refund varies depending on the mode of refund chosen by the taxpayer.

How to check the status of Income Tax Refund?

You can check the status of your income tax refund online by visiting the official website of the Income Tax Department. You need to provide your PAN number and select the relevant assessment year to check the status of your refund.

What to do if the Income Tax Refund is delayed?

If your income tax refund is delayed, you can contact the Income Tax Department through their toll-free number or email. You can also visit the nearest Income Tax office to enquire about the status of your refund.

Conclusion

Getting an income tax refund is always a relief for taxpayers in India. The time taken to receive the refund depends on various factors such as the accuracy of your ITR, the number of refunds being processed, and the mode of refund chosen. Typically, it takes around 20-45 days to get an income tax refund in India. If your refund is delayed, you can always contact the IT department for assistance.
