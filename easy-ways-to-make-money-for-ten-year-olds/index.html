Easy Ways To Make Money For Ten Year Olds

Written by Aprilia Sep 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a ten year old looking to make some extra cash? Maybe you want to save up for a new toy or treat your family to a nice dinner. Whatever your reason, there are plenty of ways for you to make money without leaving your neighborhood. Here are some easy ways to get started:

1. Dog Walking

Do you love animals? Offer your services as a dog walker to your neighbors. You can charge per walk or offer a weekly package. Make sure to bring plenty of water and poop bags on your walks. Always ask the dog owner about any special instructions or requirements before taking their pet out.

2. Lemonade Stand

Set up a lemonade stand on a hot summer day. Ask your parents for help with supplies and pricing. Make sure to have plenty of cups and ice on hand. You can also offer other treats like cookies or brownies to increase your profits.

3. Yard Work

Offer your services for yard work like raking leaves, pulling weeds, or mowing lawns. Make sure to bring your own tools and gloves. Ask your parents to help you create a pricing list and flyer to distribute to your neighbors.

4. Car Washing

Offer to wash your neighbors’ cars for a fee. Make sure to use proper cleaning supplies and ask for permission before using their water source. You can also offer additional services like waxing or vacuuming for an extra fee.

5. Babysitting

If you are responsible and enjoy spending time with younger children, offer your services as a babysitter. Make sure to have your parents help you create a list of your qualifications and experience. Always make sure to follow the parents’ instructions and keep the children safe.

6. Recycling

Collect recyclable materials like cans and bottles from your neighborhood and turn them in for cash. Make sure to ask your parents for help with transportation and sorting. You can also offer to collect materials from your neighbors for an extra fee.

7. Tutoring

If you excel in a certain subject, offer your services as a tutor to younger students. Make sure to have your parents help you create a list of your qualifications and experience. Always make sure to follow the parents’ instructions and help the student improve their understanding of the subject.

8. Garage Sale

Host a garage sale with items you no longer need or want. Ask your parents for help with pricing and organization. You can also offer to help your neighbors with their own garage sales for a percentage of their profits.

9. Social Media Management

If you are tech-savvy, offer your services to manage social media accounts for local businesses. Make sure to have your parents help you create a list of your qualifications and experience. Always make sure to follow the business’ instructions and create engaging content for their followers.

10. Photography

If you have a talent for photography, offer your services for events like birthday parties or family portraits. Make sure to have your parents help you create a pricing list and portfolio. Always make sure to follow the clients’ instructions and capture their desired moments.

Conclusion

With a little bit of creativity and hard work, there are plenty of easy ways for ten year olds to make money. Make sure to have your parents’ help and follow all safety guidelines. Good luck!
