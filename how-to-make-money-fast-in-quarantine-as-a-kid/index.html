How To Make Money Fast In Quarantine As A Kid

Written by Aprilia Aug 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

The COVID-19 pandemic has caused a lot of financial strain for many families. As a kid, you may be wondering how you can help out and make some extra money during these difficult times. Fortunately, there are several ways that you can earn money from the safety and comfort of your own home. Here are some tips on how to make money fast in quarantine as a kid:

1. Sell Items Online

If you have any old toys, books, or clothes that you no longer use, consider selling them online. You can use platforms like eBay, Amazon, or Facebook Marketplace to sell your items. Make sure to take clear pictures and write detailed descriptions to attract buyers.

2. Offer Online Tutoring Services

If you excel in a particular subject, you can offer online tutoring services to other students. You can advertise your services on social media or tutoring websites like TutorMe or Chegg. Make sure to set reasonable rates and create a schedule that works for both you and your clients.

3. Complete Online Surveys

Many companies offer paid online surveys to gather feedback from consumers. You can sign up for websites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, or Vindale Research to start taking surveys and earning money. Just be aware that some surveys may require parental permission.

4. Offer Dog Walking or Pet Sitting Services

If you love animals, consider offering your services as a dog walker or pet sitter. You can advertise your services on social media or pet care websites like Rover or Care.com. Make sure to have a clear understanding of your responsibilities and any necessary safety precautions.

5. Create and Sell Crafts

If you're creative, you can create and sell crafts online. You can use platforms like Etsy or Redbubble to sell your handmade items. Make sure to price your items fairly and include accurate descriptions and pictures.

6. Offer Lawn Care Services

If you enjoy being outdoors, consider offering lawn care services to your neighbors. You can offer to mow lawns, rake leaves, or do other yard work. Make sure to have a clear understanding of your responsibilities and any necessary safety precautions.

7. Participate in Paid Focus Groups

Some companies offer paid focus groups to gather feedback on new products or services. You can sign up for websites like FocusGroup.com or FindFocusGroups.com to find focus group opportunities. Make sure to read the requirements carefully and provide honest feedback.

8. Offer Cleaning Services

If you're organized and detail-oriented, you can offer cleaning services to your neighbors or local businesses. You can offer to clean homes, offices, or other spaces. Make sure to have a clear understanding of your responsibilities and any necessary safety precautions.

9. Sell Baked Goods

If you enjoy baking, you can sell your baked goods online. You can use platforms like Etsy or Shopify to sell your treats. Make sure to follow food safety guidelines and advertise your products on social media.

10. Participate in Online Gigs

There are several websites that offer online gigs like data entry, transcription, or graphic design. You can sign up for websites like Fiverr, Upwork, or Freelancer to start earning money. Make sure to have the necessary skills and equipment to complete the gigs.

Conclusion

There are many ways that you can make money fast in quarantine as a kid. Whether you choose to sell items online, offer services to your community, or participate in paid opportunities, make sure to follow safety precautions and provide high-quality work. With a little creativity and hard work, you can make a positive impact on your family's finances during these challenging times.
