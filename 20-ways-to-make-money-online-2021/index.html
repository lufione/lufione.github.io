20 Ways To Make Money Online In 2021

Written by Christine Jul 19, 2023 · 7 min read

In this digital age, there are countless ways to make money online. Whether you’re looking for a side hustle or a full-time income, there’s an opportunity out there for you. Here are 20 ways to make money online in 2021:

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing involves promoting other people’s products and earning a commission on every sale. You can promote products through your website, social media channels or email list. Some popular affiliate programs include Amazon Associates, Clickbank and ShareASale.

Dropshipping

Dropshipping allows you to sell products online without holding any inventory. You simply create an online store and when a customer makes a purchase, the order is fulfilled by a third-party supplier. You earn a profit on the difference between the wholesale and retail price.

Online Surveys

Companies are always looking for feedback from consumers and they’re willing to pay for it. You can earn money by completing online surveys on websites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie and Vindale Research.

Freelance Writing

If you have a talent for writing, you can make money by offering your services as a freelance writer. You can find clients on freelance websites like Upwork and Freelancer or by pitching to publications directly.

Sell Digital Products

You can create and sell digital products like eBooks, courses, and printables. You can sell these products on your own website or on platforms like Gumroad and Etsy.

Virtual Assistant

Virtual assistants provide administrative support to businesses and entrepreneurs. You can offer services like email management, social media management and customer support. You can find clients on freelance websites or by reaching out to businesses directly.

Online Coaching

If you have expertise in a particular area, you can offer online coaching services. You can offer coaching in areas like fitness, business, and personal development. You can find clients on social media or by creating a website.

Online Tutoring

You can make money by tutoring students online. You can offer tutoring services in subjects like math, science, and language. You can find clients on tutoring platforms like Tutor.com and Chegg.

Stock Photography

If you have a talent for photography, you can make money by selling your photos on stock photography websites like Shutterstock and iStock. You earn a commission every time someone downloads your photo.

Online Store

You can create an online store and sell physical products. You can use platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce to create your store.

Transcription

You can make money by transcribing audio and video files. You can find transcription jobs on freelance websites like Rev and TranscribeMe.

Website Testing

You can make money by testing websites and providing feedback to website owners. You can find website testing jobs on websites like UserTesting and TryMyUI.

Content Writing

You can make money by writing content for websites and blogs. You can find content writing jobs on freelance websites or by reaching out to businesses directly.

Online Bookkeeping

You can make money by offering bookkeeping services to businesses. You can find clients on freelance websites or by reaching out to businesses directly.

Online Data Entry

You can make money by doing data entry tasks online. You can find data entry jobs on websites like Clickworker and Amazon Mechanical Turk.

Podcasting

You can make money by starting a podcast and earning sponsorships and advertising revenue. You can use platforms like Anchor and Buzzsprout to create and distribute your podcast.

Social Media Management

You can make money by managing social media accounts for businesses. You can find clients on freelance websites or by reaching out to businesses directly.

Online Translation

If you’re bilingual, you can make money by offering translation services online. You can find translation jobs on websites like ProZ and TranslatorsCafé.

Online Research

You can make money by doing research tasks online. You can find research jobs on websites like Wonder and AskWonder.

Online Design

If you have a talent for design, you can make money by offering design services online. You can find clients on freelance websites or by reaching out to businesses directly.

These are just a few of the many ways you can make money online in 2021. With a little creativity and hard work, you can turn your skills and passions into a profitable online business.
