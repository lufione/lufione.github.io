5. Join a Club

6. Watch Ads

The game provides players with the option to watch ads in exchange for money and other resources. Make sure to take advantage of this option to earn extra money and resources.

7. Save Your SimCash

SimCash is the game's premium currency that can be purchased with real money. Save your SimCash for important purchases such as expanding your city or purchasing special items. Make sure to use your SimCash wisely.

8. Keep Your City Efficient

Make sure to keep your city efficient by providing it with services such as transportation and education. This will increase the happiness of your residents and the amount of money they generate.
