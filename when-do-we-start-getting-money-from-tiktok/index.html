When Do We Start Getting Money From Tiktok

Written by Robby Aug 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Since its launch in 2016, TikTok has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world. With over a billion active users, it's no surprise that many people are wondering when they can start making money from the app. In this article, we'll explore the different ways you can earn money on TikTok and when you can expect to start seeing some cash.

How Do You Make Money on TikTok?

There are several ways to make money on TikTok. The most popular methods include:

1. Creator Fund

The Creator Fund is a program that pays eligible TikTok creators for their content. To be eligible, you must be 18 years old, have at least 100,000 followers, and have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days. If you meet these requirements, you can apply to be part of the Creator Fund and start earning money based on your views and engagement.

2. Brand Deals

Many TikTok creators make money by partnering with brands and promoting their products on their channels. If you have a large following and create high-quality content, brands may approach you with offers for sponsored posts or product placements.

3. Merchandise Sales

If you have a loyal following on TikTok, you can sell merchandise related to your content. This can include t-shirts, hats, phone cases, and other items that your fans might be interested in buying.

When Can You Start Making Money on TikTok?

The short answer is that it depends. If you're looking to join the Creator Fund, you'll need to meet the eligibility requirements and apply to be part of the program. Once you're accepted, you can start earning money based on your views and engagement.

If you're interested in brand deals or merchandise sales, you can start looking for opportunities as soon as you have a following. However, it's important to keep in mind that brands are looking for creators who have a large and engaged audience, so it may take some time to build up your following and start getting offers.

Tips for Making Money on TikTok

If you're looking to make money on TikTok, here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Post Regularly

The more you post, the more likely you are to attract followers and build an audience. Try to post at least once a day and experiment with different types of content to see what resonates with your audience.

2. Engage with Your Followers

Respond to comments, create duets with other creators, and participate in challenges to engage with your followers and build a community around your content.

3. Create High-Quality Content

Invest in good lighting, sound equipment, and editing software to create high-quality videos that stand out from the crowd.

4. Stay Up-to-Date on TikTok Trends

Keep an eye on what's trending on TikTok and incorporate those trends into your content. This can help you attract new followers and keep your existing audience engaged.

FAQs

1. How much money can you make on TikTok?

The amount of money you can make on TikTok varies depending on your following, engagement, and the methods you use to earn money. Some creators make a few hundred dollars per month, while others make thousands or even millions.

2. How long does it take to get paid from the Creator Fund?

The Creator Fund pays out on a monthly basis, so you can expect to receive your earnings at the end of each month.

3. Can you make money on TikTok without a large following?

It's possible to make money on TikTok without a large following, but it's more difficult. You may need to focus on building up your following and engagement before you can start earning significant income.

Conclusion

If you're looking to make money on TikTok, there are several methods you can use, including the Creator Fund, brand deals, and merchandise sales. While it may take some time to build up your following and start earning money, with dedication and hard work, it's possible to turn your TikTok hobby into a lucrative career.
