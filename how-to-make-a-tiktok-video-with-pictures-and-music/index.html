How To Make A Tiktok Video With Pictures And Music

Written by William Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Introduction

TikTok is a popular social media platform that allows users to share short-form videos with their followers. One of the best features of TikTok is the ability to create videos using pictures and music. In this article, we will show you how to make a TikTok video with pictures and music in easy, step-by-step instructions.

Step 1: Download TikTok App

The first step in making a TikTok video is to download the TikTok app on your smartphone. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices and can be downloaded for free from the App Store or Google Play.

Step 2: Open TikTok App and Click "+" Button

Once you have downloaded and installed the TikTok app, open it on your smartphone. Click on the "+" button at the bottom of the screen to start creating a new video.

Step 3: Select Pictures and Music

Next, select the pictures and music that you want to use in your TikTok video. You can choose up to 12 photos and a 60-second music clip from your smartphone's library. You can also search for music using TikTok's built-in music library.

Step 4: Arrange Pictures and Adjust Music

After selecting pictures and music, you can arrange them in the order you want them to appear in your TikTok video. You can also adjust the music's volume and select the part of the song you want to use.

Step 5: Add Effects and Filters

TikTok offers various effects and filters that you can use to enhance your video's look and feel. You can add filters to your pictures, adjust the video's speed, and use various effects like slow-motion, time-lapse, and reverse.

Step 6: Record Your Video

Once you have selected pictures, music, and added effects, it's time to record your TikTok video. Hold down the red button at the bottom of the screen to start recording, and release it when you're done.

Step 7: Edit Your Video

After recording your video, you can edit it to make it look even better. You can trim the video, add text, stickers, and more.

Step 8: Post Your Video

When you're done editing your TikTok video, click on the "Next" button, add a caption, hashtags, and location, and post your video to your TikTok account.

Tips and Tricks

- Use high-quality pictures and music to make your TikTok video look professional. - Keep your TikTok video short and sweet, under 60 seconds is best. - Use TikTok's built-in features like effects, filters, and text to enhance your video. - Use trending hashtags to increase your video's visibility on TikTok. - Share your TikTok video on other social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram to gain more followers.

Conclusion

Making a TikTok video with pictures and music is a fun and easy way to share your creativity with the world. With the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can create professional-looking TikTok videos that will impress your followers and gain more likes and views. So, grab your smartphone, download the TikTok app, and start creating your first TikTok video today!
