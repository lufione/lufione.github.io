How A 10 Year Old Can Make Money

Written by William Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a 10 year old looking to make some extra cash? You're not alone! Many kids your age are interested in earning money for things like toys, games, or even saving for college. Here are some tips on how you can get started:

1. Start With Your Family and Friends

One of the easiest ways to make money is by doing chores for your family or neighbors. Offer to mow lawns, walk dogs, or wash cars for a small fee. You can also ask your parents if they have any extra work you can do around the house for a small allowance.

2. Sell Your Crafts or Artwork

Are you creative? Turn your hobby into a business by making and selling crafts or artwork. You can set up a stand at a local craft fair or even sell your items online through websites like Etsy.

3. Babysit or Pet Sit

Many parents are in need of a reliable babysitter or pet sitter. If you have experience with younger siblings or pets, offer your services to families in your neighborhood. Make sure to get permission from your parents and any necessary certifications before taking on these jobs.

4. Participate in Online Surveys

There are many companies that pay people to participate in online surveys. While the pay may not be much, it's an easy way to earn some extra cash in your free time. Make sure to get permission from your parents before signing up for any survey sites.

5. Sell Items You No Longer Need

Do you have toys, clothes, or electronics that you no longer use? Sell them! You can have a garage sale, sell items on websites like eBay or Craigslist, or even trade them in for cash at a local pawn shop.

6. Offer Your Tutoring Services

Are you good at a particular subject in school? Offer to tutor younger students in that subject for a small fee. You can advertise your services at your school or through social media.

7. Participate in Paid Focus Groups

Some companies will pay people to participate in focus groups where they give their opinions on products or services. Check with your parents to see if they know of any legitimate focus groups in your area.

8. Do Yard Work for Your Neighbors

Offer to do yard work for your neighbors, such as raking leaves or pulling weeds. Make sure to get permission from your parents and any necessary certifications before taking on these jobs.

9. Offer Your Photography Services

If you have a knack for photography, offer your services to families or businesses in your area. You can take family portraits, pet photos, or even photos for small businesses.

10. Create and Sell Digital Products

If you're tech-savvy, consider creating and selling digital products like e-books or printables. You can sell your products on websites like Amazon or Etsy.

Conclusion

As a 10 year old, there are many ways to make money that are fun and easy. Remember to always get permission from your parents and any necessary certifications before taking on any jobs. With hard work and determination, you can reach your money-making goals in no time!
