Ways For 11 Year Olds To Make Money In Quarantine

Written by Petter May 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic has affected people's lives in many ways, including their finances. Many families are struggling to make ends meet, and kids are feeling the pinch too. If you're an 11 year old looking for ways to make money during quarantine, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll explore some ideas to help you earn some extra cash.

1. Sell Your Crafts

If you're good at making things, consider selling your crafts online. You can set up a store on Etsy or eBay and sell your creations to people all over the world. Some popular crafts include handmade jewelry, crocheted items, and custom t-shirts.

2. Offer Your Services

Are you good at mowing lawns, walking dogs, or babysitting? Offer your services to people in your neighborhood. You can advertise your services on social media or community bulletin boards. Just be sure to get your parents' permission and always prioritize your safety.

3. Start a Blog or YouTube Channel

If you're passionate about a particular topic, consider starting a blog or YouTube channel. You can monetize your content through ads or sponsored posts. Just be sure to follow the rules and guidelines set by the platform you choose.

4. Participate in Online Surveys

There are many websites that offer paid surveys to people of all ages. While you won't get rich taking surveys, it's an easy way to earn some extra cash in your spare time. Some popular survey websites include Swagbucks and Survey Junkie.

5. Sell Your Old Stuff

Do you have old toys, clothes, or electronics lying around? Consider selling them online. You can use websites like eBay or Facebook Marketplace to reach a wide audience. Just be sure to get your parents' permission before selling anything.

6. Offer Tutoring Services

If you're good at a particular subject, consider offering tutoring services to younger students. You can advertise your services online or through word of mouth. Just be sure to have a clear plan in place and consider charging a reasonable rate.

7. Sell Your Photos

If you're good at photography, consider selling your photos online. You can use websites like Shutterstock or iStock to sell your images to businesses and individuals. Just be sure to have your parents' permission and follow the rules and guidelines set by the platform you choose.

8. Participate in Online Contests

Many websites and companies offer online contests with cash prizes. You can participate in these contests and earn some extra cash if you win. Just be sure to read the rules and guidelines carefully and always prioritize your safety.

9. Offer Social Media Management Services

If you're good at social media, consider offering social media management services to small businesses or individuals. You can help them create content, manage their accounts, and grow their following. Just be sure to have a clear plan in place and consider charging a reasonable rate.

10. Create and Sell Digital Products

If you're good at graphic design, coding, or writing, consider creating and selling digital products. You can sell things like e-books, printables, or website templates on platforms like Gumroad or Etsy. Just be sure to have your parents' permission and follow the rules and guidelines set by the platform you choose.

Conclusion

Making money as an 11 year old during quarantine may seem challenging, but there are plenty of opportunities out there. Whether you choose to sell your crafts, offer your services, or participate in online surveys or contests, the key is to be creative and persistent. Always prioritize your safety and get your parents' permission before starting any money-making venture. Good luck!
