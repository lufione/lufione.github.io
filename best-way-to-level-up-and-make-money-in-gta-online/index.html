Best Way To Level Up And Make Money In Gta Online

Written by Eveline Jul 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

GTA Online is a massive multiplayer game that has been around for years. The game has a lot to offer, from robbing banks to racing cars. But, if you want to level up fast and make a lot of money, there are some tips and tricks you need to know. In this article, we will share with you the best way to level up and make money in GTA Online in 2023.

1. Start with heists

Heists are a great way to make money in GTA Online. They require teamwork and planning, but the payout is worth it. To start a heist, you need to be at least level 12 and own a high-end apartment. Once you have those, you can call Lester to set up a heist. The first heist is the Fleeca Job, which has a payout of $143,750. As you progress, the heists will become more difficult, but the payout will also increase.

2. Invest in businesses

Investing in businesses is another great way to make money in GTA Online. You can buy businesses such as a nightclub, a bunker, or a motorcycle club. These businesses will generate passive income, and you can also do missions related to them to earn more money. To buy a business, you need to have enough money and be at a certain level. Owning a business will also give you access to new vehicles and weapons.

3. Participate in races

Racing is a fun way to make money in GTA Online. You can participate in races by going to the race icon on the map or through the Quick Job menu. Racing will not only give you money but also RP (reputation points), which will help you level up faster. You can also create your own races and invite your friends to participate. The more people participate in your race, the more money and RP you will earn.

4. Do VIP work

VIP work is a great way to make money in GTA Online. To become a VIP, you need to have at least $50,000 in your bank account. Once you become a VIP, you can do VIP work such as Headhunter, Sightseer, and Hostile Takeover. These missions will give you a payout of $20,000 to $30,000. You can also invite your friends to help you with these missions and split the payout.

5. Grind in survival mode

Survival mode is a challenging but rewarding way to make money in GTA Online. In this mode, you and your team need to survive waves of enemies. The longer you survive, the more money and RP you will earn. You can also do survival mode solo, but it will be more difficult. The payout for survival mode ranges from $20,000 to $30,000.

6. Sell cars

You can make money by selling cars in GTA Online. To do this, you need to steal cars and take them to Los Santos Customs to sell them. The payout for selling cars ranges from $5,000 to $9,500. You can also sell cars to other players, but the payout will depend on the car's value.

7. Do contact missions

Contact missions are a great way to make money in GTA Online. You can do contact missions by going to the job icon on the map or through the Quick Job menu. Contact missions will give you a payout of $10,000 to $25,000. You can also invite your friends to help you with these missions and split the payout.

8. Participate in double RP and money events

Rockstar Games frequently hosts double RP and money events in GTA Online. During these events, you can earn double RP and money for doing certain missions and activities. You can check the Rockstar Games website or social media pages to know when these events are happening.

9. Use the stock market

The stock market is a feature in GTA Online that allows you to invest in the stock market of the game's fictional companies. If you invest wisely, you can make a lot of money. You can also manipulate the stock market by doing certain missions and activities. However, be careful because if you invest in the wrong company, you can lose a lot of money.

10. Join a crew

Joining a crew is a great way to make money and level up in GTA Online. You can join a crew by going to the Social Club website or through the in-game menu. Being part of a crew will give you access to exclusive missions and activities. You can also do missions with your crewmates and split the payout. Additionally, being part of a crew will give you a sense of community and make the game more enjoyable.

Conclusion

These are the best ways to level up and make money in GTA Online in 2023. The game is constantly evolving, so be sure to check for updates and new features. Remember, the key to success in GTA Online is to have fun and enjoy the game. Happy gaming!
